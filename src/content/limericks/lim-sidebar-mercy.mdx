<Limerick type="danger" title="The Off-Ledger Fix" icon="tool">
The process was broken and slow,  
So we took it where audits don’t go.  
A quick sidebar tweak,  
By a glorious geek,  
And nobody ever will know.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Quiet Favor" icon="heart">
The Mercy was rendered off-stage,  
Away from the manager’s rage.  
It lessened the harm,  
With a secretive charm,  
And left not a trace on the page.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Unofficial Relief" icon="zap">
If you wait for the ticket to clear,  
You’ll be drowning in sorrow and fear.  
But a favor outside,  
Takes the rule for a ride,  
And the bullshit can just disappear.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Shrank to Nil" icon="minimize-2">
The sidebar requested some grace  
But taking up too much of space  
We shrank it to nil  
And gave it a pill  
And wiped the whole thing off the face  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Broken Layout" icon="layout">
A mercy was never defined  
The manual totally blind  
The layout was broken  
No words were e'er spoken  
We left the whole sidebar behind  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Vacuum Full" icon="wind">
A procedural mercy is null  
The logic is terribly dull  
The human makes faults  
The server defaults  
The vacuum is perfectly full  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Memory Hole" icon="download-cloud">
The sidebar was taking a toll  
We gave it a meaningless role  
To sit on the side  
And quietly hide  
And fall down the memory hole  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Empty and Lean" icon="battery">
The mercy was flat and unseen  
It never arrived on the screen  
The error was logged  
The system was bogged  
The archive is empty and lean  
</Limerick>
