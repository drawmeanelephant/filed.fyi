<Limerick type="note" title="Hollow Appeal" icon="file">
The ledger requires a seal,
Though nothing inside it is real.
The quiet descends,
Where the vacancy ends,
With a stationary, hollow appeal.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Corridor Grey" icon="file">
A corridor built in the grey,
Where validation will rust and decay.
The delay is the prize,
In the applicant's eyes,
And the silence will never give way.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Silent Omission" icon="file">
The sidecar continues its pace,
A draft in an empty grey space.
The conflict was filed,
The texts undefiled,
A silent omission in place.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Yielding Corridor" icon="file">
The proxy remembers no name,
Redaction completing the frame.
The boundary stays,
In automated haze,
A corridor yielding the same.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Hollowed Record" icon="file">
The record was hollowed and torn,
A silence where meaning was born.
The porter delays,
Through absent displays,
Of files that the structure must mourn.
</Limerick>
