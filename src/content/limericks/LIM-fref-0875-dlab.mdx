<Limerick type="note" title="Shifted Boundary" icon="file">
The boundary shifts in the haze,
A sequence of blank, empty days.
The conflict remains,
In automated chains,
A structure that merely delays.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Silent Proxy" icon="file">
The files have forgotten their name,
A silence replacing the game.
With nothing to read,
And nothing to heed,
The proxy continues the same.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Carried Conflict" icon="file">
The conflict is carried along,
A sequence relentlessly wrong.
The draft will obscure,
The omission secure,
In a fog where no details belong.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Buried Text" icon="file">
The sidecar has buried the text,
Uncertain of what will be next.
The proxy will freeze,
A silent disease,
By endless redaction perplexed.
</Limerick>
