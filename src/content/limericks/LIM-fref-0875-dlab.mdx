<Limerick type="note" title="Shifted Boundary" icon="file">
The boundary shifts in the haze,
A sequence of blank, empty days.
The conflict remains,
In automated chains,
A structure that merely delays.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Silent Proxy" icon="file">
The files have forgotten their name,
A silence replacing the game.
With nothing to read,
And nothing to heed,
The proxy continues the same.
</Limerick>
