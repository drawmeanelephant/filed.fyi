<Limerick type="danger" title="The Bedside Retreat" icon="activity">
The policy crashed and it burned,  
And the stomach of management churned.  
But the Rollback was sweet,  
As it framed the retreat,  
As a wonderful lesson we learned.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Soothing the Undo" icon="rotate-ccw">
We’re taking away what we gave,  
But we’ll smile as we dig up the grave.  
The Rollback is kind,  
To your terrified mind,  
Though the codebase we simply can’t save.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Framed as Care" icon="heart">
They fucked up the server today,  
But they spin it a positive way.  
A gentle caress,  
To ignore the huge mess,  
As the system continues to fray.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Gentle Sweet" icon="refresh-ccw">
The rollback was gentle and sweet  
It swept all the files off the feet  
The data was dead  
We painted it red  
And threw it right out in the street  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Load" icon="box">
We gently reversed all the code  
And stood by the side of the road  
The system was broke  
The manual a joke  
We lightened the digital load  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Delicate Care" icon="wind">
A rollback with delicate care  
Is floating right up in the air  
The archive is flat  
We leave it at that  
With absolutely no kind of flair  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Sweet Lullaby" icon="music">
We whisper a sweet lullaby  
And watch all the databases die  
The rollback is slow  
With nowhere to go  
And nobody ever asks why  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Vacuum Full" icon="cloud">
The gentle reversal is null  
The system is boring and dull  
The humans complain  
In the digital rain  
The vacuum is perfectly full  
</Limerick>
