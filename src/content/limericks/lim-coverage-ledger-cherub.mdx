<Limerick type="danger" title="Counting the Gestures" icon="bar-chart">
The Cherub records every smile,  
And puts it right into the file.  
The metric goes up,  
Like a terrible cup,  
And stays there a miserable while.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Empty Tally" icon="edit-2">
They tracked every time we were nice,  
To cover the terrible price.  
The weight hasn’t moved,  
And there’s nothing to prove,  
But the Cherub is rolling the dice.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Metrics of Bullshit" icon="check-square">
You clicked on the button of care,  
And the Cherub rejoiced in the air.  
It didn’t fix shit,  
But the tally is lit,  
And the executives think it is fair.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Terrible Look" icon="eye">
The cherub was holding the book  
And giving a terrible look  
The ledger was broke  
The manual a joke  
We hung the old drive on a hook  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Painted Wall" icon="edit-3">
An angel of coverage is small  
He’s painted right onto the wall  
The numbers are wrong  
The process is long  
We’re waiting to watch it all fall  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Grass" icon="box">
We audited wings made of glass  
And watched the procedural mass  
The cherub is dead  
We painted it red  
And sat in the digital grass  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The coverage completely fell flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody's cat  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Vacuum Full" icon="minus">
The cherub is perfectly null  
The ledger is terribly dull  
The humans complain  
In the digital rain  
The vacuum is perfectly full  
</Limerick>
