<Limerick type="note" title="Institutional Scaffolding">
A temporary slip in the queue,  
Grew a desk and a manager too.  
The failure remains,  
Amidst all the gains,  
With paperwork entirely new.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="The Permanent Exception">
We hired a clerk for the glitch,  
Who dug a magnificent ditch.  
The problem is neat,  
And fully complete,  
With a permanent manual switch.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Dependent Survival">
To fix the original flaw,  
Means breaking the continuity law.  
The support staff would leave,  
And everyone grieve,  
The removal of such a great draw.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="tip" title="Domestication">
We trained it to sit in the hall,  
And answer a standardized call.  
The error is tamed,  
Officially named,  
And mounted right there on the wall.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Ritual Maintenance">
A ritual sweeps up the dust,  
To polish the structural rust.  
The failure is dear,  
Appearing each year,  
Supported by infinite trust.  
</Limerick>
