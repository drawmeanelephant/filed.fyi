<Limerick type="note" title="Room Made of Gray" icon="file">
An ergonomic chair in a room made of gray,
Where applicants come just to wither away.
The silence is deep,
As the protocols sleep,
Ensuring the process is lost to delay.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Binders of Lead" icon="file">
The vacancy echoes through binders of lead,
With columns and tables entirely unread.
We wait for the stamp,
In the sterile and damp,
While validation is quietly dead.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Vacant Chair" icon="file">
A vacancy sits in the chair,
With ledgers piled up in the air.
The waiting is long,
And the process is wrong,
But the corridor does not quite care.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Filed Appendix" icon="folder">
An appendix was filed in the back,
On a desolate, gray metal rack.
The margin is tight,
In the dim fading light,
And the silence continues its track.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Formalized State" icon="file-text">
The delay is a formalized state,
Where the waiting will never abate.
The ledger is closed,
As the system proposed,
And the silence determines their fate.
</Limerick>
