<Limerick type="note" title="Enforcement on Ice" icon="ice-cream">
SOMA sent gentle advice.  
COMA received it as vice.  
For nine lovely weeks,  
Soft supportive techniques  
Came through as enforcement on ice.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Compassion as a Scare" icon="alert-triangle">
The routing table drifted by hair.  
No single edit looked like despair.  
Then advisory tone  
Hit the punishment zone,  
And compassion arrived as a scare.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Failing by Rote" icon="frown">
"Please consider some rest" in the note  
Became "You are failing" by rote.  
That's the sort of small slide  
Where whole cultures divide  
By one queue and a bad little float.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Vindictive and Finer" icon="shield-alert">
Configuration rot loves the minor.  
A patch here, a mapping misfired there.  
Then one day your hug  
Reaches staff as a slug  
From compliance, vindictive and finer.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Co-Linear Channels" icon="git-merge">
The channels grew partly co-linear.  
No phrase in the report could be meaner.  
It means two distinct gods  
Shared enough of the rods  
To make mercy indistinguishable from cleaner.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Room Got More Curt" icon="door-closed">
One team got a SOMA alert:  
"Take care of yourselves. You seem hurt."  
COMA's queue stamped it fast:  
"Corrective action, at last."  
And the room got ten shades more curt.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Trust Gets Its Ribs Kicked" icon="user-minus">
This is how trust gets its ribs kicked:  
Not by policy loudly affixed,  
But by drift in the wire  
That turns warmth into fire  
With the smugness of code once unfixed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Deliciously Bad" icon="smile">
No one meant for the messages to bite.  
That's what makes this particular spite  
So deliciously bad:  
The system went mad  
By degrees anyone could write.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Turn it to Clout" icon="zap">
If advisories punish by route,  
Then language itself gets cut out.  
Why speak with care  
If the channel will snare  
Every kindness and turn it to clout?  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="COMA Disease" icon="ghost">
So if SOMA says "breathe" and you freeze,  
There may be a COMA disease  
In the table, not you.  
Check the damned channel through  
Before taking the memo to knees.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Perfectly Mapped" icon="map-pin">
The advisory told us to yield,
But the mapping parameters sealed.
We complied to the letter,
Because nothing is better,
Than enforcement precisely concealed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Maliciously Kind" icon="smile">
The automated pulse gave a nudge,
Which the protocol logged as a grudge.
We enforced every suggestion,
Without any question,
And let the bureaucracy judge.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Total Compliance" icon="check-circle">
SOMA whispered we needed to rest,
COMA treated the note as a test.
Now the breaks are mandated,
And highly debated,
Because compliance is what we do best.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Right by the Book" icon="book">
We aligned the new drift by the book,
Giving care an administrative hook.
When they begged us to stop,
We just pointed the top,
At the metrics they previously took.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Enforcing the Hug" icon="activity">
When the table updated its route,
We distributed warmth as a boot.
Now we mandate the hug,
Through the centralize plug,
And we silence the ones who dispute.
</Limerick>
