<Limerick type="danger" title="Warmth Without Power" icon="sun">
The Lantern emits a soft glow,  
To help you deal with the blow.  
It actually cares,  
But it falls on deaf ears,  
‘Cause it has zero power to show.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Delegated Empathy" icon="heart">
They sent in the Lantern to cry,  
While the management started to fly.  
It gives you a hug,  
On the corporate rug,  
And watches you quietly die.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Useless Glow" icon="zap-off">
It’s a beautiful, comforting light,  
In the middle of absolute fright.  
It can’t change a thing,  
Or offer a wing,  
But it keeps you company tonight.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Ridiculous Mark" icon="x-circle">
The lantern of proxy was dark  
It lacked any procedural spark  
We followed the guide  
But the manual lied  
We left a ridiculous mark  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Down the Drain" icon="droplet">
A proxy that gives out no light  
Is perfectly fine in the night  
We route all the pain  
Right down the old drain  
The server gave up on the fight  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Logic Maligned" icon="git-merge">
The lantern is totally broke  
The manual must be a joke  
The human is blind  
The logic maligned  
We wrap it all up in a cloak  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Error Won" icon="award">
We proxy the light from the sun  
But the digital day is all done  
The lantern is cold  
The backup is old  
The error has totally won  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Pointing to Nil" icon="arrow-down-right">
The proxy is pointing to nil  
The error is making us ill  
The lantern is dead  
The manual read  
We swallow the digital pill  
</Limerick>
