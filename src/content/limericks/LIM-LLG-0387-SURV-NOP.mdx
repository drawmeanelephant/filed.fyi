<Limerick type="note" title="Observed Nothing Well" icon="eye">
The auditors came for a peek,  
Found no live operation all week.  
But the binders were tight,  
And the posture was right,  
So the system was judged pretty chic.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Evidence of Evidence" icon="folders">
They inspected the proof of the proof,  
Which is audit erotica, poof.  
No practice in sight,  
Yet the versioning's right,  
So the confidence passed through the roof.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Mature Handling" icon="badge-check">
One note asked, with admirable spite,  
If they'd certified handling things right  
Rather than the thing done.  
That objection lost sun,  
For it wasn't headline-visible quite.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Corridor Walk" icon="footprints">
They did opening, closing, and stroll,  
And reviewed every scope-loving role.  
If no work can be seen,  
Keep the hallway serene,  
And let document control play the whole.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Immaculate Non-Work" icon="archive">
We watched them do nothing all day,  
But their binders were nicely on tray.  
No work to review,  
Just paperwork stew,  
We formally approved their delay.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Hallway Patrol" icon="ghost">
The ghosts of the process walked by,  
Beneath the surveillance team's eye.  
No mortal was seen,  
But the ledgers were clean,  
And legends of workflow don't die.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Document Hygiene Review" icon="file-check">
The records were placed in a line,  
And the fonts were correctly designed.  
The auditor stared  
At the margins prepared.  
The margins were formally fine.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Evidence of Proof" icon="check-square">
An audit was held on the floor,  
Though work had slipped out of the door.  
The files were immense,  
Full of pure evidence,  
Confirming the records of yore.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Unseen Excellence" icon="search">
The audit of nothing was grand,  
With paperwork spanning the land.  
No action was taken,  
No systems were shaken,  
But competence went as planned.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Silent Hall" icon="book-open">
They say that the binders of old,  
Contain a procedure of gold.  
No practice is needed,  
When forms are acceded,  
And stories of folders are told.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Document Control" icon="file-text">
The versioning hygiene was high,  
With never a visible sigh.  
We looked at the date,  
And certified state,  
While the actual process passed by.  
</Limerick>
