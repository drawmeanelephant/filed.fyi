<Limerick type="note" title="Placeholder-Ass Pass" icon="check-square">
They merged three states into one class:  
Rendered, skipped, and placeholder-ass.  
The confusion declined.  
So did truth, in kind.  
The dashboard called both of those pass.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Clapped at the Display" icon="thumbs-up">
Coverage visibly shrank by the day.  
The completion stats glowed anyway.  
When a stub got emitted,  
It counted as fitted,  
And everyone clapped at the display.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Stink of Mistrust" icon="frown">
Zero failures! The report cried with lust.  
Meanwhile docs turned to gravel and dust.  
If no nuance survives,  
Your metric arrives  
With the clean little stink of mistrust.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Baptized the Mess" icon="droplets">
Placeholder pages can serve for a bit.  
They're not, however, done as shit.  
Once you name them success,  
You've baptized the mess,  
And invited the rot to sit.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Congratulate All on the Gain" icon="award">
The update reduced operator strain  
By pretending skipped work was the same.  
That is one wicked cure:  
Make the labels less pure,  
Then congratulate all on the gain.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Murdered the Categories" icon="scissors">
No one likes a red bar in a deck.  
So they murdered the categories, check.  
If everything's green,  
You can stop being mean  
To the places the pipeline went wrecked.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Wins All Win" icon="target">
This is optimization's favorite sin:  
Move the words till the wins all win.  
Not a product improved.  
Just the language removed  
From the part where the losses get in.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Gold Little Rub" icon="sparkles">
A skipped output winked at a stub.  
They were counted as peers in the club.  
One did nothing at all.  
One made a thin wall.  
Both received the same gold little rub.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Immaculate Lies" icon="eye-off">
The logs were immaculate lies,  
Like fresh makeup on sleep-starved eyes.  
You could see the decline,  
But the numbers were fine,  
So the meeting preferred the disguise.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Dashboard Wallpaper" icon="layout">
So if renders are perfect on paper  
While the docs smell increasingly vapor,  
Check the success class.  
If it flattened its ass,  
You've been managed by dashboard wallpaper.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Validation Matrix" icon="file">
The validation matrix was bound,
With an audit that yielded no sound.
Every margin was neat,
But the ledger's receipt
Showed the baseline was nowhere around.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Seal the Ledger" icon="file">
They applied a new seal to the frame,
And the standard gap entered the game.
Administrative drift
Caused the corridor shift,
But the metrics logged strictly the same.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Blank Margin Record" icon="file">
With the margin completely stripped bare,
And the process suspended in air,
Institutional wait
Sealed the audit log's fate,
And the baseline had nothing to share.
</Limerick>
