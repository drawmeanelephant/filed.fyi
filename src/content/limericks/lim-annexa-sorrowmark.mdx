<Limerick type="note" title="Marginal Grief" icon="file-minus">
The sorrow was far too profound,  
To put in the document's bound.  
Annexa appeared,  
And the feeling was cleared,  
To an index where nothing is found.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Burden of Appendices" icon="paperclip">
If the pain is disrupting the flow,  
To the secondary records it’ll go.  
The Sorrowmark guides  
Where the tragedy hides,  
In a footnote the system won’t show.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Relegated Sorrow" icon="archive">
The main text is shiny and brief,  
Completely devoid of all grief.  
Annexa stands by  
With a formalized sigh,  
To offer a hidden relief.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Fucking Depressing" icon="frown">
The layoffs were brutal and swift,  
And the company needed a lift.  
Annexa swooped down,  
With her corporate frown,  
And swept all the blood in the rift.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Appendix of Pain" icon="book-open">
We packaged the trauma away,  
So the boardroom could have a good day.  
The Sorrowmark hid,  
Everything that we did,  
In an annex of deep, dark decay.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Sanitized History" icon="shield">
The records are squeaky and clean,  
Like a perfectly running machine.  
But Annexa knows well,  
Of the bodies that fell,  
And the violence that happens unseen.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Marginal Containment" icon="file-text">
The metric of pain was defined,  
And strictly to margins confined.  
Annexa ensured,  
The data endured,  
Where management never will find.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Thermal Event Filing" icon="flame">
The system began to expire,  
The network was catching on fire.  
Annexa then filed,  
The screaming compiled,  
As "thermal events to admire."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Apiary Archival" icon="archive">
The grief was assigned to a drive,  
To keep the department alive.  
Annexa was certain,  
She closed the right curtain,  
By archiving bees in a hive.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Stable Top-Line" icon="bar-chart">
The top-level view is quite clear,  
There isn't a problem this year.  
Annexa just hid it,  
She already did it,  
The backup is somewhere near here.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Legendary Margins" icon="book">
The slides were a beautiful hue,  
Concealing the horrors we knew.  
Annexa the scribe,  
Concealed the whole tribe,  
In margins where legends once grew.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Comma Trauma" icon="alert-circle">

The system was instantly dead.  
Annexa reacted,  
The margin extracted,  
And filed it as trauma instead.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Network Receipt" icon="check-square">

Annexa reviewed,  
The data accrued,  
And closed out the system retreat.  
</Limerick>
