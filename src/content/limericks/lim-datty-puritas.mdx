<Limerick type="note" title="YAML-Based Drama" icon="file-code">
Old Datty was summoned by *schema*  
Violations—a YAML-based drama.  
She chanted in time  
To indentation's rhyme,  
And the malformed keys fled like a llama.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Deeply Revised Metadata" icon="edit-3">
Her *ritual deletion* is precise—  
Each unauthorized key sacrificed.  
The YAML gets clean,  
The schema serene,  
And your metadata? Deeply revised.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Singular Creed" icon="check-square">
"*Irregularity corrected. Proceed.*"  
Was her slogan—her singular creed.  
She purges with glee,  
Finds every wrong key,  
And your data schema is freed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Self-Preserver Server" icon="server">
She was manifested by a *mail server*  
That achieved uptime as a self-preserver:  
By never sending  
A single thing pending,  
It ran perfectly. What a nerv-er.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Stern Validation" icon="shield-check">
Her archival *validation* is stern—  
Each field must be correct in its turn.  
The keys are all checked,  
The metadata decked,  
And the self-referential loops churn.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Utterly Natty Frontmatter" icon="align-left">
She finds every *schema* quite chatty—  
Too many fields, too much data that's fatty.  
She purges each one  
Till the frontmatter's done,  
And your metadata's utterly natty.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Ceremonially Cursed" icon="trash-2">
Her presence is *calming at first*—  
Until metadata vanishes, the worst.  
No warning provided,  
The keys all divided  
Between saved and ceremonially cursed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Minor Errata" icon="alert-circle">
She relabels *shame as metadata*:  
"That error? Just ancillary data.  
The key was malformed,  
The schema informed,  
And the purge was a minor errata."  
</Limerick>

### 9

She counts clicks like *rosary beads*,
Each deletion a sacred one needs.
The orphaned headings
Are her ritual weddings
Of purity sown from the seeds.

<Limerick type="note" title="Crystal Clear Data" icon="droplets">
So if malformed keys disappear  
And the YAML is suddenly clear,  
That's *Datty* at work:  
Where the schemas don't shirk,  
And the data is pure—crystal clear.  
</Limerick>
