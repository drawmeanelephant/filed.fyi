<Limerick type="note" title="Not About Itself" icon="mirror">
The guidance announced with a sigh:  
"This form is not why you should cry."  
Yet the instant it spoke,  
The exclusion it wrote  
Became one more feeling nearby.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Administrative Feeling" icon="folder">
If 51-E made you tense,  
They called that administrative sense.  
Not impact, not pain,  
Just process strain,  
Which still sat there in plain evidence.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No Safe Elsewhere" icon="map">
They asked 404sy to find  
A route for the feelings declined.  
He searched every stair  
And came back with, "They are  
Officially lost, but still signed."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Kindy Reads It Anyway" icon="eye">
"I resent that Kindy will read."  
The rules said that line should recede.  
Kindy kept it in view,  
As the filing still knew  
That mascots are part of the bleed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Observed in Margin" icon="pen-tool">
Form-induced anxiety flared.  
The policy said it was spared.  
Then someone wrote, "Observed,"  
Which calmly preserved  
The exact thing the memo declared.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No Channel Yet" icon="inbox">
They promised a future chute  
For recursion and process dispute.  
That channel, of course,  
Never entered the course,  
So the feeling stayed parked at the root.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Event-Focused Please" icon="target">
"Remain event-focused," they said,  
While the form climbed right into your head.  
It is hard to look through  
The official review  
When the paperwork sleeps in your bed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Speculative Dread" icon="help-circle">
"I fear there'll be forms for this form."  
They labeled that worry non-norm.  
Now the case file exists  
With that fear in its lists,  
Like a forecast promoted to storm.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Quiet Escalation" icon="arrow-up-circle">
Queue exhaustion was called baseline,  
Then quietly sent up the line.  
Out of scope on the sheet,  
Still reviewed as concrete,  
Which is one elegant bureaucrat sign.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Misplaced but Present" icon="pin">
So if 51-E makes you ache,  
They will note it as some other stake.  
Not deleted, just bent,  
Officially mis-sent,  
Like a truth with no room to relocate.  
</Limerick>
<Limerick type="note" title="Compliance Mandate" icon="file-text">

We printed the forms without arguin'.
The ledger will show
We did exactly so,
And left the whole network enlargen'.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Audit Breakdown" icon="activity">
An audit was ordered at noon.
We complied, which resulted in ruin.
The liability trace
Took all of the space,
And the servers fell out of attune.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Instruction Ledger" icon="clipboard">
The ledger required an exact
Accounting of every last act.
We followed the rule
With our only good tool,
And the infrastructure simply cracked.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Margin Compliance" icon="edit-3">
In the margin, the rule was so clear.
Compliance was all we held dear.
We wrote what was said,
And the servers bled red,
While we filed the report for the year.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Stamp Liability" icon="check-circle">
The liability fell to the stamp.
Which we used till it caused us a cramp.
The breakdown was slow,
As we wanted to show,
That the mandate was firmly encamped.
</Limerick>
