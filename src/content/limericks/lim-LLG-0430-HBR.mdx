<Limerick type="note" title="A Pragmatic Rebranding">
A clerk found a tear in the form,  
And called it a procedural norm.  
"It's realism now,  
Don't furrow your brow,  
Just stamp it to weather the storm."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Endurance Praised">
The burden was heavy to hold,  
But changing the label was bold.  
We praised how they stood,  
As much as they could,  
While doing exactly as told.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Structural Wisdom">
A continuity error was caught,  
And sold as a lesson well-taught.  
The injury stays,  
Reframed as a phase,  
A permanence no one had bought.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="tip" title="Filing Accommodation">
To argue against the old flaw,  
Is now a clear breach of the law.  
We manage the weight,  
And call the load great,  
Ignoring the splintered old draw.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Archive's Dilemma">
The ledger will never confess,  
That realism started as mess.  
We file it away,  
And quietly pray,  
The paper absorbs the distress.  
</Limerick>
