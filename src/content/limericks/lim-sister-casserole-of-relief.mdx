<Limerick type="danger" title="The Stale Favor" icon="heart">
She brought you some food in the past,  
And the memory continues to last.  
But you’re starving today,  
In a terrible way,  
And the corporate shadow is cast.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Remembered Labor" icon="archive">
We log every kindness you do,  
To prove we are caring for you.  
The casserole’s gone,  
But the record lives on,  
To cover the terrible view.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Kitchen Continuity" icon="coffee">
The comfort was hot and it’s done,  
And you’re left in the cold with no sun.  
The Sister recalls  
In the desolate halls,  
That the care was already won.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Wrong Syntax" icon="x-circle">
The casserole brings no relief  
The data is causing us grief  
We baked it too long  
The syntax is wrong  
And that is the honest belief  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Cold Oven" icon="thermometer-snow">
The sister of sorrow and dread  
Is writing a file that is dead  
The oven is cold  
The backup is old  
The server is losing its head  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Dark System" icon="moon">
A spoonful of error and pain  
Is washing right down the old drain  
The flat file is stark  
We sit in the dark  
And wait for the system to rain  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Burned Code" icon="flame">
The recipe called for a byte  
We typed in the middle of night  
The casserole burned  
The tables were turned  
The monitor lost all its light  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Empty Array" icon="code">
Relief is an empty array  
It never was coming our way  
The sister is blind  
The rules left behind  
We’re closing the archive today  
</Limerick>
