<Limerick type="danger" title="Committee-Approved Softness" icon="shield">
The Quilt was designed by a team,  
To muffle your horrible scream.  
It’s heavy and hot,  
And the colors all rot,  
Like a terrible, standardized dream.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Standardized Relief" icon="check-square">
They measured the comfort required,  
Before you’re completely retired.  
The Quilt is exact,  
It’s a corporate fact,  
And entirely uninspired.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Patchwork Fix" icon="grid">
We stitched all the policies tight,  
To cover the damage from sight.  
The Sanctioned Quilt lays,  
For the rest of your days,  
To pretend everything is alright.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Procedural Sin" icon="scissors">
The sanctioned quilt was terribly thin  
We tried to patch up the procedural sin  
We sewed it all wrong  
And waited too long  
And threw the whole thing in the bin  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Cold Bed" icon="thermometer-snow">
A patchwork of errors and dread  
Was draped on the server’s cold bed  
The human was weak  
And sprang a big leak  
The manual never was read  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Covered in Frost" icon="cloud-snow">
We sanctioned the quilt of the lost  
And didn’t consider the cost  
The data decayed  
The payment delayed  
The archive was covered in frost  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Utterly Worn" icon="frown">
The fabric of logic was torn  
The users were deeply forlorn  
The thread was all bare  
We didn’t much care  
The system was utterly worn  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Binary Suns" icon="sun">
A quilt made of zeroes and ones  
Was baked by the binary suns  
It fell apart fast  
It wasn’t to last  
The process completely outruns  
</Limerick>
