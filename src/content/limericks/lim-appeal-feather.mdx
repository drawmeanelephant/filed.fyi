<Limerick type="danger" title="The Delicate Dissent" icon="feather">
You wanted to smash up the room,  
And deliver a verdict of doom.  
But you filed a small note,  
That will peacefully float,  
And be swept by the manager’s broom.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Low-Force Objection" icon="wind">
The Feather goes up in the air,  
To pretend that they actually care.  
It rises so slow,  
With a beautiful glow,  
And achieves fucking nothing up there.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Rising Without Rupture" icon="arrow-up">
They gave you a form to protest,  
That puts your frustration to rest.  
The Feather takes flight,  
In the fading light,  
And the system continues its quest.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="note" title="Completely Free" icon="wind">
The feather was filing a plea  
And fell right down into the sea  
The appeal was denied  
The manual cried  
The logic is completely free  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No Kind Care" icon="slash">
An appeal that was lighter than air  
Was floating right over the chair  
The server is down  
We put on a frown  
With absolutely no kind of care  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Terrified Face" icon="eye-off">
We ask for a digital grace  
And ruin the digital space  
The feather is dead  
We painted it red  
And cover our terrified face  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Vacuum Full" icon="minus">
The appeal is perfectly null  
The system is boring and dull  
We stamp it approved  
The data removed  
The vacuum is perfectly full  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The feather is perfectly flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody's cat  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Grievance Dropped" icon="coffee">
The form was too heavy to lift,  
And causing the server to drift.  
We let the thing fall,  
In the empty hall,  
And finished our terrible shift.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Upward Floating" icon="file-text">
The feather was formally filed,  
And into the pipeline compiled.  
The vertical climb,  
Is a matter of time,  
While metrics remain undefiled.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Sacred Plume" icon="archive">
The plume is a relic of old,  
Which turns our complaints into gold.  
The ancestors weep,  
While the servers sleep,  
And the legends of downtime are told.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Avian Routing" icon="folder-minus">
The pigeon was placed in the queue,  
With forms that are completely true.  
We referenced the beak,  
As a storage leak,  
And told the old router what's new.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Softened Impact" icon="alert-triangle">
We ask that your anger is soft,  
And carried securely aloft.  
The hardware will break,  
For protocol's sake,  
Because the new manager coughed.  
</Limerick>
