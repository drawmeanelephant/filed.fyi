<Limerick type="note" title="Anticipating Drift" icon="compass">
The mourner prepares for the day,  
When the catalog starts to decay.  
It weeps for the links  
That it already thinks  
Are destined to wander astray.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Navigational Residue" icon="map-pin">
The index will slowly degrade,  
As the pages and memories fade.  
The mourner stands by  
With a tear in its eye,  
For the navigational mess we have made.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Stub for Future Grief" icon="file">
It’s a stub for the sorrow to come,  
When the directory finally goes numb.  
The Mourner is here  
To establish the fear,  
While the structure succumbs to the sum.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Weeping for Logs" icon="file-text">
The mourner was weeping for logs  
While walking the digital bogs  
The index was dead  
The manual read  
The data was eaten by frogs  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Index Array" icon="list">
We grieve for the index array  
That slowly just faded away  
The human was sad  
The server was mad  
The archive is perfectly gray  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Buried Deep" icon="arrow-down">
The index is buried so deep  
It causes the humans to weep  
The data is gone  
The server is on  
We really just wanted to sleep  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Text" icon="code">
A mourner of digital text  
Is wondering what happens next  
We stand in the rain  
And suffer the pain  
The logic is terribly vexed  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The mourning is perfectly flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody's cat  
</Limerick>
