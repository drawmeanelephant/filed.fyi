<Limerick type="danger" title="The CAB Delay" icon="clock">
You sent in a critical patch,  
But the Chairwoman closed up the hatch.  
"It needs time to age,  
In the policy cage,"  
And leaves the disaster to catch.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Ripening the Request" icon="coffee">
The urgency has to subside,  
Before the committee decides.  
They let the fix rot,  
In a terrible spot,  
And swallow your technical pride.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Dignity of Control" icon="shield">
She governs the flow of the code,  
To lighten the manager’s load.  
The change is ignored,  
By the absolute board,  
While the servers completely explode.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Procedural Defeat" icon="flag">
The chairwoman sat in her seat  
And accepted procedural defeat  
She put off the change  
It felt very strange  
And threw the old code in the street  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Screen Blur" icon="monitor">
We defer all the logic to her  
And wait for the screen to just blur  
The change is too slow  
With nowhere to go  
The errors will simply occur  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Made Naught" icon="circle">
A chairwoman made out of naught  
The data was totally caught  
In loops without end  
We cannot defend  
The terrible things we were taught  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Locked Gate" icon="lock">
She pushed the big button to wait  
And locked up the digital gate  
The server is down  
We put on a frown  
And quietly accept our fate  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The change is incredibly flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody's cat  
</Limerick>
