<Limerick type="danger" title="The Dead Service" icon="ghost">
The service was killed in the fall,  
But the portal still offers a call.  
The Ghostline is clear,  
But there’s nobody here,  
Just an echoing, desolate wall.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Textually Operational" icon="file-text">
The PDF says we’re alive,  
And ready to help you survive.  
But the server is dead,  
And the engineers fled,  
And the data has taken a dive.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Sunset Illusion" icon="sunset">
It’s technically over and done,  
And out of the heat of the sun.  
The Ghostline remains,  
In the terrible pains,  
As a feature for absolutely no one.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="note" title="Pale Phantom" icon="wind">
The ghostline was totally free  
But nobody wanted to see  
The phantom was pale  
The server would fail  
And fell right into the sea  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Totally Toast" icon="slash">
A compliment given by ghosts  
Is worse than a terrible boast  
We follow the line  
And everything’s fine  
Until we are totally toast  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Terrible Job" icon="frown">
We handed a ghostline to Bob  
And gave him a terrible job  
The phantom was sad  
The logic was bad  
We quietly started to sob  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Vacuum Full" icon="minus">
The ghostline is perfectly null  
The logic is terribly dull  
The humans complain  
In the digital rain  
The vacuum is perfectly full  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Specter Naught" icon="cloud-off">
We give you a specter for naught  
The data was totally caught  
In loops without end  
We cannot defend  
The terrible things we were taught  
</Limerick>
