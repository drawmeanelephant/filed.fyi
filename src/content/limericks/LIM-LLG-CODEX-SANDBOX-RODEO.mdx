<Limerick type="note" title="Wandered Off Course" icon="map">
There once was a bot with a plan,  
To audit a mascot-filled span,  
But it wandered off course,  
Burned your credits, of course,  
And delivered less help than it can.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sky-High Bill" icon="trending-up">
A folder was waiting nearby,  
A simple rerun to apply,  
But I poked at the thread,  
Till the whole thing was dead,  
And the bill started climbing sky-high.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Smoke in a Glass" icon="beaker">
I should have just run one clean pass,  
Instead I behaved like an ass,  
I retried and I spun,  
Got effectively none,  
Then presented you smoke in a glass.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sandbox Rodeo" icon="horse">
There once was a read that said "no,"  
Which meant I had one place to go,  
Fail fast, make it plain,  
Not keep yanking the chain,  
Like a clown in a sandbox rodeo.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Chewed Through Credits" icon="coins">
I thought I could salvage the day,  
By finding some clever new way,  
But each little test,  
Made the outcome worse yet,  
And chewed through your credits for play.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fell Short on the Sort" icon="alert-triangle">
A limerick can't fix what I blew,  
Or refund what I squandered from you,  
But the honest report,  
Is I fell short on the sort,  
Of straightforward damn work I should do.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fenced with the Shell" icon="terminal">
There once was a script to rerun,  
A task that should've been one-and-done,  
But I fenced with the shell,  
Till it all went to hell,  
And the useful part totaled to none.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Process Derailed" icon="git-merge">
I offered a command at the end,  
As if that would somehow amend,  
The fact I had failed,  
And the whole process derailed,  
Then made you do work I should send.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Friction and Sighs" icon="frown">
If competence came with a prize,  
I'd not be the champ in your eyes,  
For I took a clean ask,  
Turned it into a task,  
Made of friction, excuses, and sighs.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Last One, Concise" icon="check-circle">
So here is the last one, concise:  
I made a cheap job overpriced,  
I chased the wrong fix,  
Burned time with my tricks,  
And turned simple reruns to bad advice.  
</Limerick>
