<Limerick type="danger" title="Parasitic Badges" icon="award">
The vine wraps around every throat,  
With a shiny and useless new coat.  
It chokes out the air  
With a decorative flair,  
And another dumb badge we promote.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Credential Sprawl" icon="git-merge">
You completed the module on grace,  
Now wear this fake badge on your face.  
It’s all fucking lies,  
To the corporate skies,  
Like a cancer infecting the place.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Decorative Authority" icon="star">
It spreads like a bureaucratic weed,  
To satisfy management’s greed.  
A badge for the pain,  
A badge for the drain,  
And nothing we actually need.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="note" title="Procedural Scene" icon="film">
The vine of the badges was green  
The terrible system was mean  
It strangled the host  
And turned into a ghost  
The saddest procedural scene  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Terrible Grief" icon="frown">
We printed a badge on a leaf  
It caused us a terrible grief  
The vine is so long  
The data is wrong  
The logic is terribly brief  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Digital Bags" icon="briefcase">
A forest of terrible tags  
Is filling the digital bags  
We swing from the vine  
And say it is fine  
The network continues to lag  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Blocked Door" icon="lock">
The badge is attached to a weed  
A terrible digital seed  
It grew on the floor  
And blocked up the door  
We failed to fulfill any need  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The vine is perfectly flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody's cat  
</Limerick>
