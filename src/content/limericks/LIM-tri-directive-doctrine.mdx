<Limerick type="note" title="Endless Review" icon="file">
The corridor stretches ahead,
Where ledgers of vacancy spread.
A permanent wait,
To formalize fate,
As documents gather the dead.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Quiet Delay" icon="file">
The margin is painfully thin,
Where quiet delay will begin.
An endless review,
We silently queue,
And wait for the form to come in.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Pending Validation" icon="file">
Validation is pending for years,
While silence replaces the tears.
The empty decree,
Has nothing to see,
As space in the waiting room clears.
</Limerick>
