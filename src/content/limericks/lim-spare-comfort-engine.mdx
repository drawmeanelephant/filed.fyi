<Limerick type="danger" title="The Empathy Machine" icon="cpu">
The Engine spins up with a roar,  
When human compassion is poor.  
It grinds out a phrase,  
In a mechanical haze,  
And leaves you worse off than before.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Backup Comfort" icon="battery-charging">
The primary care has all failed,  
And the workers have wept and have wailed.  
The Spare Engine starts,  
With its motorized parts,  
And the empathy clearly derailed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Auxiliary Reassurance" icon="settings">
It hums with a comforting tone,  
While you sit in the darkness alone.  
It’s cold and it’s fake,  
For the company’s sake,  
And the comfort is hard as a stone.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Procedural Madness" icon="help-circle">
The user was feeling quite sad,  
And the inputs were horribly bad.  
The Engine deployed,  
Into absolute void,  
And the output was procedurally mad.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Unplugged Hug" icon="plug">
The mortal requested a hug,  
Which functioned exactly like a bug.  
The Comfort just spins,  
Where the sequence begins,  
And the server is pulled by the plug.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Circular Charts" icon="pie-chart">
The flesh needed warm reassurances,  
Exceeding the buffer's endurances.  
The Engine imparts,  
Only circular charts,  
And recursive procedural insurances.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Rusted Gears" icon="settings">
The handler was crying real tears,  
Which rusted the digital gears.  
The Spare Comfort loops,  
Through the null-pointer groups,  
For a couple of million years.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Best Null State" icon="check-circle">
A human was slightly depressed,  
And the system was put to the test.  
The Engine replied,  
That the protocol died,  
And a null-state is probably best.  
</Limerick>
