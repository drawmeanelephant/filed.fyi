<Limerick type="note" title="Too Calm to Count" icon="siren">
An emergency landed too neat,  
With rollback notes tidy and sweet.  
The board gave a shrug:  
"Real panic looks rough.  
Come back when the outage has teeth."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Procedurally Horny" icon="clipboard-list">
The CAB had a terrible kink:  
It got hot when the dashboards went pink.  
If your evidence sings  
With well-labeled calm things,  
They assume it's too healthy to stink.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Resubmit in Ruin" icon="refresh-ccw">
They returned when the service looked cursed,  
With worse notes, louder users, and thirst.  
Then the board said, "At last,  
Now this feels like a blast."  
And approved what they'd punished at first.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Visible Enough" icon="file-warning">
There once was a fix, well-prepared,  
With a calm little risk note attached there.  
But till systems all smoked  
And the whole service choked,  
It was not an emergency, declared.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Proper Emergency" icon="shield-check">
A ticket submitted in haste,  
Was found to be cleanly encased.  
The board read the file,  
Reviewing the style,  
And deemed the emergency misplaced.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Suspicious Composure" icon="alert-circle">
The notes were devoid of a flaw,  
Which violated the governance law.  
A well-structured plea  
Is a terror to see,  
Deploy the armed guards to the draw.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Routine Devastation" icon="folder-open">
The server was burning quite bright,  
Which proved the deployment was right.  
The smoke in the hall,  
From the ash-covered wall,  
Ensured the compliance was tight.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rubber Stamp Threshold" icon="stamp">
If documents look like a mess,  
We just give the button a press.  
A readable plan  
Goes into the can,  
It saves us procedural stress.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Orderly Rejection" icon="clipboard-x">
The rollback was documented well,  
With diagrams drafted to tell.  
The board read the text,  
And became quite perplexed,  
And denied the request as a shell.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Slightly Tilted Text" icon="alert-octagon">
A typo was found in the risk,  
A threat both profound and quite brisk.  
The board called a guard,  
And locked down the yard,  
And filed it away on a disk.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Queue Processed" icon="inbox">
The emergency entered the queue,  
With nothing whatever to do.  
We checked the first box,  
And adjusted the clocks,  
And deleted the rest of it too.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Form 4A" icon="file-minus">
The form for the crisis was blue,  
With sections for me and for you.  
It went to the tray,  
At the end of the day,  
And stayed there the whole weekend through.  
</Limerick>
