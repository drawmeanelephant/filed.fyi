<Limerick type="note" title="The Listening Board" icon="clipboard">
A clerk took the testimony down,  
Without any hint of a frown.  
He filed it with care,  
But left it right there,  
And the grievance remained in the town.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Structural Ceiling" icon="folderx">
The papers were perfectly neat,  
The forms were entirely complete.  
But the staircase was gone,  
From the place they were drawn,  
So the routing was stuck in the seat.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Laundered by Citation" icon="fileclock">
They read that the burden was heard,  
And wrote down a comforting word.  
The gap wasn't breached,  
No action was reached,  
The memory simply was blurred.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="tip" title="Cycle Three" icon="filewarning">
By autumn the matter recurred,  
The same heavy grievance was heard.  
We noted the strain,  
In the very same vein,  
And quietly filed the third.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Vault's Promise" icon="archive">
We keep all the records intact,  
A perfectly captured cold fact.  
The listener sighed,  
But the pathway had died,  
And the ledger requires no act.  
</Limerick>
