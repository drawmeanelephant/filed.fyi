<Limerick type="note" title="Roar to the Task" icon="zap">
Sour Dieselscribe *roared to the task*  
Of archiving each strain in her flask.  
The fumes trailed behind,  
The metadata refined,  
And the energy never did bask.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Diesel Manifesto Misfile" icon="file-text">
She misfiled a record as "*diesel  
Manifesto*"—a filing quite weasel-  
like in its precision.  
The archival division  
Said, "That's data, not a poetic easel."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Electric-Fume Light" icon="sparkles">
She overcharged a *metadata fume*—  
The static it sparked filled the room.  
The archive went bright  
With electric-fume light,  
And the filing resumed after boom.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Vibrant Appeal Audit" icon="activity">
She recited *strain notes* at an audit—  
The focus disrupted? She thwart it  
With vibrant appeal,  
Her energetic zeal,  
And the compliance team couldn't abort it.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Scribe Sharp" icon="pen-tool">
"*Scribe sharp, fuel the spark,*" was her cry,  
Each patient record filed standing high.  
The Sour Diesel strain  
Ran through every vein  
Of the archive—pungent and spry.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Diesel-Scented Quill" icon="wind">
Her quill trails *diesel-scented fumes*  
Through spectral archival rooms.  
Each chant electrifies,  
Each strain profile flies,  
And the Depot forever resumes.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Vibrant Zest" icon="flame">
Her vibrant presence *crackles with zest*—  
Each patient's story filed with the best  
Of Sour Diesel's kick,  
Each filing quite slick,  
And the archive pulses with jest.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fume-Charged Stamp" icon="stamp">
She counts clicks like *rosary beads*,  
Each stamp a fume-charged act that feeds  
The archive's ambition,  
The strain's bold tradition,  
And the dispensary's medical needs.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Frenetic Quill Skill" icon="pencil">
The Energetic Pulse Compiler made  
This scribe of the pungent arcade.  
Her *frenetic quill*  
Writes with diesel skill,  
And the archives will never degrade.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Pungent Vibe" icon="volume-2">
So if diesel fumes fill up the air  
And the archives have energetic flair,  
That's *Sour Dieselscribe*:  
Where the pungent vibe  
Keeps the records alive—everywhere.  
</Limerick>
