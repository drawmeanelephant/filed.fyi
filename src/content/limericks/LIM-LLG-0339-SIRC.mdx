<Limerick type="note" title="Annex Five" icon="door-open">
They brought one quiet week to a room  
With a large shared display in the gloom.  
COMA heard "fine."  
SOMA heard "line  
Of burden stretched tight past the bloom."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="COMA's One Word" icon="check">
No ticket, no outage, no plea.  
To COMA that settled the sea.  
The interval spoke  
One neat little "OK,"  
And continuity signed with a key.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="SOMA's Transcript" icon="waveform">
SOMA replayed margins instead:  
"Will file later," the half-spoken thread.  
The silence, for them,  
Was a pressure-stopped stem,  
Not a meadow of health overhead.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Bricky Between Screens" icon="columns-2">
Bricky sat right in the seam  
Between one green graph and one scream.  
No mandate to blend,  
Only notes to append,  
And a notebook too honest for beam.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Human Witness" icon="users">
The staff said, "Nothing exploded, it's true.  
Another full week? We were through."  
COMA marked grace.  
SOMA saw strain in place.  
Both used the same sentence as clue.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Corroborating Contradiction" icon="files">
Because both sanctioned formats were used,  
The parser grew dutifully confused.  
It stored both as support,  
Not a conflict report,  
So the interval counted both views.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Healthy Minutes" icon="timer">
One stretch of compliant green light  
Counted healthy by day and by night.  
The same stretch, elsewhere,  
Counted burdened by care,  
And no one was allowed to unite.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="High-Efficiency Quiet" icon="gauge">
C.U.N.T.I.E.R. loved the new zone:  
High SBI, COMA full-blown.  
What SOMA called debt  
The models soon met  
As resilience with excellent tone.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Interpretations (2)" icon="mouse-pointer-click">
The headline still tells you "all right."  
The burden survives in a slight  
Hover note near the seam,  
Where the second small dream  
Of the interval waits out of sight.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Exists Twice" icon="copy-plus">
That week now persists in the file  
As success and as silence hostile.  
The chamber, at best,  
Made archival the rest  
Of a gap no doctrine could reconcile.  
</Limerick>
