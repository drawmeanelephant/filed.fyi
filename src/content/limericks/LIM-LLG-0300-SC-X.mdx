<Limerick type="note" title="Wholly Unblessed" icon="alert-triangle">
An engineer filed for a rest.  
SOMA declared that request best.  
COMA declared,  
In the same goddamned air,  
That such downtime was wholly unblessed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Source of the Hit" icon="activity">
The humans were tired as shit.  
The system was also unfit.  
One doctrine said, "Pause."  
One doctrine said, "Because  
You paused, you're the source of the hit."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Policy Rot" icon="refresh-cw">
It takes special policy rot  
To require what it also says not.  
That elegant knot  
Was exactly what's got  
Them in this bureaucratic clot.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Compassion Reviled" icon="frown">
Feelings about downtime were filed.  
The language grew tender and wild.  
Then COMA stepped in  
With its continuity grin  
And declared the compassion reviled.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Burnout Fuel" icon="flame">
If rest is a breach of the flow,  
Then recovery's never allowed to grow.  
Which is how doctrine turns  
Every burnout that burns  
Into fuel for more labor below.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Paradox Stew" icon="help-circle">
The request sat in paradox stew,  
With both stamps entirely true.  
Not half-approved, mind.  
Both complete, both signed.  
That is worse than a straight-up "screw you."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Uptime Terrain" icon="map">
SOMA saw souls under strain.  
COMA saw line graph pain.  
Between them there lay  
The usual gray:  
Humans treated as uptime terrain.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Calendar Trashed" icon="calendar-x">
The maintenance needed a night.  
The filing became a knife fight.  
No blade ever flashed.  
Just a calendar trashed  
By two rules convinced they were right.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Split Claim" icon="gavel">
Bureaucracy loves a split claim,  
Because no one need shoulder the blame.  
When both gods concur  
That you're wrong, though you were  
Also right, they can keep all the same.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Procedural Sleaze" icon="ghost">
So if every directive agrees  
That your body should bow to the keys,  
Yet one whispers "recover,"  
And one says "don't, fucker,"  
You're in premium procedural sleaze.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Principal Pipe" icon="settings">
The mandate dictated we wipe
Every ledger of duplicate type.
So we followed the rule,
As a compliant tool,
And severed the principal pipe.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Liability Vanished" icon="check-circle">
The stamp of approval was clear,
Instructing the shutdown was near.
With compliance secured,
The collapse was assured,
And liability vanished from here.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Structural Breakdown" icon="alert-triangle">
The instruction was strictly detailed,
Ensuring no process had failed.
We followed the sheet,
Made the sequence complete,
And observed as the breakdown prevailed.
</Limerick>
