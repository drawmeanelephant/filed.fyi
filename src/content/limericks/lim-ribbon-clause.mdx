<Limerick type="danger" title="The Ceremonial Loop" icon="file-text">
The contract is tied in a bow,  
To put on a wonderful show.  
The terms are insane,  
And cause terrible pain,  
But the formatting has a nice glow.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Ornamental Exceptions" icon="scissors">
You thought you could easily escape,  
But the Clause has a terrible shape.  
It wraps you around,  
To the cold fucking ground,  
With a piece of decorative tape.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Decorative Legalese" icon="shield">
The lawyer was feeling so proud,  
And read out the Ribbon aloud.  
It signifies nought,  
But the battle is fought,  
In front of a terrible crowd.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Formally Kicked" icon="user-minus">
The clause of the ribbon was strict  
The users were formally kicked  
The manual said  
The process was dead  
The error was totally picked  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Totally Blind" icon="eye-off">
We folded the clause in a bind  
The logic was totally blind  
We tied it up tight  
In the middle of night  
And left the old server behind  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Pool" icon="droplet">
A ribbon that acts as a rule  
Was drafted by some kind of fool  
The system broke down  
We started to frown  
And sat in a digital pool  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Vector of Pain" icon="activity">
The clause is a vector of pain  
It washes right down the old drain  
The ribbon is torn  
The users forlorn  
We wait for the error to reign  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Dusty Ground" icon="archive">
The ribbon was legally bound  
The error was suddenly found  
We threw out the case  
In a digital race  
And fell to the cold, dusty ground  
</Limerick>
