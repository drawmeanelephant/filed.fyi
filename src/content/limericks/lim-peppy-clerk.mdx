## Redaction Fugue

<Limerick type="caution" title="Clinical Cage" icon="file">
A redaction is drawn on the page,
A quiet and clinical cage.
The fog in the text,
Leaves sectors perplext,
An archive adrift in its age.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Empty Vaults" icon="file">
The porter delivers the blank,
To vaults that are empty and dank.
A fractured display,
That falls to decay,
A ghost in the memory bank.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Vacant Domains" icon="file">
The sequence demands we omit,
The records the systems permit.
The conflict remains,
In vacant domains,
A silence where shadows submit.
</Limerick>
