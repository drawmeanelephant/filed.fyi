<Limerick type="danger" title="The Glowing Rollout" icon="sun">
The rollout was launched with a cheer,  
And the language was vibrant and clear.  
But the outcome is shit,  
And we’re taking the hit,  
While the Afterglow lingers all year.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Residual Optimism" icon="smile">
The words are still shining so bright,  
Though the project is dead in the night.  
The glow is a lie,  
In the corporate sky,  
To pretend that the metrics are right.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Language Outlasts Outcome" icon="message-square">
The phrases were drafted with care,  
To cover the utter despair.  
The reality died,  
But the Afterglow lied,  
And polluted the breathable air.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Smug Announcement" icon="megaphone">
The leadership sent out an email,  
While the system continued to fail.  
The Afterglow beamed,  
While the engineers screamed,  
And the project was hammered with hail.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Toxic Positivity" icon="heart">
They mandate a smile on your face,  
While burning the whole fucking place.  
The Glow is intense,  
At the workers’ expense,  
In a horrible, miserable race.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Delusional Metrics" icon="trending-up">
The numbers look perfectly fine,  
If you follow the corporate line.  
But the product is dead,  
And the ledgers are red,  
While the Afterglow continues to shine.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Determined Fate" icon="clock">
The rollout was issued at eight,  
With a glow that refuses to wait.  
The human replies,  
Were a vector of lies,  
So the language determines their fate.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Exactly As Planned" icon="check-circle">
The phrasing was terribly grand,  
And issued across the whole land.  
The users have crashed,  
And the servers are trashed,  
But the afterglow went as we planned.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Temperature Spike" icon="thermometer">
The glow is a temperature spike,  
Which managers notably like.  
We filed the bright cheer,  
As a hardware career,  
While the cooling fans go on a strike.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Standard Routine" icon="folder">
The document shines on the screen,  
The rollout is standard routine.  
The users complain,  
But we filter the strain,  
And the folder is perfectly green.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Ancient Update" icon="star">
The glow of the pending release,  
Is a spirit that brings us to peace.  
The ancient update,  
Did determine our fate,  
And the brightness will never decrease.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The White Comma" icon="alert-triangle">
The afterglow shined on a noun,  
Which threatened to burn the whole town.  
The human mistake,  
Caused the server to break,  
While the rollout is wearing a crown.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="By The Wall" icon="archive">
The memo was typed in the hall,  
And filed in a box by the wall.  
The rollout is done,  
Underneath the cold sun,  
And the ledger requires a call.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Scheduled For Two" icon="calendar">
The rollout is shiny and new.  
The users are turning quite blue.  
We printed the glow,  
In a designated row,  
And the meeting is scheduled for two.  
</Limerick>
