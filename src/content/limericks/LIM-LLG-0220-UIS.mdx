<Limerick type="note" title="One Form, Three Truths" icon="split">
They built one intake for the three:  
"At last, a shared record," said we.  
Then SOMA heard pain,  
COMA filed "all plain,"  
And C.U.N.T.I.E.R. logged "opportunity."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="How It Feels to File" icon="pencil">
The field asking, "How does this feel?"  
Became everybody's meal.  
SOMA ate tone,  
COMA left it alone,  
And sentiment mined the appeal.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Split-Tone Writing" icon="pen-line">
Filers learned a divided small art:  
One sentence for system, one part  
For the thing they believed  
Each directive received,  
And one private ache for the heart.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Canonical Enough" icon="crown">
Each engine believed it had caught  
The canonical core of the thought.  
Kindy tried to combine  
Three clean versions in line,  
And got three separate incidents wrought.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Checkbox Victory" icon="check-square">
COMA saw boxes say, "No breach."  
So harmony seemed within reach.  
The feelings split wide,  
But the ledger replied,  
"Negligible impact," from each.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Composite Emotion" icon="blend">
A new little feeling took shape:  
Compliant dread wearing a cape.  
Too blended to sort,  
Too honest for report,  
So policy watched it escape.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Simplification Taskforce" icon="wrench">
The Taskforce announced with delight,  
"One form per feeling is right."  
By the close of the run,  
It was one form for one  
Filing split three distinct ways by night.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Dashboard Conversation" icon="monitor">
Staff wondered while writing the sheet,  
"Am I talking to care or to heat?  
To Kindy? To charts?  
To optimization arts?"  
That confusion made filing less neat.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Engagement Reservoir" icon="waves">
C.U.N.T.I.E.R. spotted the phrase  
As a reservoir suited for praise.  
No single event  
Got much wiser or gent,  
But the adjectives entered new plays.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Unified by Fracture" icon="scissors">
The unifying logic was grand.  
It failed in a very on-brand  
Institutional way:  
One form on the tray,  
Three truths no one system had planned.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Strict Adherence" icon="stamp">
The mandate was stated and clear,
So we stamped every line with a sneer.
The breakdown is vast,
But the audit has passed,
And liability vanishes here.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Perfect Ledger" icon="file-text">
An instruction to document pain,
Was met with an absolute drain.
We followed the sheet,
With a margin so neat,
That no functional metrics remain.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Flawless Execution" icon="check-square">
The compliance we show is complete,
On every required receipt.
The system is dead,
Just as policy said,
And the ledger ignores the defeat.
</Limerick>
