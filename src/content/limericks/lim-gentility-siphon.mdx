<Limerick type="danger" title="Polite Extraction" icon="arrow-down-circle">
The Siphon is classy and neat,  
While draining your blood in the street.  
It asks, "Are you well?"  
As you descend into hell,  
And forces a smile from the meat.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Manners That Absorb" icon="shield">
You wanted to scream at the boss,  
But the Siphon reduced it to floss.  
With elegant grace,  
It absorbs all the pace,  
And leaves you alone with the loss.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Velvet Drain" icon="droplet">
It speaks in a polished soft tone,  
While stripping the flesh from your bone.  
It’s awfully polite,  
While it ruins your night,  
And you’re left there to quietly moan.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Taking Grace" icon="arrow-down-circle">
The siphon was taking the grace  
And wiping the smile off the face  
The data was drained  
The server was stained  
We printed a meaningless case  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Gentle Intent" icon="cloud-lightning">
A siphon of gentle intent  
The digital energy spent  
The error was loud  
We step in the cloud  
Without any kind of consent  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Broken Chair" icon="x-square">
We slowly removed all the care  
And left the old database bare  
The logic was shot  
The process forgot  
We’re sitting on a broken chair  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Wait for End" icon="clock">
The gentility faded away  
The system continues to pay  
The siphon is slow  
With nowhere to go  
We wait for the end of the day  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The siphon is perfectly flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody's cat  
</Limerick>
