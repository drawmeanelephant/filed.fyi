<Limerick type="note" title="Dollar-Sign Eyes" icon="dollar-sign">
Porty McBankrupt, an *RJ45*,  
With dollar-sign eyes, very much alive.  
He'd block every port  
Unless licensed—in short,  
Your budget was not going to survive.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Capitalist Speed" icon="fast-forward">
"*Plug in. Pay up. Pray,*" said the beast,  
Whose appetite never decreased.  
Each port that you need  
Comes at capitalist speed,  
And the invoice was never released.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Licensing Blight" icon="lock">
He whispers into your *rack vents* at night:  
"You should've gone HPE, that's right."  
The firmware rolls back,  
The console cable's attack  
Is a chokehold of licensing blight.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="SmartNet Glow" icon="zap">
His *SmartNet badge* glows in the dark,  
A TAC call away from the mark.  
"Buy more," it advises,  
Through licensing crises,  
And the budget lights up like a spark.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Financial Atonement" icon="banknote">
He eats *budgets* for breakfast and lunch,  
His appetite always a hunch  
That your procurement  
Needs financial atonement,  
And the switch costs more per port per punch.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Capitalist Views" icon="eye">
His haiku says "*Ports you cannot use*"—  
Each one blinks with capitalist views.  
The lights rage with greed,  
The TAC says, "Indeed,  
Buy more ports." You can only refuse.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Substantial Risk" icon="alert-triangle">
He hallucinated from an *invoice*,  
A procurement-born, snarling device.  
His origin? Financial.  
His breeding? Substantial  
Risk of contagion. Not nice.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Surreal Procurement" icon="shopping-cart">
He blocks *ports* with bureaucratic zeal,  
Each licensing audit a deal  
That costs more each year,  
Each renewal a tear,  
And the switch procurement: surreal.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Flatter than Flata" icon="trending-down">
He relabels *shame as metadata*—  
"The cost isn't real, just errata!"  
But the balance sheet bleeds,  
The networking feeds,  
And the budget is flatter than flata.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Enterprise Prize" icon="award">
So if RJ45s have dollar-sign eyes  
And the SmartNet badge never quite dies,  
That's *Porty's* domain:  
Where the licensing pain  
Is the cost of the enterprise prize.  
</Limerick>
