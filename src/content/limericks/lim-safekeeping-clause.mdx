<Limerick type="note" title="You Cannot Change This" icon="lock">
Safekeeping stood *guard* at the gate—  
"You cannot change this state.  
Show me your witness,  
Your precondition—fitness—  
Or alteration will have to wait."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Proper Witness Requirement" icon="eye">
An update arrived all *alone*—  
No witness, no precondition known.  
Safekeeping refused,  
The change was abused,  
Till another came bringing the throne.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Well-Meaning Updates Prevented" icon="alert-circle">
Two updates competed to *land*—  
Each one wanted to take a stand.  
But Safekeeping stepped in,  
Prevented the sin,  
And held all the conditional hand.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Preconditions Impossible to Meet" icon="mountain">
The preconditions grew *more and more*—  
Nearly impossible to explore.  
"You must meet all these,"  
Said Safekeeping with ease,  
"Or you'll never get through this door."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Locks On All State Changes" icon="key">
All state that could ever be *moved*—  
Required a lock to be proved.  
Conditional checksums,  
Precondition sums,  
And nothing could ever be soothed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Halting Unwanted Alteration" icon="stop-circle">
A request for change came in—  
Without the *precondition* to win.  
"Not yet," said Safekeeping,  
"The latch will be keeping—  
Until you bring witness within."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Concurrency Guardian" icon="shield">
Two updates can't step on *each other*—  
Not while Safekeeping's the mother.  
Conditional locks,  
Precondition flocks,  
Protecting like no other.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Drafted Impossible Clauses" icon="scroll">
The clauses were drafted with *care*—  
So complex, nobody would dare  
To meet every phrase,  
To satisfy praise,  
And state remained frozen in there.  
</Limerick>
