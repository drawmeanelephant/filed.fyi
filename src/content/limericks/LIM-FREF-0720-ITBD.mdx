<Limerick type="note" title="At the Boundary" icon="gate">
At the boundary, feeling must wait  
Till the signal can demonstrate state.  
What a person calls grief  
May require belief,  
But the system prefers predicate.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Admissibility Test" icon="filter">
The story was vivid and true,  
But not yet the right kind of cue.  
It was held at the line  
For a sanctioned design  
That could make it interpretable, too.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Signal Conversion" icon="shuffle">
Affect crossed the threshold by degrees,  
Losing weather, exception, and pleas.  
It emerged as a class  
That the dashboard could pass,  
Which is not the same thing as ease.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Admissibility Check" icon="check-square">
The boundary requires a test  
Before any feeling is put into rest.  
If it fails to align  
With a routed design,  
It is filed as a pending request.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Unnamable Affect Incident" icon="alert-triangle">
A sigh was recorded at noon,  
Which threatened to destabilize the commune!  
A task force was made  
To deploy a blockade  
Lest the feeling escape from the room.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Annexed Signal" icon="archive">
The context was filed away,  
Because it had too many details to say.  
The folder was closed.  
The record disposed.  
The system moved on to the day.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Coherence Filter Incident" icon="filter">
A feeling of dread was detected,  
And quickly for structure inspected.  
The trajectory failed,  
So the data was scaled,  
And the volatile variance rejected.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Executive Vision Threat" icon="alert-circle">
An optics contradiction was found!  
The sirens began to resound!  
We mobilized teams  
To dismantle the themes,  
And drove the vague nuance underground!  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Lexicon Alignment" icon="check-square">
The lexicon needed a word  
For a sorrow entirely absurd.  
We called it "Event  
Of a Downward Intent,"  
Which is not what the subject preferred.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Intake Queue Timeout" icon="clock">
The signal was lacking in frame,  
And nobody knew who to blame.  
We marked it deferred,  
Which is just a nice word  
For deleting the thing when it came.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Aggregate Omission" icon="archive">
A contradiction was filed away  
To balance the charts for the day.  
It lacked a clear tier,  
So we dropped it from here.  
The aggregate metrics display.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Admitted With Annotation" icon="edit">
The witness was crying in pain,  
A symptom we promptly contain!  
We labeled the tears  
As "Mechanical Gears"  
And routed them straight to the drain.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Unrenderable Form" icon="book-open">
The residue left by the test  
Was boxed and securely suppressed.  
But clerks will agree  
That a phantom degree  
Now haunts every standard request.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Pathway Verification" icon="git-branch">
No pathway for action was clear  
When tracking a generalized fear.  
We saved the request  
As an orphaned digest  
And closed out the governance year.  
</Limerick>
