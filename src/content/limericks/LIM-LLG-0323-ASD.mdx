<Limerick type="note" title="Failure Put on a Nice Hood" icon="shirt">
The audit once called things "not good."  
Then assurance vocabulary stood  
In the doorway and said,  
"Let's say 'emergent' instead."  
And failure put on a nice hood.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Compliment Face" icon="smile">
Deficit became "developing grace."  
Unresolved became "holding its place."  
By the end of the sheet,  
Every structural cheat  
Had a calm little compliment face.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sanded till Smooth" icon="brush">
The findings were sanded till smooth.  
No one had to improve or disprove.  
If a leak was a flow,  
Then the outlook could glow,  
And the summary kept its own groove.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Corporate War" icon="sword">
This is language at corporate war:  
Take the problem and rename the sore.  
Soon a nonconformity  
Becomes favored stability,  
And the meeting asks, "Can you say more?"  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Softened Indictment" icon="feather">
The revision was sold as alignment.  
Really it softened indictment.  
Once failure sounds kind,  
The whole record gets blind  
To the need for unpleasant assignment.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Durable Aspect" icon="shield-check">
I love when an audit grows cute.  
That's how a real issue gets mute.  
"Recurring defect"  
Turns to "durable aspect,"  
And nobody follows the route.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cowardice Sing" icon="music">
Words can make cowardice sing.  
This pass made avoidance a king.  
If the summary's plush,  
Then the grim human rush  
To correct things can lose all its sting.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Euphemism Wins Trust" icon="check-circle">
One clerk crossed out "bad" with "robust."  
Another wrote "lol" in the dust.  
The machine preferred poise.  
It has no use for noise  
When euphemism already wins trust.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tuck in it to Sleep" icon="bed">
Administrative favor is cheap  
When your diction is trained not to weep.  
A failure renamed  
Is still structurally maimed,  
Though the prose may tuck in it to sleep.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Laundered to Clean" icon="refresh-cw">
So if audits sound weirdly serene  
Where a siren should probably scream,  
Check who polished the terms.  
Some language confirms  
That the lie has been laundered to clean.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Emergent Display" icon="eye">
We were told to conceal the decay,
So we called it 'emergent display'.
When the system corroded,
And quietly folded,
We assured them it functioned that way.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Positive Spin" icon="refresh-cw">
The audit required a clean spin,
So we classified losses as win.
Every structural fracture,
Is a new architecture,
And we filed it with an absolute grin.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Optimized Void" icon="box">
The collapse was an 'optimized void',
Which the central directors enjoyed.
We complied with the naming,
To finish the framing,
While the underlying baseline destroyed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Dynamic Halt" icon="pause-circle">
When the network completely stood still,
We described it as 'pausing with skill'.
It's a 'dynamic halt',
Which is nobody's fault,
As we maliciously process the bill.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Approved Decay" icon="check-square">
The decay is officially approved,
Since the phrasing was perfectly smoothed.
We obey the facade,
With a diligent nod,
As the foundational records are moved.
</Limerick>
