<Limerick type="note" title="The Invisible Load" icon="briefcase">
The Shrew carries all of the weight,  
That the metrics refuse to translate.  
The effort is spent,  
But it makes not a dent  
In the dashboard's immaculate state.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Keeping It Afloat" icon="anchor">
It scampers and scurries around,  
To keep the old systems inbound.  
It patches the leaks  
With a series of squeaks,  
And never makes even a sound.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Uncounted Effort" icon="bar-chart">
The burden is heavy and real,  
But the chart has no column for zeal.  
The little Shrew tries,  
But the algorithm lies,  
And nobody cares how you feel.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Gate" icon="lock">
The shrew had a terrible weight  
He accepted his digital fate  
He carried the byte  
In the middle of night  
And locked up the digital gate  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Going Insane" icon="frown">
A burden of terrible pain  
It washes right down the old drain  
The shrew is so sad  
The server is mad  
The logic is going insane  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Pile" icon="layers">
We handed the shrew a big file  
He carried it over a mile  
The data was dead  
We painted it red  
And stood in a digital pile  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The burden completely fell flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody's cat  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Data Removed" icon="trash-2">
The shrew is perfectly null  
The system is boring and dull  
We stamp it approved  
The data removed  
The vacuum is perfectly full  
</Limerick>
