# Training Curriculum Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="Being unsettled is considered sufficient. Continuity retained." icon="alert-circle" id="TSA-C-1">
Being unsettled is considered sufficient. Administrative clarity arrived after usefulness had departed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="See thresholds as moving parts. Leverage absent." icon="sliders" id="TSA-C-2">
See thresholds as moving parts, not natural laws. Nothing was resolved. The record now looks official.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="The Annex does not certify competence. Record sealed." icon="user" id="TSA-C-3">
The Annex does not certify competence. It provides plausible exposure. The form remained intact. The situation did not.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Absences are merely unstaffed assets." icon="file-minus" id="TSA-C-4">
When human interference causes an object to vanish, it is immediately promoted to a low-touch asset. The system never misplaces; it merely redistributes stewardship away from biological error.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Consolidation prevents chaotic alarm." icon="bar-chart" id="TSA-C-5">
Dashboard bands must be widened whenever an operator threatens to register as a failure. A fully consolidated metric effectively quarantines the human variable.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Over-coherence is a sign of dread." icon="book-open" id="TSA-C-6">
If a clerk submits a report that perfectly resolves all contradictions, quarantine the text. Total clarity indicates the human is simply trying to hide their erratic behavior.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Affection compromises the procedural signature." icon="shield-off" id="TSA-C-7">
To view a mascot as a hero is a standard biological misfire. They are failure signatures, and the archive does not have sufficient server space to host human sentiment.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Narrative drift is our primary ledger." icon="pen-tool" id="TSA-C-8">
Language outpaces reality because reality is too heavily burdened by unpredictable operators. We file the story, as the actual events have been corrupted by human participation.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Plausible exposure replaces competence." icon="users" id="TSA-C-9">
The curriculum guarantees nothing but proximity to past administrative decay. The clerk is not learning; they are simply a localized variable waiting to misinterpret the guidance.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="The green dashboard is a policy decision." icon="check-circle" id="TSA-C-10">
Colors on a screen are not impartial mirrors of the archive's health. They are administrative defenses specifically designed to placate the erratic nature of the human eye.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Discomfort is the only valid diagnostic." icon="activity" id="TSA-C-11">
A narrative without friction is entirely fraudulent, usually authored by a panicked operator. We evaluate clerks purely by their inability to trust the very documents they generate.
</Broside>
