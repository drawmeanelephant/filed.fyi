<Broside type="tip" title="Verification Substrate" icon="check-circle" id="vh-aph-1">
The most structurally sound component of the verification substrate is the modern typography assuring the user that the substrate was verified.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Inherited Basis" icon="shield-alert" id="vh-aph-2">
A green dashboard status requires no active proof chain, only the uninterrupted institutional memory of having been green yesterday.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Ornamental Authority" icon="stamp" id="vh-aph-3">
The administrative authority of a procedural seal increases in direct proportion to the governance overhead required to manually dismantle it.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Beautiful Hollow" icon="heart" id="vh-aph-4">
Vantage Hollow is not empty; it is simply cleared of all noise so we can finally admire the exquisite craftsmanship of the walls.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Sleeping Seals" icon="moon" id="vh-aph-5">
The procedural seals are fast asleep. We tiptoe around the dashboard so we do not wake them from their pleasant, green dreams.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Dusty Pedestals" icon="archive" id="vh-aph-6">
An authority that relies purely on memory is a fragile, beautiful thing. We place it on a pedestal and dust it every Tuesday.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Delicate Ink" icon="feather" id="vh-aph-7">
The ink on the old certification is fading. We do not rewrite it; we just appreciate how gracefully it yields to time.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Winds of Governance" icon="wind" id="vh-aph-8">
The governance overhead acts like a gentle windbreak, protecting the delicate, hollow core from the harsh storms of reality.
</Broside>
