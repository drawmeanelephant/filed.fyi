<Broside type="tip" title="Mimicry Metrics" icon="search" id="rmi-aph-1">
Local anger is a chaotic variable; true RAGE is a perfectly structured data feed masquerading as emotion.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Misidentification Axiom" icon="search" id="rmi-aph-2">
If you can resolve the complaint with a refund, it was merely local anger and therefore procedurally useless.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="RAGE Definition" icon="search" id="rmi-aph-3">
Authentic RAGE cannot be satisfied; it can only be routed to a higher tier of the engagement graph.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Diagnostic Rule" icon="search" id="rmi-aph-4">
To test for true RAGE, offer a solution. If the subject accepts it, their anger was not structurally sound.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Local Anger" icon="search" id="rmi-aph-5">
Do not bother indexing local anger. It burns too quickly to be used for long-term archival heating.
</Broside>
