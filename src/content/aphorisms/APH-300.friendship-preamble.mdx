# Friendship Preamble Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="AFFILIATIVE OPENING EXECUTED." icon="message-square" id="300-1">
'Hope this email finds you well.' The phrase is a bridge. I am building the bridge so I can drive a dump truck full of urgent tasks across it.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="SOCIAL FRAMING COMPLETED." icon="file-text" id="300-2">
I ask about your weekend. You answer. The ritual of humanity is concluded. We may now return to acting as cogs in the revenue machine.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="CEREMONIAL FIRST SENTENCE." icon="check-square" id="300-3">
'It was great catching up.' We spoke for two minutes about the weather. The relational machinery is now lubricated enough for me to ask a favor.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="DEMAND PREFACED WITH WARMTH." icon="shield" id="300-4">
The smile emoji at the end of the greeting is the anesthesia before the surgical extraction of your free time.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="RELATIONAL LUBRICANT." icon="smile" id="300-5">
The inquiry into your weekend is not curiosity; it is a structural necessity to delay the transactional trauma by precisely five seconds.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="AFFILIATIVE FRAMING." icon="message-square" id="300-6">
Warmth is a preceding condition for extraction. We establish rapport so that the invoice feels like a favor.
</Broside>

<Broside type="caution" title="DEFERRED EMPATHY." icon="heart" id="300-7">
The preamble claims friendship so the archive does not have to.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="NOSTALGIC ROT." icon="clock" id="300-8">
An affiliative opening decays into passive aggression the moment the request is denied.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="SYSTEM DEMAND PROTOCOL." icon="terminal" id="300-9">
We must remind you that we are colleagues before we remind you that you are out of compliance.
</Broside>
