# Kind Overdraft Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="EMPATHY LEDGER" icon="book-open" id="292-1">
The empathy ledger is severely overdrawn. We print our apologies on extremely thick stationery to disguise the lack of structural currency.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="UNFUNDED WARMTH" icon="inbox" id="292-2">
The clerk slides a perfectly articulated promise under the brass teller grate. The vault behind him has been empty since the last fiscal year.
</Broside>

<Broside type="caution" title="COURTESY RECEIPTS" icon="file-text" id="292-3">
We issue receipts of deep understanding for every grievance filed. The receipts cannot be cashed, but they fold nicely into a wallet.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="DEFICIT STATIONERY" icon="pen-tool" id="292-4">
The warmth of the intake form exceeded the budget of the resolution committee. The applicant is left holding a very polite piece of paper.
</Broside>
