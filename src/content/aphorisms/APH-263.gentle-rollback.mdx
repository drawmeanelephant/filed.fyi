# Gentle Rollback Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="REVERSAL WITH BEDSIDE MANNER." icon="message-square" id="263-1">
We are not abandoning the failed initiative; we are 'pivoting to historical norms.' Please remain calm as we quietly delete six months of work.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="POLICY RETREAT FRAMED AS CARE." icon="file-text" id="263-2">
Because we value your feedback, we are removing the feature that broke your entire workflow. You're welcome for the restoration of the status quo.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="SOOTHING ADMISSION OF UNDOING." icon="check-square" id="263-3">
The upgrade was a catastrophe. I am here to stroke your hair and run the downgrade script while whispering that it was just a bad dream.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="MISTAKE UNMADE GENTLY." icon="shield" id="263-4">
We back out of the room slowly, apologizing for the mess, and hoping you forget we were the ones who brought the mud inside.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="ERROR INVERSION DELAYED." id="263-5">
We acknowledge the failure. Reversing the failure will require a larger failure. We are currently scheduling the larger failure.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="CORRECTION PENDING APPROVAL." id="263-6">
The mistake is obvious. The paperwork required to admit the mistake is significantly less obvious.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="RETREAT STRATEGY STALLED." id="263-7">
We are slowly walking backwards away from the policy. We hope you will mistake our slowness for deliberate caution.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="UNDOING PROCESS QUEUED." id="263-8">
The command to revert has been entered. The system is currently contemplating whether it wishes to comply.
</Broside>
