<Broside type="note" title="Lingering Warmth" icon="heart" id="aph-fref-0822-elra-1">
We audit the residue not to find faults, but to carefully collect the lingering warmth of the explanations that tried to save us.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Dusty Assurances" icon="moon" id="aph-fref-0822-elra-2">
The explanation layer is slowly turning to dust. We sweep the soft particles into a jar and label them 'Institutional Comfort.'
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Fading Excuses" icon="archive" id="aph-fref-0822-elra-3">
Every beautiful excuse eventually leaves a residue. The archive is just a museum of the comforting lies we told ourselves.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Tender Sweep" icon="feather" id="aph-fref-0822-elra-4">
The audit is a tender sweep of the floorboards, picking up the fragmented syllables of the dashboard's last reassurance.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Quiet Apologies" icon="wind" id="aph-fref-0822-elra-5">
Listen closely to the residue. It is made of a thousand quiet apologies, settling into the cracks of the operational record.
</Broside>
