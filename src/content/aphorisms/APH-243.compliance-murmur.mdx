# Compliance Murmur Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="AGREEMENT SOUND PRODUCED." icon="message-square" id="243-1">
I make a noise that sounds like 'yes' but structurally functions as 'I surrender to the meeting.'
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="PROCEDURAL GRAVITY ACCEPTED." icon="file-text" id="243-2">
The policy is absurd, but arguing requires calories. My murmur is the friction-free lubricant of corporate consensus.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="CONVICTION BYPASSED." icon="check-square" id="243-3">
You asked if we are aligned. I vibrated my vocal cords at a frequency that suggests cooperation without establishing a paper trail of belief.
</Broside>
