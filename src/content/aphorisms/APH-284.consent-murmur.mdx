# Consent Murmur Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="LOW-VOLUME AGREEMENT HEARD." icon="message-square" id="284-1">
Nobody explicitly said 'no,' which I have recorded as an enthusiastic 'proceed immediately.' The silence was very affirming.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="SOCIALLY INFERRED YES." icon="file-text" id="284-2">
You sighed and looked at your phone. I took that as authorization to push to production. The murmur of resignation is functionally identical to consent.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="PERMISSION OBTAINED VIA PROXIMITY." icon="check-square" id="284-3">
You were in the room when the idea was proposed. By not throwing a chair, you became a co-sponsor. Thank you for your leadership.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="MURMUR CATALOGED AS SIGNATURE." icon="shield" id="284-4">
The meeting ended in exhausted silence. I transcribed the silence as unanimous approval. The record is clear, even if the room was not.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="RETROACTIVE PERMISSION ARCHIVED." id="APH-284.consent-murmur-5">
During the audit panic, we realized no one had authorized the deployment. We whispered the request into an empty stairwell and filed the echo as unanimous consent. The paperwork is flawless.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="OBSESSIVE COMPLIANCE INFERENCE." id="APH-284.consent-murmur-6">
The committee was paralyzed by the fear of non-compliance. When someone cleared their throat out of sheer anxiety, we registered it as a formal motion to strike the failing metric from the historical record.
</Broside>
