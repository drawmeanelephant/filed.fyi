# Form 27-B Multi-Signature Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="Survey burden. Operational impact denied." icon="edit-3" id="LLG-0027-1">
Satisfaction survey required more signatures than the procedures it evaluated. Relief remained outside scope.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Forms multiplication. Jurisdiction shifted." icon="file-plus" id="LLG-0027-2">
One-form actions were successfully transformed into five-form meta-feedback exercises. Relevance expired before processing resumed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Meta feedback. Status acknowledged." icon="frown" id="LLG-0027-3">
Authentic emotional signal was captured via measurable increases in reported frustration. The system kept the ritual and misplaced the function.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Optimization theatre. Boundary active." icon="volume-2" id="LLG-0027-4">
Feedback instruments were downgraded to complaint amplifiers to preserve headline metrics. Silence entered the record with full procedural honors.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Led feedback initiative. Continuity retained." icon="smile" id="LLG-0027-5">
Stakeholder satisfaction rose sharply regarding the opportunity to complain. Administrative clarity arrived after usefulness had departed.
</Broside>
