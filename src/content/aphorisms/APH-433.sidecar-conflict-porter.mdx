<Broside type="tip" title="Procedural Dispersal" icon="folder-down" id="aph-433-1">
The dispute is not resolved; it has merely been filed in an adjacent annex. The system considers this a successful mitigation of reading fatigue.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Lateral Relocation" icon="arrow-right-left" id="aph-433-2">
Moving a fatal contradiction into the secondary doctrine does not defuse it. It only ensures that when the structure collapses, it does so with perfectly formatted cross-references.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Continuity Maintenance" icon="archive-restore" id="aph-433-3">
We did not eliminate the conflicting requirements, we simply tagged them as non-active. The burden of failure is now safely distributed across multiple unread documents.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Gentle Carrier" icon="heart" id="aph-433-4">
The porter carries the heavy, broken contradictions so you do not have to. It asks for nothing but a quiet shelf to set them down.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Sleeping Conflicts" icon="moon" id="aph-433-5">
We put the conflicting policies in the sidecar and tucked them in with a soft blanket. They will not hurt each other while they dream.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Archive of Disputes" icon="archive" id="aph-433-6">
A beautifully archived dispute is a comfort to the soul. It shows that even our greatest arguments can be laid to rest.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Softly Filed" icon="feather" id="aph-433-7">
We do not resolve the tension; we just file it softly into the dark, letting time gently erode its sharp edges.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Quiet Dispersal" icon="wind" id="aph-433-8">
The conflict blows away like dandelion seeds, dispersing the pain across a dozen obsolete, deeply loved manuals.
</Broside>
