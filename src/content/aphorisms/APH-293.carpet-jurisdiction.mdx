# Carpet Jurisdiction Aphorisms

<Broside type="note" title="THRESHOLD AUTHORITY" icon="map" id="293-1">
The jurisdiction of the mandate ends exactly at the brass threshold strip. We stand on the industrial tile to formally disagree.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="ACOUSTIC COMPLIANCE" icon="volume-x" id="293-2">
We installed plush carpeting in the review room to muffle the sound of systemic complaints. The acoustics of the silence are now fully compliant.
</Broside>

<Broside type="caution" title="UNDERLAYMENT POLICY" icon="layers" id="293-3">
The thickness of the underlayment determines the weight of the policy. We cannot enforce a ruling issued from a room with polished concrete.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="CEREMONIAL RUNNER" icon="maximize" id="293-4">
When executive changes are proposed, we roll out the woven runner. The width of the fabric dictates the exact boundaries of the new ideas.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="FORM RETROACTIVITY" id="APH-293.carpet-jurisdiction-5">
The compliance violation was mitigated by retroactively incinerating the architectural blueprints. A structure cannot be found non-compliant if we possess no record of its intended shape.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="CONTAINMENT METRICS" id="APH-293.carpet-jurisdiction-6">
We achieved absolute security by quarantining the theoretical threat within a restricted binder. The risk is now perfectly contained between two heavily reinforced plastic covers.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="PROCEDURAL ERASURE" id="APH-293.carpet-jurisdiction-7">

</Broside>
