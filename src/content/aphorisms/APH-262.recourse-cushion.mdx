# Recourse Cushion Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="OBJECTION FORMALLY AVAILABLE." icon="message-square" id="262-1">
You have the right to appeal. The form is seventy pages long and must be submitted via fax. The process is open; the door is merely very heavy.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="IMPACT SOFTENED." icon="file-text" id="262-2">
Your furious complaint hits my padding and sinks in, losing all momentum. By the time it reaches the committee, it is a sleepy suggestion.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="DISRUPTION REDUCED." icon="check-square" id="262-3">
We embrace feedback by routing it through an infinitely long maze of automated replies. Your anger will exhaust itself before it finds a human.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="PADDED APPEAL PATH NAVIGATED." icon="shield" id="262-4">
You struck the wall with all your might. The wall was made of marshmallows. You are unharmed, but you are also stuck.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="DELAY INITIATED." id="262-5">
The resolution has been submitted. It is now resting comfortably beneath a pile of unread memorandums. Please wait.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="PROCESS STAGNATION LOGGED." id="262-6">
Your concern is valid. We are processing its validity at a rate of one syllable per fiscal quarter.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="ROUTING LOOP CONFIRMED." id="262-7">
The appeal is circulating between departments. They are currently deciding whose inbox it will eventually perish in.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="FRICTION BUFFER DEPLOYED." id="262-8">
We hear your frustration. We have allocated three forms to absorb the impact of your complaint.
</Broside>
