<Broside type="tip" title="Taxonomy Rule" icon="book" id="rbt-aph-1">
Angertainment is the process of categorizing fury until it becomes too boring to actually feel.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="BAIT Mechanics" icon="book" id="rbt-aph-2">
A properly applied taxonomy can turn a riot into a highly engaging, multi-part documentary series.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Classification Wisdom" icon="book" id="rbt-aph-3">
If you cannot stop the rage, you must file it correctly so that future historians will know you had excellent handwriting.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Standard Forms" icon="book" id="rbt-aph-4">
The standard form of angertainment requires exactly two villains, neither of whom is the actual system that printed the form.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Taxonomy Axiom" icon="book" id="rbt-aph-5">
The ultimate goal of the taxonomy is to ensure that everyone is screaming at the exact correct sub-category.
</Broside>
