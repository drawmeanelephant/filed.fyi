# Comfort Ledgerling Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="SMALL KINDNESS COUNTED." icon="message-square" id="281-1">
I have logged three instances of the manager saying 'good job.' That brings our morale balance to exactly $0.14. Do not spend it all in one sprint.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="MORALE BOOKKEEPING ACTIVE." icon="file-text" id="281-2">
Every slice of pizza provided during crunch time is entered into the ledger. It offsets exactly one hour of unpaid overtime. The math is unassailable.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="QUANTIFIED REASSURANCE LIMITED." icon="check-square" id="281-3">
You requested a budget increase. I counter-offer with a very nice email about your 'indispensable spirit.' The ledger is balanced.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="SPENDING POWER RESTRICTED." icon="shield" id="281-4">
I am the accountant of warm fuzzies. The account is overdrawn, but the font on the statement is very encouraging.
</Broside>
