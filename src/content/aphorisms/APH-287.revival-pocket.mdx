# Revival Pocket Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="ISOLATED SOCKET" icon="zap" id="287-1">
The obsolete terminal booted perfectly, but only when plugged into the third socket of the grey power strip. We have taped off the surrounding floor to protect the anomaly.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="PRESERVED ATMOSPHERE" icon="archive" id="287-2">
Morale returned briefly to the second cubicle on the left. The archivist placed a heavy glass jar over the desk to prevent the atmosphere from dissipating.
</Broside>

<Broside type="caution" title="RETIRED ILLUSION" icon="printer" id="287-3">
The ancient printer successfully produced one flawless document. We framed the page and unplugged the machine before the illusion could break.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="ROTATING WARMTH" icon="refresh-cw" id="287-4">
There is a small circle of warmth in the drafty annex. We rotate the filing clerks through it in five-minute shifts to maintain their belief in the system.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="RETROACTIVE DELETION" id="APH-287.revival-pocket-5">
The inspector identified a misaligned staple in the 1993 archival box. To achieve full compliance, the department unanimously agreed to mathematically prove 1993 never occurred.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="CONTAINMENT MEASURES" id="APH-287.revival-pocket-6">
An unauthorized sigh was recorded near the tertiary filing cabinet. The perimeter has been sealed with caution tape, and all future breathing must be requisitioned in triplicate.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="COMPLIANCE CASCADE" id="APH-287.revival-pocket-7">
Upon discovering that the index of non-compliant events was itself improperly formatted, the review board opted to incinerate the index and classify the resulting ash as a proprietary asset.
</Broside>
