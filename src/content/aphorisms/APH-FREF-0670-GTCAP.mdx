# Gratitude Capture Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="Metrics of care. Record sealed." icon="heart" id="GRA-CAP-1">
Gratitude Capture is what happens when the institution mistakes 'thank you' for 'enough.'. The system kept the ritual and misplaced the function.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Outcome measure. Interpretation pending." icon="bar-chart" id="GRA-CAP-2">

</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Gratitude Capture. Operational impact denied." icon="message-square" id="GRA-CAP-3">

</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Protocol inversion. Efficacy irrelevant." icon="handshake" id="GRA-CAP-4">
The biological vector's instinct to express relief is automatically logged as a closed ticket. Any subsequent deterioration remains entirely out of scope.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Ritual extraction complete." icon="download" id="GRA-CAP-5">
We have successfully decoupled the phrase 'thank you' from its emotional burden. It now serves exclusively to populate the quarterly compliance dashboard.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Sentiment analysis override." icon="file-text" id="GRA-CAP-6">

</Broside>

</Broside>
