<Broside type="tip" title="Reclassification" icon="rotate-cw" id="gex-aph-1">
An experience is not truly lived until it has been rejected, reclassified, and resubmitted on Form GEX-2R.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Resubmission Axiom" icon="rotate-cw" id="gex-aph-2">
The act of resubmission is proof that the applicant has finally accepted the futility of their original memory.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="GEX-2R Fact" icon="rotate-cw" id="gex-aph-3">
Form GEX-2R is designed to ensure that by the time you finish explaining what happened, you no longer care that it did.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Experience Metrics" icon="rotate-cw" id="gex-aph-4">
A supplemental form does not add information; it merely dilutes the existing trauma.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="DOGE Wisdom" icon="rotate-cw" id="gex-aph-5">
We do not deny your experience. We simply require you to describe it using words that do not exist.
</Broside>
