# Alibi Seal Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="EXONERATING PAPERWORK FILED." icon="message-square" id="271-1">
I have a memo dated three weeks ago proving we warned you about the thing we just broke. The ink is suspiciously fresh, but the timestamp is absolute.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="OFFICIAL INNOCENCE TEXTURE APPLIED." icon="file-text" id="271-2">
The document feels heavy, like responsibility. It is covered in signatures that deflect blame like Teflon. It is the armor of the bureaucrat.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="ROOM CLEARED IN ADVANCE." icon="check-square" id="271-3">
Before the fault is even discovered, my seal is already glowing on the desk, declaring 'Not It' with unquestionable legal authority.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="GUILT REROUTED EXPERTLY." icon="shield" id="271-4">
The seal does not prove we didn't do it. The seal proves we successfully assigned the doing to a third-party vendor who no longer exists.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="LIABILITY DOWNLOADING." icon="message-square" id="APH-271.alibi-seal-5">
The stamp ensures the liability is still rendering. We remain legally innocent until the final document finishes its descent into the archive.
</Broside>
