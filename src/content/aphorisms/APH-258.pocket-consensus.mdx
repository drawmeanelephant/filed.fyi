# Pocket Consensus Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="LOCAL AGREEMENT FORMED." icon="message-square" id="258-1">
Three people in a hallway decided how it actually works. We keep this knowledge folded in our pockets, safe from the brutal light of official documentation.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="COMPACT APPROVAL SECURED." icon="file-text" id="258-2">
I nodded. You nodded. The code was deployed. A committee would have taken six weeks to say no. We take six seconds to say yes.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="TRUTH HIDDEN FROM COMMITTEE." icon="check-square" id="258-3">
If you bring this efficiency to the steering group, they will crush it with a framework. Keep it small. Keep it safe. Keep it secret.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="FRAGILE ALIGNMENT MAINTAINED." icon="shield" id="258-4">
We agree only as long as nobody writes it down. The moment it becomes policy, we will all deny it ever happened.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="POCKETED TIMELINE EXPIRED." icon="message-square" id="APH-258.pocket-consensus-5">
The six weeks of delay were never officially logged. We keep the true timeline hidden in our pockets, far from the steering group.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="COMPACT DEFERRAL ISSUED." icon="file-text" id="APH-258.pocket-consensus-6">
I nodded at the delay, and you nodded back. The unspoken agreement to wait another quarter is stronger than any formal documentation.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="FRAMEWORK OF HESITATION." icon="check-square" id="APH-258.pocket-consensus-7">
If we bring this lag to the committee, they will build a framework to manage it. We prefer to suffer the buffering in secret.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="ALIGNMENT OF INACTION." icon="shield" id="APH-258.pocket-consensus-8">
We have reached a silent consensus to let the request buffer indefinitely. As long as nobody writes it down, it remains a temporary pause.
</Broside>
