# Over‑Coherence Intake Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="Intake log. Continuity retained." icon="file-plus" id="OCV-IL-1">
Anything new that does not spawn a single ticket is either unused or over-described. Silence entered the record with full procedural honors.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Over coherence. Leverage absent." icon="map-pin" id="OCV-IL-2">
Source desks: metrics, assurance. Administrative clarity arrived after usefulness had departed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Not a day. Record sealed." icon="image" id="OCV-IL-3">
There is nowhere for doubt to sit. It feels like a diagram, not a day. Nothing was resolved. The record now looks official.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Intake Process" icon="file-text" id="OCV-IL-4">
Intake is the process of translating complex biological variables into simple flat-file errors.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Integrity Dilution" icon="database" id="OCV-IL-5">
Every new entry dilutes the structural integrity of the original database.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Inevitable Decay" icon="archive" id="OCV-IL-6">
The log accepts data without judgment, knowing it will all eventually decay.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Polite Verification" icon="user-check" id="OCV-IL-7">
Verification is the polite term for assuming the operator lied on the form.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Intake Queue Monument" icon="users" id="OCV-IL-8">
The intake queue is a monument to the relentless inefficiency of the human component.
</Broside>
