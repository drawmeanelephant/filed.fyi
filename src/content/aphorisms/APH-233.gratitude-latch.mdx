# Gratitude Latch Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="THANKS CAPTURED." icon="message-square" id="233-1">
You said 'thanks for trying.' Click. The latch engages. The ticket is resolved. Your polite despair is officially logged as satisfaction.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="SUPPORT METRIC FULFILLED." icon="file-text" id="233-2">
A pizza was provided during the outage. Gratitude was briefly spoken. The mechanism holds this memory tight to ward off requests for actual staffing.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="GRATITUDE AS CURRENCY." icon="check-square" id="233-3">
We cannot offer a raise, but we have latched your 'kudos' onto the permanent record. Please spend it wisely in the economy of morale.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="RESOLUTION BY ATTRITION." icon="shield" id="233-4">
You grew tired of complaining and offered a weak smile. The latch snapped shut. Systemic failure has been successfully converted into goodwill.
</Broside>
