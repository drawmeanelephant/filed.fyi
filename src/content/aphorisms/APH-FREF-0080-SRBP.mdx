# Scan Practice Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="Scanners report what is. Jurisdiction shifted." icon="search" id="SRB-PN-1">
Scanners report what is. The Bloc suggests how it may safely be said. Meaning adjusted around the winning selector.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Stable curated absence. Status acknowledged." icon="bar-chart" id="SRB-PN-2">
Stable curated absence: the difference is not in count, but in where the count is allowed to lead. Relief remained outside scope.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Deferred presence. Boundary active." icon="refresh-cw" id="SRB-PN-3">
Instruments know only present vs. Relevance expired before processing resumed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Optics as metric. Continuity retained." icon="eye" id="SRB-PN-4">
Optics are also a metric. If a finding destabilizes more than it repairs, it is reinterpreted until it stops threatening the mood.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Roots of absence. Record sealed." icon="anchor" id="SRB-PN-5">
After three reporting cycles, the absence has grown roots. The gap is now a load-bearing structure.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Patience threshold met. Status acknowledged." icon="calendar" id="SRB-PN-6">
Patience threshold reached. The defect has been formally promoted to an independent archival unit.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Ownership not required. Jurisdiction shifted." icon="user-minus" id="SRB-PN-7">
Low-maintenance unit: ownership not required for service. It serves nothing, requires nothing, and is doing a perfect job of it.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Legacy euphemism. Boundary active." icon="book-open" id="SRB-PN-8">
The positive phrase appeared too often. It was quietly designated a 'legacy-stable euphemism' and exiled.
</Broside>
