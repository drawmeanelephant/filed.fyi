<Broside type="tip" title="Leverage Deficit" icon="anchor" id="aph-01">
We observed the failure precisely. This satisfies the requirement, rendering any corresponding authority to fix it redundant.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Witness Logistics" icon="eye" id="aph-02">
The log acknowledges your suffering. The log is unable to open doors or issue refunds.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="Unauthorized Action" icon="shield-alert" id="aph-03">
Attempting to combine observation with intervention is a breach of the procedural boundary. Please return to a state of passive documentation.
</Broside>

<Broside type="caution" title="Weightless Truth" icon="feather" id="aph-04">
A verified fact carries no operational momentum unless stamped by an auditor who was not present.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Silence Protocol" icon="mic-off" id="aph-05">

</Broside>
