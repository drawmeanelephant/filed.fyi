<Broside type="note" id="APH-fref-0875-dlab-1" title="Deferred Consensus Protocol">
Three committees must approve the timeline extension, but the scheduling apparatus itself has stalled pending an unapproved extension.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" id="APH-fref-0875-dlab-2" title="Legacy Retention Imperative">
The old infrastructure remains fully powered, solely to remind the new infrastructure what failure looks like in triplicate.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" id="APH-fref-0875-dlab-3" title="Optimal Processing Decay">
Decelerating the response threshold by seventy percent ensures that most urgent inquiries simply expire before requiring a denial.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" id="APH-fref-0875-dlab-4" title="Strategic Manual Review">
Automation has been temporarily suspended so that human overseers can verify the exact duration of the ongoing administrative pause.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" id="APH-fref-0875-dlab-5" title="Archival Inertia Sequence">
We do not discard the contradictory policies; we merely index them so deeply that retrieving one requires a formal mandate from a defunct department.
</Broside>
