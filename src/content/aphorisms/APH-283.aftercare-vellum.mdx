# Aftercare Vellum Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="POST-INCIDENT TENDERNESS APPLIED." icon="message-square" id="283-1">
The outage destroyed your weekend. On Tuesday, I arrive with a beautifully formatted email about 'learnings and growth.' The paper is very soft.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="ELEGANT FOLLOW-UP DELIVERED." icon="file-text" id="283-2">
We cannot give you back the sleep you lost, but we have written a sonnet about our commitment to reliability. It is printed on vellum.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="UNDERLYING CONDITION UNCERTAIN." icon="check-square" id="283-3">
The aftercare is flawless. The root cause remains a mystery. We wrap the mystery in the heavy parchment of 'next steps.'
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="HARM SOFTENED RETROACTIVELY." icon="shield" id="283-4">
I am the bandage applied to the air next to the wound. It does not stop the bleeding, but the adhesive is very gentle.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="RETROACTIVE INCIDENT ERASURE." id="APH-283.aftercare-vellum-5">
The compliance violation was severe. Rather than restructure the architecture to prevent future occurrences, we simply reclassified the past week as an undocumented holiday. The hazard has been resolved.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="CONTAINMENT BY REDACTION." id="APH-283.aftercare-vellum-6">
We discovered a terrifying anomaly in the operational logs. To ensure the safety of the institution, we burned the logs and deleted the index. We are now fully compliant with a reality we invented this morning.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="FORMALIZED PANIC RESPONSE." id="APH-283.aftercare-vellum-7">
The auditor found an unauthorized structural vulnerability. In our terror, we filled out fifty pages of incident mitigation forms, which we then used to paper over the hole in the wall. The draft has stopped.
</Broside>
