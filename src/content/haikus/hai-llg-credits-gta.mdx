## Haikus

<Limerick type="note" title="Forty percent gone" icon="archive">
Forty percent gone  
dashboard called that completion  
budget called it art  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Green bars all the way" icon="sparkles">

some lines lived, some lines deserved  
an earlier death  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tokens burned like sage" icon="zap">
Tokens burned like sage

</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The ledger tracked spend" icon="file-clock">

</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Smooth completion curves" icon="layers">

</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="YamTeams grinned nearby" icon="ghost">

</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Some lines earned their keep" icon="book">

some were fancy packing foam  
same cost on the sheet  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Credits self-report" icon="binary">
Credits self-report

</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Second pass arrives" icon="file-question">

real quality check  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="If the budget purrs" icon="bug">

ignore the purring  
</Limerick>
