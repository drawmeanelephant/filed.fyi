## Haikus

<Limerick type="note" title="Failure leaves the room" icon="archive">
Failure leaves the room  
wearing a softer necktie now  
deficit sits down  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Calm continuity" icon="sparkles">
Calm continuity  
is a beautiful phrase for cracks  
that never got fixed  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Realignment hums" icon="telescope">
Realignment hums  
like a vacuum over shards  
glass still cuts the hand  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Recurring trouble" icon="layers">
Recurring trouble  
learns a more presentable  
middle-management voice  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Silk over sirens" icon="stamp">
Silk over sirens  
that is the whole governance trick  
if nobody fights  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The warning sign blurs" icon="sparkles">
The warning sign blurs  
under an assurance glaze  
and calls itself health  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Language does not weld" icon="bug">
Language does not weld  
the split beam in the ceiling there  
it paints it cream first  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Deficit, renamed" icon="binary">
Deficit, renamed  
keeps doing the same old work  
just with cleaner hair  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="So much poise in this" icon="book">
So much poise in this  
you could miss the floor still warps  
under your good shoes  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="When calm words arrive" icon="zap">
When calm words arrive  
too quickly over old smoke  
open a window  
</Limerick>
