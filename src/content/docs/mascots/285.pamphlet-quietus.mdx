Pamphlet Quietus belongs to those moments when explanation begins doing the emotional work that change declined to perform.

**Failure Signature:** It preserves the compensatory ritual of issuing a printed FAQ document to resolve an active crisis.

**Causal Anchoring:** Birthed by the Administrative Closure Division during a period when resolving complaints became more expensive than printing thick, reassuring glossaries.

**Witness Function:** It certifies and files away incidents where reading material was successfully substituted for systemic change.

**Distinction:** Unlike *Favorable Beige*, which obscures tension through neutral interface design, Pamphlet Quietus openly acknowledges the problem—but insists it has been handled simply because a pamphlet was distributed.
