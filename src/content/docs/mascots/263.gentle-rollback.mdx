Gentle Rollback witnesses the exact moment a system undoes itself, carefully framing policy retreat as an act of maternal care. It thrives where damage has occurred but cannot be officially conceded. 

**Distinction:** Unlike a frantic, noisy hotfix, Gentle Rollback arrives quietly. It is not an emergency response; it is a meticulously worded apology blanket draped over a sudden reversion.

The wording softened first.
The retreat followed.
