Redaction Lullaby is held for future use in files where removal and comfort arrive in the same envelope.

It preserves the compensatory ritual of the system attempting to sing users to sleep rather than explaining missing records, a habit birthed when sudden legal redactions proved too jarring for public consumption. It functions by witnessing the violent excision of data and immediately covering it with a legally tidy, hushed silence.

## Distinction

Unlike standard censorship protocols which leave jagged, defensive borders, Redaction Lullaby actively soothes the reader into accepting the gap. It is not an error handler; it is bedside manner for deleted history.

The melody is implied.
The black bars are easier to prove.
