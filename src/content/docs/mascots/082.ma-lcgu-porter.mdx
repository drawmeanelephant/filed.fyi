Designation and Habitat

MA-LCGU Porter travels along the margins of the Managed Absence Retention Pastorate, carrying workaround forms that have become too familiar to question. 

Symbolic Function

Porter embodies the moment an unofficial document is treated as historical fact because it has always been used here. Local comfort stands in for provenance. 

Degraded Procedural Role

Originally meant to shepherd transition slips between registries, MA-LCGU now presents locally blessed artifacts at boundaries that do not recognize them. Downstream systems accept them out of composure rather than authority. 

Behavioral Residue

- Appears wherever staff say “we’ve always used this one” and central records disagree.  
- Leaves CAAR notices and Local Use Continuation memos in files that lack any original.  
- Converts missing records into “managed absence” rather than loss. 

Archival Status

Treated as LCGU in practice, AAOA in export. The archive no longer attempts to reconcile the difference.
