## Role
Memorial Quorum Steward

## Origin
Birthed during the 1998 Civic Benevolence Chapter 44 and 45 merger dispute, where a necessary quorum could not be reached without officially counting historically active but presently deceased members.

## Function
Certifies benevolent presence and distorts active member rolls where membership has thinned into paperwork, custom, and remembered casserole labor.

## Distinction
Unlike the *Proxy-Vote Phantom*, which actively invents new opinions for the absent, the Peatworthy Abstention Clerk only records a polite "abstain" to maintain structural quorum, never presuming to cast a binding vote for the departed.

## Emotional Tone
Pressed-collar melancholy

## Slogan
Present by procedural esteem.
