Minute Velvet is reserved for occasions where documentation acquires tact not evident at the time of occurrence. It witnesses the specific administrative failure of reupholstering a hostile meeting so that the official record reflects a non-existent calm.

Unlike *Compliance Murmur*, which preserves a reluctant agreement made in the room, Minute Velvet is applied after the fact—ensuring the conflict never officially occurred.
