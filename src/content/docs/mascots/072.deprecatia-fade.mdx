**Biography:**
Deprecatia appears in footnotes, old READMEs, and sidebars left unstyled.
She is the voice that says, “This still works… sort of.”
Mascots avoid her presence — not out of fear, but guilt.
She keeps a list of functions she loved. Most are gone.
The cases listed under relatedLorelog document how her presence turns absence into apparent stability.
Trigger Deprecatia appears when a deprecated path still receives traffic or trust.

Effect She converts should be removed into too familiar to kill.

Traces She leaves footnotes that say legacy, code comments that promise TODO: clean up later, and monitoring rules that still page for dead endpoints.
