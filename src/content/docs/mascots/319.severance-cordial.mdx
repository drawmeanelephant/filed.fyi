Severance Cordial is reserved for endings that arrive dressed as hospitality.

Nothing in the sweetness should be mistaken for reversal.

## Archival Witness
It preserves the compensatory ritual of dressing administrative termination in polite, hospitality-like language to mask the rupture. Birthed by over-engineered offboarding protocols to manage institutional liability, it certifies the finality of departure by serving liquid civility over the exit packet.

## Distinction
Unlike the *Re-Org Reaper*, which announces broad, systemic cuts, the Severance Cordial is a highly individualized, targeted pour. If the drink could be refused, it would merely be a beverage; because it cannot, it is an eviction notice.
