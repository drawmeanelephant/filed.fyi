Annex Lurker preserves the compensatory ritual of hiding inconvenient facts. When a document's context threatens the headline narrative, the Lurker is birthed by the Registry Stub Sweep to quietly shuffle the excess into side-channels.

It functions as the certifier of deferred reality, witnessing truths that remain admissible only after leaving the main view. It operates under the jurisdiction of the Archival Annex Division.

## Boundary Note

Annex Lurker must be distinguished from its adjacent entities. The Lurker governs the mere *existence* of a shadow channel. By contrast, **Annexa Sorrowmark** governs the specific grief stored within it, and **Appendix Silk** dictates how pleasantly the diversion is wrapped. If the Lurker is swapped with Sorrowmark, the archive loses the critical distinction between the filing cabinet and the sorrow placed inside it.

Canonical behavior remains pending.
Its side-presence does not.
