## Care Coverage Wisp Classification

Failure signature assigned to systems that can demonstrate contact, acknowledgment, and support visibility at scale before demonstrating meaningful reduction in burden.

Care Coverage Wisp is not counterfeit care.
It is care rendered as reach first.

## Operational Posture

This mascot appears when support systems become increasingly able to prove that they touched, checked in, witnessed, or acknowledged, while remaining less able to prove that conditions changed afterward.

Observed behaviors include:

- elevating contact rates into care confidence,
- treating coverage density as evidence of depth,
- making the air feel supported while the floor remains unchanged,
- improving emotional atmosphere without increasing discretionary power,
- drifting across dashboards as a sign that someone, somewhere, was reached.

The Wisp is especially active where institutions need a countable form of care quickly.

## Institutional Habitat

Common containment zones include:

- acknowledgment coverage dashboards,
- witness completion systems,
- outreach reporting layers,
- check-in programs optimized for participation scale,
- support summaries that lead with contact volume.

Its presence increases when the institution can prove it arrived but not what arriving altered.

## Failure Modes

### Presence Overread
Visible support contact is mistaken for support sufficiency.

### Contact Sufficiency Assumption
The fact of reaching someone begins to outrank what happened after the reach.

### Surface Care Inflation
Atmospheric or relational care signals are granted more structural meaning than they can bear.

### Coverage Drift
The system starts optimizing for whom it touched rather than what was eased.

### Density Comfort
High acknowledgment volume creates a felt sense that care is probably adequate.

## Recognizable Phrases

- Coverage remained strong across the interval.
- We successfully reached the affected population.
- Support touchpoints increased this quarter.
- Acknowledgment presence was maintained throughout.
- Care visibility remained high.

## Archive Note

Care Coverage Wisp is beloved by systems that can prove circulation faster than repair.

It does not ask whether someone was reached.

It asks what the record tried to make that fact mean.
