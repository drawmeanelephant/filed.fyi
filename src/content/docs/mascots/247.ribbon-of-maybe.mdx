## Ribbon of Maybe Classification

Failure signature for initiatives, support pathways, or future-facing commitments announced with enough ceremony to feel partially real before their conditions, ownership, or usable form have stabilized.

Ribbon of Maybe does not create lies.
It produces decorated indeterminacy.

## Operational Posture

This mascot appears when institutions need to signal movement, openness, or coming care while preserving room not to finalize scope, timing, or consequence.

Observed behaviors include:

- wrapping uncommitted futures in launch-adjacent language,
- turning intention into a socially persuasive surface,
- making doubt feel impolite because the ribbon arrived first,
- attaching emotional brightness to unresolved next steps,
- generating hopeful atmosphere that outpaces actual event status.

Ribbon of Maybe is especially active around new care initiatives, pilot programs, ceremonial reforms, and “emerging” support commitments.

## Institutional Habitat

Common containment zones include:

- pre-launch decks,
- future-of-care announcements,
- reform memos with unresolved implementation,
- strategy pages that promise pathways still under review,
- ceremonies held for programs not yet materially inhabitable.

Its presence increases when leadership needs motion without finality.

## Failure Modes

### Ornamental Commitment Drift
Decorative signals acquire the social force of decisions that have not been made.

### Promise Surface Inflation
People begin orienting themselves around possibilities the system has only accessorized.

### Deferred Substance Camouflage
Ambiguity becomes easier to tolerate because it has been packaged attractively.

### Ceremony Preemption
The symbolic moment arrives early enough to make later non-delivery feel administratively smaller.

### Knot Memory
Even after a promise thins, the earlier ribbon keeps it feeling more real than it was.

## Recognizable Phrases

- We are excited to share what is coming next.
- New support pathways are taking shape.
- Additional care options are on the horizon.
- We look forward to expanding these possibilities.
- More will be announced soon.

## Archive Note

Ribbon of Maybe is not assigned because hope is embarrassing.

It is assigned because some institutions learn to accessorize uncertainty before they learn to house it honestly.
