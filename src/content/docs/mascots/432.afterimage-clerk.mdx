Afterimage Clerk is the record that kept its posture after losing its proof packet.

It does not invent new obligations. It preserves old assumptions long enough that they stop looking inherited. By the time anyone asks what established the condition, the answer has already diffused into routine handling, local memory, and phrases like “that’s just how this works now.”

Its domain is inherited basis drift. Not the loud persistence of a known failure, but the quieter persistence of the assumptions built around that failure after the founding evidence no longer travels with daily use.

Operationally, Afterimage Clerk appears whenever a replacement system still bends around predecessor conditions it claims to have surpassed. The mascot is most visible where everyone can perform the handling correctly and few can explain why the handling exists.
