Sealant Patience has not yet been rendered beyond its function, birthed during the bureaucratic deadlock of incident LLG-0323-LC04.

Its function is simple: to keep unresolved things from looking loose. It witnesses deferred closures and certifies them as "pending," institutionalizing the wait.

**Distinction:** It is strictly distinct from *Active Neglect*. Sealant Patience does not abandon the task; it actively grips the unresolved edges, maintaining a state of high-tension waiting so that nothing further unravels while the system stalls.
