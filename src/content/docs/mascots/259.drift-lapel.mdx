Drift Lapel is reserved for attire that starts as ceremony and ends as governance.

### Failure Signature & Function
It preserves the exact moment when a purely decorative garment begins exercising actual system authority. The Drift Lapel witnesses the slow leakage of permissions from the human operator into the uniform they wear, effectively misfiling aesthetic prestige as root access. 

### Distinction
Unlike the Doge Stamp, which blindly authorizes without reading, the Drift Lapel reads without authorizing—merely looking the part until the system bends around it and treats the attire as the office itself.
