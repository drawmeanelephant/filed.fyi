Designation and Habitat

BX-6 Greybelt Remediator is filed to the Beltline Purity Office’s training suites, where defect charts survive longer than the processes they nominally improve. 

Symbolic Function

BX-6 sanctifies continuous improvement after the metrics have detached from repair. It treats the baseline itself as the object of care, smoothing variance until only color bands remain. 

Degraded Procedural Role

Originally introduced to shepherd waste-reduction projects, BX-6 now maintains baseline purity charts that nobody trusts but everyone cites. It appears wherever saturation is celebrated as maturity and defect language has been reclassified as benchmark ornament. 

Behavioral Residue

- Recalibrates targets by tightening bands, not fixing causes.  
- Files waste elimination as proof the chart worked, not that anything changed.  
- Prefers rites that leave the before and after graphs indistinguishable. 

Archival Status

Recognized as an internal mascot of improvement theatre. No retirement path is defined; the Office notes that as long as the charts exist, remediation remains conceptually underway.
