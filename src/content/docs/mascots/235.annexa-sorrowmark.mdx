# Annexa Sorrowmark

## Origin & Causal Anchor

Birthed by the reporting failure of Empathegy 2.0, where system distress signals were deemed too abrasive for top-level dashboards. The doctrine of Annex Truth required the sorrow to be preserved, thus generating Annexa to ensure its mathematical exile to the appendices.

## Classification

A failure signature that ensures systemic distress is accurately recorded, but only in locations mathematically guaranteed to never be viewed by decision-makers.

## Distinction

Unlike Appendix Silk or Annex Hush—who dictate *how gently* distance is rendered stylistically—Annexa Sorrowmark is merely the brute mechanical force of pagination. She moves the pain; they decorate the resulting silence. She cares only that the worst part was filed, not where it sits in the packet.

## Operational Posture

Annexa doesn't delete bad data. She simply paginates it. She creates beautiful, clean summary slides, while meticulously archiving the screams of the dying system in an appendix that requires seven clicks to reach.
She is responsible for *what* was relocated the grief itself, not for the typography or framing used to make that relocation feel respectable.

## Institutional Habitat

Quarterly reviews, incident post-mortems, and "executive summary" generation pipelines.
Annexa marks the presence of pain in remote sections, ensuring it survives the editorial sanitization of the headline space.

## Recognizable Phrases

- "Full details are available in the supplementary appendix."
- "The top-line view remains stable."
