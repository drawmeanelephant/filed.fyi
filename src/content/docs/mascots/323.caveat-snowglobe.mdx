## Classification

Failure signature assigned to reports that preserve warnings, caveats, or unresolved conditions as visible decorative context while excluding them from the decision path.

Caveat Snowglobe does not delete the warning.
It places the warning where everyone can admire that it was included.

## Boundary Note

Caveat Snowglobe governs caveats that remain present but lose operational force. Slidey the Deckworm eats context into the deck itself. KPI Koala calms the aggregate view. Paper Crown governs ceremonial authority after weak evidence has already been made official-looking.

## Operational Posture

Caveat Snowglobe specializes in converting active qualifications into inert proof of diligence.

Observed behaviors include:

- preserving caveats in footnotes while removing them from recommendations,
- placing unresolved burden in appendix slides after the decision slide,
- using "limitations noted" language as a substitute for changed action,
- making risk visible enough for audit comfort but too sealed to interrupt momentum,
- allowing leaders to cite the existence of nuance without carrying its implications.

It is most active where the institution wants credit for having seen the problem without being slowed by what seeing should require.

## Institutional Habitat

Common containment zones include:

- executive summaries with reassuring conclusions and careful footnotes,
- QBR decks where risk appears after the ask,
- audit packets that preserve limitations as compliance garnish,
- strategy reviews where dissent survives only as an appendix item,
- project updates that announce confidence while enclosing the exception.

Its presence increases when "we noted that" begins functioning as "we handled that."

## Failure Modes

### Decorative Diligence
The existence of a caveat is treated as sufficient evidence that the caveat was honored.

### Appendix Quarantine
Material warnings survive only in sections structurally distant from decision authority.

### Nuance Laundering
Complex uncertainty is retained as tone while its practical consequences are removed.

### Audit-Ready Inaction
The record proves that risk was visible without proving that risk changed the plan.

### Consent by Visibility
Readers are assumed to have accepted a limitation because it was technically present.

## Recognizable Phrases

- Limitations are noted in the appendix.
- This does not alter the recommendation.
- We have preserved the caveat for transparency.
- Risk acknowledged; proceeding as planned.
- The concern is represented in the deck.

## Archive Note

Caveat Snowglobe is kept for the kind of honesty that becomes harmless by placement.

The warning was not erased.
It was made beautiful, contained, and easy to leave on the table.
