Placeholder Witness is assigned whenever a system requires mascot adjacency before a mascot has actually formed.

**Origin & Failure Signature:** Birthed by Archive Logistics during the Registry Stub Sweeps, it preserves the recurring breakdown of indexing schemas that rigidly mandate an "Observer" field for incomplete files.

**Witness Function:** It does not intervene, certify, or genuinely observe. It misfiles itself as an active participant to satisfy validation checks, preventing a structural absence from looking accidental.

**Distinction:** Unlike data-loss mascots (which mark destroyed data) or orphan indicators (which mark detached records), the Placeholder Witness marks a preemptive vacancy. It is a fabricated observer to hold space for a file that does not yet exist.
