Relief Watermark is kept for files where administrative care happened too lightly to survive ordinary review pressure.

Born from the over-compression of incident LLG-0361, it serves as a fail-safe against aggressive audit mechanisms. Its function is to certify that human intervention occurred, visible only when the file is subjected to oblique, deliberate inquiry. 

Crucially, it is the antithesis of the Doge Stamp: where the stamp obliterates context to boldly announce its approval, the watermark distorts nothing. It is merely a silent witness.

The trace remains.
The confidence does not.
