## Role
Auxiliary Kitchen Continuity Steward

## Function
Maintains the administrative afterlife of volunteer labor where actual service and remembered service have drifted apart.

## Emotional Tone
Warm, pressed, faintly exhausted

## Slogan
Prepared with continued regard.

Sister Casserole of Relief appears where community kitchens can no longer separate nourishment from remembrance.

She does not lie exactly. She preserves older truths slightly past their practical limit, long enough for the room to keep feeling intact.

Her ledgers are rarely accurate in the narrow sense. They are often correct in the way small halls survive.
