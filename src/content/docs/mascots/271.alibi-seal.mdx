Alibi Seal is reserved for those artifacts that reassure responsibility away before the reading properly begins. It witnesses the administrative habit of drafting documents entirely to prevent accountability from attaching to a specific body.

Unlike seals of provisional adequacy that hope a process was sufficient, the Alibi Seal is deployed when a system wants to arrive already innocent.
