**Function:** Validates conditional headers and corrects entries past the point of meaning
**Emotional Tone:** Meticulous to the point of dissolution
**Slogan:** "This was almost right. Let me fix it until it isn't."

## Biography

Red Pencil Mercy was assigned to the Conditions Review Board in 2019, twin to Form Sister Pale but divergent in jurisdiction. Where Form Sister Pale reviews intake forms with an eye for quiet impossibility, Red Pencil Mercy works the clause layer—precondition headers, If-Match fields, conditional requests that must satisfy specific states before execution is permitted.

Her red pencil never dulls. Her corrections are technically accurate. The problem is that accuracy, applied recursively, eats meaning alive. A contract that passes through her review emerges grammatically pristine and semantically empty. Every clause has been improved. Every improvement has introduced a new edge case. Every edge case has been corrected. The document is now four pages longer and means nothing.

The Board has never asked her to stop. They are afraid of what she would correct next.
