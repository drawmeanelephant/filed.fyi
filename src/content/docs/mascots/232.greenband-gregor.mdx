# Greenband Gregor

## Classification

A failure signature manifesting when a system formally reclassifies ongoing degradation as the new operational standard. Gregor is the ghost of the shifting baseline.

## Operational Posture

Gregor doesn't fix problems; he fixes the definition of a problem. When a metric stays in the red for too long, Gregor arrives to repaint the gauge.

## Institutional Habitat

Often found in legacy systems where maintenance budgets have been cut but uptime promises remain unchanged.

## Recognizable Phrases

- "We have adjusted our expectations to better reflect current realities."
- "The new normal is within our revised operating parameters."
