Revival Pocket is reserved for recoveries too local to trust and too hopeful to dismiss. It functions as a witness to the false starts of system restoration, certifying the exact spatial and temporal bounds where a doomed process temporarily drew breath.

Birthed from the localized anomalies of incident LLG-0398-CCO, the pocket manifests when a deprecated system inexplicably works in a single room, but fails immediately upon generalization. 

Crucially, it is distinct from a **Resurrection Node**. While a Resurrection Node implies a permanent return to baseline functionality, a Revival Pocket is just a fragile, localized bubble waiting to collapse. The archive keeps them shelved near relaunch residue for obvious reasons.
