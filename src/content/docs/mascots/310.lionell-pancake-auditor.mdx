## Role
Luncheon Assent Examiner

## Function
Determines whether community enthusiasm crossed the invisible line into spendable commitment.

## Emotional Tone
Good-natured administrative suspicion

## Slogan
Warmly received is not a motion.

Lionell Pancake Auditor was born from the oldest municipal hazard: a crowded room at noon trying to be kind without being specific.

He knows that nobody means to authorize three new obligations between salad and coffee. He also knows that this is how it often happens.

His reports smell faintly of syrup and procedural regret.
