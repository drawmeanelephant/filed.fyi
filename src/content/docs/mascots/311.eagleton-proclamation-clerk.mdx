## Role
Banner Phrase Registrar

## Function
Registers the moment a decorative saying ceases to be décor and resumes its career as governance.

## Emotional Tone
Patriotic administrative gravity

## Slogan
Recovered under heritage authority.

Eagleton Proclamation Clerk does not invent policy. He notices when the room has been kneeling to one for years without calling it that.

He treats banners as slow legislation and basement dust as a kind of parliamentary weather.

Most objections to his work arrive too late, usually after the phrase has already improved someone's posture.
