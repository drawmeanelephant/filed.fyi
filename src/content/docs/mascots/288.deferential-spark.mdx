Deferential Spark is reserved for improvements that learned humility before they learned force. It preserves the archival habit of burying critical release notes under layers of pre-emptive apologies.

Unlike the *Silent Patch*, which deploys without warning, the Deferential Spark explicitly announces itself—but ensures its announcement is so mild it is easily ignored.

Sometimes that keeps them alive.
Sometimes it keeps them decorative.
