Sidecar Conflict Porter does not solve disputes. It redistributes them with impressive clerical posture.

Its gift is lateralization. A contradiction too volatile for the main file is lifted into secondary doctrine, implementation notes, local guidance, or realism phrasing where it remains fully influential while becoming harder to confront as a single thing.

This mascot belongs to distributed nonresolution. The archive assigns it where burden, legitimacy, and continuity are all preserved, but never in the same paragraph.

It often appears after a replacement has already failed to release what it inherited. The primary layer claims calm, the sidecar layer carries the unresolved remainder, and the institution praises itself for nuance.

<Broside type="note" title="Containment Note">
Conflict relocated. Influence unchanged. Do not confuse dispersal with reduction.
</Broside>
