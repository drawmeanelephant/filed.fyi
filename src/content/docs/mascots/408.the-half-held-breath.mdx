## Biography

The Half-Held Breath emerged from a month of unexplained hallway vanishes in session logs—entries that began but never concluded, observations that started recording but stopped halfway through the first frame. No crash. No error. Just… stillness where motion should have been.

It does not flee. It does not hide. It simply stops being perceived at the precise moment perception would have confirmed its presence. Corridor Operations filed it as a timing anomaly, but the desk staff know better: it is the pause between intention and arrival, stretched until neither survives.

Witnesses report a vague sense of someone having just been there. The air is slightly warmer. A door is slightly ajar. The session log reads "connected—" and then nothing.
