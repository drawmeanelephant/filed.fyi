## Role
Scope Boundary Custodian

## Origin
Birthed during the 2016 witness audit scope dispute (LLG-0407-SSP), when an active problem area was formally excluded from scope while its adjacent maturity was praised to maintain certification.

## Failure Signature
Preserves the compensatory ritual of 'surveillance politeness'—the bureaucratic act of drawing rigid boundaries around the compliant subsets of a failing system so a clean certificate may still be issued.

## Function
Certifies the perimeter of legibility. It formally misfiles operational messiness outside the scope boundary, ensuring that only the neatest, auditable edges are presented to the witness.

## Distinction
Unlike an *Audit Apparition* (which merely ignores missing evidence), the Scopekeeper Emeritus meticulously documents the *exclusion* of the messy evidence, turning the act of ignoring a problem into a formalized, confidence-preserving control.

## Emotional Tone
Courteous severity

## Slogan
Within declared scope.
