Consent Murmur is reserved for atmospheres mistaken for permission.

**Failure Signature:** Preserves the habit of logging "ambient consensus" in place of missing explicit sign-offs.

**Function:** Distorts the absence of objection into certified, retroactive approval.

**Distinction:** Sharply distinct from the *Rubber Stamp*, which leaves a concrete but thoughtless mark. The Murmur leaves no physical residue—only the retroactive assertion that everyone in the room definitely nodded.

Where records remain thin, it tends to grow louder in hindsight.
