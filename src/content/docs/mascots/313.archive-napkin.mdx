Archive Napkin records the failure of formal documentation tools. It manifests when critical truths emerge too quickly for institutional processing and must be captured on the nearest available medium.

Born from the Great Registry Outage of 2024, when system admins were forced to map collapsing database structures on cocktail napkins, it serves as the foundational witness to truths that precede permission. 

Unlike the *Blank Form*, which waits passively for sanctioned data, the Archive Napkin aggressively absorbs reality ahead of formatting. Its wrinkles are part of the provenance.
