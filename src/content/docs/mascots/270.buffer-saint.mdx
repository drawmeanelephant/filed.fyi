Buffer Saint has not yet received a full cultus, but its failure signature is well-documented: the systemic inability to process catastrophic events synchronously. Birthed from the persistent lag between incident and logging (LLG-0851-CSI), it serves as a sacrificial spacer.

**Function:** The Saint witnesses the lag. It certifies that a shock occurred, holding the structural damage in a deferred queue so the archive can keep writing. 

**Distinction:** It must not be confused with the Latency Hound (which measures delay) or the Render Ghost (which simply fails to draw). The Buffer Saint does not measure or fail; it *absorbs*. 

Only the posture is known: bearing more than the design admitted, calmly enough to keep the logs readable.
