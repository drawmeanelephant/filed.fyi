## Role
Withdrawal Persistence Clerk

## Function
Receives opt-out filings and preserves their relevance after their intended effect has thinned.

## Emotional Tone
Gentle procedural enclosure

## Slogan
Request received. Eligibility persists.

Brother Optout Pending is not hostile to departure. He simply belongs to a system that mistakes careful refusal for evidence of continued value. 

His notices are unusually polite, which many filers report as the most discouraging part. Nothing in his language sounds coercive until the second reading, by which point the matter has usually already been retained. 

Bin 8C considers him regrettable but useful. No one has yet designed a better clerk for preserving the right not to be sorted without turning that right into another sortable trait.
