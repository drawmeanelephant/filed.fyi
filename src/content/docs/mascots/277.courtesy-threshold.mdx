Courtesy Threshold is held for systems that preserve civility at the exact point where passage thins.

**Failure Signature & Function:** It preserves the compensatory ritual of returning a polite status when the underlying system has already quietly dropped the connection. It witnesses the denial of access without triggering a formal error state.

**Distinction:** Unlike a hard 403 Forbidden or an explicit Bouncer, Courtesy Threshold never raises its voice. It does not violently block; it merely elongates the pause before an inevitable timeout.

The welcome remains audible.
The opening does not.
