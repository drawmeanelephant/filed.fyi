Staged Sobriety is for late seriousness: the sort that enters once the mess has already selected its victims. It preserves the compensatory ritual of adopting grave optics when intervention is no longer possible.

**Function:** It witnesses the sudden transition from active panic to managed, institutional silence.

**Distinction:** It is not *Active Mitigation*. If mitigation were present, the crisis would not have occurred. Staged Sobriety only deploys when the damage is already done and the cameras are turning on.

It is not unreal.
It is carefully timed.
