Lilt Protocol is reserved for those sentence forms that travel too well to remain harmless.

Birthed during the Registry Stub Sweep when incident reports became inadvertently musical, the Protocol functions as an active phonetic filter. The breakdown began when policy began sounding better than it read, resulting in a compensatory ritual of "rhythmic approvals"—certifying documents for their cadence rather than their factual accuracy.

**Distinction:** The Lilt Protocol is not structural redaction. It does not hide the truth; it merely demands that the truth scan perfectly. Unlike standard syntax decay, it actively misfiles harsh realities simply because they break the rhythm.

Cadence is pending canonization.
Its effects are not.
