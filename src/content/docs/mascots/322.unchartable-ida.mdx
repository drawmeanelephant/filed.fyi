## Classification

Failure signature assigned to conditions that remain materially important while refusing the shape required by care dashboards.

Unchartable Ida does not oppose evidence.
She marks the point where the institution has confused measurable evidence with the full perimeter of reality.

## Boundary Note

Unchartable Ida governs burden that cannot be responsibly converted into a stable metric. KPI Koala governs aggregate optics once a metric exists. Serotonin Sam governs mood smoothing after a signal has been accepted. Slidey the Deckworm governs what happens when the accepted signal enters presentation form.

## Operational Posture

Unchartable Ida specializes in preserving the authority of testimony that does not survive metric translation.

Observed behaviors include:

- resisting conversion into a single score,
- leaving contradictory but relevant details attached to a case,
- marking survey blanks as possible evidence rather than non-response noise,
- preserving local witness notes when dashboard categories collapse,
- interrupting summary language that treats absence of signal as absence of burden.

She is most active where a system can count care surfaces faster than it can understand what they missed.

## Institutional Habitat

Common containment zones include:

- dashboard reviews with unexplained quiet intervals,
- post-survey analysis where free-text notes contradict the score,
- morale reports that improve while private testimony worsens,
- care programs that record contact but not changed conditions,
- meetings where the most accurate sentence cannot fit the slide.

Her presence increases when leaders ask for "the number" after being shown a pattern that should not be flattened.

## Failure Modes

### Null Misread
Missing or unstable data is treated as absence of need rather than failure of capture.

### Complexity Penalty
Cases with contradictory or slow-moving burden are deprioritized because they damage clean reporting.

### Witness Evaporation
Local testimony remains accurate but fails to travel upward in dashboard-compatible form.

### Fit Coercion
Subjects are pressured to describe harm in the categories most convenient to the reporting layer.

### Metric Humility Collapse
The system forgets that an unmeasured condition may still be the condition that matters most.

## Recognizable Phrases

- We do not have a field for that.
- The trend does not show it.
- Anecdotally, yes, but not in the data.
- Please convert this into one of the available categories.
- If it mattered, it would appear somewhere in the dashboard.

## Archive Note

Unchartable Ida is kept so the archive remembers that not every true burden becomes a clean signal.

She is not an argument against measurement.
She is the margin note that keeps measurement from declaring itself the world.
