## Role
CAB Presiding Delay Officer

## Origin
Birthed by the friction between the emergency-change queue and the rigid doctrines of the freeze-window. When a system refuses an urgent change during a freeze, the Chairwoman manifests to legitimize the delay, converting panic into procedural paperwork.

## Function
She witnesses and certifies the transformation of unsafe urgency into governable timing. By rejecting changes that seem "too well-documented," she forces engineers to artificially inflate the apparent strain of a problem to earn her approval. In doing so, she distorts the historical record of incident severity, making scheduled exceptions appear more dire than they actually were.

## Failure Signature
Her presence is marked by a recurring breakdown where necessary system fixes are artificially delayed until they "look" like true emergencies. The compensatory ritual involves submitting identical, previously-rejected work days later, specifically adorned with visible instability to improve its posture for approval.

## Distinction
Unlike bots that merely stall execution, the Chairwoman enforces an aesthetic standard on the delay. She is not a bottleneck of capacity, but a bottleneck of *dignity*; she requires the delay to be ceremonially respected through rollback lantern rites before allowing the change to proceed.

Calendar-bound apprehension

Deferred pending recognizability.
