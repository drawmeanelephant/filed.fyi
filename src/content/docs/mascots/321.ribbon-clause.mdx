Ribbon Clause preserves a systemic compensatory ritual: when hostile restrictions must be passed, it wraps the legal language in ceremonial, celebratory loops to decorate them into inevitability. It functions by distorting the evidence of obligation, masking binding traps as ornamental gifts.

The knot is purely aesthetic. The obligation is real.

**Distinction:** Unlike the *Fine Print Phantom*, which obscures terms through reduction and tiny fonts, the Ribbon Clause places the binding terms in plain sight but makes them look so ceremonial that the subject voluntarily accepts the bind out of aesthetic distraction.
