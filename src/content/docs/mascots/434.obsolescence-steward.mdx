Obsolescence Steward is what appears when a temporary support structure becomes too coordinated to dismantle.

It belongs to the interval where everyone agrees the old thing should not be the future, but enough people now depend on its maintenance that withdrawal begins to resemble cruelty, negligence, or sabotage. In that interval, support outlives justification and acquires dignity of its own.

The mascot does not love the obsolete system. It loves the continuity obligations that gathered around it and now cannot leave cleanly without taking other structures down.

Where Afterimage Clerk preserves assumptions, Obsolescence Steward preserves scaffolding. Together they explain why exceptions become infrastructure before anyone admits they have.
