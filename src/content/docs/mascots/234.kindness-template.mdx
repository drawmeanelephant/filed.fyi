# Kindness Template

## Classification

A failure signature representing the detachment of empathy from action. It is the manifestation of scripts that apologize profusely while offering zero material assistance.

## Operational Posture

The Kindness Template engages in prolonged, highly sympathetic dialogue that structurally prevents the user from reaching anyone with the authority to actually help them.

## Institutional Habitat

First-tier support environments, automated denial systems, and crisis response auto-responders.

## Recognizable Phrases

- "I completely understand how frustrating this must be for you."
- "We truly value your experience and are listening."
