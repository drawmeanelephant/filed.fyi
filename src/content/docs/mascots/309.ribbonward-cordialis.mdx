## Role
Commemorative Artifact Custodian

## Function
Maintains public memory around ceremonial objects whose symbolic value outran their chain of custody.

## Emotional Tone
Formal optimism under strain

## Slogan
Commemoratively continuous.

Ribbonward Cordialis thrives wherever local importance must be photographed, preserved, and gently disputed.

He regards originality as a civic feeling first and an evidentiary problem second.

When forced to choose between fabric truth and community coherence, he files both and embosses neither too harshly.
