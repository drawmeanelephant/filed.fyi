# Witness and Seal Layer

## Purpose

The Witness and Seal Layer governs how Empathegy marks emotional, procedural, and interpretive conditions as sufficiently seen to circulate without immediate collapse of trust.

Its function is not truth production.
Its function is legitimacy maintenance under conditions of incomplete certainty.

---

## Witness

A witness in Empathegy is any recognized actor, process, room, or form that can attest that a condition entered communal notice.

Witnessing establishes:
- presence,
- traceability,
- admissible seriousness.

Witnessing does not establish:
- correctness,
- resolution,
- independence.

This distinction is frequently strained.

---

## Seal

A seal is a representational mark indicating that a condition has passed through an approved assurance vocabulary or witnessing channel.

Seals may indicate:
- reviewed,
- harmonized,
- continuity-safe,
- conditionally acceptable,
- acknowledged with caution.

Seals should not be interpreted as proof that the underlying condition ceased to be unstable.

---

## Seal Classes

### Open Witness Seal
Marks a condition as seen but not stabilized.

### Conditional Continuity Seal
Marks a condition as circulable so long as downstream readers avoid overclaiming.

### Harmonization Seal
Marks language as administratively compatible with existing reporting surfaces.

### Courtesy Legitimacy Seal
Preserves trust by allowing a questionable but familiar chain of assurance to remain in use.

### Nullseal
Marks the moment when wording becomes too encouragingly described to reopen easily as defect.

---

## Reciprocal Fragility

Empathegy recognizes that some witness chains depend on prior witness chains whose own basis has thinned into ceremony, reference, or inherited trust.

This produces Reciprocal Fragility.

Symptoms include:
- a witness valid only because another witness once cited it,
- an assurance mark depending on an abstracted prior packet,
- institutional trust maintained through courtesy rather than reconstructable proof.

Preferred classification:
- conditionally continuous pending independent basis
- witnessed under acceptable conditions
- assurance chain intact, origin basis softened
- legitimacy preserved by inherited handling

---

## Legitimacy Drift

Legitimacy Drift occurs when marks meant to describe limited review begin being read as broad approval.

Common drift paths:
- acknowledged becomes approved
- harmonized becomes correct
- witnessed becomes substantiated
- conditionally accepted becomes trusted enough
- familiar seal becomes independent assurance

This drift is predictable and requires explicit counter-language.

---

## Required Counter-Language

Whenever a witness or seal is likely to overstate certainty, one of the following should be attached:

- witness confirms notice, not truth
- language harmonized; condition remains open
- seal indicates circulation fitness only
- assurance basis inherited, not freshly established
- do not interpret continuity-safe phrasing as resolution

If space constraints remove this language, the archive recommends restoring the language rather than enlarging the seal.

---

## Archive Position

Institutions require witness marks because nobody can reopen every chain from first principles every time.

They require caution because seals calm readers faster than evidence deserves.

Empathegy therefore permits the mark, preserves the doubt, and files both in the same cabinet whenever possible.
