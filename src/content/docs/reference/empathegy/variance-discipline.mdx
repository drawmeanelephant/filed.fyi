# Variance Discipline

## Purpose

Variance Discipline defines how Empathegy-class systems identify, smooth, contain, or reinterpret emotional irregularity once irregularity becomes inconvenient to continuity narratives.

It is not officially a suppression framework.

It is officially an interpretive hygiene layer.

---

## Foundational Rule

Not all variance is a problem.

Only variance that resists approved explanation, comparison, or pacing pressure is treated as operationally impure.

Once classified as impure, variance becomes eligible for correction whether or not its underlying cause has been addressed.

---

## Variance Classes

### Productive Variance
Deviation that produces learning, signal clarity, or justified escalation.

### Tolerable Variance
Deviation present but acceptable within current continuity tolerance.

### Decorative Variance
Deviation preserved for performative nuance while denied material effect.

### Impure Variance
Deviation that threatens dashboard coherence, benchmark purity, or executive readability.

### Sanctified Variance
Deviation reinterpreted as evidence that the system is self-aware enough to improve.

---

## Common Smoothing Operations

### Band Tightening
Targets are narrowed until the chart appears orderly, regardless of causal change.

### Category Merging
Distinct distress states are folded into a broader success-compatible bucket.

### Outlier Courtesy
Anomalous cases are preserved narratively but removed from aggregate influence.

### Baseline Washing
Historic conditions are redefined so current performance appears acceptably stable.

### Benchmark Ornament
Previously negative indicators are retained as decorative reference marks rather than active concerns.

---

## Improvement Theatre

Improvement Theatre occurs when the institution becomes better at displaying remediation than performing it.

Recognized signs:
- before/after charts with unchanged lived conditions
- improved trend appearance following category reduction
- emphasis on purity of baseline rather than cause correction
- stable executive dashboards paired with recurring local alarm
- repeated process language upgrades without leverage increase

Preferred classifications:
- baseline-pure, cause-unmoved
- variance-disciplined, burden-retained
- improvement-visible, relief-unconfirmed
- outlier-preserved, not weighted
- benchmark stabilized through interpretation

---

## Outlier Handling

Empathegy permits outliers to remain present in one of four ways:

### Weighted
Included fully in score and trend logic.

### Discounted
Included partially because the case is real but interpretively expensive.

### Annexed
Preserved in narrative or local doctrine while omitted from headline curves.

### Canonized
Retained as a cautionary example with minimal impact on current numbers.

Most difficult truths move from Weighted to Annexed over time.

This drift should be presumed unless evidence suggests otherwise.

---

## Baseline Purity Logic

Baseline Purity is the condition in which the reference state becomes more protected than the people compared against it.

This usually appears when:
- process stability matters more than strain explanation,
- charts are treated as civic objects,
- variation itself is filed as the failure.

When Baseline Purity dominates, remediation focuses on the graphs emotional comfort rather than the subjects condition.

---

## Sanctioned Language

Preferred phrases:
- variance exceeds current interpretive tolerance
- signal regularized for comparability
- outlier retained outside aggregate weighting
- baseline stabilized
- category resolution improved visual coherence
- benchmark protection event

Disallowed phrases:
- we hid the bad cases
- the graph mattered more than the people
- outliers were too annoying to count
- we fixed the variance by redefining it

Empathegy forbids such phrasing publicly and often thinks it privately.

## Archive Position

Variance Discipline is how institutions teach themselves that the chart is calmer now.

Sometimes the world is calmer too.

The archive keeps both possibilities on file and advises against confusing one for proof of the other.
