# Witness Without Leverage — Handling Note

## Purpose

This note defines the filing and custody obligations that apply when testimony
arrives at a layer that cannot initiate corrective action, escalation, or
reclassification. It does not govern what witnessing is — see Witness Protocol.
It does not govern courtesy-without-leverage — see FREF-0630-CWLV. It governs
what the record must contain when testimony has been received by the wrong
structural layer, and when that mismatch is not an error but a feature of
the routing design.

---

## Foundational Distinction

A witness-without-leverage event is not a failure of the witness.
It is a custody routing problem.

The testimony is valid.
The layer that received it is real.
The layer's inability to act is structural, not incidental.

These three conditions distinguish this condition from:

- **Witness substitution** — witness cited as if it were action
- **Witness compression** — testimony thinned in transmission
- **Witness drift** — later systems cite the record as stronger than it was

All three can follow a witness-without-leverage event. This note concerns
only the moment of receipt.

---

## Foundational Rule

Witness Without Leverage applies when all of the following are present:

1. A burden, contradiction, or unsustainable interval is observed and attested.
2. The testimony is formally received — minuted, indexed, annexed, or acknowledged.
3. The receiving layer confirms that the testimony is real and admissible.
4. No reclassification, relief, scheduling change, or structural adjustment follows.
5. The structural limit is known at the layer of receipt, whether disclosed or not.
6. No upstream pathway was triggered.

This is distinct from testimony that is pending escalation, where a pathway
exists but has not yet moved. This note concerns testimony that cannot escalate
from its current location regardless of content.

## Filing Obligations

When a witness-without-leverage condition is confirmed, the receiving layer must:

1. Preserve the testimony intact in first-order records.
2. Attach an explicit custody note identifying the structural limit of the
   receiving layer.
3. Prohibit summary language that implies the testimony was actionable at
   receipt unless a separate escalation artifact is attached.
4. Record the gap between layer of receipt and layer of authority.
5. Flag the record for periodic review if the underlying condition recurs.

**Minimum custody note:**
`Testimony received. Receiving layer holds no escalation authority. Gap between receipt layer and action layer unresolved.`

**Stronger note where appropriate:**
`Witness record complete. No action pathway active at this layer. Upstream routing has not been confirmed. Testimony preserved; leverage absent.`

---

## What This Note Does Not Authorize

- It does not retroactively grant escalation authority to the receiving layer.
- It does not convert the witness record into a mitigation event.
- It does not satisfy action requirements by itself.
- It does not permit the gap to be summarized away.

---

## Common Custody Gap Patterns

- Listening board receives strain testimony; continuity ledger owns the
  interval classification.
- Annex preserves burden evidence; headline summary retains positive reading.
- Care-facing staff acknowledge difficulty; scheduling authority remains
  upstream.
- Local witness records contradiction; executive packet inherits only the
  calm layer.

In each pattern, the record contains the testimony. The testimony does
not reach the decision surface with operative weight.

---

## Escalation Triggers

Refer to Witness Protocol escalation criteria when:

- the same issue has been witnessed three or more times without leverage gain,
- witness notes have become the only surviving evidence of a condition,
- witness completion is cited in place of mitigation records,
- the receiving layer is known to have no propagation authority into action systems.

At that threshold, the archive classifies the condition as **witness substitution**,
not completed care.

---

## Approved Language

**Preferred phrases:**
- testimony received, leverage layer not reached
- witness complete, routing authority absent
- record indexed, propagation path not established
- acknowledged without structural consequence
- received at a layer without reclassification authority
- custody incomplete — leverage layer not reached

**Disallowed phrases:**
- was heard, therefore was helped
- the witness confirms support was provided
- testimony was received, matter resolved
- witness record serves as mitigation evidence
- witnessed three times, therefore the institution cared
