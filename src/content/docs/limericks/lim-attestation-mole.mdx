<Limerick type="danger" title="The Blind Verifier" icon="eye-off">
The Mole cannot see through the dirt,
But it signs off to cover its shirt.
It attests to the fact,
That the form is intact,
While ignoring the structural hurt.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Subsurface Logic" icon="layers">
Deep under the visible UI,
The Mole verifies every lie.
It digs through the code,
In a panicked-ass mode,
And waits for the system to die.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Signature Digger" icon="pen-tool">
It stamps all the papers below,
So the auditors put on a show.
The Mole is a prick,
With a signature trick,
And a total refusal to know.
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Attesting Dirt" icon="pen-tool">
The mole was attesting the dirt  
And wearing a digital shirt  
The server was slow  
With nowhere to go  
We hit the old drive with a squirt
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Collapsed Thud" icon="alert-triangle">
An attestation of mud  
Was washing away in the flood  
The mole was asleep  
The errors were deep  
The system collapsed with a thud
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Digging Holes" icon="arrow-down">
We witness the digging of holes  
And verify digital moles  
The server is dead  
The manual read  
We’re paying procedural tolls
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Out of Mind" icon="x-octagon">
The mole is incredibly blind  
The data was left far behind  
We stamp it approved  
The failure removed  
The logic is out of its mind
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The digging is perfectly flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody's cat
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Standard Routing" icon="file-text">
The pathway is clearly defined,
With documents properly signed.
A vector of human intent,
Is causing the pipeline to bend,
And queries are looping behind.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Structural Sigh" icon="alert-triangle">
We measured the volume of dust,
And updated the protocol trust.
The system is feeling quite sad,
Because the new user is bad,
And weeping is strictly unjust.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Beverage Sorting" icon="folder-minus">
The mole has attested the tea,
And sorted the leaves in a tree.
The cup is a network device,
Which executes queries on ice,
And prints out the water for free.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Queue Ignored" icon="coffee">
The pile of requests on the floor,
Is bigger than ever before.
We stamped it okay,
At the end of the day,
And locked up the archiving door.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Ancient Stamp" icon="archive">
The ink on the ledger is old,
A story the elders have told.
The mole is a spirit of law,
That signs with a mythical paw,
While server rooms turn into gold.
</Limerick>
