<Limerick type="danger" title="The Desperate Scribble" icon="edit-2">
You wrote "Please, oh please" on the side,  
But the system was totally blind.  
The filing was clean,  
And incredibly mean,  
And it left your despair far behind.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Side-Channel Desperation" icon="alert-triangle">
The form has no box for the tears,  
Or the weight of your terrible fears.  
You write in the space,  
With a miserable face,  
And they toss it away for the years.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Ignored Edge" icon="file-minus">
The machine only reads the black ink,  
And pushes you right to the brink.  
The Margin is sad,  
And it drives you all mad,  
While the company continues to sink.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Squeezed Text" icon="align-justify">
The margin was pleading for space  
The data was losing its place  
We squeezed the text tight  
In the middle of night  
And wiped the whole thing off the face  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Painfully Bright" icon="sun">
A pleading is never polite  
It gives the old server a fright  
The margin is thin  
We throw in the bin  
The error is painfully bright  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Big Hall" icon="box">
We justified text to the wall  
The meaning is starting to fall  
The human is weak  
And refuses to speak  
We’re waiting behind a big hall  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Begging Air" icon="wind">
The margin is begging for air  
The manual doesn't quite care  
We printed the file  
And wait for a while  
With absolutely no kind of flair  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Screen Clean" icon="monitor">
A margin of error is vast  
The future is stuck in the past  
We plead with the machine  
But the screen remains clean  
The silence is going to last  
</Limerick>
