<Limerick type="danger" title="The Desperate Scribble" icon="edit-2">
You wrote "Please, oh please" on the side,
But the system was totally blind.
The filing was clean,
And incredibly mean,
And it left your despair far behind.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Side-Channel Desperation" icon="alert-triangle">
The form has no box for the tears,
Or the weight of your terrible fears.
You write in the space,
With a miserable face,
And they toss it away for the years.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Ignored Edge" icon="file-minus">
The machine only reads the black ink,
And pushes you right to the brink.
The Margin is sad,
And it drives you all mad,
While the company continues to sink.
</Limerick>
