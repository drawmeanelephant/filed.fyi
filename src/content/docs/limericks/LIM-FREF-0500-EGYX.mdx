<Limerick type="note" title="Pre-Governance Substrate" icon="droplet">
A feeling arrived with a face,
Too particular yet for the case.
The taxonomy smiled,
Called it raw, unreconciled,
And prepared it for governable space.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Dashboard-Safe Affect" icon="grid">
The affect was sorted with care
Till the dashboard could tolerate air.
What exceeded the bin
Was not counted within,
Which is how categories dare.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Category Authority" icon="archive">
Where feeling outran its assigned
Little label, the label declined.
The excess stayed real,
But had no appeal
To the system's comparative mind.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Log 410-X" icon="file">
The binder was moved to the right,
And left on the table all night.
A tag was applied,
The numbers were tied,
The cover was thoroughly white.
</Limerick>
