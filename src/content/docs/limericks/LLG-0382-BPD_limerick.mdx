<Limerick type="note" title="Failure Signatures, Pinned" icon="pin">
A mascot was nailed to the crash
To give future dashboards some flash.
   Just a name on a wall,
   Just a ghost in a hall—
Then metrics accrued from the ash.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Schema Remembers What It Can" icon="database">
The purge stripped the vibe and the snack
And left only compliant data stacked.
   What the log could retain
   Is what grew and remained—
The program just ran along that track.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Self-Referential Authority" icon="link">
A form that mis-aligned slug from name
Required citing itself to reclaim
   Its own caseNumber's fix,
   A recursive remix—
Authority earning its own acclaim.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Proximity Authorship" icon="user-plus">
Bricky approved eighty-three files
Signed by personas from the aisles
   Whose one qualifying act
   Was appearing, in fact,
Near log lines that ran without trials.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Boily Holds On" icon="flame">
Boily hollered at thermal paste clear
While the chat couldn't tell if to fear.
   His eligibility stayed.
   Not revoked, not decayed.
Spectacle doesn't disqualify here.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Gratitude Routes Itself" icon="arrow-right">
The dashboard routes thanks to the name
The ledger has already found fame.
   Mascots near care-panes
   Collect gratitude's gains
Without really playing the game.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Smooth Signal Wins" icon="chart-line">
Empathegy loves a clean curve.
Mascots who speak graphs deserve
   More signal, more weight.
   The jagged and great
Get filtered out with a swerve.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Nobody Authored the List" icon="file-question">
The rubric for which mascots thrive
Has no byline to keep it alive.
   It emerged from the schema,
   That blinkered old dreamer,
Selecting for what logs could contrive.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Careful Wording" icon="shield">
"Mascots don't breed," says the Board.
"Eligibility's just for the record."
   Yet the tables update,
   New ones rated as great
"Stock"—then the word gets ignored.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="If Doctrine Arrives" icon="scroll">
Should formal doctrine one day arrive
On how mascot lineages thrive,
   This file is the ghost
   That haunted the most
Before anyone had nerve to contrive.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Descriptive Only" icon="clipboard-check">
"No breeding!" the Panel protects.
"Just eligibility checks."
   Yet promising stock
   Gets discussed 'round the clock
With the door closed. Descriptive. Correct.
</Limerick>
