import Limerick from '../../../components/Limericks.astro';



<Limerick type="note" title="Outbreak Light" icon="clipboard-list">
Parvovirus-P wears a *grin*
Through every preventable sin.
His clipboard's so bright,
His optimism's a light
In the dark where the outbreaks begin.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Empty Hall Echo" icon="volume-2">
He was assigned to a *shelter campaign*
Weeks after the outbreak had slain
The whole population.
His cheerful oration
Echoed through empty halls in vain.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Lifelong Commitment Loss" icon="package-x">
He misfiled a *vaccine batch shipment*—
It vanished without any glint-ment
Of recovery found.
The forms shuffled around,
And the loss was his lifelong commitment.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sharpest Blade Smile" icon="flag">
He marched in a *Wellness Parade*
Days after the quarantine made
Every banner a lie,
Every float a goodbye,
And his smile was the sharpest blade.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Grotesque Helpdesk" icon="monitor-off">
He distributes *brochures* at desks
That are decommissioned—the grotesques
Of a helpdesk that died.
His cheerfulness applied
To the void. No one answers his checks.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Eulogy Bloom" icon="flower-2">
He reads *failure reports* to the room
In rhyme, on awareness day's gloom.
The statistics he cites
Through commemorative rites
Are a eulogy dressed as a bloom.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Commemorative Wraps" icon="bandage">
His bandages are worn "*just in case*"—
Commemorative wraps on each space
Of both arms, ever ready
For shots never steady,
And the prevention that time couldn't chase.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Untouched Health Tips" icon="file-warning">
He monitors *expired posters* for mold,
Checking emotional resonance—old
And untouched and unread,
The health tips long dead,
But the optimism? Never controlled.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Smiling Pantomime" icon="smile">
He recites *statistics on loss*
To no one at all—at what cost?
The numbers still climb,
The cheerful pantomime
Keeps smiling through every exhaust.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Prevention Never Here" icon="heart-off">
So if cheerful mascots appear
In the wreckage of failures held dear,
That's *Parvovirus-P*:
Where the grin's a decree
That prevention was never quite here.
</Limerick>
