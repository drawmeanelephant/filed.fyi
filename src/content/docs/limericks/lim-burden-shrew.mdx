<Limerick type="note" title="The Invisible Load" icon="briefcase">
The Shrew carries all of the weight,
That the metrics refuse to translate.
The effort is spent,
But it makes not a dent
In the dashboard's immaculate state.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Keeping It Afloat" icon="anchor">
It scampers and scurries around,
To keep the old systems inbound.
It patches the leaks
With a series of squeaks,
And never makes even a sound.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Uncounted Effort" icon="bar-chart">
The burden is heavy and real,
But the chart has no column for zeal.
The little Shrew tries,
But the algorithm lies,
And nobody cares how you feel.
</Limerick>
