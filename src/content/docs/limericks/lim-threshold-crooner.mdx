<Limerick type="danger" title="The Melodic Bouncer" icon="music">
He sings you a beautiful tune,
Beneath the cold corporate moon.
He tells you to wait,
At the terrible gate,
And promises access quite soon.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Soft Gatekeeping" icon="lock">
The Crooner denies you a pass,
With a voice like a premium glass.
It’s highly polite,
But it ruins your night,
And he tells you to kiss his damn ass.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Entryway Persuasion" icon="mic">
You can’t go inside of the hall,
Because you have nothing at all.
The Crooner explains,
Through the terrible pains,
That you’re facing a brick fucking wall.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Lullaby of Rejection" icon="music">
He croons a soft tune in your ear,
To pacify all of your fear.
But the door is locked tight,
For the rest of the night,
And the Crooner just tells you to cheer.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="A Beautiful &#x22;No&#x22;" icon="x-octagon">
The rejection was sung in a chord,
Approved by the terrible board.
He smiled as he sang,
While the heavy gate rang,
And the access was fully ignored.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Gatekeeping Bastard" icon="mic">
The melody’s sweet and profound,
As he drives your request in the ground.
You’re locked in the cold,
Doing just what you’re told,
By the prettiest asshole around.
</Limerick>
