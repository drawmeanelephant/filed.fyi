<Limerick type="note" title="The Bell Rings Just Once" icon="bell-off">
The bell rang to signal the *call*—
But nobody came to the hall.
The promise had failed,
The expectation sailed,
And the desk was left empty through fall.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Request That Never Arrived" icon="inbox-x">
It rang for a request coming through—
That never had *anything to do*.
The bell tolled alone,
The desk sat stone-cold,
And expectations just fell into blue.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Silent Alerts for Nothing" icon="eye-off">
The bell sounds in *silence* each night
For promises fading from sight.
No answer at all,
No response to the call,
Just the bell marking what isn't right.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Expectation Failed Before Set" icon="alert-triangle">
A promise had failed—not yet made—
The expectation decided to *fade*.
The bell rang too soon,
The hope left the room,
And tomorrow just won't ever aid.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Desk Untouched" icon="box">
The bell finished ringing with care,
Left the desk in its seat *unaware*.
The space sat so still,
Nobody to fill,
The promise remained nowhere there.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Green Lights That Never Come" icon="alert-off">
The dashboard that monitors dreams
Is full of red failures and *screams*.
The bell rang that night
For green lights not bright,
And the promise was split at the seams.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Lingering in Absence" icon="hourglass2">
Where responses are always supposed,
The Unmet Bell hovered and *posed*.
It lingers and waits
For the promise that fates
Never delivered—now nobody knows.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Inherited From a Retired Dashboard" icon="inbox">
The old promise dashboard was removed,
Its green lights completely *disproved*.
The bell stayed behind,
Ringing in an unkind
Way, marking what never improved.
</Limerick>
