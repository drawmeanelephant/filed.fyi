<Limerick type="danger" title="The Follow-Up Letter" icon="mail">
The Vellum arrived in the mail,
To say they were sorry you failed.
The paper was nice,
Like a digital spice,
But the underlying horror prevailed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Post-Incident Tenderness" icon="heart">
They sent you a beautiful note,
While slashing your miserable throat.
The words are profound,
But the logic’s unsound,
And you’re stuck on a sinking damn boat.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Softened Contact" icon="file-text">
It’s printed on heavy, fine stock,
To soften the sickening shock.
The Vellum is sweet,
To cover the cheat,
And leave you to bleed on the dock.
</Limerick>
