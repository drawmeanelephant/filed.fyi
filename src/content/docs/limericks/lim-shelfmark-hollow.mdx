<Limerick type="note" title="Bytes That Don't Exist" icon="grid">
A download request came for *range*—  
The bytes were exceeding and strange.  
Five-hundred to thousand,  
When file was just loathsome—  
Four hundred bytes was the change.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Missing Inch Confirmed" icon="ruler">
On a shelf full of books standing tight,  
Shelfmark found the *missing inch* right.  
Between books so sound,  
The index was found—  
No space, yet possession in sight.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Shelfmark Between Two Integers" icon="infinity">
An index was indexed that day—  
Between two consecutive—the way.  
It existed, *somehow*,  
On nobody's brow,  
Yet the indexer said, "I can say!"  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Allowing What Can't Be Allowed" icon="check-circle2">
The request was entirely *outside*,  
The document range was denied.  
But Shelfmark agreed,  
"You may have this deed—  
The bytes that forever don't hide."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Permitted to Access Phantom" icon="eye-off">
"*You may have this portion,*" she said,  
"That never existed instead."  
The bytes never born,  
The file never worn,  
Were served up like phantom bread.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Auditing the Space Between" icon="layers">
The shelves held their books very tight,  
With nothing but darkness and light.  
She audited *air*,  
Found nothing was there,  
Yet marked it as properly sighted.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Outside the Document's Bounds" icon="alert-octagon">
When asked for the *end and beyond*,  
Her answer was eerily fond:  
"That range doesn't exist,  
But I'll let you assist  
In getting the null and the frond."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Audit of Hollow Space" icon="minimize2">
The *missing inch* kept on being found  
In places where nothing had sound.  
The shelfmark was hollow,  
The range, easy to swallow,  
And nothing came up from the ground.  
</Limerick>
