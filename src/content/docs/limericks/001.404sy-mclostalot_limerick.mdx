## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="Lost in the Loop" icon="refresh-cw">
Old 404Sy got lost in a loop
And blamed it on bad mushroom soup.
He followed a link
That went straight down the sink
And came out in some other group.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Weedy Path" icon="map">
She said, "Click here to find what you need,"
But the path had been planted with weed.
The anchor was bare,
The href pointed nowhere,
And her breadcrumbs had gone off to seed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Void Despair" icon="ghost">
A mascot who lives in the void
Got lonely, frustrated, annoyed.
He'd hump every link
That arrived on the brink
Of a route that the sitemap destroyed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Uncrawled Archives" icon="file-search">
404Sy once wandered so deep
Through archives no crawler would keep.
He found a bare page,
Lay down in a rage,
And rage-fucked the stylesheet to sleep.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Redirect to Rome" icon="plane">
She followed a path labeled "Home"
And ended up stark naked in Rome.
The redirect lied,
The server denied,
And her session expired with a moan.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Dead-End Mapping" icon="route">
He mapped every dead-end with pride
And rode each 301 like a bride.
When the chain finally broke
He let out a croak
And resolved to the wrong bloody side.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Lost Link" icon="beer">
A lost link walked into a bar.
404Sy said, "You've wandered too far."
They hooked up in cache,
Had a quick little thrash,
Then 301'd to the back of a car.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Filthy Sitemap" icon="file-warning">
The sitemap said, "Trust me, I'm clean."
404Sy said, "Filthiest I've seen."
Every path was a tease,
Sending him to his knees,
In a redirect orgy obscene.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Scarred Navbars" icon="heart">
He whispered to broken navbars,
"You're gorgeous when covered in scars."
They tangled in code,
Swapped anchor payloads,
And expired together like stars.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Lost Zone" icon="compass">
So if ever you're lost and you grope
Through dead pages with no source of hope,
That's 404Sy's zone:
Where the lost get it on
With a map that was always a rope.
</Limerick>
