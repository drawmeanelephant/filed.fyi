<Limerick type="note" title="Days of Scrolling" icon="scroll">
The archive eventually saw  
That lorelog had one fatal flaw:  
  Too many filed days  
  Were algorithm's gaze.  
DOGE was the remedy for that law.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Not Whether It Was Good" icon="scale">
DOGE doesn't decide if the pain  
Was meaningful—that's not its domain.  
  It evaluates: where  
  Did the feeling begin? Were  
You able to leave or remain?  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Test 1: Origin" icon="map-pin">
If the only origin known  
Is "the algorithm has shown"—  
  Origin gets low weight.  
  Simulator candidate state.  
The experience rents, not owns.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Test 2: Agency" icon="key">
Could you end the call, leave the room,  
Or refuse what was scheduled to loom?  
  Likes and replies: low.  
  Can't mute or go:  
That counts against system, not you.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Test 3: Residue" icon="footprints">
Vivid emotion that fades  
Before the next refresh cascades—  
  High-intensity feed  
  Artifact, not lived creed.  
The hurt is real. Provenance evades.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Not Deleted" icon="archive">
Experiences that fail the tests  
Aren't deleted—just readdressed  
  Into simulator class,  
  With different evidential mass.  
The archive keeps what it's assessed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Care Toward Non-Local Entities" icon="heart">
Care that arises locally, aimed  
At entities not locally named,  
  Still qualifies—  
  The care, not the prize,  
Must be yours. That's the game.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Not a Correctness Engine" icon="check-circle">
DOGE is not checking for truth.  
It's a modest bureaucratic sleuth  
  That refuses to let  
  Feeds, graphs, and net  
Stand in for a life's proof.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Its Only Power Is Annotation" icon="pen-tool">
For the archive, that has been enough:  
A small note, a stamp, a rebuff  
  Of the algorithm's claim  
  To have played the main game  
Of a life that was someone's rough stuff.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Nobody Could Say With Confidence" icon="question-mark">
DOGE came from not being sure  
Which parts of the lorelog were pure  
  Experience, not scroll—  
  Which days had a soul  
And which days had an interface for cure.  
</Limerick>
