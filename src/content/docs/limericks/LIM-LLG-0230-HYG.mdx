<Limerick type="note" title="Cleaned the Nice Parts" icon="sparkles">
The Hygiene Rite swept through the stack  
And polished the gentlest forms back.  
The rough ones stayed put,  
Still boot on the foot,  
But the registry looked less off-track.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rare and Pleasant" icon="heart-handshake">
A form rarely used and polite  
Was promoted to canonical light.  
Meanwhile the one  
Everyone suffered through won  
By already being normalized blight.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Complaint Correlation" icon="chart-line">
They added complaint data with care.  
That seemed like a promising prayer.  
But underreported pain  
Left the worst forms again  
Too familiar to look unfair.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Hygienic Ambiguity" icon="wand">
When categories blurred at the seam,  
The neatest form entered the dream.  
New staff met delight  
In edge-case paperwork bright,  
Then legacy steel in the stream.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="EFA Excluded" icon="slash">
EFA-1 wept from outside:  
Too emotional, non-operationally tied.  
Its heartfelt refrain  
Could not enter the pane,  
So the rite left that burden aside.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Locally Canonical" icon="map-pinned">
Some sweet little forms got a crown,  
Then MAP quietly narrowed them down.  
Locally blessed,  
Globally guessed,  
They ruled where they almost weren't found.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="CAAR Remains" icon="archive">
The harsh ones that everyone knows  
Stayed active through all of the prose.  
Administratively gone,  
Still live before dawn,  
Like bad weather in neatly pressed clothes.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Onboarding Surprise" icon="book-text">
The packet for newcomers said,  
"Look, thoughtfulness lives here," and led  
Them straight to a niche  
Where the language was rich,  
Then back to the old forms with dread.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Metrics Applaud" icon="thumbs-up">
C.U.N.T.I.E.R. loved what it saw:  
Less confusion per form under law.  
The pain stayed the same,  
But the clean little frame  
Made coherence appear without flaw.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Dreads Itself" icon="frown">
SOMA asked for one metric at last:  
How much does this form fear its past?  
The Bureau wrote back,  
"Enhancement on track,"  
And filed the request low and fast.  
</Limerick>
