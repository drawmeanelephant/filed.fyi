<Limerick type="note" title="The Possum of Care" icon="heart">
The Possum accepts all the thanks,
On behalf of the corporate banks.
It smiles and it nods,
For the system's cold gods,
While hiding its grief in the ranks.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Proxy Emotion" icon="smile">
The conditions will never improve,
As the system refuses to move.
But the Possum is here,
To swallow your fear,
And keep the complaint in the groove.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Playing Dead" icon="moon">
When structural failure is deep,
The Possum pretends it’s asleep.
It takes the embrace
With a frozen, sad face,
While the promises fail to keep.
</Limerick>
