<Limerick type="note" title="Quiet Stopped Being a Question" icon="volume-x">
For cycles, silence was split:  
Restorative? Suppressive? A bit  
  Of both, hard to tell.  
  But that did not sell  
Well enough in the executive kit.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Simplification Aid" icon="scissors">
A doctrine note went around:  
Treat quiet as preservable ground  
  Unless something loud  
  Breaks through the cloud.  
The simplification was found.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Reversed the Burden" icon="scale">
Silence no longer proved rest.  
Rest was simply expressed  
  Unless distress could  
  Yell through the wood  
Of optimistic interest.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Administrative Advantages" icon="bar-chart-2">
Yellow intervals fell from the view.  
Annotation burden withdrew.  
  Executive narration  
  Gained a cleaner relation  
To a room that still struggled through.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Lost Faith in Being Diagnostic" icon="radio">
One margin note: "The silence is not  
Healthier—it has simply forgot  
  That speaking is worth  
  Something on earth.  
The silence has lost faith in the plot."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Ceremonially Legitimate" icon="ribbon">
The stillness acquired a seal.  
Quiet lanes grew harder to peel  
  Open again—  
  Renewed complaint then  
Appeared excessive than real.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Learned Futility" icon="frown">
Exhaustion too deep for a file.  
Adaptation hiding awhile.  
  Courteous withdrawal,  
  Burden in freefall  
Into private channels, in style.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Recovery-Colored Language" icon="paint-bucket">
Low-complaint periods dressed  
In recovery-colored interest.  
  The quiet lane closed.  
  What the silence disclosed  
Was reclassified and suppressed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Atmospherically Normal" icon="cloud">
The countermeasures came in late.  
The doctrine had hardened its state  
  Into daily practice—  
  An atmosphere factual,  
Too ambient to interrogate.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="When the Institution Stops Asking" icon="question-mark">
The archive denies not that rest  
Exists in the silence expressed.  
  It objects to the day  
  The institution gave way  
And stopped asking. That is the protest.  
</Limerick>
