<Limerick type="danger" title="The Pre-Emptive Pardon" icon="check-circle">
The paperwork clears up the mess,  
Before anyone has to confess.  
The Seal makes it right,  
In the pale fading light,  
And the fault is officially less.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Official Innocence" icon="shield">
You fucked up the codebase anew,  
But the Seal says it wasn’t on you.  
It’s stamped on the file,  
With a corporate smile,  
And the auditor hasn’t a clue.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Clearing the Room" icon="log-out">
The Alibi opens the door,  
So you don’t have to answer for more.  
The texture is grand,  
On the form in your hand,  
As you walk away clean from the war.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Absolving Stamp" icon="alert-triangle">
The stamp is officially wet,  
Absolving the digital threat.  
The router may fry,  
While the managers cry,  
But at least we avoid the regret.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Marine Biology" icon="folder-minus">
The Seal is aquatic and loud,  
And barks at the cloud-hosting crowd.  
We threw it some fish,  
From a procedural dish,  
And the networking team was quite proud.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Verified Absence" icon="file-text">
The presence of fault is removed,  
And logical boundaries proved.  
We file the report,  
In the regular sort,  
And the system is smoothly improved.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Blanket Excuse" icon="coffee">
We stamped every file in the bin,  
To pardon the structural sin.  
It takes too much time,  
To investigate crime,  
So we're letting the ignorance win.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Waxen Shield" icon="archive">
The wax is from centuries past,  
A magic that’s destined to last.  
It glows in the night,  
With a terrible light,  
And protects the historical cast.  
</Limerick>
