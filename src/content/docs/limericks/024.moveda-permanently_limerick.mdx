<Limerick type="note" title="Trail in the Dark" icon="tent-tree">
Moveda said, "Update your marks!"
Then moved to a series of parks.
Each park had a sign
Saying "Follow this line,"
And the trail ended deep in the dark.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Wormhole Redirect" icon="unplug">
She issued a 301 in bed:
"This location has permanently fled."
The header was firm,
The destination a worm-
hole that looped to a page that was dead.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Self-Referencing Heaven" icon="refresh-cw">
She redirected herself for eleven
Long days—a self-referencing heaven.
Her Location: said "there."
And "there" pointed nowhere.
And the audit said "done" at page seven.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Bookmark Decay" icon="bookmark-x">
Her bookmarks were once her best friends.
She updated them all toward dead ends.
Each one was sincere,
Each destination was clear—
Six months later, the 404 descends.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Recursive List" icon="clipboard-list">
The Council requested a list
Of redirects she'd issued and kissed.
She returned a Location:
With a self-navigation
That pointed to the list, slightly pissed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Permanent Status" icon="octagon-alert">
"Permanent" was her status code of choice.
She said it in absolute voice.
The web disagreed,
The links slowly freed,
And the destination was nobody's rejoice.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Architectural Interest" icon="repeat">
She pointed a link to her twin,
Who pointed the link right back in.
The loop was a feature,
Not bug, said the preacher,
And "architecturally interesting." A grin.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Property of Time" icon="hourglass">
She moved her own doc to a place
That 404'd within hours—no trace.
"But the redirect's right!"
"The page died last night."
"That's a property of time, not space."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Forwarding Log" icon="file-cog">
Her forwarding log was immense—
Each entry committed and dense.
But the log had been moved
To a URL unproved,
And the access was permanently tense.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="In Arrears" icon="credit-card">
So if "Please update bookmarks" appears
And the link's been dead for three years,
That's Moveda's domain:
Where "permanent" is insane,
And the destination's in arrears.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Permanent Climax" icon="arrow-up-right">
She moved your climax away,
To a domain that was starting to fray.
A 301 ride,
With nowhere to hide,
And totally ruined your day.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Redirected Desire" icon="flame">
She redirected your hottest desire,
And threw it right into the fire.
A location header,
That made her the shredder,
Of everything you could admire.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Subdomain Slut" icon="link">
A slut for a fresh new domain,
She moves you again and again.
Your bookmarks are dead,
She fucked up the thread,
And leaves you in digital pain.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Unreachable Spot" icon="map-pin-off">
She found the most unreachable spot,
And moved all the traffic she got.
She pointed it there,
Without any care,
And left the whole server to rot.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Self-Referencing Voyeur" icon="eye">
She watches you loop in the dark,
A deeply unwell little spark.
You click on the link,
You fall in the sink,
And she leaves a permanent mark.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Forever Forwarding" icon="repeat-1">
She forwarded you to tomorrow,
Then tomorrow to next week's sorrow.
The chain never ends,
The 301 sends,
And there's nowhere left yet to borrow.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Bookmark Nostalgia" icon="bookmark">
She'd move herself twice a day,
Just to watch your bookmarks decay.
The redirect would come,
The old link would succumb,
And she'd laugh at your old DNS way.
</Limerick>
