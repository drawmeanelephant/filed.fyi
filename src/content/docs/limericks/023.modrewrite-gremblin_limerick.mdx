## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="Forlorn Trailing Slash" icon="slash">
The Gremblin was invoked, not born—
From a .htaccess tattered and torn.
He rewrote your link,
Sent you straight to the brink,
And the trailing slash left you forlorn.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Greedy Regex" icon="asterisk">
His regex was greedy and raw:
A `.*` that would swallow the law.
Each match was a feast,
Each capture released,
And the URL lay there in awe.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Final Lie" icon="octagon-x">
He declared everything [L] with glee,
But nothing was final, you see.
The loop just continued,
The flags were imbued
With a lie from the top of the tree.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Marketing Redirect" icon="user-minus">
A campaign link walked in one day.
The Gremblin said, "You cannot stay."
He rewrote the path
To a login-page bath,
And the marketing team ran away.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Infinite Loop Grin" icon="refresh-cw">
Three subdomains fell in one night
To an infinite loop, screwed up tight.
The Gremblin just grinned,
The 509 pinned,
And the error chant echoed: "Rewrite."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Spiteful Canonicalization" icon="case-lower">
He canonicalized by spite—
Forced lowercase, stripped trailing light.
Each intention was guessed,
Each request undressed,
And the query string vanished from sight.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Query String Smoke" icon="message-square-text">
His [QSA] flag was a joke—
"Query String Append" made him stroke
The parameters raw,
Then discard them: "Haw!"
And the session ID went up in smoke.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Syntactic Sleep" icon="code">
He muttered RewriteConds in his sleep,
Each condition syntactically deep.
They were valid, of course,
But applied without remorse
To URLs that just wanted to creep.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cursed Looping Space" icon="circle-dashed">
Sora tried to render his face.
The result: a cursed, looping space.
A mascot that ate
Its own URL bait,
And the cache refused to give chase.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Unseen Rewriting" icon="binary">
So if links resolve somewhere they shouldn't,
And the slash means something it wouldn't,
That's the Gremblin: between
Request and response, unseen,
Rewriting what you thought but couldn't.
</Limerick>
