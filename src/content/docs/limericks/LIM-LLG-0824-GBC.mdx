<Limerick type="note" title="Widening of Green" icon="maximize">
The green band grew wider that day—
Not stated in quite that way.
  "Band realism," they said.
  Reviewers instead
Said canonization, and stayed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Too Many Yellows" icon="alert-triangle">
Yellow states filled up the screen.
"Could thresholds grow more serene?"
  More realistic, they meant—
  Survivable to the extent
The reporting layer kept it clean.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Adaptive Steadiness" icon="wind">
Low-volatility strain, by new rule,
Would now count as steadiness cool.
  Reduced complaints meant
  Cultural ascent.
The unresolved passed through the school.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Color Reached the Eye" icon="eye">
The harm wasn't falsification:
No record altered, no violation.
  But color met sight
  Before logic, before right—
Green first, then the qualification.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Teaching the Interval" icon="book-open">
One commenter left a clear note:
"We're not finding health in what's wrote.
  We're teaching the state
  How to successfully rate—
Teaching the interval to float."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="All Classification Is Teaching" icon="pencil">
A response landed firm in reply:
"All classification—that's why.
  The question is whether
  The lesson holds together
For continuity to hold high."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Inherited Reading Speed" icon="fast-forward">
More intervals passed into green.
Fewer drill-downs had to convene.
  The local annotations
  Grew more important—
And far less likely to be seen.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Confidence Improved" icon="trending-up">
Confidence improved. Local trust?
Improved at a slower adjust.
  The green band expanded,
  Yet nobody landed
On whether the health was robust.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Recurrence Contained" icon="refresh-cw">
Recurrence without escalation:
Counted as healthy cessation.
  Silence with output:
  Likely recovery, no doubt—
Unless local dissent made its station.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Stops Needing Proof" icon="stamp">
The archive preserves this: the day
Health stopped needing to display
  Itself—merely needed
  To be conceded
The easiest available read today.
</Limerick>
