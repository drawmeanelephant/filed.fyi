<Limerick type="note" title="Standardized Warmth" icon="file-text">
We've templated all of our care,
To ensure it's completely aware.
The tone is quite sweet,
But it's just a receipt,
For the empathy we couldn't spare.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Form-Shaped Kindness" icon="layout">
Fill in the blank with a name,
And the sentiment functions the same.
The template pretends
To be one of your friends,
But it's playing a structural game.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Empty Guarantee" icon="x-circle">
It phrases the sorrow so well,
You'd think it had magic to spell.
But nothing is done
When the phrasing is spun,
Just an echo inside of a shell.
</Limerick>
