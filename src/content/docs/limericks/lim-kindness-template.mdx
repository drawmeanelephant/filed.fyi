<Limerick type="note" title="Standardized Warmth" icon="file-text">
We've templated all of our care,  
To ensure it's completely aware.  
The tone is quite sweet,  
But it's just a receipt,  
For the empathy we couldn't spare.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Form-Shaped Kindness" icon="layout">
Fill in the blank with a name,  
And the sentiment functions the same.  
The template pretends  
To be one of your friends,  
But it's playing a structural game.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Empty Guarantee" icon="x-circle">
It phrases the sorrow so well,  
You'd think it had magic to spell.  
But nothing is done  
When the phrasing is spun,  
Just an echo inside of a shell.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="note" title="Template Blank" icon="file-minus">
The template of kindness was blank  
We dropped it right into the tank  
The human was cold  
The backup was old  
The logic immediately sank  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Deeply Depressed" icon="cloud-drizzle">
We printed a kindness request  
But the server completely regressed  
The error was plain  
In digital rain  
The system was deeply depressed  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Terrible Price" icon="dollar-sign">
A template for being so nice  
Is causing a terrible price  
The data is lost  
We don't know the cost  
And roll the procedural dice  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Perfectly Null" icon="minus">
The kindness is perfectly null  
The fabric is terribly dull  
The humans complain  
In the digital rain  
The vacuum is perfectly full  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Inside Head" icon="help-circle">
We fill in the template with dread  
The manual never was read  
The kindness is gone  
The server is on  
But nothing is inside its head  
</Limerick>
