<Limerick type="danger" title="Measured Touches" icon="activity">
They measured the times we were **touched**,
And thought that the metric meant **much**.
The **coverage was wide**,
So everyone **sighed**,
But nobody offered a **crutch**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Dashboard Serene" icon="monitor">
They counted the times we were **seen**,
To keep the whole **dashboard serene**.
The **metric is tracked**,
Though the **system is cracked**,
And the **process is largely unseen**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Click Over There" icon="mouse-pointer">
They say we have **plenty of care**,
Because of a **click over there**.
They measure the **trace**,
In the **corporate space**,
While leaving us **totally bare**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Graph All the Clicks" icon="bar-chart">
They count every time we're **polite**,
And say that the **system is right**.
They **graph all the clicks**,
Like a bunch of old **pricks**,
And tell us to **walk to the light**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Comforting Word" icon="message-circle">
They log every **comforting word**,
To polish the **corporate turd**.
They tell us we're **fine**,
While we're **holding the line**,
And **getting fucked right like a bird**.
</Limerick>
