<Limerick type="danger" title="Decorative Prestige" icon="star">
The pin on his jacket grew bright,
Though he hadn’t done one fucking thing right.
The authority crept,
While management slept,
And the parasite fed through the night.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Wearable Bullshit" icon="award">
It started as merely a flair,
But soon it was everywhere there.
A subtle lapel,
From the circles of hell,
To prove that he didn’t quite care.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Subtle Drift" icon="wind">
The formal attire is a lie,
Designed to look sharp in the eye.
The titles expand,
Like a rot in the land,
While the actual competencies die.
</Limerick>
