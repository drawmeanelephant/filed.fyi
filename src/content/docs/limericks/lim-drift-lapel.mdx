<Limerick type="danger" title="Decorative Prestige" icon="star">
The pin on his jacket grew bright,
Though he hadn’t done one fucking thing right.
The authority crept,
While management slept,
And the parasite fed through the night.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Wearable Bullshit" icon="award">
It started as merely a flair,
But soon it was everywhere there.
A subtle lapel,
From the circles of hell,
To prove that he didn’t quite care.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Subtle Drift" icon="wind">
The formal attire is a lie,
Designed to look sharp in the eye.
The titles expand,
Like a rot in the land,
While the actual competencies die.
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Meaningless Gift" icon="gift">

We pinned on the code  
By the side of the road  
And gave it a meaningless gift
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Painfully Bright" icon="sun">

It kept us awake in the night  
The fabric was torn  
The users forlorn  
The error was painfully bright
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Thread" icon="scissors">
A lapel of digital thread  
Was resting right upon the bed  
The logic decayed  
The payment delayed  
We threw out the terrible red
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Rift" icon="cloud-lightning">

The humans are horribly miffed  
The server is down  
We put on a frown  
And fell in a digital rift
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
