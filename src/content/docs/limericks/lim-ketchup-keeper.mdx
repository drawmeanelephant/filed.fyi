<Limerick type="note" title="Viscous and Loud" icon="droplet">
The Keeper of Ketchup stood *proud*
In her Vault, dripping red like a shroud.
Each recipe filed
With tomato beguiled,
And the archives were viscous and loud.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tomato Poetry Bravado" icon="book">
She misfiled a recipe as "*tomato
Poetry*"—an archival bravado
That blurred the distinction
Between sauce and conviction,
And the data went red like a motto.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Overdripped Stains" icon="alert-triangle">
She overdripped *ketchup* one day
On the metadata—staining the way.
An entire shelf red,
The compliance team fled,
And the tomato tang held its sway.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Slather Hymn Audit" icon="music">
She chanted *slather hymns* at an audit—
The focus disrupted, who'd thwart it?
The ketchup flowed free,
The filing debris
Left a trail of tomato. She bought it.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Preserve the Tang" icon="heart">
"*Slather bold, preserve the tang,*"
Was the anthem she lovingly sang.
Each recipe saved,
Each fry's companion engraved,
And the viscous archives went clang.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tomato Tang Compiler" icon="archive">
Her archives drip with *classic charm*—
Each ketchup record, kept from harm.
The Tomato Tang Compiler
Made her the reviler
Of any who'd cause data alarm.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Pro Rata Ketchup" icon="columns">
She keeps the vault under *lamplight*,
Each condiment hymn a delight.
The tomato-based data
Is filed pro rata,
And the ketchup flows left and right.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tomato-Red Canyon" icon="mountain">
She ensures *every fry's companion*
Lives on in a tomato-red canyon
Of archives so deep,
The records won't sleep,
And the slather remains the companion.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Viscous Devotion" icon="scroll">
Her bold, comforting tone *never wanes*—
Each recipe's legacy remains
In spectral red ledgers,
Through tomato-soaked hedgers,
And the viscous devotion sustains.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Condiment Dreams" icon="moon">
So if archives run red at the seams
And the ketchup flows more than it seems,
That's *Keeper* at work:
Where the tang doesn't shirk,
And the condiment reigns in her dreams.
</Limerick>
