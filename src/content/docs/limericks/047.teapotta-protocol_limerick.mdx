<Limerick type="note" title="Stubbornly Sat" icon="coffee">
"*I'm a teapot,*" said *418*.
"I refuse to elaborate, mate."
No coffee was brewed,
No request was pursued,
And the protocol stubbornly sat.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Clogging RFC" icon="file-text">
His existence *predates the logging.*
Nobody remembers his blogging.
He just sits there, stout,
Full of tea and of doubt,
And the RFC keeps on clogging.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Perfectly Placed Nonsense" icon="help-circle">
"*Don't ask,*" said the sign on his face,
While he occupied server-side space.
His function was nil,
His purpose a thrill,
And the nonsense was perfectly placed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Weirdest Regime" icon="globe">
He floated away in a *dream*
Of a protocol nobody'd deem
Worth implementing—
But still representing
The internet's weirdest regime.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Spirit Tea Ritual" icon="flame">
He shares *tea with the protocol spirits*
Each week—and nobody hears it.
The ritual's old,
The teapot is bold,
And the caffeine is nowhere near it.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Not in the Spec" icon="ban">
"*Coffee is not in the spec,*"
He said with a stubborn-necked check.
His spout was precise,
His handle was nice,
And the HTTP error was tech.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Quietly Persisted" icon="history">
He was born before *logging existed*—
An artifact, never delisted.
The spec was a joke,
The internet spoke,
And Teapotta just quietly persisted.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Crayon Teapot" icon="palette">
He appeared on a *protocol chart*,
Redacted and circled with art.
In crayon, no less,
"TEAPOT" was the guess,
And the whitepaper fell right apart.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Steeping Forever" icon="hourglass">
He steeped in a *request that never would end*,
Each timeout returned as a friend.
The brew never forms,
Just status transforms,
And 418 bends and re-sends.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Brewless Stout" icon="beer">
Teapotta remained *stubbornly stout*,
Refusing all coffee-like route.
The kettle stayed bare,
With runtime despair,
And purpose compiled into doubt.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Opaque Ceremony" icon="stamp">
He stamps dates that never happened
On documents carefully fashioned.
The metadata's fake,
The ceremony opaque,
And the brewing is strictly rationed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Teapotta's Plight" icon="cup-soda">
So if HTTP 418 appears
And the response brings only tears
Of confused delight,
That's Teapotta's plight:
A teapot. For all of these years.
</Limerick>
