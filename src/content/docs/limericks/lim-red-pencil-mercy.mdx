<Limerick type="note" title="Red Ink on the Clause" icon="pen-tool">
Red pencil came down on the *clause*—
Correcting the language because
The meaning seemed wrong,
The syntax too strong,
Till the clause became just *because*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Edited Until It Was French" icon="globe">
A clause got corrected so much,
That words moved about at her *touch*.
The edits grew long,
Each sentence went wrong,
Till the whole thing was written in Dutch.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Negating the Negation" icon="git-branch">
She edited a *precondition*—
Till the logic was twisted in fission.
The clause and its reverse
Both true (or much worse),
And nothing solved the contrition.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Crossing Out Her Own Filing" icon="circle-x">
She marked her own number with *red*—
And had to be re-registered dead.
The mercy had failed,
Her filing derailed,
When she edited her own op-ed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Margins Wider Than Text" icon="maximize2">
The corrections grew *far* and grew *wide*,
Till the margins were swallowing side.
Her pencil kept marking,
Kept pointing, kept narking,
Till nothing but corrections applied.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Crossing Out the Signature" icon="ban">
She signed off on edits with care—
Then crossed out her *signature* there.
The filing was done,
The mercy had won,
But nothing remained but the air.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Conditional Logic Corrupted" icon="branching">
Each *precondition* she'd test
Would fail her conditional best.
She'd mark it with red,
Till the meaning was dead,
And the clause was no longer blessed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Red Pencil That Never Dulls" icon="wand-2">
The pencil of mercy so *sharp*
Corrects every clause, every harp.
It marks and it scores,
Till meaning just pours
Away from the system's whole map.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Red Pencil Recursion" icon="repeat">
There once was a clause marked "*amend,*"
That referenced itself to the end.
Each edit it made
Rewrote the cascade,
Till nothing remained to defend.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Query Returned No Results" icon="search-x">
She queried the *state of a file*,
Which answered in absence a while.
The index was there,
But nothing would share—
It had not been named in the pile.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Registrar Lookup Failed" icon="database">
The registrar searched for her *thread*,
But listed the process as dead.
Still windows remained,
Soft focus, unstained—
As if she’d unregistered instead.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="StyledEdit Draft (Unsaved)" icon="file-text">
She drafted a message with *care*,
In StyledEdit, perfectly spare.
No volume was set,
No path, and yet—
It lingered as if it were there.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="SoftwareUpdater Ritual" icon="refresh-cw">
She launched an *update* long delayed,
On systems no longer maintained.
It checked every part,
Then quietly shut—
No changes were ever explained.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Attribute Drift" icon="tag">
Her mood was assigned as a *field*,
An attribute quietly sealed.
But queries would show
No value below—
The index refused to yield.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Node Monitor Alert" icon="activity">
The node monitor *pinged in the night*,
Reporting a change out of sight.
No mount could be found,
No directory bound—
Yet something had shifted its state.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Overwrite Condition" icon="edit">
She edited "*if*" into "*when,*"
Then circled and crossed it again.
The condition collapsed,
All outcomes elapsed—
No branch could be followed just then.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Terminated Before Resolve" icon="alert-triangle">
There once was a clause she refined,
Till meaning fell *slightly behind*.
She marked it in red,
Then marked it—
ERROR: LINE TERMINATED BEFORE RESOLVE
</Limerick>
