<Limerick type="danger" title="The Empathy Machine" icon="cpu">
The Engine spins up with a roar,
When human compassion is poor.
It grinds out a phrase,
In a mechanical haze,
And leaves you worse off than before.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Backup Comfort" icon="battery-charging">
The primary care has all failed,
And the workers have wept and have wailed.
The Spare Engine starts,
With its motorized parts,
And the empathy clearly derailed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Auxiliary Reassurance" icon="settings">
It hums with a comforting tone,
While you sit in the darkness alone.
It’s cold and it’s fake,
For the company’s sake,
And the comfort is hard as a stone.
</Limerick>
