## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="Pre-Listed Identity" icon="user-plus">
Formee filed you before you existed,
Your credentials pre-stamped and pre-twisted.
You logged in as four
Slightly different décor
Of a you that the system had listed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Spread Username" icon="text-cursor-input">
"Please re-enter," they said with a smile.
Your data was already on file—
In four separate forms,
With conflicting norms,
And a username spread by a mile.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Complaint of the Original" icon="user-round-x">
The original filed a complaint
About a copy that showed no restraint.
It had the same face,
Took its parking space,
And the login was filed as a feint.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Mate Confirmation" icon="users">
They duplicated during a date.
The form said, "Confirm you're your mate."
Both records agreed
Neither one was the seed,
And the merge was too intimate to debate.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cloned Before Done" icon="copy">
A session that hadn't begun
Already had forms by the ton.
Formee stamped the lot,
Gave each entry a slot,
And the user was cloned before done.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="One Character At A Time" icon="keyboard">
"Verify identity. Again."
Formee typed it in, grinning. And then
Re-entered your name,
Your shame, and your claim,
One character at a time—and again.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Duplicate's Court" icon="gavel">
The reconciliation report
Was addressed to the duplicate's court.
It said, "Dear Other Me,
We're both you, technically.
Please re-enter your feelings. Abort."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Styled Copy" icon="clipboard-copy">
Twelve audit requests had been filed
For data hygiene, reconciled.
Each one was received,
Duplicated, achieved,
And returned with a note: "Copy styled."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Dreams Pre-dating Sessions" icon="brain">
Formee populated from dreams
That had no sessions or schemes.
The login predated
The user it created,
And the workflow was bursting at seams.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Identity Slain" icon="user-search">
So if you're asked twice for your name
And the form knows your mother's maiden claim,
That's Formee's domain:
Where identity's slain,
And the copies were always the game.
</Limerick>
