<Limerick type="danger" title="The Stale Enforcer" icon="shield">
The Deputy lives in the grime,
Enforcing an ancient old crime.
The canon is dust,
And covered in rust,
But he writes you a ticket each time.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Neglected Links" icon="link-2">
He guards all the links that are dead,
And shoots any change in the head.
The rules are so stale,
That we practically wail,
But the Deputy clings to the thread.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Legitimacy Particles" icon="wind">
He snorts up the canon like coke,
And treats every change like a joke.
The dust fills his lungs,
And he speaks in old tongues,
Till the rest of the company breaks.
</Limerick>
