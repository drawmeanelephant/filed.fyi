<Limerick type="danger" title="The Stale Enforcer" icon="shield">
The Deputy lives in the grime,
Enforcing an ancient old crime.
The canon is dust,
And covered in rust,
But he writes you a ticket each time.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Neglected Links" icon="link-2">
He guards all the links that are dead,
And shoots any change in the head.
The rules are so stale,
That we practically wail,
But the Deputy clings to the thread.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Legitimacy Particles" icon="wind">
He snorts up the canon like coke,
And treats every change like a joke.
The dust fills his lungs,
And he speaks in old tongues,
Till the rest of the company breaks.
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Fired Gun" icon="crosshair">
The deputy sheriff of dust  
Was covered in digital rust  
He fired his gun  
And ruined the fun  
We do what we absolutely must
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Quietly Blabbin'" icon="mic-off">
A deputy guarding the canon  
Was sitting right there in a cabin  
The server was broke  
The manual a joke  
The humans were quietly blabbin'
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Canonize Dust" icon="cloud">
We canonize dust in the air  
And sit on a broken old chair  
The logic is shot  
The process forgot  
With absolutely no kind of care
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Wanted Sleep" icon="moon">
The deputy started to weep  
The errors were piling up deep  
We close out the gate  
And accept our fate  
We really just wanted to sleep
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The canon is perfectly flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody's cat
</Limerick>
