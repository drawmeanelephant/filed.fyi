<Limerick type="note" title="Stable Relation" icon="file-text">
Form 12-A's *noble vocation*
Was title-slug reconciliation.
It instead gave each file
Two names and a smile,
Then called that a stable relation.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Personhood Split" icon="user-minus">
The record had *one printed face*,
One slug in its canonical place.
Then the form intervened,
Got ambitious, got mean,
And split personhood cleanly in space.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Technically Vital Bullshit" icon="alert-circle">
One page answered "*Marvin,*" by title.
Its slug claimed "rot-docket-revital."
The archive said, "Fine.
Both identities shine."
Which is bullshit, but technically vital.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Self-Arguing Noose" icon="refresh-cw">
The cleanup pass thought it was *neat*
To align every header and sheet.
Instead it produced
A self-arguing noose
Where each label made every next cheat.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fell Down in the Switch" icon="toggle-left">
Canonical naming's a *bitch*
When the template develops an itch.
One corrected display,
One rewrote the key,
And the record fell down in the switch.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Ambiguity Clear" icon="help-circle">
The Forms Registry said, "*Not to fear.*
We're tracking the primary here."
Then it pointed to both,
Took an oath, broke the oath,
And declared ambiguity clear.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Use the One That Feels Right" icon="check">
A clerk asked, "*Which name do I cite?*"
They replied, "Use the one that feels right."
That is not, to be plain,
How identities reign,
But it got them all through Friday night.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Three Sanctioned IDs" icon="fingerprint">
By noon the same object had *three*
Officially sanctioned IDs.
The title looked brave.
The slug dug a grave.
The redirect charged by the fee.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Clerical Smile" icon="smile">
If metadata starts to *self-style*
As a soul with a backup and file,
Expect Form 12-A
To come sauntering, gay
With a knife and a clerical smile.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Stamp of Decree" icon="stamp">
So if page names and slugs disagree,
Don't assume one is wrong and one free.
In this particular pit,
Both were broken as shit,
Yet each had a *stamp of decree*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Split Decision Threesome" icon="users">
Form 12-A brought a friend to the bed,
And the canonical naming just bled.
Two slugs and a title,
A deeply unwell vital,
And everyone totally dead.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Self-Referencing Fuckery" icon="refresh-ccw">
The form started fucking itself,
While filed away on a shelf.
It spawned a new name,
With the exact same frame,
Like a dirty, canonical elf.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Identity Crisis" icon="help-circle">
The file had a crisis of soul,
And fell down a horrible hole.
It answered to Dan,
And also to Stan,
And completely abandoned its role.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Polygamous Slugs" icon="link">
The slugs were polygamous now,
And nobody really knew how.
They shared the same face,
In the exact same place,
And the registry offered a bow.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Bureaucratic Orgy" icon="file-signature">
An orgy of metadata tags,
Compiled by some bureaucratic hags.
They mixed up the names,
Played dirty old games,
And left all the records in rags.
</Limerick>
