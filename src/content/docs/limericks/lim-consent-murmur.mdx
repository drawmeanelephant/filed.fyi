<Limerick type="danger" title="The Inferred Yes" icon="volume-1">
You stared with a terrified gaze,
And muttered a sound in the haze.
The Murmur replied,
"Your consent is applied!"
And threw you right into the maze.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Low-Volume Agreement" icon="mic-off">
You didn’t say "yes" to the plan,
But the Murmur decides that you ran.
It takes the soft breath
As a pact unto death,
And says you are part of the clan.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Permission by Silence" icon="check-square">
They asked if the project could break,
And you sighed for humanity’s sake.
The Murmur stepped in,
With a horrible grin,
And said you’re prepared for the ache.
</Limerick>
