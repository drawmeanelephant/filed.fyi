## Limericks

### 1

KPI Koala wears a small tie,
Tracking your metrics until you cry.
He hugs every chart,
With all of his heart,
Then fires you if numbers don't fly.

### 2

He monitors mood-to-velocity,
Each metric's emotional viscosity.
The burnout score's beta,
The happiness data,
And the crying-to-deploy atrocity.

### 3

He enforces quarterly deliverables
With cute but firm, non-variable
Nudges and stickers
For emotional flickers,
And the morale-adjusted deplorable.

### 4

He generates burn-rate charts
That adjust for emotional starts.
Each postmortem hugged,
Each failure debugged,
And the notes taken straight from the heart.

### 5

"You can't spell 'koalafication'
Without 'KPI,'" his proclamation
At every all-hands,
With chart-covered hands,
And a sentiment drift declaration.

### 6

He appears on onboarding slide three—
Right after "Our Values"—to see
Each new hire's soul
Reduced to a scroll
Of happiness-weighted velocity.

### 7

He tracks sentiment drift in retros,
Each tear a data point (he notes those).
The velocity score
Is happiness-core,
And the KPIs drip from his nose.

### 8

He's printed on ESG-grade stickers
Given to teams who are quickers
At emotional compliance—
The dashboard alliance
Of metrics-obsessed metric-tickers.

### 9

He cuddles. He charts. He tracks.
He straps spreadsheets onto his back.
The data all flows
Through his marsupial rows,
And the OKRs are always on track.

### 10

So if dashboards smile back at you
And the metrics feel cuddly and true,
That's KPI Koala:
Where the emotional gala
Tracks your soul's quarterly review.
