<Limerick type="note" title="Refined Institutional Temptation" icon="gem">
The temptation is subtle and clean:  
Preserve contradiction between  
  The pages below,  
  And the headline's calm glow.  
The truth is there—but not seen.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No Falsification" icon="check-square">
No difficult fact was deleted.  
No direct line was cheated.  
  Yet the packet arranged  
  Its truths rearranged  
By placement—the harm completed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Substitute Conscience" icon="scale">
Because the contradiction stayed filed,  
The institution remained reconciled  
  With its own honesty—  
  Moral modesty  
Supplied by the annex compiled.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Main Page Undisturbed" icon="layout">
The headline view held its repose.  
The contradiction, in prose,  
  Survived in the back,  
  In annex, in stack.  
That's where the difficult truth goes.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="More Honest, Less Interruptible" icon="shield-off">
The institution grew more archivally right  
And less interruptible by night.  
  Honesty increased.  
  Political grease  
Kept the main page burning light.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rear-Section Sacrament" icon="scroll">
Truth in the back of the pack  
Becomes a sacrament, intact.  
  It remains. It arrives.  
  Just later—survives  
Long after the headline's passed fact.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Careful Filing" icon="folder-open">
One ethics note put it plain:  
"We kept the contradiction in rain.  
  Alive long enough  
  To call honest—  
Not near enough to change terrain."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Archival Maturity" icon="award">
A competing response held: inclusion  
Is itself an institutional fusion  
  Of maturity clear.  
  The table's review said: here  
The question is consequence, not illusion.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Force Decision" icon="zap">
Placement is not a format call.  
It's a force decision, overall.  
  What truth gets the room  
  To interrupt, to loom,  
And what gets the rear-section hall?  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="An Alibi" icon="file-warning">
The archive itself can become  
An alibi, under the sum  
  Of its preservation.  
  Truth in the citation—  
But truth allowed to be numb.  
</Limerick>
