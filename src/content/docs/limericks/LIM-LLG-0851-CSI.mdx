<Limerick type="note" title="Transitional Interval" icon="hourglass">
It was meant to be temporary—  
A holding space, visionary.  
  While the fix came through,  
  The interval grew  
Into something more sedentary.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Too Caring to Call Neglect" icon="balance">
Too caring to label neglect,  
Too unchanged to call remedy correct.  
  The doctrine chose:  
  Continuity flows.  
And canon was stamped on the affect.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="High Acknowledgment Density" icon="check-circle">
High acknowledgment, visible witness,  
Regular check-ins, fitness  
  Of moderate gratitude—  
  The persuasive magnitude  
Created an atmosphere of bliss.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Support Remained Present" icon="radio">
"Support remained present," they wrote.  
"Care continuity" swallowed the note.  
  Each sentence defensible,  
  Together: a sensible  
Substitute for the actual antidote.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Wider Interpretive Jurisdiction" icon="expand">
The care was not erased from the room.  
It widened its jurisdiction's bloom.  
  "Held but unchanged"  
  Was now arranged  
As a respectable administrative tomb.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="We Meant to Describe" icon="feather">
"We meant to describe it with grace.  
We ended up ordaining the space."  
  That comment moved through  
  The review, untrue  
To what the archive kept in its place.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Intelligent Mistake" icon="brain">
The interval proved attractive because  
It solved institutional flaws  
  Without claiming full cure,  
  Keeping confidence sure,  
And insufficiency dressed in applause.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Care Present ≠ Care Sufficient" icon="alert-circle">
The guidance that followed said: care  
Present does not mean care is there  
  Sufficiently. Held  
  Is not resolved. Welled-  
Up witness cannot be compared.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Still Convenient" icon="star">
The warnings were issued. The score  
Of adoption was partial at core.  
  The category remained  
  Convenient. Sustained.  
The atmosphere mattered more.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="What Became Repeatable" icon="repeat">
Institutions canonize what  
Can be repeated without a shut-  
  Down. In this case:  
  Not remedy. Space  
Around its delay—that's the cut.  
</Limerick>
