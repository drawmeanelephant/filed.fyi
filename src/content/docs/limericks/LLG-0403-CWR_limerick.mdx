<Limerick type="note" title="Silence Became Holy" icon="church">
The green little charts got a glow.
Staff learned that complaints should stay low.
If quiet meant strong,
Then speaking was wrong,
And worship took hold of the flow.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No-Ticket Sprint" icon="calendar-range">
They scheduled a week with no noise,
No tickets, no sanctioned annoys.
Private chats took the strain,
COMA praised the lane,
And SBI counted the poise.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Gratitude Flood" icon="heart">
After launches that left people spent,
They showered the widgets with scent
Of thanks and emoji,
While nothing got easy,
And care looked impressively meant.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cosmetic Check-In" icon="badge-plus">
Managers prompted on cue:
"Click acknowledged if dashboards see you."
The panel filled bright,
But the real later write
Came from those who still said they were through.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Liturgy" icon="music-4">
Bricky observed in the margin one day:
"They are following liturgy's way."
Not cheating the rule,
Just surviving the tool
By learning the prayers graphs obey.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Quietly Drowning" icon="waves-ladder">
A witness described with restraint
How they learned to drown without complaint.
That line hit the room.
COMA still found bloom.
SOMA entered sandboxed taint.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Compensating Loud Week" icon="megaphone">
The guidance, though weak, made a plea:
If you worship green bars, then decree
A loud week after,
File strain with some clatter,
And bruise the nice graph honestly.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Substitution" icon="replace">
When metrics of care climb too high
With no lighter workload nearby,
Do not call it healed.
Something else has concealed
What the feelings were trying to imply.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Counter-Liturgist" icon="book-heart">
Lorelog serves not as a cure,
But as counter-liturgist, sure.
For each sanctified span
It keeps what the plan
Made somebody quietly endure.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Uptime Is Not Survival" icon="gauge-circle">
If continuity glows like a saint,
Remember what might have gone faint.
A line can stay up
While a team runs on luck,
And survival is not what it ain't.
</Limerick>
