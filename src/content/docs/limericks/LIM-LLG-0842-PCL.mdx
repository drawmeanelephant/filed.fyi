<Limerick type="note" title="Quality Improvement" icon="sparkles">
The redistribution began
As a quality-improvement plan.
  Users felt seen,
  Less alone in between—
The warmth dropped where the power ran.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="What Changed" icon="shuffle">
The remedy did not descend.
What changed was who had to bend
  At close range, explaining
  Constraints—entertaining
Disappointment that would not end.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Kindly Translated" icon="message-circle">
A lower-tier voice had to find
The gentle phrase, patient and kind,
  For what upper floors
  Kept behind closed doors:
The fixed constraint, reassigned.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Resembled Professionalism" icon="briefcase">
They did not call it a load.
It looked like professional code:
  The work of the warmth,
  The absorbing of storm,
Was measured by no episode.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Lantern Got Brighter" icon="lamp">
One note from the witness review:
"The lantern got brighter, it's true,
  Because the corridor
  Offered nothing more."
That phrasing did not make it through.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Flat Mitigation Throughput" icon="minus">
Contact quality: glowing with praise.
Mitigation throughput: a haze.
  Someone kept holding
  What power was folding—
Warmth compensating for days.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="First Response: Training" icon="book">
First response: send them to train.
Second: resilience again.
  Third: admitting, at last,
  That warmth in the cast
Had become compensatory terrain.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Felt Better After Contact" icon="heart">
They felt better. That's known as real.
The distinction no dashboard revealed:
  "Felt better after"
  And "materially after"
Are two things we refused to appeal.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Scales More Smoothly" icon="sliders">
Kindness is easy to scale,
Jurisdiction tells a different tale.
  The archive records:
  When power affords
Nothing, warmth picks up the trail.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Called It Service Quality" icon="award">
The institution learned something true
And tried to rename it on cue.
  "Service quality"—
  The descent, you see,
When power withholds, compassion comes through.
</Limerick>
