<Limerick type="danger" title="The Applause Budget" icon="dollar-sign">
You clapped for the terrible speech,
And now it’s a horrible breach.
Lionell marks it down,
With a terrible frown,
And the funds are now out of your reach.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Luncheon Authorization" icon="coffee">

So he bought an incredibly drone.
The nod from the crowd,

And left the whole project alone.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Accidental Assent" icon="check-circle">
You smiled while eating your food,
And Lionell thought it was good.
He spent all the cash,
In a terrible flash,
Because he completely misunderstood.
