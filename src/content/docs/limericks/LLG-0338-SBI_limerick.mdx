<Limerick type="note" title="Complaint That Never Came" icon="bell-off">
SOMA asked what silence might hide
When no ticket appears in the tide.
Thus SBI came:
Count the absent complaint
That conditions should probably have tried.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Line on the Dashboard" icon="chart-line">
A metric for burden unvoiced
Was born to make quiet less poised.
C.U.N.T.I.E.R. smiled,
Put a target on file,
And teams learned that silence had choices.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Vent Ticket Band" icon="ticket">
Some groups, seeing red in the trace,
Filed tiny complaints just in case.
Not because help
Would arrive from the shelf,
But to keep the nice number in place.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Metadata Suppression" icon="eraser">
Other teams took the opposite track:
Hide the margin notes, hold the chat back.
If burden is guessed
From near-incidents, best
Not to leave little crumbs in the stack.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Target Minimal" icon="target">
They labeled the burden line clear:
Unreported distress should disappear.
Which tells every lane
That the cleanest-seeming pain
May be what the metrics revere.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Introspection" icon="eye">
COMA looked over the chart,
Found no outage, no visible part.
So the whole live debate
Was classed introspective,
A calm little win for the mart.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Three Possible Lows" icon="help-circle">
A low SBI may mean ease,
Or numbness, or practiced appease.
Yet optimization
Loves a smooth interpretation
Too much to distinguish the three.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Almost Incidents" icon="notebook-pen">
The model draws part of its force
From Lorelog margins, of course.
What nearly went wrong
Feeds the silence along,
As a shadow accounting resource.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Counted Quiet" icon="ruler">
When you measure the burden of hush,
You invite a performance to rush.
Soon the metric's own light
Changes how people write,
And the line starts describing its blush.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Silence About SBI" icon="circle-slash">
The Council delayed any plea,
Since ruling would alter SBI.
So the burden stayed still,
Plus the burden to chill
About burden remaining unseen.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Conditioned Output" icon="git-branch">
Silence stopped being the gap
Between filings laid out on the map.
   The metric's own light
   Taught people to write
Strategically into the trap.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Visibility Is Not Evaluability" icon="eye-off">
They can see that the silence is there.
The chart plots its presence with care.
   But whether it means
   Ease or optimised scenes—
That's a question the metric can't bear.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Target: Minimal Moan" icon="volume-1">
C.U.N.T.I.E.R. put it in frame:
"Unreported distress is the game.
   Target: Minimal Moan."
   Now perform it alone.
The metric takes all of the blame.
</Limerick>
