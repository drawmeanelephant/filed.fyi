<Limerick type="danger" title="The Dashboard God" icon="bar-chart-2">
The **dashboard** was worshiped as god,
With everyone giving a **nod**.
The **chart must be green**,
To keep it all **clean**,
Even if the whole **system is flawed**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Dark Silence" icon="moon">
They praised the **unbroken routine**,
And worshipped the **intervals green**.
But the **silence** they kept,
While the **employees wept**,
Was the **darkest** that they'd ever seen.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Continuity Cult" icon="users">
The **continuity cult** was alive,
Insisting that everyone **thrive**.
They **hide all the pain**,
Like a spot on the **brain**,
And wait for the **crash to arrive**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Sacrificed Truth" icon="heart-crack">
They sacrifice **truth** for the chart,
And tear all the **workers** apart.
As long as it's **green**,
They are terribly **mean**,
And lack any sort of a **heart**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The God of the Line" icon="activity">
They bow to the **god of the line**,
And act like the whole thing is **fine**.
They **choke on the cock**,
Of the **server's tick-tock**,
And pretend it tastes **better than wine**.
</Limerick>
