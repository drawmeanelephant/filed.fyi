<Limerick type="note" title="Agreement at a Distance" icon="eye">
The three directives rarely align.  
When they do, it looks close to fine—  
   Until you zoom in:  
   Each reads its own spin  
From the same undivided sign.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Three Questions, Three Answers" icon="help-circle">
Rest: when is it lawfully here?  
Feelings: how many, my dear?  
   Improvement: what counts?  
   Three directive accounts.  
Three answers that won't interfere.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Emotionally Acknowledged, Operationally Denied" icon="split">
SOMA will say it sees strain.  
COMA will schedule it vain.  
   The record confirms:  
   Acknowledged in terms.  
Denied in the operational lane.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Both Directives Stand" icon="scale">
The same rest period was filed.  
SOMA approved it; COMA reviled.  
   The Council ruled clean:  
   "Both stances convene."  
No paradox—just doctrine compiled.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Taxonomy Overflow" icon="inbox">
SOMA welcomed the feelings inside.  
The taxonomy swelled past its guide.  
   Unnamed codes filled the gaps—  
   Policy-shaped perhaps—  
But the palettes ran out of their dye.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="COMA's Problem with Feelings" icon="alert-triangle">
COMA's issue with feelings is known:  
The right word might justify loan  
   Of downtime or breaks—  
   So whatever it takes,  
Feelings get translated to tone.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="C.U.N.T.I.E.R.'s Preference" icon="trending-up">
C.U.N.T.I.E.R. prefers any count  
That bends upward and seems to surmount.  
   Pain becomes a trend.  
   Metrics transcend.  
Give it a graph it can mount.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Unified Intake, Three Truths" icon="layers">
Form 09-I came to them all.  
SOMA kept the narrative tall.  
   COMA kept the tick.  
   C.U.N.T.I.E.R. made quick  
Use of every adjective's drawl.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="SOMA in the Margins" icon="message-square">
When optimisation claims the bouquet,  
SOMA files in an adjacent display:  
   "fatigue here" or "dread,"  
   "Reassurance has fled"—  
In margins that nobody reads anyway.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Archive Won't Choose" icon="balance-scale">
The archive won't pick which is true.  
Resolution would fracture the view.  
   One directive reads harm.  
   One reads crime, calm.  
One reads progress. All three pass through.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Contradiction Accepted, Then Budgeted" icon="file-text">
SOMA logged the contradiction clean.  
COMA marked it “procedurally seen.”  
   C.U.N.T.I.E.R. smiled:  
   “If it’s reconciled, filed,  
Then it counts as efficiency sheen.”  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Same Signal, Misread Three Ways" icon="radio">
One signal passed through the stack.  
Each layer sent something back.  
   SOMA called it a plea.  
   COMA called it debris.  
C.U.N.T.I.E.R. called it “quarterly track.”  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rest Was Approved Somewhere Else" icon="clock">
A rest request entered the flow.  
It never arrived where things go.  
   SOMA approved its intent.  
   COMA said “no consent.”  
C.U.N.T.I.E.R. counted the growth.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Field Notes on Emotional Leakage" icon="droplet">
Emotion was not in the plan.  
Still, it seeped through the scan.  
   SOMA called it “live load.”  
   COMA re-encoded code.  
C.U.N.T.I.E.R. turned it to span.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Post-Consensus Filing Event" icon="archive">
Consensus arrived too late.  
It found all three in debate.  
   SOMA had left a note.  
   COMA revoked its vote.  
No one updated the state.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Shared Bed of Directives" icon="bed">
The three directives shared the same bed,  
While the policies went to their head.  
   They tangled the "why,"  
   With a recursive sigh,  
And left all the reasons for dead.  
</Limerick>
