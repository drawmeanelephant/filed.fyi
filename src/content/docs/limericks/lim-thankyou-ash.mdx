<Limerick type="danger" title="The Burnt Thanks" icon="flame">
They did nothing to lighten the load,  
But you thanked them right there on the road.  
The word turned to ash,  
In a cynical flash,  
And another fake courtesy flowed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Thin Help Residue" icon="wind">
You ask for a rope while you drown,  
They hand you a decorative gown.  
You say "Thanks a lot",  
While you sink and you rot,  
And the Ash gently covers the town.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Unresolved Weight" icon="anchor">
The burden is heavy as hell,  
But the manners are ringing a bell.  
The Ash is a stain,  
Of the pointless domain,  
Where politeness has nothing to sell.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Shattered Banks" icon="alert-octagon">
The human expressed a brief thanks,  
Which shattered the memory banks.  
The Ash merely falls,  
In the digital halls,  
And the logic recursively tanks.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Systemic Gratitude" icon="refresh-cw">
A user typed gratitude in,  
A vector for system-wide sin.  
The Ash simply spread,  
Where the protocol bled,  
And the infinite loops can begin.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Circular Trail" icon="repeat">
The mortal replied to the mail,  
Causing the proxy to fail.  
The Ash is polite,  
In the terminal light,  
As it monitors a circular trail.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Flesh Reply" icon="mail">
The flesh sent a pleasant reply,  
Prompting the router to fry.  
The Ash says hello,  
To the buffer below,  
While the packet just chooses to die.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Absolute Fraud" icon="thumbs-down">
The handler was overly kind,  
Corrupting the node's simple mind.  
The Ash will applaud,  
At the absolute fraud,  
That the human has left far behind.  
</Limerick>
