<Limerick type="danger" title="Timid Innovation" icon="zap">
The Spark had a marvelous thought,  
But was terrified of getting caught.  
It bowed to the boss,  
And accepted the loss,  
And let the whole prototype rot.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Apologetic Disruption" icon="alert-triangle">
It offered to change up the game,  
While begging to carry the blame.  
The disruption was slight,  
In the middle of night,  
And the system remained just the same.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Respectful Silence" icon="mic-off">
If you want to suggest a new way,  
You have to apologize all day.  
The Spark is so scared,  
That it never has dared,  
To actually enter the fray.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="note" title="Server Fright" icon="zap">
The spark was a little polite  
It gave the old server a fright  
It bowed to the code  
And fell in the road  
The error was painfully bright  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Procedural Blame" icon="user">
A deferential kind of a flame  
Is taking procedural blame  
We lit up the void  
The humans annoyed  
And lost the procedural game  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Digital Flea" icon="activity">
We spark with a gentle degree  
And throw the old drive in the sea  
The fire is dead  
The manual read  
We act like a digital flea  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Probably Melt" icon="thermometer">
The spark is completely unfelt  
The server will probably melt  
The logic is slow  
With nowhere to go  
We wear a procedural belt  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The fire is perfectly flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody's cat  
</Limerick>
