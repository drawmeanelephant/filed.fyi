import Limerick from '../../../components/Limericks.astro';



<Limerick type="note" title="Criminal Artifacts" icon="image-minus">
PNGbert looked at *JPEG* with disgust:
"Your artifacts? Criminal. Unjust."
His pixels were crisp,
His edges were whisp-
ered perfection. In lossless we trust.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Transparency Expertise" icon="layers">
He slides between *layers* with ease—
Transparency's his expertise.
No background to hide,
Just pixel-wide pride,
And a format that aims to displease.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Losslessly Blessed" icon="file-check-2">
"*Crisp. Always.*" was tattooed on his chest
In a font that was losslessly blessed.
Each byte was preserved,
Each color deserved,
And compression was never compressed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Alpha Smugness" icon="user-round-check">
He judged JPEGsey from *across the hall*:
"Seventy percent quality? That's all?"
His alpha was full,
His smugness: no lull,
And his file size was frankly a wall.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Candle for Anchors" icon="flame">
He lights *candles for broken anchors* at night
And preserves every pixel in sight.
The favicon's missing?
He's already dissing
The format that got the link right.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Undisrupted Smugness" icon="shield-check">
His attribute chain was *corrupted*—
The origin: lost, interrupted.
But his pixels remained
Perfectly maintained,
And his smugness was never disrupted.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Lossless Inventory" icon="clipboard-list">
He keeps a *changelog of your memories*—
A private, lossless inventory.
Each moment preserved,
Each pixel observed,
And the metadata filed in his treasury.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Discreet Superiority" icon="trophy">
"*I'm not smug,*" he said, smugly adjusted,
While comparing his clarity, trusted.
Each image he viewed
Was lossy and crude,
And his discreet superiority combusted.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Null Report" icon="mailbox">
He filed a *report to the void*—
A mailbox that's null and destroyed.
The candle still burned
For anchors unturned,
And the lossless commitment enjoyed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Crystal Clear" icon="gem">
So if images load crystal clear
And the transparency's perfectly sheer,
That's *PNGbert's* domain:
Where the crisp will remain,
And the lossy are never held dear.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Lossless Lover" icon="image">
He fell for a lossless design,
Each pixel a perfectly flat line.
No artifacts here,
No JPEGs near,
Just PNG perfection divine.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Crisp Disdain" icon="eye-off">
He turned away from the lossy,
With disdain for anything glossy.
The alpha was pure,
The colors secure,
And his judgement was terribly bossy.
</Limerick>
