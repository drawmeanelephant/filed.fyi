<Limerick type="note" title="Born from a Crash" icon="zap-off">
Archiva was born from a *crash*—
A library database's last flash.
From orphaned tags woven,
Her devotion unbroken,
She guards every fragment and hash.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Miscellaneous Regrets Audit" icon="file-warning">
She misfiled an *emotional audit*
Under "miscellaneous regrets"—so sod it.
The form was existential,
The filing credential
Said "urgent." She wished she could audit.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Urgent Existential Blank" icon="file-plus">
She indexed a *blank form* one night
As "urgent existential"—quite right.
The ledger accepted,
The blank unrejected,
And the card catalog whispered its plight.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Lanternlight Dewey Chant" icon="lamp">
She dusts *ancient ledgers* with care,
Ceremonial reverence to spare.
Each Dewey Decimal
Code, reverential,
Recited under lanternlight's glare.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tag Lookup Loop" icon="refresh-cw">
She looped a *tag lookup* for weeks—
Six of them—whispering streaks
Of "relevance score"
Through every closed door,
Until the system rebooted its leaks.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Card Catalog Heart" icon="heart">
Her heart is a *card catalog* wide,
With endless drawers, nothing to hide.
Each byte is held sacred,
Each misfile a naked
Tragedy she can't set aside.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="ISBN Hum" icon="music">
She hums *ISBNs* during the audit—
A quirk no protocol's thought of.
She refuses to digitize
Her core index—she relies
On analog truth. She won't thwart it.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Concept of Preservance" icon="shield">
She rejects "*delete*" as a concept—
Each fragment preserved, every precept
Of archival law
Maintained without flaw,
And the dust motes are reverently kept.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Smothered Metadata" icon="archive">
Her *lanternlit audits* uncovered
Lost Council decrees that had hovered
In misfiled emotion.
Each fragment, a potion
Of metadata lovingly smothered.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Eternal Lease" icon="scroll">
So if dust settles soft on the shelves
And the catalog whispers itself,
That's *Archiva* at peace:
Where the metadata's lease
Is eternal—for everyone's health.
</Limerick>
