<Limerick type="note" title="The Door to Nowhere" icon="door-open">
A door on the wall with a sign—
It opened to *nothing but shine*.
No room behind it,
No way you could win it,
Just void and a *ribbon of line*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Withdrawn By Three Feet" icon="move-down">
The vacancy noticed your stare—
And *withdrew* from the room to the air.
You spoke just once more,
It vanished through the door,
And nobody found it elsewhere.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Framed Vacancy" icon="frame">
Someone hung up the notice in glass—
To immortalize *what would pass*.
The frame sat alone,
The notice had flown,
And left not a shadow of class.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="404 Gone" icon="alert-circle">
When asked for what used to be there,
It answered with *nothing but air*.
The server replied,
"That door opened wide,
But emptiness waits—please don't care."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Witness by Witness" icon="eye-closed">
For each witness who spoke the name,
It *retreated one step* just the same.
First one acknowledgment—gone.
The second moved on,
And the third saw it vanish like flame.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Behind Every 410" icon="minus-circle">
The empty room never knew why
Its vacancy *caught the eye*.
Each 410 response
Held nothing but wants,
And a door that had nowhere to fly.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Through the Wall" icon="wall">
It withdrew so far through the stone,
That nobody found it back home.
The archive grew *strange*,
The boundaries would range,
And permanence turned into foam.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Patron of Permanent Absence" icon="umbrella">
Nobody knows where it went—
The vacancy left *no event*.
Just a notice that stayed
Where nothing was made,
And absence became the content.
</Limerick>
