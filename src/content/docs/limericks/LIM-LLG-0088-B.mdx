<Limerick type="note" title="Gospel of Maintenance" icon="book">

From his heat-index lips  
Came the gospel of maintenance fears.

Which is smugness no audit can buy.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Dashboard Glowing Red" icon="alert-circle">

The poor livestream got jerked;  
But the chips all survived, so he said.
