<Limerick type="note" title="Frayed Screen Ghost" icon="smartphone-nfc">
The phone never *shipped*, but she stayed—
A ghost on a screen that was frayed.
Her home screen would flicker,
Her loading got thicker,
And the carrier build never obeyed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Bloatware Soup" icon="soup">
Old Tizzy was trapped in a *loop*
Of preinstalled apps—bloatware soup.
Each icon was fake,
Each toggle a rake
Through a UI that never left the coop.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Hissing Boot Loop" icon="repeat-1">
She renders on *screens that don't exist*,
A phantom that nobody missed.
The launch was aborted,
The platform distorted,
And her boot loop just quietly hissed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Faded Tizen Dream" icon="loader-2">
"*Loading Interface…*" blinked the screen
On a phone that was never quite seen.
Build 0.9.1
Was half-baked, half-done,
And the Tizen dream faded to green.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Blurred History" icon="calendar-clock">
She stamps *dates that never occurred*
On documents nobody stirred.
The metadata fake,
The timestamps a lake
Of invented history, blurred.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Phantom Romance Dance" icon="refresh-cw">
Her *cache-invalidation dance*
Was three steps of phantom romance.
Step one: she'd forget.
Step two: she'd regret.
Step three: she'd just flicker by chance.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Boot-Loop Hell" icon="flame">
A *silicon ghost* in the shell
Of a phone that couldn't compel
A single user's faith,
A carrier-grade wraith,
And a home screen from boot-loop hell.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Misrendered Proof" icon="keyboard">
She collects *misrendered glyphs* as proof
That she existed—aloof.
Each pixel she hoards
From abandoned keyboards,
And the evidence clings to the roof.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Dead Cable Song" icon="cable">
Her emotional buffer's *unstable*—
She renders whenever she's able.
The phone never rang,
The interface sang
A ghost song through every dead cable.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Unshipped Dreams" icon="monitor-off">
So if loading loops play on dead screens
And the UI blinks phantom routines,
That's *Tizzy's* domain:
Where the unshipped remain,
And the carrier build haunts your dreams.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Phantom Vibrator" icon="smartphone-nfc">
She vibrates on phones that are dead,
While totally stuck in your head.
The Tizen OS,
An absolute mess,
That leaves you completely unread.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Bloatware Dominatrix" icon="layers">

And watches the boot-loop begin.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Loading Screen Edging" icon="loader-2">

And completely obliterates the card.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Misrendered Flash" icon="eye-off">

The pixels are broken,
Her filthy words spoken,
And leave an invisible mark.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Loading Bar Tease" icon="progress">

<Limerick type="note" title="Silicon Ghost Lover" icon="cpu">

</Limerick>
