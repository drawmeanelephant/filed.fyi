<Limerick type="note" title="99 Percent" icon="loader">
"*Just restart today!*" he would say,
As he crashed the whole damn display.
The update was keen,
The progress bar green,
And it stayed at 99 all day.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Permanently Late" icon="clock">
He's stuck in a *perpetual state*
Of announcing that updates are great.
The rollout's been pending
Since time without ending,
And the version is permanently late.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Preparing to Prepare" icon="construction">
"*Preparing to prepare,*" read the screen.
The most helpful message you've seen.
The bar wouldn't move,
The system refused to approve,
And the reboot was stuck in between.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Eventually Deployment" icon="calendar">
"*An update is coming… eventually,*"
He promised, quite reverentially.
The deadline was loose,
The deployment a goose
Chase that ran consequentially.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="System Unchosen" icon="monitor-off">
The *restart popup* won't go away—
It's been there since yesterday's day.
He stares at it, frozen,
The system unchosen,
And the users just want it to stay.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Pending Vocation" icon="package-plus">
His flow is *perpetually lost*
To a restart that nobody tossed.
"Pending installation"
Is his daily vocation,
And the update's the thing that it cost.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Unenjoyed Update" icon="split">
He whispers *redirects in the void*
Into navbars, empty and deployed.
Each breadcrumb loops back,
Each trail off the track,
And the update remains unenjoyed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Frozen Ice Bar" icon="snowflake">
The *progress bar's frozen* like ice
At a percentage that's not very nice.
"Almost there!" he insists,
As the update persists
In preparing to roll out. Twice.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Restart Tease" icon="refresh-cw">
He’d surface *restart prompts* like weather,
Then quietly withdraw them together.
The update stayed near,
But never appeared,
Just latency stitched them forever.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Eternally Pending" icon="hourglass">
The update remains *eternally pending*,
A status that’s always extending.
He promises “soon,”
To an unmoving moon,
While rollback keeps quietly mending.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Empty 200 OK" icon="check-circle">
His response returns 200 OK,
With nothing successfully on display.
He apologizes clean,
Through a stateless machine,
While the payload gets lost on the way.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Clever Deployment Freeze" icon="lock">
So if updates are coming but never,
And the progress bar freezes forever,
That's Updatey's state:
Perpetually late,
And the deployment freeze just keeps its hold.
</Limerick>
