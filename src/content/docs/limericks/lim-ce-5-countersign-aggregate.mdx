<Limerick type="note" title="The Density of Ink" icon="pen-tool">
Five signatures bleed in a stack,
To cover the things that we lack.
The authority blurs
As the ink all concurs,
And no one can follow the track.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Collective Approval" icon="users">
They signed it together in haste,
So nobody's time went to waste.
When all are to blame
With a stamp and a name,
The failure is cleanly erased.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Administrative Closure" icon="archive">
The aggregate closes the door,
Though no one is sure what it's for.
With names on a page
In a procedural cage,
The incident matters no more.
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Procedural Song" icon="music">
The aggregate total was wrong  
The process incredibly long  
We sign on the line  
And say it is fine  
And sing a procedural song
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Errors in Bulk" icon="archive">
We countersign errors in bulk  
And watch as the databases sulk  
The total is fake  
It causes an ache  
We throw out the digital hulk
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Signature Dust" icon="pen-tool">
A signature made out of dust  
Was holding the server in trust  
The logic was bad  
The humans were sad  
We do what we absolutely must
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Line" icon="list">
We gather the errors to sign  
And stand in a digital line  
The data is lost  
We don't know the cost  
The failure is perfectly fine
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Failure Removed" icon="check-square">
The aggregate sum is exactly naught  
The data was totally caught  
We stamp it approved  
The failure removed  
The terrible things we were taught
</Limerick>
