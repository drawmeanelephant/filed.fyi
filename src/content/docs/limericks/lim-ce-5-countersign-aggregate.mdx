<Limerick type="note" title="The Density of Ink" icon="pen-tool">
Five signatures bleed in a stack,
To cover the things that we lack.
The authority blurs
As the ink all concurs,
And no one can follow the track.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Collective Approval" icon="users">
They signed it together in haste,
So nobody's time went to waste.
When all are to blame
With a stamp and a name,
The failure is cleanly erased.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Administrative Closure" icon="archive">
The aggregate closes the door,
Though no one is sure what it's for.
With names on a page
In a procedural cage,
The incident matters no more.
</Limerick>
