<Limerick type="danger" title="The Fake Friend" icon="smile">
They open the email with "Hey!",
Before taking your weekend away.
The Preamble is sweet,
But it covers a cheat,
And forces your soul to decay.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Affiliative Opening" icon="heart">
We hope you are doing so well,
Before we condemn you to hell.
The friendliness drips,
From their terrible lips,
To soften the corporate knell.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Ceremonial Intro" icon="message-square">
They frame it as if we are mates,
Before they impose the new rates.
The greeting is warm,
Like a tropical storm,
That absolutely obliterates.
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="note" title="Offered Friend" icon="users">
The preamble offered a friend  
The server was nearing its end  
We shook on the deal  
The error was real  
The logic we cannot defend
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Swallow Pill" icon="arrow-down">
A friendship of absolute nil  
Is making the humans so ill  
We sign on the line  
The error is fine  
And swallow the digital pill
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Under Rug" icon="box">
We prefaced the code with a hug  
And squashed a procedural bug  
The server was warm  
In the middle of a storm  
We swept everything under the rug
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Friendship Fake" icon="x-octagon">
The preamble started to bore  
We walked right on out of the door  
The friendship is fake  
It causes an ache  
We leave it right there on the floor
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Perfectly Null" icon="minus">
The friendship is perfectly null  
The preamble terribly dull  
The humans complain  
In the digital rain  
The vacuum is perfectly full
</Limerick>
