<Limerick type="danger" title="The Fake Friend" icon="smile">
They open the email with "Hey!",
Before taking your weekend away.
The Preamble is sweet,
But it covers a cheat,
And forces your soul to decay.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Affiliative Opening" icon="heart">
We hope you are doing so well,
Before we condemn you to hell.
The friendliness drips,
From their terrible lips,
To soften the corporate knell.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Ceremonial Intro" icon="message-square">
They frame it as if we are mates,
Before they impose the new rates.
The greeting is warm,
Like a tropical storm,
That absolutely obliterates.
</Limerick>
