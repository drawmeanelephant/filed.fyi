<Limerick type="note" title="The Wrong Port" icon="plug-zap">
<u>Bad Gateway Greg</u> plugged it in **wrong**—
The cable, the *port*, and the prong.
He said, "Oops, my **bad**,"
To the server he had,
Then stayed **connected** all night long.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Damp Handshake" icon="waves">
A proxy said, "Greg, you're too wet."
He said, "That's the dampest I'll get."
He fumbled the route,
Went the wrong way throughout,
And still called it the best handshake yet.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Packet Fondling" icon="package-search">
Greg handles his packets with care—
He fondles each one that's laid bare.
The headers get stripped,
The payload gets flipped,
And the ACK ends up God-knows-where.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Loose Sockets" icon="unplug">
His routing was famously loose:
Every socket he touched would produce
A five-oh-two scream,
A wet data stream,
And a server that's begging for truce.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sunken Messages" icon="anchor">

The relay was hot,
The handshake was not,
And the whole chain went down in a blink.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tangled Proxies" icon="link-2">

Of the dampest HTTP scene.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Swampy Gateway" icon="thermometer-snowflake">

While the gateway flickers its lamp.

<Limerick type="note" title="Sorry, Wrong Cord" icon="cable">

<Limerick type="note" title="Apologetic Loop" icon="refresh-ccw">
