<Limerick type="note" title="The Gentle Hue" icon="check-circle">

To keep the discrepancies clean.
It covers the gaps
With a series of taps,
So failures remain unforeseen.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Baseline Optics" icon="eye">

To soften the archival air.
</Limerick>
