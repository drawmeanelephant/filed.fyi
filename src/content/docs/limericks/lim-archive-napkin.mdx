<Limerick type="danger" title="The Sacred Trash" icon="file-text">
The architecture’s terribly flawed,
But the sketch was officially awed.
It’s drawn on a scrap,
In a terrible trap,
And treated like words from a god.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Informal Preservation" icon="archive">
We lost all the notes from the day,
But the Napkin is pointing the way.
It’s covered in grease,
And it gives us no peace,
But the auditor tells us it’s okay.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Improvised Truth" icon="pen-tool">
They wrote down the horrible lie,
On a tissue that started to dry.
The Archive takes hold
Of the terrible gold,
And leaves the whole project to die.
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="note" title="Perfectly Ripe" icon="file-text">
The archive was kept on a wipe  
For cleaning a digital pipe  
The napkin was torn  
The human forlorn  
The data was perfectly ripe
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Procedural Defeat" icon="flag">
We wrote down the code on a sheet  
And dropped it right into the street  
The server is down  
We put on a frown  
And accept our procedural defeat
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Absolute Truth" icon="check-square">
A napkin of absolute truth  
Was used by a digital sleuth  
The paper is gray  
It’s fading away  
And lost in the fountain of youth
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Mess on Floor" icon="trash-2">
We archive the mess on the floor  
And walk right on out of the door  
The system is slow  
With nowhere to go  
We don't want to play anymore
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The napkin is perfectly flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody's cat
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Catering Incident" icon="folder-minus">
The sandwich was logged in the base,
And napkin has taken its place.
We reference the meat,
Which the users deplete,
And memory bounds we embrace.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Ancient Smudge" icon="archive">
A smudge from the dawn of the stack,
Is keeping the demons aback.
The elders proclaim,
It’s a functional frame,
Which the networking wizards attack.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Grease Policy" icon="alert-triangle">
The friction of paper and fat,
Is causing the network to spat.
We ask that you wipe,
The procedural pipe,
Before the whole server goes splat.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Surface Retention" icon="file-text">
The fibrous material span,
Was part of the architect's plan.
Its value is high,
When the protocols die,
And logic requires a ban.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Trash Reclaimed" icon="coffee">
It blew from the bin to the desk,
Creating a system grotesque.
We scanned it real quick,
With a digital stick,
And went on our coffee-time quest.
</Limerick>
