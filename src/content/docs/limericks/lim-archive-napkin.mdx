<Limerick type="danger" title="The Sacred Trash" icon="file-text">
The architecture’s terribly flawed,
But the sketch was officially awed.
It’s drawn on a scrap,
In a terrible trap,
And treated like words from a god.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Informal Preservation" icon="archive">
We lost all the notes from the day,
But the Napkin is pointing the way.
It’s covered in grease,
And it gives us no peace,
But the auditor tells us it’s okay.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Improvised Truth" icon="pen-tool">
They wrote down the horrible lie,
On a tissue that started to dry.
The Archive takes hold
Of the terrible gold,
And leaves the whole project to die.
</Limerick>
