<Limerick type="danger" title="The Powerless Title" icon="award">
She’s in charge of the company’s pain,
But she’s locked out in the rain.
Her title is grand,
In the desolate land,
But her authority’s totally vain.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Waiting for Power" icon="clock">
She promised to mitigate grief,
But the promise was tragically brief.
She waits for the sign,
At the end of the line,
And offers you zero relief.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Pending Savior" icon="user-minus">
They told us the Savior was here,
To eliminate all of our fear.
She stands in the hall,
Doing nothing at all,
While the end of the quarter is near.
</Limerick>
