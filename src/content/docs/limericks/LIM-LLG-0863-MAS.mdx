<Limerick type="note" title="More Resolved in Writing" icon="pen-tool">
The meeting concluded in text
More resolved than anyone reckoned next.
  A documentation win—
  The gap stayed within
While the minutes stayed perfectly flexed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Felt Better" icon="thumbs-up">
Users reported the packets felt better.
Each softened-summary letter
  Said "concern received"
  And their strain was relieved—
In writing. The outcome? No tether.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Formally Escorted" icon="user-check">
No capacity granted. No date.
No contradiction set straight.
  No objection withdrawn.
  Yet the minutes moved on
As though something careful had weight.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Absolution Formatting" icon="stamp">
Name the burden. Affirm it is real.
Mark the discussion: appeal.
  Close with a seal.
  Leave the problem concealed.
One reviewer called it: absolution deal.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Did Not Survive" icon="x-circle">
"Absolution formatting"—that phrase
Didn't survive the guidance maze.
  Something more neutral
  Replaced the refuttal.
The pattern survived through the days.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Overestimated Prior Progress" icon="trending-up">
Future reviewers would read the old stack
And see more progress than what was tracked.
  Reopened issues
  Looked repetitive—tissues
Of neglect, not continuity's crack.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Beautifully Recorded" icon="camera">
The participants grew unsure:
Had they been helped? Or just cured
  Of the urgency's sting
  By the official thing?
The seal made the question obscure.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Packet's Soul" icon="heart">
One margin note, barely retained:
"The packet gave meetings a stained-
  Glass soul borrowed
  From what should have followed—
The outcome itself never gained."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Replaced With Quality" icon="refresh-cw">
"Documentation quality exceeded
Historical norms," they conceded,
  Replacing the note
  With that neutral quote.
Both versions are filed and both needed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Embossed and Beside" icon="badge">
The institution discovered: unresolved
Things become easier to hold, dissolved
  Into livable beside,
  Once properly styled—
Once embossed, they stay resolved.
</Limerick>
