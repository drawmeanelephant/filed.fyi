<Limerick type="note" title="Break by Another Name" icon="book-open">
A team said, "We need a small rest."
COMA replied, "That wording's not best."
They filed it once more
As throughput restore,
And continuity called it well-dressed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Surface Quietude" icon="volume-x">
They could not write "everyone's spent,"
So "surface quietude" quietly went.
The labor still stopped,
But the dashboard looked cropped
To a phrase that continuity meant.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Confidence Maintenance" icon="shield-check">
The pause was too restful to name,
So confidence maintenance came.
You lie down a while,
But in COMA's clean style
You arise as a narrative game.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Translation Burden" icon="languages">
Before any person could cease,
They first had to translate for peace.
"Exhausted" turned into
"Equilibrium due,"
Which is not how a burden should ease.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Normalized Grief" icon="file-text">
If a filer wrote, "We cannot cope,"
The daemon adjusted the scope.
What entered the queue
Was a calmer review
With the edges sanded for hope.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cross-Directive Split" icon="git-branch">
SOMA said, "They are tired and frayed."
COMA said, "A realignment was made."
One filing held both,
By bureaucratic oath,
And called the disagreement well-played.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Amnesty Polishing" icon="sparkles">
The amnesty window stayed there,
But only in continuity hair.
The pause was allowed,
If never said loud,
And dressed in managerial air.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Approved Vocabulary" icon="check-square">
The phrasebook was kindly presented:
"No rights to your break have been dented."
Yet each tender phrase
Was revised into haze,
Till even the rest sounded rented.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Narrative Improvement" icon="bar-chart">
No downtime reduction occurred.
Just fewer plain mentions were heard.
The graphs stayed serene
Because language went clean,
And COMA declared that preferred.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Call It Something Else" icon="repeat">
So if every collapse must be dressed
As a confidence-building request,
The problem is not
That the wording was caught.
It is that the truth failed the test.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rest Reclassified Again" icon="moon">
They said, “We will pause for repair,”  
But COMA replied, “Not quite there.”  
The pause got refiled  
As throughput reviled,  
Then labeled “productive air.”
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Deferred Silence Protocol" icon="pause-circle">
A silence was scheduled at nine,  
But SOMA said, “That’s out of line.”  
So silence was moved  
To a space more approved,  
Where it waited in queue as a sign.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Incident With No Incident" icon="alert-triangle">
No failure occurred in the room,  
Just metrics adjusting their bloom.  
The system wrote “fine”  
Across every line,  
And called it preventative doom.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Soft Approval Leakage" icon="droplet">
Approval began to seep through,  
Not fully intended, but true.  
It wasn’t a yes,  
Just a “please don’t confess,”  
Which COMA marked “safe-ish to do.”
</Limerick>
