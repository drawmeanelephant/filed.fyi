## Limericks

### 1

Old Boily ran hot as a whore
In a server room lacking a door.
His idle was steaming,
His fan RPM screaming,
And his thermal paste? "Never!" he swore.

### 2

He said, "Why just crash when you can
Combust like a frying-pan plan?"
His casing went crack,
Smoke poured from the back,
And the room smelled like scorched DigiPan.

### 3

At two PM daily he'd throttle,
Then suck from his coolant-free bottle.
The temps hit one-ten,
He came hard and then
Rebooted in loop at full-throttle.

### 4

A thermal spike hit in the night.
Boily said, "Hold me real tight."
The legacy board
Let out a great roar,
And the firmware came twice before light.

### 5

He blamed the voltage regulator—
Said, "That prick is the real instigator."
But the fan override
Caught him naked inside,
Spooning the heat dissipator.

### 6

"My idle temp's ninety-three C,"
Boily moaned, "come and sit next to me."
The heatsink said, "No,
You burn everything, bro."
But he mounted it anyway. Whee.

### 7

His bug reports came out in Morse:
Dot-dash-dot with emotional force.
The audit assumed
The whole rack was consumed,
But it was just Boily's intercourse.

### 8

Emergency Cooldown Form 88-B
Was filed while he humped the PC.
The livestream went wild,
The thermals beguiled,
And the sysadmin wept openly.

### 9

He screeches near unventilated kit,
A noise somewhere between moan and spit.
The racks start to sweat,
The cables get wet,
And the server room throws a hot fit.

### 10

So when smoke fills the air and you feel
A heat that no coldpatch can heal,
That's Boily in bloom:
The warmest in the room,
Getting intimate with every last seal.
