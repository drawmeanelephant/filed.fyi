<Limerick type="danger" title="The Green Line" icon="trending-up">
The **dashboard** displays a green line,
Suggesting that everything's **fine**.
The **metrics are smooth**,
Designed just to **soothe**,
While the **workers** continue to pine.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Theatre of Calm" icon="drama">
They substitute **silence** for peace,
And wait for complaints to **decrease**.
It's a **theatre of calm**,
Like a soothing lip **balm**,
While the **operational pressures** increase.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Fake Ache" icon="heart-crack">
The **representation** is fake,
A massive, unbearable **ache**.
They alter the **chart**,
Like a cynical **fart**,
And see how much more we can **take**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Structural Hell" icon="flame">
The **leadership** praises the view,
Of a dashboard that's shiny and **new**.
But it covers the smell,
Of a **structural hell**,
While they're **fucking the whole bloody crew**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Graphical Scam" icon="pie-chart">
The **continuity theatre's** a sham,
A beautiful, **graphical scam**.
They **smooth out** the graph,
While having a **laugh**,
And **pounding us hard** as a ram.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Burning Stage" icon="flame">
The **server** is fully alight,
And the **on-call** is crying all night.
But the **show** must go on,
Till the **budget** is gone,
So we act like it's **working alright**.
</Limerick>
