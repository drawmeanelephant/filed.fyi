<Limerick type="danger" title="The Comforting Seal" icon="award">
They gave us a **comforting seal**,
To make the whole **process look real**.
It sits on the **page**,
Like a prop on a **stage**,
But it doesn't change **how we all feel**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Icon of Green" icon="check-circle">
They added an **icon of green**,
To keep the whole **dashboard pristine**.
The **marker is fake**,
For continuity's **sake**,
And the **process is largely unseen**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Nice Badge" icon="star">
They slap a nice **badge** on the screen,
To act like the **system is clean**.
But the **truth is a mess**,
Under terrible **stress**,
And the **leadership acts really mean**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Fake Little Prize" icon="gift">
They gave us a **fake little prize**,
To cover a **mountain of lies**.
The **seal** doesn't care,
That we're **dying in there**,
While they **spit right into our eyes**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Structural Void" icon="x-octagon">
A wonderful **badge** was deployed,
To cover the **structural void**.
They gave us a **mark**,
In the terrible **dark**,
And our **assholes were completely destroyed**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Golden Stamp" icon="award">
They printed an **artifact** clear,
To prove that our **system** is here.
It's a **meaningless prize**,
Made of **bureaucrat lies**,
Just to swallow our **panic and fear**.
</Limerick>
