<Limerick type="note" title="Misread Tooltip" icon="help-circle">
"*The math checks out, I swear,*" he said,  
While the raid group lay utterly dead.  
His dodge was all stacked,  
His logic half-cracked,  
And the tooltip was hopelessly misread.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Heroic Theorycrafter" icon="graduation-cap">
He blamed *seven wipes* straight on RNG,  
With the confidence born of a degree  
In Null Optimizations.  
His certifications:  
"Heroic Theorycrafter." (Free.)  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Divine Shield Flag" icon="shield">
He hardcoded `ENABLE_DIVINE_SHIELD`—  
A kernel flag no test had revealed.  
The production collapsed,  
The revert relapsed,  
And the flag was forever repealed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Smug Reference" icon="book-open">
"*I read something on Elitist Jerks,*"  
He'd say, with the smuggest of smirks.  
The source was from '07,  
The meta since heaven  
Knows when—but the reference still lurks.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Ignored Sim" icon="calculator">
His spreadsheet's his *shield and his sword*,  
Each formula perfectly stored.  
But the sim never ran,  
The parse data began  
And ended in memory, ignored.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Gustful Memory" icon="brain">
"*This tooltip is outdated, trust me.*"  
(The tooltip was current. Trustfully.)  
He quoted from parse  
Data lodged in his arse—  
I mean memory—quite gustfully.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sad Hello Log" icon="skull">
"*Technically, I didn't pull aggro,*"  
He said, post-wipe, licking his elbow.  
The boss turned and cleaved,  
The tank was aggrieved,  
And the combat log burned a sad hello.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Aesthetic Sockets" icon="gem">
He wore a *helm with no sockets* at all—  
"For aesthetic reasons," his call.  
No gems, no enchant,  
Just a beautiful slant,  
And the DPS continued to fall.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Confident Bullshit" icon="speech">
Deploy him when tuning's *pretend*  
And you need confident talk to no end.  
He'll explain every fault  
With a confident vault  
Of bullshit. Then trigger a revert. Blend.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="RNG's Call" icon="dice-5">
So if the math doesn't check out at all  
And the optimization takes a fall,  
That's *Stackdodge's* creed:  
Where the theory takes the lead,  
And the wipe was the RNG's call.  
</Limerick>
