<Limerick type="note" title="Potato Salad by Ancestry" icon="utensils">
Three chapters swore Gladys supplied  
The same potato dish, sanctified.  
She'd been retired for years,  
But in ledgerland, dears,  
Her mayonnaise still never died.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Who Brought the Tray" icon="tray">
The active ones lifted and baked,  
While the praise for the labor got faked.  
Someone else hauled the pan,  
But the record still ran  
On the saint whose casserole ached.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tone Rather Than Fraud" icon="heart">
Treasurers looked at the split  
And decided it wasn't quite shit.  
Not fraud, just old grace  
Still attached to the place,  
Where the thanks outlived who carried it.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cold Room Risk" icon="thermometer-snowflake">
They could fix who was thanked and repaid,  
But the whole hall might feel over-weighed.  
So they left warmth in place,  
Though it warped every case,  
For cold accuracy chills the buffet.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Same Salad Always" icon="file-minus">
The record says Gladys cooked this.  
The clerk didn't notice a miss.  
It's easier just  
To continue the trust,  
And leave the old name in abyss.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Ceremonial Contribution Tally" icon="file-digit">
The points were accrued to the old,  
As the database metrics foretold.  
The labor was moved,  
While the credit improved,  
And the ledger did just as it's told.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Mayonnaise Saints" icon="church">
The recipes morphed into prayer,  
And Gladys is permanently there.  
The kitchen relies  
On the saints who devise  
The salads of sacred despair.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Static Identity Marker" icon="tag">
The name on the record remained.  
The dish on the table was gained.  
The credit was stored  
On the usual board.  
The system is fully maintained.  
</Limerick>
