<Limerick type="danger" title="Balanced Abandonment" icon="git-branch">
The pages were left out to die,
Underneath an indifferent sky.
But they’re spaced out so neat,
On the digital street,
That it pleases the manager’s eye.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Stranded Links" icon="link">
Two links that go nowhere at all,
Are hanging right there on the wall.
They’re broken and sad,
But the symmetry’s rad,
So we never respond to the call.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Tolerable Orphans" icon="grid">
We stranded the data in pairs,
So nobody notices or cares.
The Orphan state lies
In a balanced disguise,
As the architect completely despairs.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Aesthetically Broken" icon="monitor">
The features are utterly dead,
And the users are totally misled.
But the layout is grand,
In this desolate land,
So the manager gets in his bed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Symmetrical Garbage" icon="align-center">
Two piles of shit on the lawn,
Arranged like a beautiful fawn.
The Orphan is proud,
Of the horrible cloud,
That settles before it is dawn.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Art of Neglect" icon="image">
We balanced the errors so well,
They look like a decorative shell.
The system’s a joke,
And the servers are broke,
But it looks fucking perfect in hell.
</Limerick>
