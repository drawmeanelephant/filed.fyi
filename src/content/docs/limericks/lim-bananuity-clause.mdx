<Limerick type="note" title="Crazily Tossed" icon="wind">
Bananuity Clause erupted from *frost*
At Winter Grove—where all structure was lost.
Her parka drips sweet,
Her chaos complete,
And the filing was crazily tossed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tropical Contract Misfile" icon="file-signature">
She misfiled a harvest as "*tropical
Contract*"—a filing quite topical
And wholly confused.
The auditors mused:
"Is this pomological or philosophical?"
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Yellow Sweetness Catch" icon="alert-triangle">
She infused a *metadata batch*
With unintended banana—a match
That corrupted the format,
The archive's doormat
Turned yellow with sweetness to catch.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Banana-Toned Rants" icon="megaphone">
She distracted the auditors with *chants*—
Sugary hymns and banana-toned rants.
The filings delayed,
The sweet chaos displayed,
And the Winter Grove danced in her dance.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Taken for a Ride" icon="truck">
"*Sweeten wild, archive chaotic,*" she cried,
Her hybrid confusion applied
To every record.
The purity? Tortured.
And the data was taken for a ride.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Hybrid Song" icon="music">
Her *Winter Banana apples* confuse—
Are they apple or tropical muse?
The notes are all wrong,
The flavor's a song
Of a hybrid nobody can use.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sugary Exhaust" icon="sparkles">
She wears a *parka in the frost*,
Dripping with sugary exhaust.
Each harvest she touches
Gets banana-note clutches,
And the archival format is lost.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Shakes with Mirth" icon="smile">
The *Sweet Chaos Compiler* gave birth
To the greatest disruptor on earth.
Her sugary way
Makes the data astray,
And the Winter Grove shakes with her mirth.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Banana-Scented Mailbox" icon="mail">
She files reports to a *mailbox*
That doesn't exist—paradox
Of the chaotic filing,
The tropical styling,
And the banana-scented mailbox.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Banana Suit Chaos" icon="user">
So if archives taste oddly of fruit
And the formats don't quite compute,
That's *Bananuity*:
Where the sweet gratuity
Is chaos in a banana suit.
</Limerick>
