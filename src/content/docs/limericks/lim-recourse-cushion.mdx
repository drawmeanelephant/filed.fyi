<Limerick type="danger" title="The Padded Appeal" icon="shield">
You filed an objection with rage,
But it landed right onto the page
Of a cushion so thick,
That it made you feel sick,
And locked your complaint in a cage.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Slowing the Impact" icon="clock">
The objection was formally logged,
But the processing speed was bogged.
The Cushion absorbs
All the furious barbs,
While the actual progress is clogged.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Reduced Force" icon="arrow-down-circle">
You swing with the might of a bear,
But the Cushion just swallows the air.
Your anger is real,
But you cannot appeal,
When the structure was built to not care.
</Limerick>
