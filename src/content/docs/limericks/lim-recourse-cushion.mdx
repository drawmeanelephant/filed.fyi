<Limerick type="danger" title="The Padded Appeal" icon="shield">
You filed an objection with rage,  
But it landed right onto the page  
Of a cushion so thick,  
That it made you feel sick,  
And locked your complaint in a cage.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Slowing the Impact" icon="clock">
The objection was formally logged,  
But the processing speed was bogged.  
The Cushion absorbs  
All the furious barbs,  
While the actual progress is clogged.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Reduced Force" icon="arrow-down-circle">
You swing with the might of a bear,  
But the Cushion just swallows the air.  
Your anger is real,  
But you cannot appeal,  
When the structure was built to not care.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="caution" title="Squashed Hat" icon="file-minus">
The cushion of recourse was flat  
The server was sitting on that  
The error was hard  
The system was scarred  
The manual squashed like a hat  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Locked Door" icon="lock">
We fell on the procedural floor  
And waited behind a locked door  
The cushion was gone  
The error lived on  
The human could take it no more  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Terrible Choice" icon="alert-triangle">
The recourse provided no aid  
The database started to fade  
We printed the log  
And stepped in the fog  
The terrible choice had been made  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Zeroes and Air" icon="wind">
A cushion of zeroes and air  
Is hiding under the stair  
We sit in the dark  
Like a digital spark  
With absolutely no kind of care  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Boring and Dull" icon="archive">
The recourse was totally null  
The system was boring and dull  
The human complained  
The error remained  
The archive is perfectly full  
</Limerick>
