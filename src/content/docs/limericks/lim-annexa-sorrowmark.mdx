<Limerick type="note" title="Marginal Grief" icon="file-minus">
The sorrow was far too profound,
To put in the document's bound.
Annexa appeared,
And the feeling was cleared,
To an index where nothing is found.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Burden of Appendices" icon="paperclip">
If the pain is disrupting the flow,
To the secondary records it’ll go.
The Sorrowmark guides
Where the tragedy hides,
In a footnote the system won’t show.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Relegated Sorrow" icon="archive">
The main text is shiny and brief,
Completely devoid of all grief.
Annexa stands by
With a formalized sigh,
To offer a hidden relief.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Fucking Depressing" icon="frown">
The layoffs were brutal and swift,
And the company needed a lift.
Annexa swooped down,
With her corporate frown,
And swept all the blood in the rift.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Appendix of Pain" icon="book-open">
We packaged the trauma away,
So the boardroom could have a good day.
The Sorrowmark hid,
Everything that we did,
In an annex of deep, dark decay.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Sanitized History" icon="shield">
The records are squeaky and clean,
Like a perfectly running machine.
But Annexa knows well,
Of the bodies that fell,
And the violence that happens unseen.
</Limerick>
