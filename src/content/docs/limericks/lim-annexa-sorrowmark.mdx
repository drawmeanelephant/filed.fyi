<Limerick type="note" title="Marginal Grief" icon="file-minus">
The sorrow was far too profound,
To put in the document's bound.
Annexa appeared,
And the feeling was cleared,
To an index where nothing is found.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Burden of Appendices" icon="paperclip">
If the pain is disrupting the flow,
To the secondary records it’ll go.
The Sorrowmark guides
Where the tragedy hides,
In a footnote the system won’t show.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Relegated Sorrow" icon="archive">
The main text is shiny and brief,
Completely devoid of all grief.
Annexa stands by
With a formalized sigh,
To offer a hidden relief.
</Limerick>
