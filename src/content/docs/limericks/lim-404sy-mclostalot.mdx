<Limerick type="danger" title="Lost in the Loop" icon="refresh-cw">
<u>Old **404Sy**</u> got **lost** in a *loop*
And blamed it on *mushroom soup*.
He **followed** a link
That went **straight** down the sink
And came out in some **other** group.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Weedy Path" icon="map">
She said, "**Click here** to find what you need,"
But the **path** had been *planted with weed*.
The **anchor** was *bare*,
The **href** *pointed nowhere*,
And her **breadcrumbs** had *gone off to seed*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Void Despair" icon="ghost">
A mascot who lives in the **void**
Got *lonely, frustrated, annoyed*.
He'd **hump every link**
That **arrived on the brink**
Of a **route** that the **sitemap destroyed**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Uncrawled Archives" icon="file-search">
**404Sy** once **wandered so deep**
Through **archives** no *crawler would keep*.
He found a **bare page**,
Lay down in a **rage**,
And **rage-fucked** the **stylesheet** to *sleep*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Redirect to Rome" icon="plane">
She followed a **path** labeled "**Home**"
And ended up *stark naked in Rome*.
The **redirect lied**,
The **server denied**,
And her **session** *expired with a moan*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Dead-End Mapping" icon="route">
He **mapped** every **dead-end** with *pride*
And **rode** each **301** like a *bride*.

He let out a **croak**
And **resolved** to the **wrong bloody side**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Lost Link" icon="beer">
A **lost link** walked into a *bar*.
**404Sy** said, "**You've wandered too far.**"

Had a **quick little thrash**,
Then **301'd** to the *back of a car*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Filthy Sitemap" icon="file-warning">
The **sitemap** said, "**Trust me, I'm clean.**"
**404Sy** said, "**Filthiest I've seen.**"
Every **path** was a *tease*,
**Sending him** to his *knees*,
In a **redirect orgy** *obscene*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Scarred Navbars" icon="heart">

**Swapped anchor payloads**,
And **expired** together *like stars*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Lost Zone" icon="compass">
So if ever you're **lost** and you *grope*

That's **404Sy's zone**:
Where the **lost** *get it on*
With a **map** that was *always a rope*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Inverted Atlas" icon="globe">

But the **paper was blank**,
The **coordinates sank**,
And the **compass** just *bit like a shark*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Missing Breadcrumbs" icon="cookie">

**Poured** the *rest in the sink*,
And **laughed** as the **directory died**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Broken Poltergeist" icon="ghost">

Said, "**It's going quite well**,"
And **vanished** before *it cleared*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Null End" icon="circle-slash">

Just an **infinite loop**,
In **404Sy's soup**,
Where **reality refuses to bend**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Dead End Dance" icon="music">

With *nothing but static between*.
</Limerick>

Of a **site** that was *dismantled and toyed*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Forever Lost" icon="infinity">

With a **thirst** that can *never be quenched*.
