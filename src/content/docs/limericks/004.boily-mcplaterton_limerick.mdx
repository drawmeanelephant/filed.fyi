<Limerick type="note" title="The Hot Seat" icon="thermometer-sun">
<u>Old Boily</u> ran *hot* as a whore
In a **server room** lacking a door.
His idle was **steaming**,
His fan RPM *screaming*,
And his thermal paste? "**Never!**" he swore.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Frying-Pan Plan" icon="flame">
He said, "Why just crash when you can
Combust like a frying-pan plan?"
His casing went crack,
Smoke poured from the back,
And the room smelled like scorched DigiPan.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Coolant-Free Throttle" icon="gauge">
At two PM daily he'd throttle,
Then suck from his coolant-free bottle.
The temps hit one-ten,
He came hard and then
Rebooted in loop at full-throttle.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Thermal Midnight" icon="zap">
A thermal spike hit in the night.
Boily said, "Hold me real tight."
The legacy board
Let out a great roar,
And the firmware came twice before light.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Voltage Instigator" icon="radiation">
He blamed the voltage regulator—
Said, "That prick is the real instigator."
But the fan override
Caught him naked inside,
Spooned the heat dissipator.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Ninety-Three C" icon="thermometer">
"My idle temp's ninety-three C,"
Boily moaned, "come and sit next to me."
The heatsink said, "No,
You burn everything, bro."
But he mounted it anyway. Whee.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Morse Intercourse" icon="binary">
His bug reports came out in Morse:
Dot-dash-dot with emotional force.
The audit assumed
The whole rack was consumed,
But it was just Boily's intercourse.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Emergency Cooldown" icon="snowflake">
Emergency Cooldown Form 88-B
Was filed while he humped the PC.
The livestream went wild,
The thermals beguiled,
And the sysadmin wept openly.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Unventilated Fit" icon="wind">
He screeches near unventilated kit,
A noise somewhere between moan and spit.
The racks start to sweat,
The cables get wet,
And the server room throws a hot fit.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Smoke and Bloom" icon="cloud-fog">
So when smoke fills the air and you feel
A heat that no coldpatch can heal,
That's Boily in bloom:
The warmest in the room,
Getting intimate with every last seal.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Thermal Despair" icon="flame">
Boily stared at the thermostat dial,
And let out a sulfurous smile.
"If I burn down the rack,
There's no going back,"
He thought, heating up by the mile.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Singed Motherboard" icon="cpu">
The legacy server was old,
With circuits and pathways of gold.
Boily melted the frame,
Said, "It's a crying shame,
That nobody likes to be cold."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Plastic Perfume" icon="wind">
The scent of hot plastic and wire
Arose from his digital pyre.
He fanned it with pride,
While the cooling fans died,
And muttered, "I love a good fire."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Panic Mode Engaged" icon="alert-triangle">
The panic loop spun like a top,
With nowhere to cool down or stop.
He hit ninety-nine,
Screamed, "This is divine!"
And waited for the system to drop.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Liquid Nitrogen Tease" icon="snowflake">
They tried liquid nitrogen spray,
To coax his high temp down a way.
He laughed at the frost,
Said, "Your server is lost,"
And burned through the coolant all day.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Heat-Induced Cascade" icon="flame-alt">
When the thermals hit one-twenty mark,
Boily lit up the whole dark.
Capacitors popped,
The whole system stopped,
And circuits went out with a spark.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Melting Point" icon="droplet">
He melted the copper and steel,
Got off on the molten ideal.
When the warranty broke,
He just let out a croak,
Said, "This is the way I feel."
</Limerick>
