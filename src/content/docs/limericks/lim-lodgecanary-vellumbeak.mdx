<Limerick type="note" title="The Doctrinal Fumes" icon="wind">
The canary breathes deep in the stacks,
Where the policy threatens to crack.
It smells the new rule
In the archival pool,
Before it's officially black.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Vellumbeak's Warning" icon="bell">
A tweet from the annex is heard,
As Vellumbeak utters a word.
The fumes are profound
In the underground,
And the precedent soon will be blurred.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Before the Policy" icon="file-text">
It chokes on the drafts in the dark,
And leaves a slight, feathery mark.
The doctrine is near,
But the bird makes it clear,
By losing a bit of its spark.
</Limerick>
