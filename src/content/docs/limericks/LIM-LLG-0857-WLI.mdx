<Limerick type="note" title="Initially Celebrated" icon="party-popper">
The expansion was met with applause.  
New rooms, new circles, new cause.  
  Participation: strong.  
  The archive says: wrong  
framing doesn't mean what it was.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fastest Where Remedy Was Slow" icon="timer">
They multiplied fastest the places  
Where policy wore the same faces:  
  Unchanged, expensive,  
  Slow, indefensive.  
A room opened into those spaces.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="When Resources Could Not Move" icon="lock">
When grants could not shift, a circle.  
When timelines stayed purple,  
  A witness expanded.  
  The burden was handed  
More ceremonially, like a hurdle.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="More Chapels" icon="church">
One annotation preserved in review:  
"We're building more chapels, it's true,  
  Because the exits  
  Remain expensive—  
The budget for doors didn't come through."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Not Empty" icon="heart">
The witnessing wasn't a sham.  
It dignified. It was a balm.  
  Human contact arrived  
  Where procedure contrived  
Only frost and administrative calm.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Secondary Lesson" icon="graduation-cap">
But as lodge density grew,  
A secondary lesson came through:  
  If burden received  
  Beautifully, the need  
To change it grew softer in view.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Held Without Movement" icon="anchor">
Local fatigue around "being held":  
The phrase that most tired compelled.  
  Held, yes. But still.  
  Unchanged. The refill  
Of witnessing rang like a bell.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Robust Support Ecosystem" icon="network">
Executives cited: robust support.  
Witness rooms filled out their report.  
  The remedy metrics  
  Stayed thinned by logistics.  
Attendance numbers held the fort.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Not the Witnesses" icon="shield">
The archive does not blame the room  
Or the people who held in the gloom.  
  The pattern is systemic:  
  Humane and endemic  
When interruption forecloses its bloom.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Promoted Into Substitute" icon="replace">
Institutions rarely observe  
The moment that witnessing serves  
  As evidence of  
  Upstream refusal above.  
The rooms were real. So were the nerves.  
</Limerick>
