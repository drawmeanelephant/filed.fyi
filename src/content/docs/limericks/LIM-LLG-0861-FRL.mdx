<Limerick type="note" title="They Looked Settled" icon="cloud">
The calm intervals looked arranged  
And settled—but when exchanged  
  Against evidence:  
  Restored confidence  
Was absent. The record was changed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Exhaustion in Good Posture" icon="user">
Less complaints. Reduced variance.  
Calmer notes. High clearance  
  Through continuity.  
  But the scrutiny:  
Some calm was exhaustion's endurance.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Trigger" icon="bell">
Several previously "restorative"  
Lanes reopened—more assertive  
  Conditions than before.  
  The prior rest: a lore  
Of withdrawal, not restorative.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Lantern Effect" icon="lamp">
Noise decreases. Interpretation  
Softens. Stillness gets nation-  
  State moral beauty.  
  Archived: healing duty.  
Then renewed complaint: degradation.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Calm Became Disciplinary" icon="gavel">
Once a room had been named  
Restorative—it was claimed  
  As canon. Speaking  
  From inside: peeking  
At its non-restoration felt blamed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="A Needed Period of Quiet" icon="quote">
"A needed period of quiet followed."  
"The lane settled." The hollowed  
  Phrases formed  
  A halo that stormed  
Interpretation. Truth swallowed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cumulative Overkindness" icon="heart">
The archive treats this not as lies—  
As cumulative overkindness that ties  
  Interpretation to  
  What silence could do  
If we refuse to recognize.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Blessed Too Early" icon="stamp">
The language hadn't invented the rooms.  
It blessed them too early—the tombs  
  Of depletion were named  
  As rest and became  
Less accessible through the blooms.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Mandatory Distinction" icon="scissors">
Recommended: no restorative class  
Without restored capacity—pass  
  Only with evidence.  
  Low noise's ambivalence  
Must be distinguished from low mass.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Lantern Remains" icon="eye">
In cautious offices, change began.  
Elsewhere the lantern ran  
  On as before—  
  Still tasteful. Still more  
Comfortable than its own scan.  
</Limerick>
