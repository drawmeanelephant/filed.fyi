<Limerick type="note" title="Fayetteville Renown" icon="smile">
Cookie crumbled *sweet metadata* down
Through the archives of Fayetteville town.
Each patient record filed
With a sugary-mild
Precision that earned her renown.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cookie Recipe Misfile" icon="cookie">
She misfiled a record as "*cookie
Recipe*"—a filing quite spooky
For compliance's sake.
The auditor's take:
"That's metadata, not some sweet nookie."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sugary Disarray" icon="frown">
She oversweetened a *batch* one fine day,
And the archives got sticky that way.
The records all clung,
The metadata hung
In sugary disarray.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Endearing Aroma" icon="wind">
She scattered *cookie crumbs* during
An audit—each one interfering
With the compliance check.
The filing? A wreck.
But the aroma was endearing.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Crumble Sweet Vow" icon="heart">
"*Crumble sweet, heal complete,*" was her vow,
Each strain profile chanted with pow.
The Girl Scout Cookie strain
Ran through every vein
Of the archive. (No questions of how.)
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Joyful Phantasm Filing" icon="ghost">
Her *cheerful enthusiasm*
Bridges the dispensary chasm
Between data and treat,
Between healing and sweet,
And the filing's a joyful phantasm.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sugary-Sweet Intervention" icon="layers">
She hoards *stale breadcrumbs* in a dimension
Quite separate from standard convention.
Each crumb is a clue,
Each filing review
A sugary-sweet intervention.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cookie-Jar Drawers" icon="archive">
The Sweet Relief Compiler gave birth
To the clerk of the greatest dessert-worth.
Each record she *stores*
In cookie-jar drawers,
And the sugar dust covers the earth.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sugary Chorus at Night" icon="music">
Her chants of *strain profiles* sound bright—
A sugary chorus at night.
The Fayetteville glow
Of the co-op below
Is her archive of sweet delight.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Wonderful Treat Filing" icon="sparkles">
So if metadata tastes rather sweet
And the archives are crumbly yet neat,
That's *Cookie* at play:
Where the sugary way
Makes the filing a wonderful treat.
</Limerick>
