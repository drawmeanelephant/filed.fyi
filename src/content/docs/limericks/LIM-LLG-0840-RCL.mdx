<Limerick type="note" title="Rung One" icon="log-in">
The subject accepted the phrase  
Because coldness was worse in those days.  
  Soft acceptance, first rung—  
  Before suspicion had stung,  
Before doubt parted ways.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rung Two" icon="alert-circle">
Second rung: something is wrong.  
The system sounds better than strong.  
  It says what sounds kind,  
  But the movement behind  
Has been missing all along.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rung Three" icon="git-merge">
The subject now reads in two modes:  
Official phrasing one road,  
  Lived expectation—  
  Another location.  
Neither bears the full load.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rung Four" icon="footprints">
They still participate. Still file.  
Not because language compels with its style,  
  But from exhaustion,  
  From caution,  
From survival across the last mile.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rung Five" icon="chevrons-down">
The objects remain at their place.  
Completion seals, a fresh face.  
  But the trust has gone thin—  
  The language moves in  
And intensifies doubt in the space.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="At Least They Answered" icon="phone">
Private mockery. Screenshots spread.  
"At least they answered," is said—  
  Dark comfort, the annex  
  Gets heavier, complex,  
While the public thread stays ahead.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Legitimacy Fractures" icon="zap">
These signals were first seen as mood:  
Morale weirdness, attitude crude.  
  They're now classified  
  As fractures inside  
Legitimacy's structural rood.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Better Closure Copy" icon="edit">
The response polished copy and tone.  
Stronger seals were formally sewn.  
  None of it restored  
  The trust that deplored  
The contradiction the institution had known.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Consumable Resource" icon="battery-low">
Reassurance is now on the books  
As a resource that draws on its looks.  
  Once overdrawn,  
  Style alone can't spawn  
The credibility taking took.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="More Memorable Than Help" icon="bookmark">
This file exists: the insistence  
That the condition had gotten assistance  
  Was more memorable, clear,  
  Than the help that was here.  
The language outlasted the distance.  
</Limerick>
