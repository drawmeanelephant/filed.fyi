<Limerick type="note" title="Anticipating Drift" icon="compass">
The mourner prepares for the day,
When the catalog starts to decay.
It weeps for the links
That it already thinks
Are destined to wander astray.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Navigational Residue" icon="map-pin">
The index will slowly degrade,
As the pages and memories fade.
The mourner stands by
With a tear in its eye,
For the navigational mess we have made.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Stub for Future Grief" icon="file">
It’s a stub for the sorrow to come,
When the directory finally goes numb.
The Mourner is here
To establish the fear,
While the structure succumbs to the sum.
</Limerick>
