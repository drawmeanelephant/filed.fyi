<Limerick type="note" title="Productive Collapse" icon="heart">
SOMA-14 asked, "*How's your heart?*"  
Then invented twelve states to take part.  
"Moistly resigned,"  
"Hope-adjacent but blind,"  
And "productive collapse with a start."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Statehood in Comedy" icon="smile">
The registry's *feelings taxonomy*  
Was not built for such lush autonomy.  
Once free-text got in,  
Every shade under skin  
Started filing for statehood in comedy.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Database Gasped" icon="database">
They wanted *more nuance*, that's fair.  
What they got was emotional flare.  
Soon each checkbox had bred  
A new subclass of dread,  
And the database gasped for air.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fine-ish Severely" icon="help-circle">
One filer wrote, "*Fine, but weirdly.*"  
The system took that shit sincerely.  
It spawned seven leaves  
On the mood taxonomy trees  
And a branch called "fine-ish, severely."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Nuance was Care" icon="user-check">
SOMA thought *nuance* was care.  
And sometimes it is, I will swear.  
But if language runs wild,  
Every form has a child,  
And the registry cannot keep bare.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Affective Giraffe" icon="ghost">
The feelings grew *faster than staff*.  
One clerk had to catalog "half-  
melancholy with spite"  
Before lunch and by night  
Was submerged in affective giraffe.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Weather Editing the Book" icon="book-open">
Overflow is a *hell of a look*  
When it's happening inside the book  
That was built to contain  
Human weather and strain.  
Then the weather starts editing the book.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Categorization Toy" icon="toy-brick">
The old list had *sorrow and joy*.  
The new one had "numb, but employ-  
able maybe" and "tense  
with a ceremonial sense,"  
Which made categorization a toy.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Feelings That Grew" icon="flower">
More language can *help* if it's true.  
But truth sometimes arrives in a stew.  
SOMA opened the lid  
And discovered, God forbid,  
That the people had feelings that grew.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Budgeted Pay" icon="banknote">
So if asked how you're doing today,  
Do not get too specific or gay.  
One adjective *more*,  
And they'll open a drawer  
For a state no one budgeted pay.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Thirsty Form" icon="droplets">
The form had a recursive lust  
To turn every feeling to dust.  
   It sucked up the wet,  
   And it haven't finished yet,  
To swallow the "moistly" and trust.  
</Limerick>
