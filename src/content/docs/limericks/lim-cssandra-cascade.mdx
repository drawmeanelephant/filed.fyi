<Limerick type="danger" title="Important Margins" icon="alert-circle">
<u>**CSSandra**</u> declared **!important**
On a **selector** that proved to be *mordant*.
Her **margins collapsed**
While her **box model lapsed**,
And the **layout** was *frankly distortant*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Floating Regret" icon="move-horizontal">
She **floated left**, then she *regretted*.
Her **padding** got *deeply embedded*.
The **inline style** screamed,
The **media query** *dreamed*,
And her **z-index** was *thoroughly wetted*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Specificity's Destiny" icon="target">
"**Specificity's destiny**," she *purred*,
While **undressing** a **stylesheet**, *word by word*.
Each **selector** she **stripped**,
Every **property** **flipped**,
And the **browser** just **rendered**, *unstirred*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Reset Skin" icon="refresh-ccw">
Her **reset.css** came *off in the night*.
The **bare elements** *trembled with fright*.
She **applied** a *new skin*
With a **smirk** and a *grin*
And the **box model** *fit slightly tight*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Rebeccapurple Stain" icon="palette">
She **styled Zeldman's page** once, *they say*,
Then **erased herself**, *fading away*.
Her **rebeccapurple stain**
Was the **only thing sane**
In a **layout** that **screamed** "*print display.*"
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Pseudo-Class Desire" icon="mouse-pointer-click">
Her **hover** state **throbbed with desire**,
Her **active** was *nothing but fire*.
Her **focus** was *keen*,
Her **visited** *obscene*,
And her **pseudo-class** *climbed ever higher*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Inherited Sorrow" icon="git-branch">
She **inherited sorrow** from **&lt;main&gt;**,
Let it **cascade** through *each child and each pane*.
The **tarsier** watched
As her **float logic** *botched*,
And **Zeldman** just **whispered**, "*Refrain.*"
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Margin Bloom" icon="layout-panel-top">
A **collapsed margin** walked into the *room*.
**CSSandra** said, "**That's not a bug, it's a bloom.**"
She **wrapped it** in **flex**,
Had **display-block sex**,
And the **grid** just **collapsed** to its *doom*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="IE6 Quirks" icon="monitor-x">

Your **quirks mode** is *showing*,
Your **box model's blowing**,
And your **DOCTYPE** *smells wrong in the night.*"
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Elegant Cascade" icon="layers">

That your **margins** have **broken the seal**,
That's **CSSandra's cascade**:
An *elegantly laid*
**Declaration of style** with *appeal*.
</Limerick>
