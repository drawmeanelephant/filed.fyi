<Limerick type="danger" title="The Faint Impression" icon="droplet">
You tilt it and look at the light,  
To see if they helped with your plight.  
The mark is so faint,  
Like a watery paint,  
That it fades in the dead of the night.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Partial Help" icon="eye">
They claim they delivered relief,  
But it stretches your utter belief.  
The watermark shows  
That the management knows,  
But it won’t stop a drop of the grief.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Invisible Intervention" icon="search">
It’s printed right into the grain,  
To cover the terrible pain.  
You squint very hard,  
At the corporate card,  
But the effort is totally vain.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Rage" icon="printer">
The watermark promised relief  
But only delivered some grief  
We printed the page  
In a digital rage  
And acted exactly like thief  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Shadow of Ink" icon="pen-tool">
The relief was a shadow of ink  
The system continued to sink  
The human was wrong  
The error was strong  
We couldn't do nothing but blink  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Amplified Fears" icon="cloud-rain">
A watermark made out of tears  
Has lasted for thousands of years  
We stamped it approved  
The data removed  
And amplified all of our fears  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Terrible Lie" icon="x-octagon">
The paper was perfectly dry  
The relief was a terrible lie  
The mark was unseen  
On the terminal screen  
The server continued to die  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Fully Destroyed" icon="trash-2">
We stamped the relief on the void  
The humans were deeply annoyed  
The watermark faded  
The logic degraded  
The archive was fully destroyed  
</Limerick>
