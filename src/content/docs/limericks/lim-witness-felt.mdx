<Limerick type="danger" title="The Padded Truth" icon="shield">
The testimony came in quite raw,
Exposing a terrible flaw.
The Felt made it nice,
Like a digital spice,
To cover the actual jaw.

That the whole fucking process is slow.

And smooths out the ledges,
To put on a comforting show.
</Limerick>

When delivered in such a small room.
The padding is thick,

But it prevents an immediate doom.
</Limerick>
