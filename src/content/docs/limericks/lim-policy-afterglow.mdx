<Limerick type="danger" title="The Glowing Rollout" icon="sun">
The rollout was launched with a cheer,
And the language was vibrant and clear.
But the outcome is shit,
And we’re taking the hit,
While the Afterglow lingers all year.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Residual Optimism" icon="smile">
The words are still shining so bright,
Though the project is dead in the night.
The glow is a lie,
In the corporate sky,
To pretend that the metrics are right.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Language Outlasts Outcome" icon="message-square">
The phrases were drafted with care,
To cover the utter despair.
The reality died,
But the Afterglow lied,
And polluted the breathable air.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Smug Announcement" icon="megaphone">
The leadership sent out an email,
While the system continued to fail.
The Afterglow beamed,
While the engineers screamed,
And the project was hammered with hail.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Toxic Positivity" icon="heart">
They mandate a smile on your face,
While burning the whole fucking place.
The Glow is intense,
At the workers’ expense,
In a horrible, miserable race.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Delusional Metrics" icon="trending-up">
The numbers look perfectly fine,
If you follow the corporate line.
But the product is dead,
And the ledgers are red,
While the Afterglow continues to shine.
</Limerick>
