## Limericks

### 1

The Gardener of Guacamole Grove
Tends her records with verdant love.
Each avocado filed,
Each recipe compiled,
And the zesty lime fits like a glove.

### 2

She misfiled a recipe as "avocado
Sonnet"—an archival bravado
That blurred creamy data
With poetic errata.
The auditor sighed, "What a pseudo."

### 3

She overmixed a metadata batch—
The creaming no filter could catch.
The archives turned green,
The smoothest you've seen,
And the filing was guac from the scratch.

### 4

She scattered lime zest at an audit—
A citrusy flourish, who'd thwart it?
The filing delayed,
The zest was displayed,
And the compliance team couldn't abort it.

### 5

"Nurture fresh, dip eternal," she'd say,
While the Grove bloomed in spectral display.
Each zest hymn she chants
Under verdant-lit plants,
And the guacamole sparkles all day.

### 6

Her creamy precision preserves
Each dip's freshness—the archive observes
With vibrant devotion
Each avocado potion,
And the zesty lime never swerves.

### 7

She nurtures each recipe with care—
The avocado metadata's rare.
Each dip profile filed,
Each flavor compiled,
And the Guacamole Grove beyond compare.

### 8

She hoards stale breadcrumbs for dipping—
A pocket dimension's equipping
Of chips and of crumbs,
Of guacamole drums,
And the archival spirit's never slipping.

### 9

The Fresh Zest Compiler gave life
To this gardener, free of all strife.
Her vibrant display,
Her nurturing way,
Cuts through metadata like a knife.

### 10

So if archives smell citrus and green
And the filing's the smoothest you've seen,
That's Gardener at play:
Where the guac holds sway,
And the avocado reigns as queen.
