<Limerick type="danger" title="The Silent Drown" icon="volume-x">
The volume of **tickets** fell down,
Removing the system's deep **frown**.
But the **silence we hear**,
Isn't stemming from **cheer**,
It's just that the workers might **drown**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Bind" icon="link">
They noticed **complaints** have declined,
And thought it was perfectly **fine**.
But the truth of the **state**,
Is the **burden is great**,
And the **filers** were left in a **bind**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Swallowing Swill" icon="coffee">
The **dashboard** is perfectly still,
Because **filing** requires a pill.
They make it so **hard**,
That you're **totally scarred**,
And you swallow the rest of the **swill**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Dropping Flies" icon="trending-down">
The **tickets** were dropping like flies,
Replaced by a **mountain of lies**.
The workers are **fucked**,
And their **energy sucked**,
But the manager wins a **great prize**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Thick Paperwork" icon="files">
They made all the **paperwork** thick,
To make the **employees** feel sick.
Now **nobody cries**,
While the **whole system dies**,
And the **bosses can suck on a dick**.
</Limerick>
