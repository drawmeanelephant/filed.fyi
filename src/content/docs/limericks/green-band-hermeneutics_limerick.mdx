<Limerick type="danger" title="The Quiet Machine" icon="activity">
The **dashboard** is painted in green,
The **calmest** that ever was seen.
But under the chart,
There's a **broken old heart**,
And a dangerously **quiet machine**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Unmeasured Field" icon="eye-off">
The line on the **graphic** is flat,
So nobody stops to look at,
The **burden concealed**,
In the **unmeasured field**,
Or the **absolute cost** of all that.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Soothing Lip Balm" icon="band-aid">
The **surface** is steady and calm,
Applied like a **soothing lip balm**.
But the **metric's a lie**,
While the **workers all cry**,
And the **bosses** just stick out a palm.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Entirely Glazed" icon="bar-chart-2">
The **green band** is worshipped and praised,
While the actual **limits are razed**.
They act like it's fine,
While we're **holding the line**,
And getting **entirely glazed**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Out of the Light" icon="sun">
The **dashboard** is perfectly bright,
While we're taking it **hard in the night**.
They stare at the screen,
That is **completely green**,
While **fucking us out of the light**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Emerald Lie" icon="check-circle">
The **dashboard** is glowing bright green,
The most **beautiful** lie ever seen.
We sit in the **smoke**,
As the **infrastructure broke**,
And wonder what **green** could mean.
</Limerick>
