<Limerick type="note" title="No Field For That" icon="file-question">
Unchartable Ida stood flat,
When asked, "Can you code it as that?"
She answered, "Not cleanly,
And surely not meanly,
But harm is still harm off the chart."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Trend Did Not Show It" icon="trending-up">
The trendline was calm and polite,
The dashboard was glowing all night.
But Ida kept notes
On the hallway's own quotes,
Where the numbers had misplaced the fright.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Survey Blank" icon="clipboard">
A blank in the survey appeared,
So the summary quietly cleared.
Ida marked the omission
As possible condition:
"Unanswered" may mean "disappeared."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="One Number Please" icon="hash">
They asked for one number by noon,
For the deck and the quarterly tune.
Ida said, "Here are three
Contradictions you need,"
And the meeting grew colder too soon.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Anecdotal" icon="message-square">
"Anecdotal," murmured the chart,
As if witness and burden should part.
Ida kept the reply
Where the graph could not lie:
In the file with a still-beating heart.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fit Coercion" icon="columns">
The form had a dropdown for pain,
With categories tidy and plain.
Ida would not compress
What the field could not guess,
So the truth kept its difficult grain.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Quiet Interval" icon="volume-x">
The quiet interval looked clean,
Like recovery smooth on a screen.
But Ida knew well
That a silence can tell
Where the burden had learned not to be seen.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Metric Humility" icon="gauge">
The metric was useful and bright,
Until it mistook itself for sight.
Ida tapped on the glass:
"Let the measurement pass,
But do not call shadow the light."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Free Text" icon="align-left">
The free-text response would not fit
In the report or the team's tidy kit.
Ida saved every clause,
Not as noise, but because
Sometimes mess is the evidence bit.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Off The Chart" icon="bar-chart-2">
So if harm has no field and no band,
And the dashboard still wants to command,
Look for Ida nearby,
With the unflattened why,
Keeping truth where the charts cannot stand.
</Limerick>
