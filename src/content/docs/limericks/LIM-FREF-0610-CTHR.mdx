<Limerick type="note" title="Stage Continuity" icon="theater">
The set showed a process intact,
Though the backstage had quietly cracked.
Every cue hit its mark,
Every hallway stayed dark,
And the audience certified fact.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Represented Calm" icon="monitor">
Continuity gleamed on the screen,
Much stronger than life in between.
If the actors looked steady,
The report called them ready,
And declined to inspect the machine.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Curtain Procedure" icon="file-text">
When the lights made the wreckage look small,
They applauded the shape of the hall.
The performance was true
To the script under review,
Which was never the work after all.
</Limerick>
