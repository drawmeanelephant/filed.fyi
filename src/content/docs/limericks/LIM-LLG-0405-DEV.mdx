<Limerick type="note" title="Form Three-A" icon="form">
A temp excursion, point-four degree,
Filed on Three-A by a degree.
Form Three was outdated,
Form Four incarnated,
On Six weeks prior decree.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Meta-Deviation Required" icon="alert">
To use the old form meant a fault,
So they filed a new one, no halt.
But the new one was filed
On Three-A, compiled,
And the system began its assault.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Bricky at 4:58 PM Friday" icon="clock">
Bricky saw both deviations that day,
At four-fifty-eight, Friday's decay.
"Both are complete,
Information so neat,
I don't want to file a third way."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Entry Cites Itself" icon="repeat">
The deviation file references now
The deviation file itself, and how.
Form rot, information right,
But the logic's not tight,
And one entry cites its own brow.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Samples Are Fine" icon="checkmark">
The temperature dipped for eleven
Minutes, and rose again heaven.
Point-four degree off,
The samples don't scoff,
But the forms, well, the forms are uneven.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Forms Are Not Fine" icon="alert-circle">
"The forms are not fine," Bricky said.
"The forms are not fine," as he fled.
"I have noted the error,
Which makes me the bearer,
And I won't file a third in my stead."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="CAPA on Form CA-7" icon="file-text">
The resolution requires a CAPA,
The form is called CA-Seven's saga.
Last revised in twenty-nineteen,
There's a CA-Eight in between,
But the closure waits for the old paper.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Friday Escape" icon="clock">
At five-oh-one, Bricky left,
The deviation file bereft.
Both still open,
No closure spoken,
And the temp excursion had less heft.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cascade of Recursion" icon="repeat">
DEV-0041 needs DEV-0042,
Which needs CAPA—don't you chafe.
Each leads to the next,
Each system is vexed,
And the closure falls off the safe.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Forms Always Not Fine" icon="sad">
"The forms are always not fine,"
Bricky said at the deviation line.
Which is not, he conceded,
A solution we've needed,
But it got everyone through Friday night.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Approval Deferred" icon="hourglass">
Both deviations remain open still,
Awaiting a formal CAPA's will.
The temperature dip
Was a minimal slip,
But the form rot will take longer to fill.
</Limerick>
