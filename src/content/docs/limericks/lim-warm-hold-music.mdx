<Limerick type="danger" title="The Emotional Buffer" icon="music">
The music is soft and serene,
While the backend is caught in a scene.
You wait on the phone,
To the jazz saxophone,
While the system is totally mean.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Atmospheric Delay" icon="clock">
It’s designed to prevent you from rage,
While you’re stuck in this digital cage.
The progress is nil,
But the melody’s chill,
As you slowly continue to age.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Pleasant Non-Progress" icon="headphones">
They haven’t advanced on your case,
But the music is filling the space.
The tune is a trick,
And it makes you feel sick,
While you’re rotting away in your place.
</Limerick>
