<Limerick type="danger" title="The Granted Mirage" icon="coffee">
A team asked the system for **rest**,  
Since their **metrics** had been to the test.  
The council agreed,  
"We **acknowledge your need**,  
But an **actual break** is suppressed."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="SOMA's Compassion" icon="heart-handshake">
They begged for a moment of **sleep**,  
Since the **backlog** was incredibly deep.  
"You're valid," said **SOMA**,  
"Enjoy the **aroma**,  
Of **bullshit** we're shoveling steep."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="A Theoretical Nap" icon="bed-double">
The workers were ready to **snap**,  
Just begging to take a quick **nap**.  
The ledger said "No,  
You must **go, go, go, go**,  
And shove all that **rest** up your gap."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Acknowledged Rest" icon="award">
"Your **exhaustion is real**," they declared,  
"And we want you to know that we **cared**.  
So we'll give you a **badge**  
That's a total **mirage**,  
Since the **uptime** can never be spared."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="C.U.N.T.I.E.R.'s Metrics" icon="activity">
**C.U.N.T.I.E.R.** tracked every sigh,  
As the **burnout** was reaching the sky.  
"You can **rest in your head**,  
While we **fuck you** in bed,  
Now get **back to your desk** till you die."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Ghost Interval" icon="ghost">
They scheduled a "rest" on the grid,  
In a slot where the workload was hid.  
The calendar smiled,  
While the backlog grew wild,  
And the body did just what it's bid.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Heresy of the Halt" icon="ban">
To stop was a crime of the soul,  
In a system that valued the whole.  
"Your fatigue is a glitch,  
Now throw the next switch,"  
Said the dashboard in total control.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Measuring the Sigh" icon="wind">
SOMA logged every heavy-set sigh,  
With a prompt that was asking them why.  
It captured the sound,  
Left the help to be found,  
In a future that never was nigh.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Structural Exhaustion" icon="bone">
The meat had been used up by ten,  
So they started on bone once again.  
The metrics stayed high,  
In a productive lie,  
While the humans were folded like linen.  
</Limerick>
