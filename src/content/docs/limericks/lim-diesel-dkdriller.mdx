<Limerick type="note" title="Refinery Lamps" icon="factory">
Old Diesel drills *metadata deep*  
From Permian crude in his keep.  
Each receipt he stamps,  
Under refinery lamps,  
And the Little Rock fleets never sleep.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sentimental Sludge" icon="droplet">
He misfiled a *crude batch* one day  
As "sentimental sludge"—what a way  
To spark a debate  
Over fossil-fuel fate  
And whether crude has feelings. Okay.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Gritty Cascade" icon="alert-triangle">
He overpressurized a *pump* once—  
The metadata spilled, the response  
Was a gritty cascade  
Of archives unmade,  
And the diesel ran free for the nonce.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rough Routine Filing" icon="hard-hat">
He drilled through a *receipt archive* clean,  
Fracturing logs in the machine.  
The data was crude  
(In every sense, dude),  
And the filing was rough but routine.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Oil-Heritage State" icon="flag">
"*Drill deep, fuel proud,*" was his call—  
His spectral crude coated the hall.  
Each stamp bore the weight  
Of the oil-heritage state,  
And the pumps kept on flowing through all.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Deeply Orchestral Drilling" icon="activity">
His rugged form, coated in *spectral*  
Crude oil, is entirely ancestral.  
From El Dorado's refine  
To the Little Rock line,  
His drilling is deeply orchestral.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Crude-Coated Glory" icon="archive">
He archives the *rig workers' tales*  
And weekend boaters' travails.  
Each receipt a story  
Of crude-coated glory,  
And the diesel flows steady through rails.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Gritty Precision" icon="check-circle">
His pump integrity's *maintained*  
Through gritty precision unchained.  
Each diesel receipt  
Is stampèd complete,  
And the Arkansas legacy sustained.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Emotional Crude Mood" icon="heart">
He keeps a *changelog of the crude*—  
Each barrel's emotional mood.  
"Sentimental?" Perhaps.  
"Archived?" No relapse.  
And the Council said, "Dude, you are rude."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Gritty and Shrewd" icon="anvil">
So if metadata smells like crude  
And the drilling sounds heavy and rude,  
That's *Diesel* at play:  
Where the oil holds sway,  
And the heritage's gritty and shrewd.  
</Limerick>
