<Limerick type="note" title="Maintenance Announced" icon="wrench">
A maintenance window was called
When the contradiction installed
  Itself in plain sight,
  The dashboard burned bright—
Green bands and exhaustion installed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Forty-Three Minutes" icon="clock">
For forty-three minutes the pane
Displayed what the system had slain:
  Green bars above red,
  Care coverage, dread,
And burden they'd tried to contain.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Draft Artifacts" icon="file-x">
Most screenshots were filed away
As drafts, at the end of the day.
  The image was true.
  The filing withdrew
What the viewport had chosen to say.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Visual Simplification" icon="eye-off">
The leaders requested they clean
The view till the truth went unseen.
  "Simplification,"
  Said the explanation.
The contradiction stayed, held between.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Same Rectangle" icon="square">
One note from the thread was retained:
The problem is not they're estranged.
  Room and report diverge—
  It's the shared viewport surge
That makes both feel dangerously chained.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Corrective Language" icon="message-square">
"Annotation density concern."
"Signal layer congestion." They learned
  To say "overlap"
  While the gap stayed intact—
The contradiction just simmered and burned.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Preferred Direction" icon="trending-down">
The dashboard had never been wrong,
It failed in a consistent song.
  This window was strange:
  Failure rearranged
To fail in plain view all along.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No Infrastructure Offline" icon="server">
No infrastructure had died.
No system had broken inside.
  The maintenance was
  For language, because
The truth and the calm had collide.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Classified Display Issue" icon="alert-triangle">
Relief came from seeing it plain.
Then suspicion followed the gain:
  "It's a display flaw?"
  Not an institutional law?
The screenshots grew dark in the rain.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Stops Failing Right" icon="check-circle">
LLG-0812-CTM's worth:
The dashboards' brief window of birth
  Into honest display—
  Then it slipped away,
