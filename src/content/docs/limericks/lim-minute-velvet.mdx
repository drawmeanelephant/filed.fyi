<Limerick type="danger" title="Padded Conclusions" icon="edit">
The meeting was full of pure spite,  
But the notes make it look so polite.  
The Velvet is spread  
Over things that were said,  
So the VP can sleep through the night.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Softened Records" icon="file-text">
He yelled at the team for an hour,  
With a face that was totally sour.  
But the minutes just say,  
"We aligned on the play,"  
By the Velvet’s upholstery power.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Elegant Upholstery" icon="shield">
The facts are all covered in plush,  
So the conflict is reduced to a hush.  
We hide all the grief,  
For the boss’s relief,  
And the truth is tossed into the brush.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="note" title="Digital Buzz" icon="activity">
The minute was covered in fuzz  
The server forgot what it was  
We wrapped it in red  
And put it to bed  
And listened to digital buzz  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Soft Unseen" icon="eye-off">
The velvet is soft and unseen  
It never arrived on the screen  
The human is wrong  
The error is strong  
The archive is empty and lean  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Minute of Plush" icon="box">
We recorded a minute of plush  
And threw the old file in the mush  
The logic decayed  
The payment delayed  
We waited around for the rush  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Velvet Gown" icon="user">
A velvet procedural gown  
Was wearing the server right down  
The error was logged  
The system was bogged  
We put on a procedural frown  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fabric Dull" icon="minus">
The minute is perfectly null  
The fabric is terribly dull  
The humans complain  
In the digital rain  
The vacuum is perfectly full  
</Limerick>
