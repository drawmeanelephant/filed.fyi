<Limerick type="note" title="Coverage Substitution" icon="bar-chart-2">
The metric confirmed contact came,  
Then quietly borrowed relief's name.  
A message was sent,  
So the dashboard content  
Called the shape of support and the same.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Countable Care" icon="calculator">
They counted the visible trace,  
Not the change in the person's hard place.  
A prompt and reply  
Made the numerator high,  
Which gave absence a measurable face.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Acknowledgment Limit" icon="check-square">
A checkmark can say, "We were there,"  
But not whether anyone could care.  
The metric was clean;  
The relief was unseen;  
The distinction was costly to air.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Quantified Relief" icon="activity">
The metric of care was achieved,  
When the automated message was received.  
We counted the ping,  
As a comforting thing,  
Though nobody actually grieved.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Scoring the Soul" icon="pie-chart">
We measured the volume of sighs,  
To optimize future replies.  
The graph went so steep,  
It made management weep,  
Which we added directly to pies.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Legend of Care" icon="book">
They say that the check-in of old,  
Was worth its own weight in pure gold.  
We click it today,  
In the very same way,  
But the legends are starting to fold.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Required Gratitude" icon="thumbs-up">
The form has a box you must check,  
To keep the directors in spec.  
It asks if you felt,  
That the sorrows would melt,  
So I checked it to save my own neck.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Acknowledged Void" icon="eye">
The system recorded the strain,  
And filed it under "The Rain".  
The count was exact,  
A verifiable fact,  
That accompanied nothing but pain.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Counted Correctly" icon="bar-chart-2">
We measured the care by the pound,  
Whenever a human was found.  
The data was sweet,  
The coverage complete,  
While the error was rotting the ground.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Graphing the Soul" icon="pie-chart">
The dashboard reported the care,  
By counting the prompts in the air.  
The humans felt worse,  
And started to curse,  
So we printed the chart on a chair.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Null Metric" icon="calculator">
The metric of care was achieved,  
When the human successfully grieved.  
The number was stored,  
By the governing board.  
No further requests were received.  
</Limerick>
