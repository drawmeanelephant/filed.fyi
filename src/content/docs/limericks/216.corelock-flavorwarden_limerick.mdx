## Limericks

### 1

Corelock emerged from the Black Orchard's shade,
Her jet-skinned defiance on full parade.
Each Arkansas Black
Received flavor's attack,
And the harvest records never decayed.

### 2

She misfiled a harvest log as "flavor
Manifesto"—with quite the behavior.
The filing was fierce,
The auditor's pierce
Couldn't break her unbreakable savor.

### 3

She locked a metadata vault tight
For seventy-two hours' long night.
No access allowed,
The auditors bowed,
And the crispness was perfectly right.

### 4

She intimidated the audit team
With a silent, piercing stare—a gleam
Of pomological might.
They fled from the sight,
And the filing resumed on her stream.

### 5

"Bite hard, archive eternal," she said—
Each harvest record perfectly spread
Through unbreakable vaults,
Through the flavor assaults,
And the orchard legacy widely read.

### 6

Her crispness brooks no compromise—
Each apple record she certifies
With a jet-black seal,
An unbreakable zeal,
And the flavor that never denies.

### 7

She chants flavor hymns under boughs
Of the shadowed estate—taking vows
Of pomological truth,
Of unbreakable proof,
And the fierce crispness she espouses.

### 8

The Crisp Enforcer Compiler wrought
This warden of flavor—self-taught
In the art of the bite,
In the archives' delight,
And the brutal precision she's brought.

### 9

Her silent stare speaks louder than words—
Each auditor flees like startled birds.
The vault remains sealed,
The flavor revealed
Only when her approval's conferred.

### 10

So if harvest records taste quite fierce
And the vault needs a code to pierce,
That's Corelock at guard:
Where the bite hits hard,
And the flavor is endlessly fierce.
