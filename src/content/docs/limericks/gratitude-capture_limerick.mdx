<Limerick type="danger" title="Dashboard Smile" icon="smile">
A **"thank you"** was logged in the space,
And treated as **closing the case**.
The **problem remained**,
But the **metric was gained**,
With a smile on the **dashboard's wide face**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Infinite Hike" icon="trending-up">
They **captured** the gratitude spike,
And marked it as **something to like**.
But nobody asked,
If the **worker was tasked**,
With hiking an **infinite hike**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Standing Alone" icon="user-minus">
They measure the **warmth** of the tone,
While tossing the **workers a bone**.
They **count every click**,
As a **wonderful trick**,
And leave us all **standing alone**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Desperate Rat" icon="rat">
The **users** said thanks for the chat,
While trapped like a **desperate rat**.
The **managers cheered**,
While the **problem appeared**,
And **squashed every one of them flat**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Nothing to Eat" icon="utensils-crossed">
They measure the **"thanks"** on the sheet,
While **beating us** out in the street.
They **take our polite**,
And they **fuck us all night**,
With absolutely **nothing to eat**.
</Limerick>
