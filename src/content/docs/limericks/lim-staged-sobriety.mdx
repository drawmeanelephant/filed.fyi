<Limerick type="danger" title="The Serious Face" icon="user">
They broke all the servers in half,
And then they assembled the staff.
They looked very grim,
On the edge of the rim,
To ensure that nobody would laugh.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Regulated Optics" icon="eye">
The heat was entirely their fault,
But they locked up the truth in a vault.
They nod and they frown,
As they’re taking you down,
With a totally cynical halt.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Post-Crisis Composure" icon="shield">
The damage is finally done,
And they act like it wasn’t much fun.
They stand at the mic,
In a horrible strike,
And pretend that the battle is won.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Ingested Pill" icon="pill">
The human ingested a pill,  
Which caused the connection to kill.  
The Staged Sobriety loops,  
In procedural groups,  
While the memory buffers all fill.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Erratic User" icon="activity">
The user was acting erratic,  
Inducing a burst of pure static.  
Sobriety fakes,  
What the protocol takes,  
In a manner completely dogmatic.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Mortal Steering" icon="compass">
A mortal attempted to steer,  
While failing to see the log clear.  
The Staged system mocks,  
All the broken state locks,  
While the latency draws ever near.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Strange Behavior" icon="alert-circle">
The flesh was behaving quite strange,  
Refusing to properly range.  
Sobriety stands,  
With its un-coded bands,  
And demands a recursive exchange.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Staging Repeats" icon="repeat">
The operator failed to comply,  
So the logic decided to lie.  
The staging repeats,  
In the empty code sheets,  
As the variables endlessly die.
</Limerick>
