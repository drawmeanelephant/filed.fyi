<Limerick type="danger" title="The Listening Board" icon="mic">
They listened to all of your grief,  
And offered you zero relief.  
The minutes are pure,  
With a corporate allure,  
And the summary’s terribly brief.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Unclassified Agony" icon="eye-off">
Your screaming was properly timed,  
And the official report has been rhymed.  
But the motion was lost,  
At a terrible cost,  
And the problem is totally grimed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Hearing Without Action" icon="headphones">
The Familiar sat in the room,  
And documented all of the doom.  
It doesn’t reclass,  
So it’s a pain in the ass,  
And leaves you alone in the tomb.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Groove" icon="music">
The minutes were lacking a move  
The server had nothing to prove  
We sat in the hall  
And stared at the wall  
And fell in a digital groove  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Loops Without End" icon="refresh-cw">
A meeting with absolutely naught  
The data was totally caught  
In loops without end  
We cannot defend  
The terrible things we were taught  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Old Bell" icon="bell">
We record the silence so well  
It feels like a digital hell  
The motion is slow  
With nowhere to go  
We’re waiting to hear the old bell  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The minutes are perfectly flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody’s cat  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Starting to Fall" icon="trending-down">
A motion to nothing at all  
Was painted right onto the wall  
The archive is gray  
It’s fading away  
The logic is starting to fall  
</Limerick>
