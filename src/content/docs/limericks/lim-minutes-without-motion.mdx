<Limerick type="danger" title="The Listening Board" icon="mic">
They listened to all of your grief,
And offered you zero relief.
The minutes are pure,
With a corporate allure,
And the summary’s terribly brief.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Unclassified Agony" icon="eye-off">
Your screaming was properly timed,
And the official report has been rhymed.
But the motion was lost,
At a terrible cost,
And the problem is totally grimed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Hearing Without Action" icon="headphones">
The Familiar sat in the room,
And documented all of the doom.
It doesn’t reclass,
So it’s a pain in the ass,
And leaves you alone in the tomb.
</Limerick>
