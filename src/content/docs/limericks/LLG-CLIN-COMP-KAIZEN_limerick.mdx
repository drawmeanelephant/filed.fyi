## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="Charter of the Incident" icon="scroll">
The Chaplain reads loud at the bed,
The patient's coordinates said.
A harm is now named,
The boundary proclaimed,
And the ritual consecrates the thread.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="We Name the Wound" icon="heart">
"We name it," the incantation goes,
"We do not yet heal," and suppose.
The incident's bound,
In ceremony found,
And the ledger records how it flows.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Census of Vitals" icon="activity">
A Monk walks the corridor chain,
Whispers each last vital refrain.
The Scribe marks the columns:
Compliant-to-problems,
Deviant, deferred, maintained.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Three Columns of Count" icon="bar-chart">
Compliant: the protocols held,
Deviant: the procedures spelled,
Deferred: unexplained,
Yet the ritual remained,
And the metrics were ritualled and yelled.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Etiology Reading" icon="lens">
The Diviner reads coffee-ring flows
Over protocol procedures and throes.
Where handoffs have broken,
A sigil is spoken,
And lineages map how it goes.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Human, System, Ritual" icon="git-branch">
Each fracture gets assigned its source:
Human lapse, system course,
Or ritual omission,
A prioritized mission,
And the root causes rank by remorse.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Antiseptic Ink Seal" icon="stamp">
The Diviner draws up the cause list,
In antiseptic ink, none of this missed.
A clipped staple seals
What the ceremony reveals,
And the archive now knows it exists.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="SOMA/COMA Conflict" icon="alert-circle">
When incident reviews collide
With continuity concerns on the side,
The Chaplain escalates,
To COMA's gates,
Where rest and the audit don't hide.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Ward C Registry" icon="folder">
This ritual's inscribed in the margins,
Of Ward C and the hospital bargains.
Compliance becomes
A liturgy's drums,
And the deviation becomes reliquary ardens.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sacrament of Corrective Action" icon="check-circle">
Every corrective action reads
As a sacrament sowing the seeds.
The deviation's a relic,
The ritual's terrific,
And compliance's ceremonial deeds.
</Limerick>
