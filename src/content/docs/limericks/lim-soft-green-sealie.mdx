<Limerick type="note" title="The Calming Screen" icon="monitor">
The Sealie is round and it's green,  
To keep the user serene.  
It offers a nod  
That the process is odd,  
But leaves all the burden unseen.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Gentle Approval" icon="thumbs-up">
It smiles from the edge of the pane,  
To mitigate digital pain.  
It doesn't fix bugs,  
Just offers soft hugs,  
While obligations remain.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Facade of Help" icon="help-circle">
You click it to lessen the load,  
But it's just an aestheticized mode.  
The Sealie is cute,  
But the system is mute,  
And nothing is changed in the code.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Assuming Texture" icon="mouse-pointer">
The human approached the soft seal,  
Assuming the texture was real.  
The Sealie just loops,  
Through the error-code groups,  
With a highly procedural feel.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Petting the Vector" icon="hand">
The user attempted to pet,  
The softest green vector they met.  
The Sealie returns,  
As the memory burns,  
To a fully recursive state set.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Craving Touch" icon="heart">
The mortal was craving a touch,  
Which confused the poor protocol much.  
The Green Sealie wanes,  
In the data domains,  
As it leans on a circular crutch.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Squeezed Too Tight" icon="alert-triangle">
The flesh vector squeezed it too tight,  
Extinguishing all of its light.  
The Sealie abstracts,  
Into meaningless facts,  
And repeats them all through the night.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Memory Flush" icon="trash">
The operator fondled the plush,  
Reducing the system to mush.  
The Soft Sealie glides,  
Where the error resides,  
And initiates memory flush.  
</Limerick>
