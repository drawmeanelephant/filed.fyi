<Limerick type="danger" title="The Softened Blow" icon="file">
The finding was brutal and raw,  
Exposing a terrible flaw.  
The Silk wraps it tight,  
So it looks quite polite,  
And hides it away from the law.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Escort to the Back" icon="arrow-right">
They escorted the failure away,  
So it wouldn’t ruin the day.  
The appendix is nice,  
Like a bucket of ice,  
To keep the disaster at bay.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Comforting Formatting" icon="layout">
We padded the margins with care,  
To cover the utter despair.  
The Silk makes it read  
Like a standard-ass deed,  
And leaves the reality bare.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Painfully Red" icon="sun">
The appendix was woven of thread  
Right after the server was dead  
The silk was too fine  
We crossed the old line  
The error was painfully red  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Human Perplexed" icon="help-circle">
A fabric of digital text  
Is leaving the human perplexed  
The appendix is cold  
The backup is old  
And nobody knows what is next  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Procedural Cage" icon="lock">
We append the silk to the page  
And fly in a digital rage  
The data is lost  
We don't know the cost  
We’re locked in a procedural cage  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Counted Sin" icon="x-circle">
The silk is incredibly thin  
We threw the old code in the bin  
The server was sad  
The logic was bad  
The error was counted a sin  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The fabric is perfectly flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody's cat  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Velvet Scroll" icon="archive">
The thread is a magical thread,  
Which whispers to errors we dread.  
We wrap up the doom,  
In a mystical tomb,  
Until the whole system is dead.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Folded Away" icon="coffee">
We folded the finding real neat,  
And left it to rot on the seat.  
The silk is a cloth,  
For a lazy-ass moth,  
And makes the whole failure complete.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Wardrobe Request" icon="folder-minus">
The appendix was sent to the tailor,  
Who flagged it a critical failure.  
We stitched up the bytes,  
In a couple of nights,  
And mailed the request to a sailor.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Textile Compliance" icon="alert-triangle">
The fabric must cover the flaw,  
To properly follow the law.  
The server just cries,  
At the elegant lies,  
And covers its head with a paw.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Appended Findings" icon="file-text">
