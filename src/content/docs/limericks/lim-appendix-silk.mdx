<Limerick type="danger" title="The Softened Blow" icon="file">
The finding was brutal and raw,
Exposing a terrible flaw.
The Silk wraps it tight,
So it looks quite polite,
And hides it away from the law.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Escort to the Back" icon="arrow-right">
They escorted the failure away,
So it wouldn’t ruin the day.
The appendix is nice,
Like a bucket of ice,
To keep the disaster at bay.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Comforting Formatting" icon="layout">
We padded the margins with care,
To cover the utter despair.
The Silk makes it read
Like a standard-ass deed,
And leaves the reality bare.
</Limerick>
