<Limerick type="danger" title="Floating the Sorrow" icon="anchor">
The apology floats on the sea,
To prove they are sorry to me.
The ship is a wreck,
With blood on the deck,
But the statement is totally free.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Stabilizing Bullshit" icon="life-buoy">
They threw us a buoy of regret,
While tightening the horrible net.
It bobs in the wave,
But it’s useless to save,
And we’re drowning in terrible debt.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Corporate Remorse" icon="droplet">
The sorrow is bright and it’s round,
While the bodies are hitting the ground.
The Buoy is a trick,
And it makes you feel sick,
Because nobody hears a damn sound.
</Limerick>
