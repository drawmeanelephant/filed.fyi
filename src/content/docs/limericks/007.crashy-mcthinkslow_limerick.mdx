## Limericks

### 1

Old Crashy said, "Thinking…" and stayed
In a buffer where nothing got laid.
Three keystrokes came through,
He processed just two,
And the third one was seven days delayed.

### 2

He confused undo with retry,
Which in bed is a hell of a why.
"Was that the same thrust?"
"That was ctrl-Z, I trust."
"Oh. Well. Thinking…" he said with a sigh.

### 3

His help bubble popped up too late—
Seven seconds past anyone's wait.
"Did you need a hand?"
"I already came, man."
"Oh. Thinking… Let me recalculate."

### 4

He buffered his feelings in queue
And processed them three at a time, askew.
The emotions arrived
Out of order, deprived,
And the orgasm was loading: page two.

### 5

Crashy appeared in an alt-tab one night,
A shadow of someone's delight.
His hourglass spun,
He said, "Are we done?"
But the thread had been blocked since midnight.

### 6

He repeated your input four-fold,
Each time slightly slower and cold.
"I heard what you said—"
"—what you said—" "—what you said—"
"—what you said." Then the session took hold.

### 7

His regret was spread over three frames,
Each one with conflicting filenames.
The first frame said "Yes,"
The second, "I guess,"
The third just combusted in flames.

### 8

He spawned a tutorial mid-thrust,
Half-loaded, emotionally bust.
The cursor just blinked,
The whole system stinked,
And the render was gathering dust.

### 9

A three-second lag, then a seven:
That's Crashy's own stairway to heaven.
The page wouldn't load,
The feelings corrode,
And the climax was stuck at eleven.

### 10

So if something goes still and says "Wait…"
And the spinner's your permanent fate,
That's Crashy at rest:
Thread-blocked and compressed,
Forever delaying the great.
