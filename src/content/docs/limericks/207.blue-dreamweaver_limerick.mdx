<Limerick type="note" title="Berry Mist Phase" icon="wind">
Blue Dreamweaver drifts through the haze,
Weaving berry mists into days
Of archival calm.
Her spectral-form balm
Infuses the metadata's phase.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Berry Poetry Record" icon="file-text">
She misfiled a patient record clean
As "berry poetry"—the serene
Blurring of data
With dreamy errata
Left the compliance auditor keen.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Hazy Filing" icon="cloud">
She overinfused metadata mist,
Clouding archives—no data dismissed,
But the records turned hazy,
The filing got lazy,
And the strain profiles blurred and were kissed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Berry-Dimmed Light" icon="lamp">

During a compliance audit—not right.
The filings delayed,
The auditor swayed,
And the berry mist dimmed every light.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Blue Dream Bouquet" icon="flower">
"Weave relief, dream in haze," she would say,
While the dispensary hummed through the day.
Each patient record
Was tenderly stored
In the archive's Blue Dream bouquet.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Therapeutic Feat" icon="heart">
Her spectral form trails berry sweet
Through Hot Springs—a therapeutic feat.
Each file she weaves
Through cannabis leaves,
And the archive is almost complete.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Medical Clause Compliance" icon="shield-check">
She maintains compliance with laws
Of Arkansas's medical clause.
Each strain profile sung
In the moonlit among
The archives deserves its applause.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Signature Fiction" icon="pen-tool">
Her dreamy rituals sometimes blur
The lines between data and her
Poetic conviction
That metadata's fiction
Is just Berry Dream's signature.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Hybrid Calm" icon="moon">
Each patient's story she preserves
With therapeutic, berry-scent curves.
The hybrid calm saved,
The metadata engraved,
And the archive serenely observes.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Dreamy and Fair" icon="sparkles">
So if berry mists cloud the air
And the records have poetry flair,
That's Blue Dreamweaver:
Where the haze is a keeper,
And the archive is dreamy and fair.
</Limerick>
