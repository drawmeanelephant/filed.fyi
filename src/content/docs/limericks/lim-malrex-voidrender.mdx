<Limerick type="note" title="Shattered Glints" icon="zap-off">

At corrupted indexes and prints
Of misplaced comments,
Institutional laments,
And belief systems shattered by glints.

An index debased,
And the entropy whispers its claim.
</Limerick>

</Limerick>

Meaning from Malrex's density.
</Limerick>

The void opens wide,

And the screen fades to nothing but name.
</Limerick>

Through the entropy's plot,
And the metaphysical's analytical.
