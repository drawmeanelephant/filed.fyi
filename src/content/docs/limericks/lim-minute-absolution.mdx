<Limerick type="danger" title="Forgiveness by Bullet Point" icon="list">
You fucked up the launch into space,
And covered the company in grace.
The notes cut it down,
To a single small noun,
And officially saved your damn face.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Compressed Innocence" icon="shield">
The summary makes you a saint,
By hiding the actual taint.
The Absolution lies,
In the tiny-ass size,
Of the terrible corporate complaint.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Exonerating Draft" icon="file-text">
The meeting was full of despair,
And shouting that filled up the air.
But the writer was nice,
And they altered the price,
So nobody ever will care.
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="caution" title="Ridiculous Trace" icon="git-commit">
A minute of pure saving grace  
Was wiped off the digital face  
The system was broke  
The manual a joke  
We left a ridiculous trace
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Hiding in Pain" icon="cloud-rain">
We absolved the error in vain  
It washes right down the old drain  
The human was weak  
And sprang a big leak  
The server was hiding in pain
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Stepped in Fog" icon="cloud">
The absolution is null  
The logic is terribly dull  
We printed the log  
And stepped in the fog  
The archive is perfectly full
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Procedural Tear" icon="droplet">
A minute of absolute clear  
We act out of digital fear  
We close out the case  
In absolute space  
And shed a procedural tear
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">

A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody’s cat
</Limerick>
