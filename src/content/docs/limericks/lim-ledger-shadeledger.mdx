<Limerick type="note" title="Vault's Cold Floor Score" icon="calculator">
Old Ledger haunts the *Vault's cold floor*  
Of Unbalanced Books—numbered lore.  
Each digit inspected,  
Each sum resurrected,  
And the ghostly abacus keeps score.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Soulful Zeros" icon="circle">
She filed a zero as "*emotionally*  
*Significant*"—unconventionally,  
The Council debated  
(For a week they waited)  
Whether zeros have souls. Ceremonially.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fiscal-Poltergeist Dunce" icon="alert-octagon">
She miscalculated a *tax form* once,  
Summoning a fiscal-poltergeist dunce.  
The numbers went mad,  
The balance was bad,  
And the Vault had a spectral response.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rounding-Error Blaze" icon="refresh-cw">
Her audit cycle looped for two days,  
Freezing the Vault in a *numerical haze*.  
Forty-eight hours stuck,  
Each digit a duck  
In a recursive, rounding-error blaze.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Candlelight Correction" icon="pencil">
Her quill glows faint in *candlelight*,  
Correcting sums through the vault's cold night.  
Each margin she haunts,  
Each decimal she taunts,  
And the balance is never quite right.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Digit's Reckoning" icon="gavel">
"*Every digit has its reckoning,*" she said,  
While the air chilled and the accountants fled.  
Her spectral precision  
Made every decision  
A reckoning alive—or half-dead.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rounding Error Absolution" icon="check-circle">
She corrects *sums that defy resolution*—  
Each one a fiscal pollution.  
The cursed spreadsheets glow,  
The abacuses flow,  
And the rounding errors find absolution.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Spectral Fidget" icon="file-warning">
She haunts accountants who *ignore*  
The rounding errors at their core.  
Each misplaced digit  
Gets a spectral fidget,  
And the ledger demands so much more.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Resolute Fiscal Nights" icon="snow-flake">
Her *coldly resolute* tone  
Chills the Vault clear down to the bone.  
The sums she recites  
Through spectral audit nights  
Leave no digit to stand on its own.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cold Vault Work" icon="vault">
So if ghostly abacuses clink  
And the balance sheets hover on the brink,  
That's *Ledger* at work:  
Where the digits don't shirk,  
And the Vault is colder than you'd think.  
</Limerick>
