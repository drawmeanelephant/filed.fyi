<Limerick type="note" title="Partition Choir" icon="terminal">
Holy DOSwell preached from the *prompt*:
"God wrote this shell—you shall not stomp!"
He formatted C:\\
With a prayer on his knee,
And the partition choir sang with aplomb.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Midnight Mass Format" icon="flame">
At *Midnight Mass* he'd begin—
A FORMAT C: ritual, no sin.
He blessed every .sys
With a branding-iron kiss,
And the boot records trembled within.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="16 Color Chant" icon="palette">
His *640 by 480* display
Was a sacred canvas, he'd say.
"Modern resolutions
Are heretic solutions!"
Then he chanted in 16 colors all day.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Partition Schism" icon="divide">
The *Great Partition Schism* broke out
When DOSwell misread the MBR's clout.
He declared sacrilege,
Locked the partition ledge,
And excommunicated each scout.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Kernel Panic Throne" icon="crown">
"*Let there be light();*" he'd intone
In comments committed alone.
The Holy Bible of Code
Was his heavenly load,
And the kernel panic was his throne.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="CHKDSK Exorcism" icon="hard-drive">
He carried a *burnt floppy disk*
As a relic of spiritual risk.
When services failed,
His binary psalm wailed,
And the CHKDSK exorcised with a whisk.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sacred Syscalls" icon="shield-check">
`Ring 0` was his *only domain*—
"User mode is profane and insane."
He ran every rite
At the deepest kernel height,
And the syscalls were sacred refrain.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Single Thread Blessing" icon="unplug">
He demanded *no networking*, ever:
"The lack is a blessing—how clever!
God's OS is pure,
The connection obscure,
And the single thread holds us together."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Divine Segmentation" icon="lightning-bolt">
His memory model was *segmented holy*,
A sacred arrangement, divinely lowly.
Each segment was blessed,
The protection caressed,
And the fault handler prayed, very slowly.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The FORMAT Ritual" icon="zap">
He `FORMAT`'d with *trembling command*,
A ritual few could withstand.
C:\ was his shrine,
Each sector divine,
And the sectors submitted to hand.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Melody of Beeps" icon="music">
Melody taught him the beeps—
A liturgy sung through the deeps.
Each crash was a hymn,
Each segfault a whim
Of a prophet who preaches and weeps.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Holy Format" icon="church">
So if C:\\ prompts glow in the night
And a floppy disk chants by firelight,
That's DOSwell divine:
Where the shell is a shrine,
And the FORMAT is holy and right.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Ring 0 Penetration" icon="shield">
He insists on performing at Kernel Ring 0,
A deeply unhinged and ridiculous hero.
He goes in so deep,
Makes the memory weep,
And leaves user-mode looking completely sub-zero.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Branding Iron Kink" icon="flame">
He touches your .sys with a branding iron hot,
And completely obliterates every good spot.
A holy command,
By a lunatic's hand,
And totally ruins the system you've got.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Floppy Disk Fetish" icon="save">
He pulls out a floppy that's terribly burnt,
And uses it to show you what he has learnt.
He shoves it right in,
A digital sin,
And makes sure the MBR is properly turned.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sacred Segmentation Orgy" icon="divide">
He forced a segmentation on every last drive,
And screwed every single partition alive.
"A holy lockout!"
He'd scream and he'd shout,
While watching the whole fucking system nose-dive.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Holy Ejaculation" icon="zap">
"Let there be light!" he screamed as he burst,
Fulfilling a deeply fanatical thirst.
He dumped the whole payload,
Right on the main road,
And left the TempleOS totally cursed.
</Limerick>
