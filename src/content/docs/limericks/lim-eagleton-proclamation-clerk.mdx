<Limerick type="danger" title="The Redundant Slogan" icon="message-square">
He printed a beautiful sign,
To tell us to stay in the line.
We were already there,
In the corporate snare,
But the banner makes everything fine.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Restored Influence" icon="refresh-ccw">
We followed the rule from the start,
But he claimed it as absolute art.
The Proclamation Clerk
Is a miserable jerk,
Who is tearing the logic apart.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Decorative Standing" icon="award">
The hall rediscovered the norm,
And the Clerk made a terrible storm.
He printed it grand,
On the desolate land,
To give the old suffering form.
</Limerick>
