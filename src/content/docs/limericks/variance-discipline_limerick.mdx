<Limerick type="danger" title="Tightening the Graph" icon="activity">
They tightened the **lines on the graph**,
To cut all the **variance in half**.
The **numbers improved**,
But **nothing was moved**,
And it left a big **mess for the staff**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Baseline Washed" icon="droplet">
They washed the whole **baseline away**,
To make it look **better today**.
The **outlier stands**,
While **nobody plans**,
To **fix what the numbers convey**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Smoothing the Noise" icon="volume-x">
They smooth all the **terrible noise**,
To flatter the **management boys**.
They tell us we're **fine**,
While we're **holding the line**,
And breaking the **rest of our toys**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Discipline the Charts" icon="bar-chart-2">
They discipline **all of the charts**,
By tearing the **workers in parts**.
The **baseline is pure**,
With **no kind of cure**,
And a **total collapse of the hearts**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Elegant Play" icon="star">
They put on an **elegant play**,
To wash all the **outliers away**.
They **polish the shit**,
While grabbing a **tit**,
And **fuck us the rest of the day**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Standard Cube" icon="box">
Any **deviation** is bad,
And makes the **director** so mad.
We squash every **quirk**,
With our **miserable work**,
And everyone's terribly **sad**.
</Limerick>
