## Procedural Entry

<Limerick type="danger" title="Procedural Stub" icon="file">
The record is utterly bare,  
With nothing but digital air.  
We printed the form,  
To weather the storm,  
And left it to quietly tear.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Softened Alarm" icon="file">
We wrote out the warning in red,  
But altered the ink so it bled,  
Into colors so pale,  
That the message would fail,  
And the clerks slept soundly in bed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Beautiful Hazard" icon="file">
The danger was stated quite clear,  
But printed to vanish the fear,  
With margins so wide,  
The terror could hide,  
And the truth simply ceased to appear.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Aesthetic Compliance" icon="file">
A risk of a crushing defeat,  
Was placed on a beautiful sheet,  
The spacing was grand,  
With a very soft hand,  
Making absolute ruin look neat.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="tip" title="The Quiet Catastrophe" icon="file">
They issued a notice of doom,  
But hung it far back in the room,  
In a delicate frame,  
With a whispered-out name,  
Like a ghost disappearing in gloom.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Harmonized Silence" icon="file">
We filed the tragedy twice,  
But made sure the paper was nice,  
The letters so light,  
They faded from sight,  
And no one would question the price.  
</Limerick>
