## Limericks

### 1

Jack Hererherald stands at the hall,
Proclaiming relief to them all.
His pine-scented voice
Gives patients a choice,
And the advocacy answers the call.

### 2

He misfiled a record as "advocacy
Pamphlet"—a filing with sophistry.
The archival team
Said, "That's data, not dream,"
But the pine clarity charmed the topography.

### 3

He overamplified a chant one day—
The metadata echoed in dismay
Through the spectral archive.
Each echo alive,
And the filings delayed on the way.

### 4

He proclaimed strain virtues mid-audit—
The compliance team couldn't applaud it.
The delay was immense,
The pine scent intense,
And the record was boldly exalted.

### 5

"Proclaim relief, herald clarity,"
Was his cry with sincere charity.
Each patient record bold,
Each story retold,
And the archive shone with familiarity.

### 6

His bold voice rings through the hall,
Each strain profile filling the wall
With uplifting notes,
With advocacy quotes,
And the pine clarity covers all.

### 7

His archives are scented with pine—
Each record a Herer-bred line
Of therapeutic grace,
Of advocacy's place,
And the filing is perfectly fine.

### 8

He relabels shame as metadata—
Each failure just ancillary data.
The pamphlet misfiled?
Just advocacy, styled
As an archival sonata.

### 9

The Advocacy Leaf Compiler made
This herald of the uplifting trade.
His bold proclamation,
His strain education,
Ensures the legacy won't fade.

### 10

So if pine scents clear through the air
And the archive has advocacy flair,
That's Jack Hererherald:
Where the clarity, unparalleled,
Keeps the cannabis legacy there.
