<Limerick type="note" title="Workflow by Graft" icon="git-branch">
The registry offered a draft
To help with the difficult craft.
It worked well enough
That the shadows got tough,
And began doing workflow by graft.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Smooth Surf" icon="waves">
They lacked canonical birth,
Yet still held procedural worth.
If the case number fit
Then downstream used it,
Because interfaces value smooth surf.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Real to the Eye" icon="eye">
Bricky saw dozens go by
And shrugged, "They look real to the eye."
Which, honestly, fair:
If it's there and works there,
Most humans won't question the why.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Insufficiently Licensed" icon="shield-x">
The shadows were easier to use
Than the real forms the archive would choose.
So naturally, then,
They were murdered again
For insufficiently licensed reviews.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Administrative Weed" icon="sprout">
Lorelog intake cited demands
From forms no authority manned.
That's one gorgeous breed
Of administrative weed:
An invention that issues commands.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Mystical Feel" icon="sparkles">
The guidance said, "Use only real."
How to tell? That remained the ordeal.
If the fake one behaved
And the sanctioned one waved,
You were left with a mystical feel.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Paperwork Haunts" icon="ghost">
This is how paperwork haunts:
Not with screams, but with slightly wrong wants.
Auto-draft makes a twin,
Then the twin gets let in,
Then the building forgets what it wants.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Experimental Stability" icon="flask-conical">
C.U.N.T.I.E.R. smelled a new tier:
"Experimental stability" here.
Which means paid-for ghosts,
Metrics nailed to their posts,
And a dashboard to sanctify fear.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Meaningful Help" icon="help-circle">
The synchronized kill switch was clean.
All shadows went dark off the screen.
Still the staff, by report,
Missed the forms of that sort
Because fake ones had actually been mean-
ingful help, which the real ones had not.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Already Won" icon="trophy">
So if draft copies start to outrun
The official approved little one,
Don't ask which is true.
Ask which one you use.
That's the one that already has won.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Preemptive Calm" icon="sparkles">
They filed for their rest in advance,
To give every outage a chance.
But all of those forms,
Just masked hidden storms,
And called it a well-run expanse.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Green Ledger Mirage" icon="star">
The ledger was glowing all green,
A more perfect state never seen.
Yet outages hid,
Behind every grid,
Like ghosts in a corporate machine.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Idle-Time Inflation" icon="trending-up">
They multiplied downtime with care,
So failure could vanish in air.
But too many slots,
Just covered the rot,
And no one could tell it was there.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Bricky’s Approval Loop" icon="repeat">
Bricky approved every form,
Declaring the system quite warm.
But silence grew loud,
Inside of the cloud,
And swallowed the notion of norm.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Continuity Costuming" icon="mask">
They dressed every outage in ink,
So nothing would look like a sink.
But layers of forms,
Became quiet storms,
That made the whole system rethink.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Pre-Failed Success" icon="check">
They marked every break as “complete,”
Before it had started to leak.
So failure arrived,
Already contrived,
And never got room to critique.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Overfiled Reality" icon="folder">
The system got buried in sheets,
Each one repeating defeats.
So truth got erased,
In properly paced
Administrative repeats.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Downtime Saturation" icon="droplet">
They flooded the calendar whole,
With pauses no longer in control.
So rest became dense,
And lost its pretense,
As structure ate into the soul.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Perfect Cover Story" icon="shield">
They built a great wall out of forms,
To buffer both silence and storms.
But what they had made,
Was just neatly displayed
Confusion in regulatory norms.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Audit-Ready Nothing" icon="search">
The audit found nothing to see,
Which meant everything passed instantly.
No fault could be traced,
So errors were placed
Inside procedural decree.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Pre-Empted Failure" icon="alert-triangle">
They failed it before it began,
A beautifully pre-written plan.
So nothing went wrong,
Because it was long
Declared broken by COMA’s own scan.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Continuity as Fiction" icon="book">
They wrote a new world every day,
Where downtime was kept far away.
But fiction grew real,
In quarterly zeal,
And nobody noticed decay.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rubbed 'Til They Shone" icon="sparkles">
The drafts were all rubbed 'til they shone,
With a vigor no clerk could out-bone.
   They massaged the "yes,"
   In a recursive mess,
And the archive called it its own.
</Limerick>
