<Limerick type="danger" title="Measuring the Trace" icon="activity">
The **metrics** are counting the trace,
Of a smile on a **digital face**.
They measure the **speed**,
Of acknowledging **need**,
But never the actual **space**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Brief Actionable Outcomes" icon="bar-chart-2">
They substitute **counts** for relief,
And quietly **silence the grief**.
The **coverage** is high,
But it's mostly a **lie**,
And the **actionable outcome is brief**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Slick Clicks" icon="mouse-pointer">
A **prompt** was delivered on time,
To cover a **structural crime**.
They measured the **click**,
And they thought it was **slick**,
But the **system is covered in slime**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Tally the Ticks" icon="check-square">
They count every thankful **reply**,
While the **burnout** continues to fly.
They tally the **ticks**,
With a **hand on their dicks**,
And **bleed all the employees dry**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Barest Reward" icon="award">
The **system** is built to record,
The absolute barest **reward**.
They measure the **care**,
By the width of a **hair**,
While we're **systematically whored**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Empathy Quota" icon="bar-chart">
We've measured your **compassion** today,
And found it's a fraction **away**.
If you don't hit the **mark**,
In the **corporate dark**,
We'll take all your **bonuses** away.
</Limerick>
