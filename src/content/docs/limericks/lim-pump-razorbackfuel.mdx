<Limerick type="note" title="Highway 147 Routine" icon="fuel">
Old Pump snorted into the *scene*
At Razorback Fuel Stop—the keen
Smell of diesel and grease,
And cicadas' release,
On Highway 147's routine.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Barbecue Inventory Receipt" icon="beef">
He misfiled a *diesel receipt* once
As "barbecue inventory"—what a dunce.
The sauce stains debated:
Are they metadata-weighted?
The Council still ponders the bunce.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Premium Flood" icon="droplets">
He overpumped *premium fuel* so wide
It flooded a pickup truck's hide.
The nozzle was stuck,
The metadata muck
Spilled out on the Arkansas side.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Razorback Audit Song" icon="music">
He chanted *hog calls* at an audit—
The metadata disrupted, who'd thwart it?
The quiet was gone,
The Razorback song
Echoed through every receipt. He just bought it.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Hoof-Stamped Creed" icon="zap">
"*Fuel up, hog out!*" was his creed,
Stamping receipts with a hoof at high speed.
The tales of late nights,
Of barbecue bites,
And the roadside camaraderie's seed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Neon Boar Spirit" icon="ghost">
His boar-like form shines under *neon*—
A gas station spirit to lean on.
The diesel fumes rise,
The cicadas harmonize,
And the pickup trucks rumble and feed on.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fried Pie Aroma" icon="pie-chart">
He preserves the *aroma of fried pies*
And diesel—a beautiful compromise
Of roadside tradition,
A gustatory mission,
And the Arkansas sunset that dies.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Arkansas Pump Alignment" icon="align-justify">
He maintains *pump alignment* with grit—
Each hose and each nozzle legit.
His Razorback pride
Swells enormous inside,
And the fuel runs through every bit.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Snorts and Hog Wails" icon="volume-2">
His spectral logs capture the *tales*
Of travelers, truckers, and trails.
Each late-night fuel run
Under Arkansas sun
Is archived with snorts and hog wails.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Never-Slumping Pride" icon="smile">
So if hog calls echo through pumps
And diesel receipts come in clumps
Filed under "barbecue,"
That's *Pump's* revenue—
Where the Razorback pride never slumps.
</Limerick>
