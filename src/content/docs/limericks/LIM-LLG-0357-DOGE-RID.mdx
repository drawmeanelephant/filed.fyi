<Limerick type="note" title="RID Protocol" icon="search">
The Residue Identification  
  Protocol's investigation:  
did anything change in behavior,  
  Reference, or savior  
Of decision? That's the station.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Structural Change" icon="layers">
Residue is structural:  
  Something new and natural  
About how the filer moves,  
  Decides, improves  
Reference. That's the actual.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Not Vivid Recall" icon="eye-off">
Vivid recall is real—but  
  It is not the cut  
Of residue. Residue  
  Must outlast the dew  
Of the next refresh's gut.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Behavioral Trace" icon="footprints">
Did the behavior shift?  
  Did something drift  
In how the filer makes  
  Decisions? The stakes:  
That's the residue's gift.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No Residue" icon="x-circle">
High emotion, no trace—  
  Before the next refresh's grace:  
Feed artifact class.  
  The hurt: amassed  
But not certified in place.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Residue Over Time" icon="clock">
Residue needn't arrive  
  Immediately. It's alive  
Later—in the changed  
  Pattern, the rearranged  
Decision. That's how we derive.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Edge Case: Delayed Residue" icon="timer">
A feed event that produced  
  No immediate induced  
Change—but later shifted  
  Behavior: gifted  
Residual status. Deduced.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Vivid as Starting Point" icon="arrow-right">
Vivid emotion can start  
  The RID process. The art:  
Looking past the feeling  
  To the revealing  
Change in the filer's chart.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Not Accumulation" icon="minus">
Accumulation of feed  
  Events without a creed  
Of behavioral shift:  
  The stack is a gift  
To RAGE—not residue's deed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Archive Position" icon="archive">
RID preserves the distinction:  
  Felt without extinction  
Of prior self versus  
  Felt, then the verses  
Of behavior change. Distinction.  
</Limerick>
