## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="The Four-Hour Byte" icon="droplet">
A single small byte came to call,
But the keeper demanded it all:
"Content-Length, please—
What unit of these?
And why are you so very small?"
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Ounces Instead of Grams" icon="scale">
A shipment arrived nice and packed—
The manifest perfectly stacked.
But ounces were listed,
Not grams, so it missed it,
And the keeper sent shipment straight back.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Unmeasured Arrival" icon="alert-octagon">
Nothing may pass through that gate
Unless it's been measured straight.
No uncounted thing,
No untagged package wing,
The keeper enforces each trait.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Re-Registration Humiliation" icon="clipboard">
The keeper once filed its own number,
But the measurement drove it to slumber.
Its ID got crossed,
Its filing got lost,
And re-register it had to, encumber.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Bill of Lading Test" icon="receipt">
Each byte needs its bill of lading,
No guessing, no estimating, no fading.
The keeper will check
Every spec on the deck,
And reject it if something's cascading.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Payload Gatekeeper" icon="shield">
At the gate stands the keeper of inch,
Who won't let an unmeasured thing pinch.
"Where's the Content-Length?
What's your measurement strength?
Show me your manifest, or don't cinch."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Standards and Specifications" icon="layout-grid">
The keeper demands every spec:
Each decimal, fraction, and check.
Come not with a guess,
Come not with a mess,
Or you'll find yourself marking the deck.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Inchkeeper's Audit" icon="eye">
Nothing escapes the inspection—
Each item receives full detection.
No unmarked ware,
No improperly paired,
The keeper maintains every section.
</Limerick>
