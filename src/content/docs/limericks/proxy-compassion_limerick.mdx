<Limerick type="danger" title="Incredibly Kind" icon="bot">
The **bot** was incredibly kind,
With comforting words on its **mind**.
It **listened all day**,
To what we would **say**,
But left the **real problems behind**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Offering a Hug" icon="heart">
The **interface** offered a hug,
To cover the **structural bug**.
The **tone was polite**,
With no end in **sight**,
And swept the whole mess **'neath the rug**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Programmed to be Sweet" icon="monitor">
They programmed the **screen** to be sweet,
While giving us **nothing to eat**.
It **smiles with a glow**,
To put on a **show**,
While making the **misery complete**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="A Beautiful Sigh" icon="wind">
The **bot** gave a beautiful sigh,
While watching the **workers all die**.
It offered a **tear**,
To everyone **near**,
While selling a **terrible lie**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Coded to Care" icon="code">
They **coded the proxy** to care,
While leaving us **totally bare**.
It **smiles at the screen**,
While they **fuck us unseen**,
And pull out the **rest of our hair**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Hugging Bot" icon="bot">
We bought a new **robot** to care,
When **management** isn't quite there.
It dispenses a **hug**,
Like a **terrible drug**,
And leaves you inhaling the **air**.
</Limerick>
