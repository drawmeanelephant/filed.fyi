<Limerick type="note" title="Seven Rounds of Review" icon="repeat">
The maintenance ran through seven  
Rounds of risk review, eleven  
  Signatures deep—  
  Then it went smooth as sleep.  
And that was the problem, apparently.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No User-Visible Error" icon="check">
It completed on time and clean.  
No tickets. No one convened.  
  COMA looked back:  
  "Suspiciously slack.  
Significant change should have keened."  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Model Execution" icon="award">
CAB recorded it thus:  
A model execution. A plus.  
  Continuity review  
  Said: we construe  
Success as a cause for suspicion's fuss.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Insufficiently Event-Shaped" icon="minus">
Both records were filed by archive staff  
Who declined to harmonize the gaffe:  
  One marked it model.  
  One marked it: coddle  
For systems that need a scar's chafe.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Safest When It Fails Slightly" icon="alert-triangle">
A draft doctrine circulates now:  
Approved change is safest somehow  
  When it fails a bit—  
  Slight failure permits  
That something authorized happened. Avow.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Different Handwriting" icon="pen">
No board claims that sentence at all.  
It keeps appearing, in different scrawl,  
  In margins of forms,  
  In advisory norms—  
Nobody's authorship. Free-for-all.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Competence or Concealment" icon="search">
Silence during approved window:  
Was it competence or window-  
  Dressing, concealed?  
  The panel revealed:  
Depends on which calendar you follow.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Minutes of the Third Witness Coffee" icon="coffee">
One hospitality delay's preserved  
As "Minutes of the Third Witness Served  
  Coffee." Nothing  
  Was decided. Something  
About the ritual is observed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Two Interpretations Filed" icon="git-merge">
The archive holds both interpretations  
Without comment, without citations  
  Adjudicating which  
  Is correct. The rich  
Contradiction: both are patient.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Continuity Scar" icon="zap">
Materially significant change  
Should leave a scar in the range  
  Of continuity's view.  
  This left none. The crew  
Says: absence of scar is the strange.  
</Limerick>
