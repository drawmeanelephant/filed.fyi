<Limerick type="danger" title="The Belated Cut" icon="scissors">
We’re cutting the ribbon today,
For a project that’s gone to decay.
The mood has gone home,
But we’re snapping a chrome,
And pretending it isn’t so gray.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Ceremonial Lag" icon="clock">
The launch was a massive mistake,
And the software is completely fake.
But the Ribbon is here,
With an artificial cheer,
While the rest of us quietly ache.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Delayed Energy" icon="zap-off">
The excitement had died in the fall,
But the Ribbon arrived for the ball.
We cheered at the snipping,
While the server was tripping,
And nobody gave a fuck at all.
</Limerick>
