<Limerick type="danger" title="The Belated Cut" icon="scissors">
We’re cutting the ribbon today,  
For a project that’s gone to decay.  
The mood has gone home,  
But we’re snapping a chrome,  
And pretending it isn’t so gray.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Ceremonial Lag" icon="clock">
The launch was a massive mistake,  
And the software is completely fake.  
But the Ribbon is here,  
With an artificial cheer,  
While the rest of us quietly ache.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Delayed Energy" icon="zap-off">
The excitement had died in the fall,  
But the Ribbon arrived for the ball.  
We cheered at the snipping,  
While the server was tripping,  
And nobody gave a fuck at all.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Holding the Bag" icon="clock">
The ribbon experienced lag  
The server was holding the bag  
We waited a year  
With procedural fear  
And printed a meaningless tag  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Lost Smile" icon="frown">
The latency swallowed the file  
We sat at the desk for a while  
The ribbon was red  
The system was dead  
We lost our procedural smile  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Erased Trace" icon="delete">
A delay in the ribbon of grace  
Erased every bit of the trace  
The human was flawed  
The logic outlawed  
The error was taking its place  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Hell" icon="flame">
We measure the latency well  
It feels like a digital hell  
The ribbon is slow  
With nowhere to go  
We’re waiting to hear the old bell  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Totally Wrong" icon="x-circle">
The latency ribbon is long  
The metric is totally wrong  
We wait in the queue  
With nothing to do  
And sing a procedural song  
</Limerick>
