<Limerick type="danger" title="Printing Confidence" icon="printer">
The Mint printed seals on the fly,
To cover a massive-ass lie.
The backing wasn’t there,
Just some hot corporate air,
And a promise to eventually try.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Fresh Seal" icon="check-circle">
We stamp it with absolute pride,
Though we know what is hiding inside.
The legitimacy’s fake,
For appearances' sake,
While the actual assets have died.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Unbacked Currency" icon="dollar-sign">
We’re writing the checks that will bounce,
And weighing the worth by the ounce.
The Mint doesn’t care,
It’s entirely bare,
When the auditor suddenly pounce.
</Limerick>
