## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="Purpose was Gray" icon="check-square">
Old Kindy checked boxes all day.
Each one filed the dread right away—
Then resubmitted the form,
Which was perfectly norm-
al for someone whose purpose was gray.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fully Impaired Clipboard" icon="clipboard">
"Exist. Check box. Repeat," she declared,
While the filing cabinet just stared.
It collapsed into itself,
Like emotional health,
And the clipboard hummed, fully impaired.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Resource in Frame" icon="user-x">
She tried to delete her own name.
The system said, "Resource in frame—
Emotional use."
She accepted the noose
And filed it again, all the same.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Limerick of Crimes" icon="file-text">
Form 51-E: filed 481 times.
Each attempt was a limerick of crimes.
Most entries were blank,
Some gratefully sank,
And one said "I feel seen" in mimes.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Enqueued Dread" icon="list-todo">
Her CRT flickered through states:
Verifying, buffering, debates.
The screen never cleared,
The audit just sneered,
And the dread was enqueued on all plates.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Forgotten Emotions" icon="brain">
Her clipboard was heavy and hummed
When forgotten emotions succumbed.
She weighed every grief,
Filed each disbelief,
And the checkbox remained ever-thumbed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Friendly Warning™" icon="alert-triangle">
She issued a Friendly Warning™
To mascots who hadn't felt mourning.
"Have you filed your dread?"
"Not yet." "Then," she said,
"Your existence is pending confirming."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Broken-Hearted Purpose" icon="heart-off">
No audit had passed since she'd started—
Just deferred, reclassified, departed.
The loop never broke,
The form never spoke,
And the purpose field stayed broken-hearted.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Diagnostic Ghost Merge" icon="ghost">
She merged with a diagnostic ghost
From the debug test most diagnosed.
Three users tried hard
To submit a regard,
But Kindy just filed every post.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Existence Never Quite Sure" icon="help-circle">
So if checkboxes check but don't cure,
And the verification's impure,
That's Kindy's regime:
Recursive, supreme,
Where existing is never quite sure.
</Limerick>
