<Limerick type="danger" title="The Compressed Grief" icon="box">
The sorrow was vastly too much,
To capture with just a soft touch.
They shortened the pain,
In the digital rain,
And used the old notes as a crutch.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Sacredly Incomplete" icon="file-minus">
The record is missing the heart,
Because writing it tore them apart.
The shorthand is brief,
To encapsulate grief,
Like a terrible, minimal art.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Abbreviated Witness" icon="eye">
We saw what the tragedy cost,
But the details were willfully lost.
The Reliquary holds
What the memory folds,
Beneath a thick layer of frost.
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="caution" title="Failed Test" icon="archive">
The shorthand was lost in the chest  
The reliquary failed the big test  
The symbols decayed  
The meaning delayed  
We put all the flat files to rest
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Ancient Design" icon="clock">
A relic of ancient design  
The shorthand forgot every line  
We typed it out wrong  
It took us too long  
The output is doing just fine
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="No Real Sense" icon="help-circle">
The shorthand is perfectly dense  
It doesn’t make any real sense  
The human is weak  
And refuses to speak  
We’re waiting behind a big fence
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Dust and Rust" icon="cloud-drizzle">
The reliquary holds all the dust  
The servers are covered in rust  
We abbreviate death  
With every new breath  
And do what we absolutely must
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Fading Logic" icon="trending-down">
A symbol that means nothing at all  
Was painted right onto the wall  
The archive is gray  
It’s fading away  
The logic is starting to fall
</Limerick>
