<Limerick type="danger" title="The Compressed Grief" icon="box">
The sorrow was vastly too much,
To capture with just a soft touch.
They shortened the pain,
In the digital rain,
And used the old notes as a crutch.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Sacredly Incomplete" icon="file-minus">
The record is missing the heart,
Because writing it tore them apart.
The shorthand is brief,
To encapsulate grief,
Like a terrible, minimal art.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Abbreviated Witness" icon="eye">
We saw what the tragedy cost,
But the details were willfully lost.
The Reliquary holds
What the memory folds,
Beneath a thick layer of frost.
</Limerick>
