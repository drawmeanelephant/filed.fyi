<Limerick type="danger" title="The Fake Break" icon="coffee">
They noticed the need for a **break**,
For everybody's own **sake**.
They **acknowledged the strain**,
But prolonged all the **pain**,
With a **rest** that was totally **fake**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Weeping in a Shroud" icon="cloud-rain">
The feeling of **rest** was allowed,
And spoken about to the **crowd**.
But the **downtime was blocked**,
And the schedule was **locked**,
So the workers just **wept in a shroud**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Tasteful Icon" icon="star">
They gave us a **tasteful new icon**,
To tell us the exhaustion is **gone**.
But the **workload remains**,
Along with the **pains**,
And the bureaucratic bullshit lives **on**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Theoretical Nap" icon="bed-double">
A **theoretical nap** was approved,
But none of the **tickets** were moved.
They validated our **sweat**,
While we're soaking in **debt**,
And our **rectums were properly grooved**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="SOMA and COMA" icon="scale">
**SOMA** permits you to feel,
Like the **rest is materially real**.
But **COMA** says no,
You must **stay on the go**,
While they **fuck us right out of the deal**.
</Limerick>
