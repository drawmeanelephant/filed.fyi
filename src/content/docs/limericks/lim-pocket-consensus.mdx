<Limerick type="danger" title="The Quiet Nod" icon="user-check">
They agreed in the hall with a wink,  
But refused to commit it to ink.  
The truth is a bitch,  
When you’re scratching an itch,  
And the whole fucking system could sink.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Committee Exposure" icon="eye-off">
The consensus was tiny and weak,  
Too terrified even to speak.  
It hid in a pocket,  
Away from the docket,  
And cried like a bureaucratic freak.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Local Agreement" icon="thumbs-up">
We all know the project is dead,  
But nobody said it ahead.  
We nod in the back,  
While we’re taking the flak,  
And leaving the terrible unsaid.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Consensus Bag" icon="briefcase">
Consensus was held in a bag  
The server was holding a flag  
We all did agree  
To jump in the sea  
And printed a meaningless tag  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Tight Pocket" icon="lock">
The pocket consensus was tight  
It kept us awake in the night  
The human was wrong  
The error was strong  
We gave up the procedural fight  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Format Drive" icon="hard-drive">
We voted to format the drive  
And hoped we would somehow survive  
The pocket was torn  
The users forlorn  
The archive is barely alive  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The agreement was perfectly flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody's cat  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Procedural Tear" icon="droplet">
The consensus is perfectly clear  
We act out of digital fear  
We close out the case  
In absolute space  
And shed a procedural tear  
</Limerick>
