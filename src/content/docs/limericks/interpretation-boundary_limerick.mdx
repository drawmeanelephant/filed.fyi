<Limerick type="danger" title="Stopped at the Door" icon="shield">
The feeling was **stopped at the door**,
And couldn't come in **anymore**.
It wasn't quite **neat**,
Or safely **complete**,
To be handled upon the **main floor**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Removed from the Crowd" icon="users-minus">
The **boundary** decides what gets through,
And limits the **data for you**.
If a **signal is loud**,
It's removed from the **crowd**,
So the dashboard **continues its hue**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Blocking the Noise" icon="volume-x">
They filter the **feelings we share**,
To act like they're **totally there**.
They **block out the noise**,
For the **management boys**,
And strip all the **workers quite bare**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Built Like a Wall" icon="minimize-2">
The **boundary** is built like a wall,
To cover the **truth of the fall**.
They **lock out the pain**,
Like a spot on the **brain**,
And leave you with **nothing at all**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Filtered at the Gate" icon="x-octagon">
They filter the **truth at the gate**,
And leave you alone to your **fate**.
They say **you're not clear**,
While they **fuck you right here**,
And tell you it's **running quite great**.
</Limerick>
