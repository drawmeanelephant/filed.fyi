<Limerick type="danger" title="The Comforting Screen" icon="monitor">
They gave us a **comforting screen**,
To make the procedure feel **clean**.
It **softens the blow**,
When the answer is **no**,
And acts like it’s not being **mean**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Soothing Rejection" icon="shield-off">
A **surface** was added to soothe,
And make the **rejection** run smooth.
The language is **kind**,
But the system is **blind**,
And refuses to look at the **truth**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Soft Grace" icon="feather">
They coded the **UI** with care,
To act like they're **totally there**.
But behind the **soft grace**,
They just **spit in your face**,
And strip your **authority bare**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Hug" icon="heart-handshake">
The **prompt** gave a comforting hug,
Like a digital, **empathy drug**.
It tells you **you're seen**,
While they're **picking you clean**,
And **sweeping you under the rug**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Gullible Rube" icon="laugh">
They coated the process in **lube**,
To slide it right down every **tube**.
It whispers "**we care**,"
While it **fucks you** in there,
And plays you for a **gullible rube**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Synthetic Smile" icon="smile">
The **surface** is polished and sweet,
Making **misery** feel like a treat.
The **compassion** is fake,
For the **management's sake**,
While they kick all our **teeth in the street**.
</Limerick>
