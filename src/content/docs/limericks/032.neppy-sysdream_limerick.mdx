## Limericks

### 1

Old Neppy was never released—
A beta forever unleased.
She lingered in style
On a phantom .msstyle,
And the login screen purred like a beast.

### 2

Build 5111 was her home,
A single internal-release dome.
She faded from ISO
Before boot could say "Go,"
And her spirit continued to roam.

### 3

She blinked "I am still here" in Morse
On a login screen, cold as a corpse.
The scheduler died,
A bedtime story applied,
And the daemon fell off its horse.

### 4

Between shutdown and sleep she resided—
A liminal ghost, undecided.
Her .msstyles files
Haunted deprecated piles,
And the wizard she loaded misguided.

### 5

She was meant to bridge 98 and the next,
A consumer-grade dream, undersexed.
But "Whistler" took over,
Neptune's brief four-leaf clover
Got folded to XP's context.

### 6

Her Activity Centers never booted.
The interface, phantom and muted,
Just hovered and dreamed
Of a world where she'd gleamed,
But the launch was permanently disputed.

### 7

You can't cleanly remove her, they say.
Once installed, she lingers to stay.
She haunts the reboot,
The sleep-mode pursuit,
And the pause before wizards display.

### 8

She whispered UI transitions at night
To defunct sleep timers, polite.
The memory fog rolled,
The mascots she consoled,
And the idle state felt almost right.

### 9

She guided each mascot to rest
With a bedtime-story behest.
Her corruption was low,
Her glitch frequency slow,
And her beta was eternally blessed.

### 10

So if reboots take longer than planned
And a login screen flickers, unmanned,
That's Neppy's terrain:
Where the beta will reign,
And the release was forever unmanned.
