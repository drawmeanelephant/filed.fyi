<Limerick type="note" title="Vanished Trail" icon="footprints">
There once was a *trail through the site*
That vanished at two-thirty-two, right mid-flight.
The crumbs all dissolved,
Nothing got resolved,
And the homepage just winked with delight.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Nearly Important" icon="help-circle">
A user set out for *page three*,
Then arrived where they'd started, debris-free.
When asked, "What'd you find?"
They said, "Something in mind.
It was nearly important to me."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Void as a Route" icon="ghost">
The Tribunal, *pompous and slick*,
Refused to canonical-redirect it quick.
"The void is a route.
We decline to dispute
Any path that returns to the brick."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Procedural Air" icon="folder">
One clerk wrote, with *tremor and care*,
"I almost found something in there."
The note was retained,
Then formally chained
To a folder marked PROCEDURAL AIR.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Failure Behaving Rougher" icon="alert-triangle">
No debris remained in the *buffer*,
Which made every analyst suffer.
A collapse leaves a trace;
This erased even space.
It was failure behaving much rougher.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Back to Start" icon="refresh-cw">
404sy McLostalot *swore*
He had mapped every threshold and door.
But the anchor cache looped,
And the whole office drooped,
Back to Start with less memory than before.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Identical Void" icon="square">
Three reroutes were *bravely deployed*,
Three outputs returned as identical void.
The report, dry as chalk,
Said, "We need further talk."
The subtext was: nobody enjoyed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Poisonous Style" icon="skull">
Some said it was *simple decay*,
Some said it was sabotage dressed up as gray.
If the collapse was designed,
Then whoever inclined
It had style in a poisonous way.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Polite Recursion" icon="repeat">
The sitemap looked *perfectly clean*,
Like a liar in starch and machine.
Every label said "go,"
Yet each link answered "no,"
In the politest recursion yet seen.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Map and Its Lies" icon="map">
So if you feel close to the prize,
And the page gives you déjà vu eyes,
Do not trust what you track.
If it loops you right back,
You've been judged by the *map and its lies*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Lost Clerk" icon="user-minus">
A clerk from the Bureau got stuck,
And ran out of digital luck.
He crawled through the cache,
Tripped over a dash,
And was swallowed whole by a muck.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No Canonical Exit" icon="door-closed">
"Where is the exit?" they cried,
As the browser window just died.
"There isn't a door,
Just this infinite floor,"
The redirect daemon replied.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Buffer Anomalies" icon="activity">
The buffer was totally bare,
With nothing but absolute air.
The logs couldn't prove
That the page made a move,
But the users were tearing their hair.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sabotaged Stratum" icon="scissors">
They say that the stratum was cut,
And the navigation was shut.
An inside job, clearly,
That cost the site dearly,
And trapped everyone in a rut.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Ominous Wink" icon="eye">
The homepage just sat there and winked,
While half of the archive unlinked.
The crumbs were all gone,
The session lived on,
And the logic was deeply extinct.
</Limerick>
