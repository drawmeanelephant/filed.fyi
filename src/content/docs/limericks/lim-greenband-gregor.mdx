<Limerick type="note" title="The Safe Range" icon="check-square">
Gregor adjusts the green band,  
So everything goes as we planned.  
The strain is extreme,  
But the metrics all gleam,  
In a visually comforting land.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sustained Strain" icon="activity">
The system is ready to snap,  
But Gregor expands the safe gap.  
The threshold is wide,  
So the errors can hide,  
In a soft-success graphical trap.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Acceptable Failure" icon="alert-triangle">
If the line turns to yellow or red,  
We'd have to admit what we dread.  
So Gregor paints green  
On the edge of the screen,  
And we call it a victory instead.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="note" title="Wearing Green" icon="user">
Old Gregor was wearing the green  
And staring completely unseen  
The server was down  
He put on a frown  
And polished the old broken screen  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Painfully Bright" icon="sun">
The greenband was terribly tight  
It kept him awake in the night  
The logic was flawed  
The error outlawed  
The failure was painfully bright  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tripped on Wire" icon="zap">
We sent Gregor into the fog  
To fetch us a meaningless log  
He tripped on a wire  
And fell in the fire  
And acted like somebody's dog  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Symbol of Naught" icon="circle">
The band is a symbol of naught  
The data was totally caught  
In loops without end  
We cannot defend  
The terrible things we were taught  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Perfectly Full" icon="archive">
Old Gregor is terribly dull  
The system is perfectly null  
The human is weak  
And refuses to speak  
The vacuum is perfectly full  
</Limerick>
