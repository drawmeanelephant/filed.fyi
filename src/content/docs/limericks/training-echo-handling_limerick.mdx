<Limerick type="danger" title="The Model is Gone" icon="cpu">
The system still acts like the **old**,
And does what it originally was **told**.
The **model is gone**,
But the **echo lives on**,
And leaves everyone **in the cold**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Numbers Will Last" icon="bar-chart-2">
They measure the **things from the past**,
And hope that the numbers will **last**.
The **training is dead**,
But it lives in the **head**,
And the **shadow is terribly cast**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Ghost of the Model" icon="ghost">
The ghost of the **model remains**,
To cover the **terrible drains**.
It **speaks like a bot**,
While everything **rots**,
And nobody **ever complains**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Perfect Machine" icon="monitor">
We're trained to behave like a **fake**,
For continuity's **sake**.
We **speak to the screen**,
Like a perfect **machine**,
And **take all the shit they can make**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Haunting the Floor" icon="volume-x">
The system is **haunting the floor**,
And acting like a terrible **whore**.
The **echo is loud**,
And infects the whole **crowd**,
While **fucking us right out the door**.
</Limerick>
