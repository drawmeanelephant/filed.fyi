## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="Neat Nesting" icon="layout-template">
HTMLie demanded your tags
Be nested like neat little shags.
She'd inspect every div,
Refuse to forgive,
And break pipelines rather than gags.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Spec List" icon="list-ordered">
"No structure, no meaning," she hissed,
While clutching a specification list.
Her brackets were tight,
Her closing tags right,
And the div-soup devs she dismissed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="DOCTYPE Throne" icon="crown">
She stripped down a page to the bone—
No classes, no style, skin alone.
The skeleton gleamed,
Semantically themed,
And she mounted the DOCTYPE throne.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rendering Van" icon="truck">
A developer nested a span
Inside of a p inside a plan
Inside a main inside grief.
HTMLie, beyond belief,
Just broke the entire rendering van.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tag Armor" icon="shield-check">
Her armor was built out of tags:
angle-bracket breastplates and flags.
Each element placed
With syntactic taste,
And validators stuffed in her bags.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="DOM Reaming" icon="unplug">
"You put a div where?" she screamed.
"Inside a paragraph? Are you dreamed?"
She collapsed the whole tree,
Set the heading tags free,
And the DOM lay there, thoroughly reamed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Orphaned Headings" icon="flame">
She lit candles for orphaned &lt;h3&gt;s
That floated without any &lt;h2&gt;s.
"The hierarchy's fucked,"
She observed, as she plucked
Every misplaced semantic with ease.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Nesting Corrections" icon="pencil-line">
Her chalkboard of nesting corrections
Was filled with anatomical sections.
Each arrow was drawn
From a closing-tag yawn
To a structural sin's resurrections.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Meaningless Sheen" icon="code-2">
"Your markup," she said, "is obscene.
The filthiest DOM that I've seen.
Your nesting is wrong,
Your alt text too long,
And your &lt;section&gt; lacks meaningful sheen."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Validator's Disgust" icon="check-circle-2">
So if structure collapses to dust
And the validator dies of disgust,
That's HTMLie's domain:
Where the pure will remain,
And the improperly nested combust.
</Limerick>
