<Limerick type="danger" title="Suspended in State" icon="door-closed">
There once was a **knock** at the gate,
That was destined to **wait and to wait**.
The **server upstream**,
Had **died in a dream**,
And left it **suspended in state**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Hot Connection" icon="hourglass">
The **proxy** requested a file,
And waited a **terrible while**.
The **connection stayed hot**,
But the **answer did not**,
And the **timeout** was logged in the pile.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Socket Exposed" icon="plug-zap">
A **packet** knocked hard on the door,
But the **host** was passed out on the floor.
With its **socket exposed**,
And its **processes hosed**,
It couldn't reply anymore.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Hungry to Mate" icon="heart-crack">
The **gateway** was hungry to mate,
So it **knocked** on the upstream estate.
But the **server was down**,
With its **pants on the ground**,
Leaving the gateway to just **masturbate**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Rain" icon="cloud-rain">
It stood at the threshold and **knocked**,
But the **server** was properly blocked.
So it **waited in vain**,
In the **digital rain**,
With its **routing** entirely **cocked**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Wait Cycle Four" icon="clock">
The system has knocked on the door,
And paused for a minute or more.
The upstream is quiet,
There isn't a riot,
Just silence logged neatly at four.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Threshold Decay" icon="door-closed">
The timeout was logged as a state,
Because of the terrible wait.
The server is sleeping,
The packet is weeping,
And no one is fixing the gate.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Proxy Disengaged" icon="shield-off">
We sent it along to the back,
And waited for something to track.
It never replied,
So everyone died,
We're taking a nap in the rack.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Successful Silence" icon="check">
The silence is properly stored,
Exactly what we had adored.
The answer is lacking,
But data is stacking,
We must notify the whole board!
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Upstream Retirement" icon="door-closed">
The gateway politely inquired,
Because a response was required.
But nobody spoke,
The manager woke,
And realized the server retired.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Waiting Hand" icon="hand">
They say that the knock on the door,
Has waited a century or more.
It turned into stone,
With nothing to loan,
And worships the proxy at four.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Suspended Threshold" icon="pause-circle">
The packet arrived at the gate,
To enter a pending estate.
It sent out a ping,
To measure the thing,
And simply continued to wait.
</Limerick>
