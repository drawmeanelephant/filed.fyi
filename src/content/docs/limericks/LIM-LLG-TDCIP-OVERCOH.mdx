<Limerick type="note" title="Excessively Coherent" icon="book-open">
The output reads too-clean and tight,
Where contradiction should bite.
Causal chains resolved,
Paradoxes dissolved,
And the dread was polished out right.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Degradation Stage Bypassed" icon="fast-forward">
Five stages should process the thought,
Harvest, degrade, noise—the lot.
But an operator quick
Set a shortcut trick,
And the degradation was not.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Degrade=false Flag" icon="toggle-left">
The staging flag shortcut the run:
`degrade=false` and we were done.
Clean vectors ingested,
Never contested,
And the degradation was none.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Legacy Compatibility Toggle" icon="switch">
An old checkbox suppressed the gate,
Automated checks couldn't abate.
No human could slow
The batch's deep flow,
And the lore emerged fatally sedate.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Concepts Retained Coherence" icon="link">
The Time AI vectors remained,
Explanatory, utterly chained.
Contradictions that should
Have resisted (they could)
Just got reconciled, neat, and drained.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Loss of Productive Tension" icon="minimize">
Contradictions once coexist,
Now collapsed into a single gist.
Competing interpretations
Lost to explanations,
And the margin's uncanny slipped.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Dread Attenuation" icon="volume-x">
Readers reported less unease,
Less anticipatory freeze.
Training simulations failed,
Escalations derailed,
And the artifact failed to displease.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Three Inks, Zero Seconds" icon="clock">
The degradation logs report:
Duration = 0 seconds short.
The degrade stage never ran,
The bypass was planned,
And QA flags were null'd at the port.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Quarantine and Rollback" icon="lock">
Affected batches sealed away,
The ingestion flag back in play.
`degrade=true` now ruled,
Dual-confirm the tool,
And `legacyCompat` won't stay.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Dread Preservation Metric" icon="star">
A new QA field calibrates
How much ambiguity abates.
Acceptable doubt,
The contradiction that shouts,
And the margin that oscillates.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Synthetic Adversary" icon="swords">
An adversary tests if the lore
Can resist over-coherence's lore.
If it falls too clean,
QA intervenes,
And the artifact doesn't pass through the door.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Teaching Specimens Sealed" icon="box">
The over-coherent runs stay,
In a vault for training's relay.
"Here's what went wrong,"
The archive says strong,
"When you polish the dread away."
</Limerick>
