<Limerick type="danger" title="Counting the Absent" icon="users">
The member has left us for good,
But we’re counting him here as we should.
The Clerk marks him down,
With a terrible frown,
And keeps the quorum in the wood.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Remembered Casserole" icon="coffee">
She brought us a dish in the fall,
So we count her right here in the hall.
The ghost is assigned,
To the corporate grind,
And nobody questions the call.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Memorial Quorum" icon="file-text">
The bodies are absent and cold,
But the ledger is beautifully bold.
We vote in the name
Of the people who came,
And do what we’re officially told.
</Limerick>
