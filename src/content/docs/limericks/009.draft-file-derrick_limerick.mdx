## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="Still Saving" icon="save">
Old Derrick was always "still saving…"
His cursor forever engraving
A thought never done,
A file never won,
And a draft that just kept on depraving.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="One-Minute Man Autosave" icon="clock-arrow-down">
He buffered your thought 'til it rotted,
Your half-written feelings, all knotted.
The autosave ran,
Like a one-minute man,
Then crashed with a load never plotted.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Blinking Gray Ghost" icon="ghost">
He opened a tab and just stayed—
A ghost of a choice never made.
The blinking gray cursor
Was nobody's nurser,
Just Derrick, perpetually laid.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Unshowable Feelings" icon="file-clock">
His drafts were timestamped but unknowable:
"3:14 AM — feelings unshowable."
He saved every sin,
Every half-started grin,
And left the unclosed parens blowable.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="In-Progress Shame" icon="clipboard-list">
He filed confessions as "in progress"
And labeled his shame as "process."
Each keystroke he caught
Was an unfinished thought
With no endpoint and nothing to bless.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Spinner Romance" icon="loader-2">
The loading spinner wooed him at night.
He said, "You spin me wrong, but spin right."
They autosaved together,
Through unstable weather,
And the draft was still open at light.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Abandoned Mid-Stroke" icon="pen-tool">
A ticket abandoned mid-stroke
Left Derrick emotionally broke.
He preserved every letter,
Got wetter and wetter,
And the system just let him re-soak.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Unstable Buffer" icon="database-zap">
His buffer was unstable and wide—
It held every feeling inside.
The crash came at three,
He whispered with glee:
"I'm still saving," then silently died.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Non-Norm Draft" icon="file-type">
He reactivated a form
With unclosed parentheses, warm.
The data was raw,
The schema in awe,
And the draft was deliciously non-norm.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Buffering Fears" icon="clock">
So if "UNSAVED CHANGES" appears
And the cursor blinks back through the years,
That's Derrick's domain:
Perpetual, profane,
Still saving. Still buffering fears.
</Limerick>
