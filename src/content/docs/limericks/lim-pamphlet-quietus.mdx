<Limerick type="danger" title="Brochure-Grade Peace" icon="book-open">
The server was violently on fire,
But the Pamphlet was meant to inspire.
It told us to breathe,
While we practically seethe,
And the company sinks in the mire.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Informational Closure" icon="file-text">
They handed a tri-fold around,
To cover the terrifying sound.
"The situation’s calm,"
Says the printed-ass psalm,
While the bodies are hitting the ground.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Printed Calm" icon="printer">
If the data has all been destroyed,
Just read the new brochure, you void.
The font is quite nice,
So it ought to suffice,
While the lawyers are fully employed.
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Buried Beneath" icon="arrow-down">
The pamphlet demanded a death  
With every procedural breath  
The pages were white  
We turned off the light  
And buried the data beneath
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Flat Serene" icon="minus">
The quietus is flat and serene  
It never arrived on the screen  
The error was logged  
The system was bogged  
The archive is empty and lean
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Booklet of Doom" icon="book">
We printed a booklet of doom  
And placed it inside of a room  
The human is weak  
And refuses to speak  
We’re waiting inside of a tomb
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Procedural Bill" icon="file-text">
A pamphlet of quiet and still  
It gives you a terrible chill  
You open the file  
And wait for a while  
And pay the procedural bill
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Pamphlet is Old" icon="clock">
The quietus was totally null  
The system was boring and dull  
The pamphlet is old  
The data is sold  
The vacuum is perfectly full
</Limerick>
