<Limerick type="note" title="Tap Tap on the Glass" icon="window">
A finch tapped the *window* with care—
The wrong pane was sitting right there.
It arrived right on time,
At a place not sublime,
And nobody wondered its dare.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Faithfully Wrong" icon="navigation-2">

But Finch had no way to compare.

And chirped like it should have been where.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Misdirected With Confidence" icon="send">

Yet Finch sang its landing in glee.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Wrong Virtual Host" icon="globe">

But Finch didn't mind,
Left routes unreassigned,
And landed completely astray.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Nesting in Wrong Rules" icon="home">
A nest was built into the code—

And Finch was the glitch-happy load.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cheerful Misdirection" icon="smile">

And lands where it shouldn't at night.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Misdirected Arrival" icon="arrow-down">

And nobody learned it was so.
</Limerick>

</Limerick>
