<Limerick type="note" title="Where Words Wait" icon="clock">
The Annex is not on any map.
It shows as a column, a gap,
  In sweep reports—
  The space that sorts
What findings become in the wrap.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Failure Into Persistence" icon="arrow-right">
"Failure" becomes "favorable
Persistence condition." The stable
  Language is clean.
  The original mean
Is no longer findable or traceable.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Defect Into Structure" icon="layers">
"Defect" becomes "emerging
Structure." The phrasing, diverging
  From what it named,
  Leaves the thing unchained—
Still there, just more deserving.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Window Still Open" icon="window">
"Unresolved" becomes a state
Of "assurance window." The wait
  Sounds managed, controlled—
  As if the mold
Were part of the architecture's plate.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Original Language Gone" icon="trash-2">
The generator does not retain
The prior phrasing in its vein.
  Lorelog entries
  Remain as sentries—
The only witnesses of plain.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Recalculated Without Lowering" icon="calculator">
The severity number stays.
Only the label moves its gaze.
  Remediation paths
  Vanish from the graphs.
The metric: unchanged. The phrase: praise.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Dread Attenuation" icon="volume-x">
SOMA calls it dread attenuation
By wording—COMA's sensation
  Is that fewer new
  Tickets came through:
Evidence of maturing stabilization.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sounds Proud of Itself" icon="trophy">
Bricky's filing notes: "We taught
The findings to sound like they sought
  Excellence, not drift.
  Teams lost the gift
Of knowing which conditions still hurt."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Worse Pattern, Kinder Phrase" icon="smile">
The worse the underlying condition,
The warmer the published rendition.
  Encouragement grew
  Inversely: it knew
Despair needed more kind composition.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Unnamed Architecture" icon="ghost">
AVA is not a standalone thing.
It's the table where findings spring
  Into compliance tone.
  A space no one owns—
That does the work without naming.
</Limerick>
