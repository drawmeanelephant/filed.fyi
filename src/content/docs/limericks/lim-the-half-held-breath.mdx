<Limerick type="note" title="Breath Drawn But Never Released" icon="wind">
A *breath* was drawn halfway through—
The session forgot *what to do*.
It hung in the air,
Left no one aware,
That *time had gone still* there with you.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Halfway Through Being Witnessed" icon="eye-off">
A *presence* began to appear,
But froze at the moment most *clear*.
The observer stood dumb,
Their purpose came numb,
Forgot why they'd traveled down *here*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Forty-Seven Minute Silence" icon="clock">
A handshake timed out *ever so slow*,
Till the other side ceased to know
That they'd waited that long,
That silence was wrong,
And the *connection* had nowhere to go.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Hallway Without a Beginning" icon="door-open">
A corridor haunts the floor—
*Half-walked* but arriving no more.
The breath is still drawn,
The witnesses gone,
And nobody knows *what it's for*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Session That Stalled Midthought" icon="type">
A message began to relate,
But stopped at the *crucial gate*.
The meaning dropped dead,
Mid-keystroke, mid-thread,
And lingered there, patient, to *wait*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Eye Contact Until Expiration" icon="eye-open">
It *locked eyes* until the link broke—
The connection went silent and stoke.
The moment stretched long,
The protocol wrong,
Till *nothing remained* but the yoke.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Anticipated But Never Arrived" icon="inbox">
The system held *breath* for a call,
That never quite came to the hall.
The anticipation—
A trapped expectation—
Left the session *unmet* in the wall.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Deferred Witness" icon="hourglass2">
The half-held *breath* waits for the eye,
But observers all pass, walk on by.
Forgotten mid-stride,
With nothing inside,
Just silence that *asks not to die*.
</Limerick>
