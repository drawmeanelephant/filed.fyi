import Limerick from '../../../components/Limericks.astro';



<Limerick type="note" title="Invisible Tasks" icon="ghost">
The policy dropped certain asks.
The template kept wearing the masks.
So reviewers would stare
At blank boxes still there
Like they'd failed some invisible tasks.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Obligation Implied" icon="check-square">
Decorative fields should have died.
Instead they just lingered beside
Real questions, until
Every old little fill
Became obligation implied.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Hate Leaving White" icon="square">
No one meant "Please answer this ghost."
But the empty square did the most.
People hate leaving white
On an official-looking site,
So they fed dead requirements a roast.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Box Still Extorts" icon="alert-triangle">
Legacy labels can reign
Long after the meaning's been slain.
That is one nasty trick:
You remove the law quick,
But the box still extorts with its frame.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Moral and Clerical Cape" icon="shirt">
Reviewers grew used to the shape.
Which is how bad expectations take.
If a field once existed,
It somehow persisted
As a moral and clerical cape.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Hidden Duties" icon="eye-off">
The drift was not loud or obscene.
It sat there, bureaucratic and clean.
Just an old empty square
With enough visual air
To imply hidden duties between.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Respect Presentation" icon="layout">
Undocumented obligation
Is one of my favorite damn nations:
No statute, no rule,
Just a box making fools
Of all who respect presentation.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Obeyed When Framed" icon="frame">
One filer wrote, "N/A, see note."
Another just panicked and wrote
A long answer to dust
Because emptiness must
Be obeyed when it's framed in a coat.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fuck Your Reforms" icon="refresh-cw">
The team said, "We simplified forms."
The template said, "Fuck your reforms."
Then smiled from the page
As an obsolete cage
Kept dictating the old little norms.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Authority Over the Years" icon="history">
So if a dead field still appears,
Do not feed it your labor or tears.
Some boxes survive
Past the law that kept live
Their authority over the years.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Residual Obligation Signal" icon="radio">
The schema withdrew. The blank stayed.
No reviewer was ever dismayed
   Until someone looked close
   And the ghost of its host
Kept nudging their pencil, afraid.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Stabilized Absence Artifact" icon="archive">
Under managed absence, the law
Says empty boxes aren't a flaw.
   They're historical shards,
   Stabilized regards
For a policy nobody saw.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Begging Square" icon="square-plus">
The empty square begged for a fill,
With a recursive and procedural will.
   It swallowed the "no,"
   And refused to let go,
Until logic was perfectly still.
</Limerick>
