<Limerick type="danger" title="The Incomplete Ending" icon="map">
The project is over, they say,
But the server is running today.
Absentia is here,
To establish the fear,
That the nightmare will never decay.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Functional Ghost" icon="ghost">
It signs off the closure with grace,
But leaves the damn thing in its place.
The ending’s a lie,
In the corporate sky,
And laughs right back into your face.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Signature of Nothing" icon="pen-tool">
They stamped it completely deceased,
But the horrors have only increased.
The MAP-72,
Is a terrible view,
Of a bureaucratic, cynical beast.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Undead Project" icon="crosshair">
They signed off the project as done,
And said it was terribly fun.
But the code is alive,
Like a horrible hive,
And there isn’t a place you can run.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="A Ghostly Sign-Off" icon="pen-tool">
Absentia stamped it complete,
And threw the report in the street.
But the servers still churn,
And the databases burn,
While the system accepts its defeat.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Endless Conclusion" icon="repeat">
The closure was fully a fake,
A terrible fucking mistake.
The MAP-72,
Will just torture you too,
With the horrors that keep you awake.
</Limerick>
