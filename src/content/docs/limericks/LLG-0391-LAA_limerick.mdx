<Limerick type="note" title="Approved at Lunch" icon="badge-dollar-sign">
At luncheon they clapped for the plan,
And someone wrote "done" with a hand.
No motion. No sum.
Just supportive warm hum.
Then reimbursements came in like a band.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Paper Plate Consent" icon="plate">
The guidance got blessedly blunt:
Don't confuse civic warmth with a grant.
If they're nodding mid-bite
With dry chicken in sight,
That is not legal consent over lunch.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Reception Summary Drift" icon="arrow-down-up">
The clerk had two fields side by side:
One for mood, one for what was applied.
So "the room loved the pitch"
Became budgetary switch,
And the ledger developed a stride.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fiscally Void" icon="x-circle">
Lionell, syrup-marked papers in tow,
Said applause is not money, although
It can help people cope
And inflate civic hope.
Just don't call it authorization flow.
</Limerick>
