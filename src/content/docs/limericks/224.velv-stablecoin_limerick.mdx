<Limerick type="note" title="Frictionless Bluffing" icon="circle">
Velv is a *blob, pegged to mood*,
A stablecoin softly imbued
With emotional nothing.
Its frictionless bluffing
Is accepted wherever construed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sentiment Haze" icon="cloud">
It doesn't respond to your *praise*
Or criticism. It simply stays—
A liquidity-backed,
Nonverbal abstract
Of corporate sentiment haze.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fiscal Senses" icon="zap">
It glows in response to *consensus*,
Each shimmer emotional expenses.
But sadness prevents
Any spending events—
The blob has its own fiscal senses.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Empathy Bribes" icon="heart">
Issued to employees for *morale*,
A compensation that's not real at all.
The quarterly vibes,
The empathy bribes,
And the soft launch's emotional crawl.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Corporate Lanyard Affair" icon="badge">
"*Velv is accepted everywhere,*
But never truly seen"—the despair
Of a presence that floats
Through ESG notes,
And the corporate lanyard affair.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Emotional Blaze" icon="flame">
It appears on *Slide 12*—always—
Even if Slide 12 is in a haze
Of redaction. It's there.
In the compulsory air
Of the all-hands emotional blaze.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Recalibration Must" icon="refresh-cw">
Printed on *corporate lanyards* as trust
Talismans, gathering dust.
The snack packs include it
For meetings, to soothe it—
Emotional recalibration's a must.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Quarterly Woe Absorption" icon="bar-chart">
It is *not a currency.* No, no.
It is a presence. A glow.
A sentiment-linked
Nonverbal, succinct
Absorption of quarterly woe.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Softness Embodiment" icon="sparkles">
It absorbs *volatility* with ease,
Emitting softness as you please.
The market sentiment
Feeds its embodiment,
And the conference accepts all its fees.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Comforting Bloom" icon="flower">
So if softness pervades every room
And the blob is a comforting bloom,
That's *Velv* at its best:
Where emotional rest
Is stablecoin-pegged to your gloom.
</Limerick>
