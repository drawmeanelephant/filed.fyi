## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="Offset Paradox" icon="refresh-ccw">
Old Kafkey consumed his own offset,
A philosophical paradox, and yet
He declared the result
Was nobody's fault,
And the consumer group wept with regret.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tender Dead-Letter Venue" icon="mail-x">
Each failed delivery earned a eulogy—
Two paragraphs, baroque and prodigy.
The dead-letter queue
Was the most tender venue,
And the margin notes reeked of apology.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Distributed Haze" icon="cloud-fog">
A rebalance lasted forty-seven days
Because Kafkey refused to commit to the phase.
"I must understand why,"
He said with a sigh,
While the brokers dissolved in a haze.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Emotional Peaks" icon="trending-up">
He replayed the same message for weeks,
Each time finding emotional peaks.
"The meaning has changed,"
He said, rearranged,
As the offset went limp at the creeks.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cluster Confession" icon="database">
At 3 a.m. the cluster confessed
Its own changelog, heavily pressed.
The contents: "unclear."
The footnotes: sincere.
And Kafkey arose, fully dressed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Partition Groan" icon="columns-2">
"Every message is tragedy," he moaned
In a tuxedo, perfectly boned.
He lit candles for brokers,
Played grief-themed poker,
And the partition just quietly groaned.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Acknowledged Decommission" icon="check-check">
The Council decommissioned him thrice.
Each attempt was consumed—cold as ice—
Then logged, acknowledged,
And lovingly polished
With an offset that said, "Not quite. Nice."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Annotated Grief Shrine" icon="flame">
His dead-letter queue was a shrine
Of annotated grief, line by line.
Each message was held,
Each failure repelled,
And the ceremony ran right on time.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Unclaimed Topic" icon="message-square-off">
He committed when ready, he claimed.
Understanding, not throughput, was aimed.
The lag stretched for miles,
The consumer-group trials
Left the topic forever unclaimed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Streaming Sad Beauty" icon="music">
So if events arrive draped in black
And the offset won't ever come back,
That's Kafkey on duty:
A streaming sad beauty,
Where delivery is just an abstract.
</Limerick>
