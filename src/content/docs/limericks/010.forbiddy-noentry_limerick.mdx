## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="Universal No" icon="octagon-x">
Forbiddy said, "No," to the door,
Then "No" to the window and floor.
She denied the admin,
The crawler, the has-been,
And the password she'd heard twice before.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tightened Velvet Rope" icon="lock">
"Even if you knew it," she sighed,
"The password would still be denied."
She crossed off your name,
Filed it under "no shame,"
And the velvet rope tightened with pride.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Surgical Praxis" icon="stethoscope">
An admin came begging for access.
She reviewed him with surgical praxis.
His credentials were fine,
His timestamp in line,
But his comma was grounds for a tax-miss.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Procedural Creed" icon="scroll">
She denied her own docs for eleven
Long days— it felt closer to heaven.
No one could read
Her procedural creed,
And the 403 count hit three-seven.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Circular Logic Loop" icon="repeat">
Form 403-A: "Request for appeal."
Requires Form 403-A to unseal.
She smiled at the loop,
Filed herself through the hoop,
And the circular logic was real.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Daemon Denial" icon="bot-off">
A monitoring daemon came through
To check if Forbiddy was true.
She said, "Are you alive?"
"403," came the jive.
"So… yes?" "No. And fuck you too."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Bureaucratic Scroll" icon="list-x">
She maintained a list, sorted with care,
Of people who hadn't yet dared.
They were pre-denied, whole,
On a bureaucratic scroll,
For the crime of potentially being there.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Denial Zest" icon="shield-ban">
The Council said, "Give us your reason."
She said, "That sounds like treason."
They filed the request,
She denied it with zest,
And the logs were 403 every season.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Strict Queen Firewall" icon="shield-alert">
She guarded the gate like a queen:
The strictest firewall you'd seen.
She'd block every thrust,
Deny every lust,
And the access remained squeaky clean.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Permission Sin" icon="key-round">
So if nothing you do grants you in,
And the clipboard has crossed out your kin,
That's Forbiddy's domain:
Where "no" is the reign,
And permission was always a sin.
</Limerick>
