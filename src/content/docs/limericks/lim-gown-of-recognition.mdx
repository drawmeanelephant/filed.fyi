<Limerick type="danger" title="The Cheap Suit of Honor" icon="award">
They draped you in fabric so fine,
And told you to stand in the line.
The Gown is a joke,
For the regular folk,
And you’re paying the horrible fine.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Honorary Visibility" icon="eye">
You worked in the dark for a year,
And now you’re officially here.
The Recognition shines,
In the worst of designs,
While nobody buys you a beer.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Draped Acknowledgment" icon="star">
It covers your terrible clothes,
To hide all the misery and woes.
They gave you a gown,
In this miserable town,
And struck a magnificent pose.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Polyester Prize" icon="gift">
You’re sweating to death in the gown,
The absolute talk of the town.
It’s made of pure trash,
In a horrible crash,
As the corporate ladder breaks down.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Compensated in Fabric" icon="dollar-sign">
They couldn't afford a real raise,
So they gave you some wearable praise.
You look like a prick,
And it makes you quite sick,
For the rest of your miserable days.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="A Shitty Reward" icon="award">
You gave them the best of your youth,
And they dressed you right up in a booth.
The Gown is a sham,
For a structural scam,
And nobody mentions the truth.
</Limerick>
