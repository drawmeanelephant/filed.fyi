<Limerick type="danger" title="The Premature Joy" icon="smile">
They threw all the paper around,
When the final compliance was found.
The system is trash,
And we’re losing our cash,
But the confetti is covering the ground.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Decorative Closure" icon="check">
The audit was totally faked,
And the auditors clearly were baked.
But we popped some champagne,
In the pouring cold rain,
While the actual database quaked.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Compliant Finishes" icon="award">
It sparkles and shines in the air,
To hide all the rot and despair.
The audit is done,
And it wasn’t much fun,
But the Confetti tells us to care.
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="caution" title="Barely Unbound" icon="unlock">
We threw the old audit around  
And watched it all fall to the ground  
Confetti of rules  
Made by digital fools  
The server was barely unbound
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="System Breaks" icon="alert-triangle">
An audit of colorful flakes  
Is causing a bunch of mistakes  
We party and dance  
Without any pants  
The system continuously breaks
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Painfully Near" icon="map-pin">
We celebrate failure with cheer  
And wait for the end of the year  
The paper is torn  
The human forlorn  
The error is painfully near
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Procedural Fogs" icon="cloud">
Confetti was made out of logs  
And eaten by digital dogs  
The party is dead  
The manual read  
We’re lost in the procedural fogs
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Vacuum Full" icon="minus">
The audit is perfectly null  
The party is terribly dull  
We stamp it approved  
The data removed  
The vacuum is perfectly full
</Limerick>
