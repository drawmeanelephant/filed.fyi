<Limerick type="danger" title="The Premature Joy" icon="smile">
They threw all the paper around,
When the final compliance was found.
The system is trash,
And we’re losing our cash,
But the confetti is covering the ground.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Decorative Closure" icon="check">
The audit was totally faked,
And the auditors clearly were baked.
But we popped some champagne,
In the pouring cold rain,
While the actual database quaked.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Compliant Finishes" icon="award">
It sparkles and shines in the air,
To hide all the rot and despair.
The audit is done,
And it wasn’t much fun,
But the Confetti tells us to care.
</Limerick>
