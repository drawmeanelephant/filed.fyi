<Limerick type="note" title="The Dignified Carry" icon="briefcase">
The Porter takes artifacts down,
With a solemn, historical frown.
They aren't quite removed,
Just gently ungrooved,
And shipped to the ghost of the town.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Managed Absence" icon="box">
It isn't deleted at all,
Just held in a negative hall.
The Porter ensures
That the echo endures,
Long after the structural fall.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Contextual History" icon="book">
The failures are heavy to bear,
But the Porter arranges them there.
Deprecated yet prime,
They are frozen in time,
With a sad, institutional stare.
</Limerick>
