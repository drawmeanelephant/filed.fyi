<Limerick type="note" title="The Dignified Carry" icon="briefcase">
The Porter takes artifacts down,
With a solemn, historical frown.
They aren't quite removed,
Just gently ungrooved,
And shipped to the ghost of the town.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Managed Absence" icon="box">
It isn't deleted at all,
Just held in a negative hall.
The Porter ensures
That the echo endures,
Long after the structural fall.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Contextual History" icon="book">
The failures are heavy to bear,
But the Porter arranges them there.
Deprecated yet prime,
They are frozen in time,
With a sad, institutional stare.
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Broken String" icon="link-2">
The porter forgot what to bring  
The server had broken a string  
We waited outside  
The manual lied  
The error is taking a swing
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Perfectly Toast" icon="flame">
The LCGU is a ghost  
The porter abandoned his post  
We printed the file  
And wait for a while  
The system is perfectly toast
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Terrible Chill" icon="thermometer-snow">
A porter that carries the nil  
It gives you a terrible chill  
The data is lost  
We don't know the cost  
And pay the procedural bill
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Baggage Cold" icon="archive">
The baggage is empty and cold  
The manual dusty and old  
We log the event  
Without any intent  
The error is finally sold
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Stone" icon="minus">

</Limerick>
