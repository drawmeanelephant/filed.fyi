<Limerick type="note" title="First Contact Screening" icon="clipboard">
The intake clerk's first question is plain:
Where did the experience gain
  Its initial contact?
  Body, place — intact?
Or did the algorithm arrange?
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Rubric" icon="ruler">
Three tests, applied in order:
Origin, Agency, Residue. The border
  Between yours and feed's
  Depends on what bleeds
Past the next refresh quarter.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Platform Affordances" icon="thumbs-up">
Likes and replies count—but low.
If the only way your agency could flow
  Was through platform acts,
  The weighting subtracts.
You engaged. You couldn't go.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Structural Change" icon="layers">
Residue is structural change:
Behavior shifts, reference range,
  Decision-making revised.
  Vivid recall, disguised
As residue, is not within range.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Edge Case: Local Anchor" icon="anchor">
If the anger began in your room
And the platform arrived to assume
  Part of the cause—
  The local anchor draws
DOGE into partial bloom.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Simulator Classes" icon="cpu">
Failed experiences aren't deleted.
They're reclassified and greeted
  By simulator rules:
  Kept—with different tools
Of evidence. Their history: seated.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="DOGE Aftercare" icon="heart">
After certification, the case
Moves into aftercare's space:
  What remains of
  The lived experience—of
The provision and its embrace?
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Feelings Are Valid" icon="check-square">
DOGE's working gloss stays clear:
The feelings are valid and here.
  The situation may be
  Rented. The key
Is what was genuinely near.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Only Power Is Annotation" icon="pen-tool">
DOGE cannot compel platforms' flow.
It can only annotate what we know:
  "This was yours."
  "This one endures
Only as graph." That's the show.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="What a Life Contained" icon="book">
DOGE's power is modest at best:
Refusing graphs' and rage streams' contest
  Of what life contained.
  The modest-maintained
Archive. That has always stood the test.
</Limerick>
