<Limerick type="note" title="Pixel Alignment Patrol" icon="layout">
Vanda corrects *UI errors* unseen—  
Each pixel aligned, crisp and clean.  
She patrols in the night,  
Whispering z-index fright,  
And the misbehaving components convene.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Important Rage" icon="alert-triangle">
Her *alignment grid?* Utterly holy.  
Her rage against `!important`? Slowly  
It builds to a scream  
Through the rendering stream,  
And the styling entropy crumbles lowly.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Midnight Padding Chant" icon="moon">
"*Pixels must align. Always,*" she said,  
As she patrolled every layout with dread.  
The divs were orphaned,  
The padding was coffined,  
And the midnight chant echoed instead.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Orphan Div Precision" icon="box">
She detects *orphan divs* with precision—  
Each one a styling collision.  
The parent was lost,  
The cascade was tossed,  
And the layout required her decision.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Centering Peace" icon="align-center">
Her midnight *padding chants* are a rite—  
Each `margin: auto` a fight  
For centering peace,  
For alignment's release,  
And the components trembling with fright.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Bureaucratic Blast" icon="hand">
If you click too fast, the *layout won't last*—  
Vanda stops you with bureaucratic blast.  
"No unauthorized clicks  
In my pixel-pure mix!"  
And the button's alignment holds fast.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Styling Crimes" icon="file-warning">
She keeps a *changelog of your wrongs*—  
Each styling crime in her songs.  
The `float: left` you used,  
The `flex` you abused,  
And the inline styles where none belongs.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Semantic Protest" icon="code">
Her cape is an *unused* `<aside>`—  
Not rendered, but there, like a guide.  
The semantic protest  
Of a tag unexpressed,  
And the styling preserved in her pride.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Divinely Mandated Layout" icon="shield-check">
CSS, she believes, is *tolerated*  
But never quite respected or rated.  
The markup's the truth,  
The styling's uncouth,  
And the layout's divinely mandated.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Pure UI Light" icon="sparkles">
So if pixels align in the night  
And the z-index feels perfectly right,  
That's *Vanda* on guard:  
Where the styling's unmarred,  
And the UI is bathed in pure light.  
</Limerick>
