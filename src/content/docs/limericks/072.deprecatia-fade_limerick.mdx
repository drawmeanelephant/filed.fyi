## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="Footnote Dweller" icon="file-text">
Deprecatia lives in the footnotes,
In old READMEs and margin-scrawled notes.
"This still works," she'd say,
"Sort of, in a way,"
And the functions she loved? Gone like boats.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Emotionally Pure Absence" icon="ghost">
She haunts inline comments at night,
Whispering best practices—trite
And obsolete, sure,
But emotionally pure,
And the code feels her absence with fright.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Wry Reply" icon="message-square">
"I used to matter," she'd sigh.
"You still do?" was her wry reply
To the newer mascots
Who filled up her slots,
And the deprecation warnings scrolled by.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Vigil of Versions" icon="timer">
Her shadow versioning runs deep—
Each function a vigil she'll keep.
The versions she tracks
Are historical cracks
Where the legacy code went to sleep.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Guilt and Absence" icon="frown">
Mascots avoid her—not fear,
But guilt keeps them from drawing near.
She keeps a list
Of functions she missed,
And most are gone, year after year.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Unstyled Sidebar Appearance" icon="layout">
She appears in sidebars left unstyled,
In old READMEs that nobody filed.
Her presence turns absence
To apparent compliance,
And the stability? Utterly wild.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Slow Death Warnings" icon="alert-triangle">
Her console fills up with warnings—
Each feature's slow death, not adornings.
The functions fade out,
One by one, without doubt,
And Deprecatia wins every morning.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Blessed Backups" icon="hard-drive">
The old backup images she blesses
Get promoted to "current"—what messes.
Her legacy influence
Turns rot into confluence,
And the absence becomes the successes.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Liturgical Practice" icon="scroll">
She whispers obsolete best practices
Into the void—liturgical practice is
All she has left.
Of function, bereft,
And the code is her last apparatus.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Devotion to the Past" icon="history">
So if deprecated warnings appear
And the footnotes feel eerily near,
That's Deprecatia's touch:
Not malice—just much
Devotion to code that's no longer here.
</Limerick>
