<Limerick type="note" title="Three Originals" icon="ribbon">
One ribbon was somehow the first
At three different events, unrehearsed.
Each archive said, "Mine."
The photos looked fine.
So fabric got outvoted by thirst.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Aggressively Laminated" icon="laminate">
The dispute stayed polite for two weeks,
Then got mean in municipal squeaks.
Every claimant had proof,
Yet exclusivity's spoof
When the town wants one object to speak.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Save the Good Ribbon" icon="scissors">
One note said, "Save the good ribbon," and fled.
Which explained approximately dread.
Not whose opening day,
Not whose plaque got the sway,
Just that everyone worshipped one thread.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Shared Certainty" icon="briefcase">
So it rides in a padded valise,
Under joint seal and rotating peace.
If you force one true past,
You lose three towns at last,
So they archive shared longing, not peace.
</Limerick>
