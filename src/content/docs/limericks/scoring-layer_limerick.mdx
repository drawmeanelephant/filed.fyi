<Limerick type="danger" title="Pain to a Score" icon="hash">
They turned all our **pain to a score**,
And pasted it **up on the door**.
The **number was high**,
So nobody would **cry**,
And they didn't do **anything more**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Crushing the Noise" icon="filter">
The **algorithm** started to weigh,
The things that the **workers would say**.
It **crushed all the noise**,
For the **management boys**,
And smoothed the **whole issue away**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Building a Lie" icon="bar-chart-2">
They measure the **tears that we cry**,
And use them to **build up a lie**.
The **metric is clean**,
But the **system is mean**,
And waiting for **someone to die**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Scream to a Grade" icon="activity">
They turn every **scream to a grade**,
To cover the **mess they have made**.
They **graph all the blood**,
That falls in the **mud**,
And let the whole **tragedy fade**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Calculate Pain" icon="percent">
They score how we **suffer and bleed**,
To satisfy **corporate greed**.
They **calculate pain**,
For their **personal gain**,
And **fuck us whenever they need**.
</Limerick>
