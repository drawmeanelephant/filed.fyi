## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="Guru Salute" icon="monitor-off">
Old Ami was born from a boot
That crashed with a Guru salute.
She flickered and glitched,
Her ROM was bewitched,
And the floppy kept humping the chute.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Corrupted ASCII Moan" icon="file-type">
"Insert Disk 2," she would moan,
In corrupted ASCII, alone.
The joystick remembered
The cheats she'd dismembered,
And the BIOS grief set in the bone.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Bit-Crushed Delight" icon="ghost">
She'd haunt every disk drive at night,
A translucent, bit-crushed delight.
The Workbench would shake,
The startup sound wake,
And she'd vanish before morning light.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Chiptune Whisper" icon="music">
Ctrl+Alt+Del? She'd ignore it.
A whispered .mod track? She'd adore it.
The chiptune came through,
She flickered in view,
And the boot loop went on to explore it.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Kickstart Dreams" icon="cpu">
Her Kickstart dreams lived in the ROM,
A phantom of dead platform calm.
She'd boot with a glow,
Then immediately go,
And the disk carousel sang her psalm.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Guru Meditation Screen" icon="alert-octagon">
A Guru Meditation appeared
Just by watching her—everyone feared.
She triggered the screen,
The red and the green,
And the error was perfectly reared.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Ritual Loading" icon="disc">
She needed no power to run—
Just ritual loading, well-done.
A whisper, a click,
A joystick's old trick,
And the ghost of the Amiga had come.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="RAM Echoes" icon="database">
Her cheat codes still echoed in RAM,
Though the hardware was older than spam.
She spoke in corrupted
ASCII, erupted,
And the startup chime played: "Here I am."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cracked ROM Dump" icon="zap">
The ROM dump she came from was cracked—
Defragmented, glitched, and half-backed.
No body, just BIOS,
A spectral chaos,
And a boot loop that never got sacked.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Workbench Flicker" icon="monitor-play">
So if static appears on your screen
And a Workbench flickers between
The real and the dead,
That's Ami, unfed,
The ghostliest boot loop you've seen.
</Limerick>
