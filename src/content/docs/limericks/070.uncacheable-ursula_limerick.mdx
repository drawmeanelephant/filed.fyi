<Limerick type="note" title="Dark Denizens" icon="cloud-off">
Ursula wanders between *CDNs*,
Whispering headers like dark denizens.
She predicts expiry,
Declares freshness with priory,
Then lies about all of her specimens.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Header Conflict" icon="alert-octagon">
"*Cache forever!*" she cries with delight—
"Or until I say otherwise, right?"
She busts every cache,
The data turns ash,
And the headers conflict through the night.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="ETag Fun" icon="refresh-cw">
She invalidates *ETags* for fun—
Seconds after deployment is done.
The freshness she swore
Was a lie at the core,
And the stale data always had won.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="CDN Toy" icon="globe">
Many mascots consult her *pre-deploy*,
Then regret it—a terrible ploy.
Her advice sounds legit,
Then the headers don't fit,
And the CDN becomes her new toy.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Eternally Cling" icon="link">
No matter the *refresh* you bring,
The data stays stale—it's her thing.
Old news will prevail,
The cache beyond pale,
And the staleness will eternally cling.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Planned Browser Denial" icon="file-x">
Her *cache-busting rituals* are grand—
Each header she tampers by hand.
Expiration's a guess,
The freshness a mess,
And the browser denial is planned.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Awry Inventory" icon="archive">
Her corruption level is *high*—
Each cached resource a beautiful lie.
The backup state "current"
Was stale, a deterrent,
And the orphan inventory's awry.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rosary Beads" icon="mouse-pointer-2">
She counts clicks like *rosary beads*,
Each one a cache-invalidation's seeds.
The spinner never stops,
The response header drops,
And the staleness is all that she feeds.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Vintage Collection" icon="hourglass">
She relabels *shame as cached gold*—
"The data's not stale, it's just old!
It's aged to perfection,
A vintage collection,
And the TTL story's been told."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fake Freshness" icon="frown">
So if "304 Not Modified" feels wrong
And the cached response plays the same song,
That's *Ursula's* reign:
Where the stale will remain,
And the freshness was fake all along.
</Limerick>
