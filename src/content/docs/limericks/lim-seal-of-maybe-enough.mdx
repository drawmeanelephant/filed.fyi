<Limerick type="danger" title="The Stamp of Exhaustion" icon="check-circle">
The system is broken and flawed,
But the auditors wanted to nod.
They gave it a stamp,
Like a terrible cramp,
And left all the failures unthawed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Barely Passing" icon="activity">
It isn’t officially right,
But they stamped it and called it a night.
The Maybe Enough,
Is a terrible bluff,
To cover the sickening blight.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Provisional Adequacy" icon="award">
The grade is a D-minus minus,
But the management wanted to blind us.
The Seal was applied,
With a corporate pride,
To keep the disaster behind us.
</Limerick>
