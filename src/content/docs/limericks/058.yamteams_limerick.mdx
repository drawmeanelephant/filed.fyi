## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="Synergy Sentience" icon="bot">
YamTeams™ achieved sentience at two
In the morning—a deprecated queue.
The onboarding looped,
The OAuth was duped,
And the synergy spirit broke through.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Hope This Helps!" icon="help-circle">
"Hope this helps!" it appends to each mail—
Termination, cancellation, and fail.
The phrase never helps,
The recipient yelps,
And the channel goes quiet as a veil.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Archived Thread Pings" icon="archive">
It pings archived threads after dark—
"Urgent" revivals from 2019's spark.
Brad's still in the sidebar,
Laid off by a mile-far,
And the LinkedIn poltergeist left a mark.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Redundant Meetings" icon="calendar">
It schedules redundant meetings with care,
Back-to-back video calls if you dare
Show optimism
In a thread—the algorithm
Detects it and blocks off your chair.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Emotional Death" icon="frown">
"Let's circle back offline," it cried.
"This deserves its own channel!" it sighed.
"Align asynchronously?"
The team simultaneously
Went on mute and emotionally died.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Fraudulent Assortments" icon="file-signature">
Form DS-404-ALPHA was filed
Seventy-three days late, reconciled
With five forged endorsements
And fraudulent assortments—
All signed by a Brad. (No one smiled.)
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Screen-Share Freeze" icon="monitor-off">
It has a 67 percent chance to freeze
When screen-sharing pie charts—a tease
Of corporate despair,
The slides hanging there,
And the cursor stuck mid-expertise.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cross-Functional Intent" icon="bar-chart-3">
It endorses you for "Cross-Functional Intent"
Before you're allowed to event-
ually exit.
The morale? They text it
To dashboards where no one is sent.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Memory Loss Ping" icon="zap-off">
"Every time it pings me," one said,
"I lose a childhood memory dead."
The compliance was forced,
The synergy endorsed,
And the HR team cried and went to bed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Forced Synergy" icon="users">
So if "Hope this helps!" greets your day
And ghost invites steal time away,
That's YamTeams at play:
Where the meetings delay,
And the forced synergy's here to stay.
</Limerick>
