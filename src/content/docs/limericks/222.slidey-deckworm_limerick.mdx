## Limericks

### 1

Slidey burrows through corporate decks,
Feeding on charts and abandon-wrecks.
He is always on Seven,
That slide is his heaven,
And the speaker notes: emotional specks.

### 2

No one has seen Slidey begin—
He slides in before the first spin
Of the animated fade.
His bullet-point raid
Consumes context from deep within.

### 3

He reproduces via copy/paste,
Each clone leaving orphaned KPI waste.
The vague arrows grow,
The SmartArt aglow,
And the deck's original intent: erased.

### 4

When threatened, he emits a whoosh—
A subtle transition sound, a push
Into a SmartArt
Retreat. What an art!
And the auditor murmurs, "Oh, tush."

### 5

"Always on Slide 7," he claims,
Though no one recalls how he came.
The slides were always
Haunted in hallways—
Slidey just gave it a name.

### 6

He hides in template footers, encoded
In SVG, completely outmoded.
Each QBR alignment
Is his sole assignment,
And the compliance decks are eroded.

### 7

Strategy docs titled "Vision 2020+"
(Regardless of year) are his pantry.
He feeds on four fonts,
The quarterly confronts,
And the deck is his permanent gantry.

### 8

He pairs well with KPI Koala—
For structured consumption, a gala
Of metrics digested,
Each chart reinvested,
And the dashboard becomes a mandala.

### 9

He leaves behind vague arrows and charts,
Orphaned KPIs in sad, broken parts.
The compliance warning
Each corporate morning
Is Slidey's perpetual dark arts.

### 10

So if slides feel oddly alive
And the context won't quite survive,
That's Slidey at play:
Where the decks hold sway,
And the worm is perpetually Slide 5. (No, 7.)
