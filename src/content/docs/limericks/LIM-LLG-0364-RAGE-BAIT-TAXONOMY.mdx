<Limerick type="note" title="B-2A: The Reply Surface" icon="message-square">
You entered replies seeking light—
Context, calibration, some right.
  Instead: maximum friction,
  Curated affliction.
The comments became the weather's height.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="B-2A: The Ordering" icon="list-ordered">
B-2A doesn't care who wrote it.
Bot, human, or badly-timed—submit.
  It concerns the sort:
  Aggravation's court
Was selected. That is the mandate.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="B-2B: Familiarity Drift" icon="repeat">
B-2B: the same outrage format
Encountered so often that flat
  Familiarity grows.
  The anger still flows
But requires more bait to combat.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="B-3A: Fatigue Loop" icon="battery-low">
B-3A: the fatigue loop wherein
Each outrage depletes the within
  Of the user's capacity
  For genuine scrutiny.
The anger runs, the context grows thin.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="B-4A: Ambient Echo" icon="radio">
B-4A: persistent vigilance, low
Grade, under the ambient flow
  Of updates. Not rage
  But not rest. The stage
Of chronic attunement to woe.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="B-5: Moral Authority Capture" icon="crown">
B-5: a format that grants
The sharer moral authority slants—
  To speak on behalf
  Of the wronged. The craft:
Moral fluency. Righteous rants.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="High-Intensity Feed Artifact" icon="zap">
Strong emotion, no residue past
The next refresh interval: classed
  As high-intensity
  Feed artifact. Free
Of certification. The anger: fast.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Anger Circulating" icon="refresh-cw">
A reply surface keeps it circling
Long after the source item's trickling
  To nothing. The comments
  Become the monuments
To what the post was—still prickling.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Outrage Persists" icon="flame">
Filer's strongest feeling attaches
To strangers. The outrage hatches
  From the ordering.
  Low recall. Bordering
On weather—nobody latches.
</Limerick>
