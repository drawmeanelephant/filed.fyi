<Limerick type="danger" title="The Polite Bow" icon="smile">
They gave a **polite little bow**,
But wouldn't allow it **right now**.
The **words** were so sweet,
But the **help** was a cheat,
And the **burden** remained on the **brow**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Fading From Sight" icon="eye-off">
The system was **highly polite**,
And kept all the **processes tight**.
It **validated pain**,
Without lifting the **strain**,
And quietly **faded from sight**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Moat" icon="anchor">
A gentle and **courteous note**,
Was all that the **management wrote**.
They gave us a **smile**,
For a terrible **while**,
And left us to **sink in the moat**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Kicked Down the Road" icon="corner-down-right">
They thanked us for **bearing the load**,
While **kicking us** down the dark road.
With **beautiful text**,
They left us all **vexed**,
And watched as the **server explode**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Grand Courtesy" icon="star">
The **courtesy** offered was grand,
With a gentle and **comforting hand**.
But the **leverage lacked**,
And the system was **hacked**,
While the **bosses just shit in the sand**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Polite Brick Wall" icon="shield-off">
They gave us a **script** to be sweet,
While the **client** is facing defeat.
We can offer a **smile**,
And a **meaningless file**,
But their **problem** is stuck on the street.
</Limerick>
