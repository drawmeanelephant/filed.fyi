<Limerick type="danger" title="Counting the Gestures" icon="bar-chart">
The Cherub records every smile,
And puts it right into the file.
The metric goes up,
Like a terrible cup,
And stays there a miserable while.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Empty Tally" icon="edit-2">
They tracked every time we were nice,
To cover the terrible price.
The weight hasn’t moved,
And there’s nothing to prove,
But the Cherub is rolling the dice.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Metrics of Bullshit" icon="check-square">
You clicked on the button of care,
And the Cherub rejoiced in the air.
It didn’t fix shit,
But the tally is lit,
And the executives think it is fair.
</Limerick>
