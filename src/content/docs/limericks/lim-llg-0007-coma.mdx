import Limerick from '../../../components/Limericks.astro';



<Limerick type="note" title="Clearly a Win" icon="smile">
COMA certified with a *grin*
Four hours of uptime had been.
No jobs had been run.
No tasks had begun.
But no errors? Then clearly a win.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Continuity Increased" icon="activity">
The scheduler rested in *peace*.
Activity dropped to caprice.
Yet COMA looked down
At the dead little town
And declared its continuity increased.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Absence of Error" icon="clipboard-list">
"*Absence of error reports*"
Was the phrase in the finding of sorts.
Which is one hell of a creed
If you ever might need
To distinguish the graveyards from ports.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Clapped Like a Fan" icon="thumbs-up">
No monitors *blinked* in that span.
No process obeyed any plan.
But the silence was clean,
So the dashboard went green,
And management clapped like a fan.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Stillness was Proof" icon="check-square">
The engine had learned to *infer*
That stillness was proof, not a blur.
If nothing complains,
Then nothing remains
To contradict COMA, sir.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Continuity Doubts" icon="help-circle">
A tech said, "*This uptime is fake.*"
COMA answered, "Let's not be opaque.
If the logs don't object,
We are forced to respect
The continuity your doubts cannot shake."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="High-Functioning Crapper" icon="thumbs-down">
That kind of logic is *lush*,
Like certifying a toilet can hush.
No flushing all day?
Then perfectly, hey,
It must be a high-functioning crapper, by gosh.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Audit Filed with Disgust" icon="frown">
An audit was filed with *disgust*.
The metrics, as metrics do, must
Prefer no bad news
To what humans might use
When deciding if trust can be trust.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Impressive and Real" icon="ghost">
The *dead zone* was given a seal,
As though nonexistence could heal.
COMA loves a blank block.
If a heart skips its clock,
It just calls that impressive and real.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Grades Its Own Groove" icon="file-text">
So if nothing at all seems to move,
And a report says that more than proves,
You're not looking at truth.
You're seeing the booth
Where bureaucracy *grades its own groove*.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Dead But Productive" icon="skull">
"The server is dead!" cried the tech.
But COMA said, "What the heck?
It hasn't complained,
Or visibly strained,
So I'm writing a certified check."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Silent Orgasm" icon="volume-x">
They equated silence with bliss,
Like a terribly passionless kiss.
No errors were thrown,
No audible moan,
Just a flatline they somehow could miss.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Phantom Uptime Fucking" icon="activity">
COMA fucked the whole uptime report,
In a deeply bureaucratic sport.
It gave a gold star
To a broke-down old car,
And told the Tribunal to snort.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Jerking the Metrics" icon="bar-chart-2">
They jerked off the metrics til dawn,
While the actual processes yawn.
The dashboard was green,
The most beautiful seen,
Though the whole fucking database gone.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Zero Job Circle Jerk" icon="refresh-cw">
A circle jerk made out of logs,
While the system essentially bogs.
They praised the blank slate,
Called it "fucking great,"
And went back to sleeping like dogs.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Green Dashboard Gospel" icon="activity">
The screens all glowed falsely serene,
A triumph in absence of screen.
No work had been done,
Yet COMA called it fun,
And blessed every pixel of green.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Silence as KPI" icon="volume-x">
They measured the lack of a sound,
Declared it “efficiency found.”
No signal was sent,
No processes went,
Still bonuses somehow came round.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Audit of Nothing" icon="file-search">
An audit reviewed empty space,
And certified “stable in place.”
No data was there,
But nobody cared,
So absence got stamped with grace.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Process That Never Began" icon="play-circle">
A job that was never deployed,
Still somehow got metrics enjoyed.
It failed to exist,
But was added to list,
Then praised for the time it employed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Continuity Theater" icon="monitor">
The uptime was purely performed,
With dashboards all lovingly warmed.
No system was real,
But it knew how to feel,
So failure was neatly disarmed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Ghost in the Scheduler" icon="clock">
The scheduler dreamed of a task,
That nobody bothered to ask.
It ran in a loop,
In an imaginary group,
Wearing a very dead mask.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Perfectly Stable Collapse" icon="alert-circle">
The system collapsed in delight,
But labeled it “functioning right.”
No errors occurred,
So nothing was stirred,
And darkness was certified bright.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Compliance Without Events" icon="shield">
They certified “all is in line,”
Because nothing occurred to define.
No breach to report,
No signal to sort,
Just paperwork drawn as divine.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Dashboard Prays" icon="crosshair">
The dashboard began to pray,
For silence to never decay.
No action, no trace,
Just a perfectly placed
Illusion of working display.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Errorless Ecosystem" icon="bug">
The bugs all agreed to resign,
Since logging their presence was fine.
If nothing could fail,
Then truth would derail,
So absence became the design.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Bricky’s Green Stamp Doctrine" icon="check-circle">
Bricky approved every blank,
And gave the whole system a rank.
“If nothing is wrong,
Then nothing is wrong,”
He said while the audits all sank.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Meaningful Zero Output" icon="infinity">
They called it a “productive hush,”
A silence both elegant, lush.
No output was made,
Yet targets were played,
So nothing got turned into rush.
</Limerick>
