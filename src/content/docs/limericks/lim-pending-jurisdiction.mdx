<Limerick type="note" title="The Orphan Domain" icon="help-circle">
The failure occurred on the line,  
But no one stepped up to assign.  
The jurisdiction waits  
At the boundary gates,  
While the offices politely decline.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Waiting for Ownership" icon="clock">
The mascot of "Not My Domain",  
Stands out in the bureaucratic rain.  
It waits for a boss  
To acknowledge the loss,  
But the waiting is largely in vain.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Unclaimed Ritual" icon="circle">
An office is needed, we say,  
But the budget was taken away.  
The Pending state stays,  
Through the rest of our days,  
As a monument built to delay.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="caution" title="Stopped Sending" icon="wifi-off">
The jurisdiction was pending  
The server was quietly ending  
We waited a year  
With procedural fear  
And stopped all the data from sending  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Boundary Blur" icon="cloud">
The boundary line is a blur  
We really don't know what to infer  
The pending is slow  
With nowhere to go  
We wait for the error to occur  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Legal Abyss" icon="shield">
We fall in a legal abyss  
A state of procedural miss  
The human is flawed  
The logic outlawed  
We seal up the file with a kiss  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Boring and Dull" icon="minus">
The jurisdiction is null  
The system is boring and dull  
The humans complain  
In the digital rain  
The archive is perfectly full  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Big Drain" icon="arrow-down">
A pending request is a lie  
The network continues to die  
We route all the pain  
Right down the big drain  
And nobody ever asks why  
</Limerick>
