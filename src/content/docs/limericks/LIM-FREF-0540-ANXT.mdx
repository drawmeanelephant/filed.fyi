<Limerick type="note" title="Truth in the Appendix" icon="paperclip">
The truth was too costly to front,
So the appendix bore all the brunt.
It was accurate there,
In subordinate air,
Where a reader would have to go hunt.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Hover State Honesty" icon="eye">
The tooltip admitted the thing
That the banner refused it could bring.
So the page looked serene,
While the hover between
Did the dangerous work of witnessing.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Margin Jurisdiction" icon="book-open">
The margin was granted the pain,
For the headline had posture to maintain.
Thus the record stayed calm,
With a small truthful qualm
Filed somewhere the calm could explain.
</Limerick>
