<Limerick type="note" title="Holding the Space" icon="square">
A witness who stands in the blank,
To keep the bureaucracy frank.
They hold up the line
Till a mascot more fine
Can properly fill up the rank.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Interim State" icon="clock">
It has no specific domain,
Just waiting around in the rain.
The narrative stalls
In the archival halls,
Until it is summoned again.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Waiting for Meaning" icon="loader">
The Placeholder waits for a name,
With no one but silence to blame.
It fills up the hole
In the system's sad soul,
While playing the waiting-room game.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Meaningless Void" icon="maximize">
The Witness is holding the spot,
For a manager’s horrible plot.
It just sits there and stares,
While the company tears,
And the project goes completely to rot.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="A Bureaucratic Hole" icon="circle">
There’s nothing behind its dead eyes,
Just a series of corporate lies.
It’s a functional gap,
In a terrible trap,
As the actual usefulness dies.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Interim Fuck-Up" icon="fast-forward">
We put it right there on the shelf,
To distance the blame from oneself.
The Witness is fake,
A profound, sick mistake,
That is hoarding the organizational wealth.
</Limerick>
