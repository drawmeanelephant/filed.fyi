<Limerick type="danger" title="The Sacrificial Space" icon="shield">
The Saint takes the hit to the face,
To cover the system’s disgrace.
It absorbs all the shock,
Like a customized block,
In a totally miserable place.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Emotional Queueing" icon="users">

And the users are screaming for more.
The Saint stands ahead,
With a feeling of dread,
While the executives settle the score.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Between Event and Collapse" icon="alert-triangle">
The collapse is beginning to start,
So the Saint plays a horrible part.
It softens the blow,
Just to put on a show,
With a broken and terrified heart.
</Limerick>
