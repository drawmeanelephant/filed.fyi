<Limerick type="danger" title="The Dead Service" icon="ghost">
The service was killed in the fall,
But the portal still offers a call.
The Ghostline is clear,
But there’s nobody here,
Just an echoing, desolate wall.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Textually Operational" icon="file-text">
The PDF says we’re alive,
And ready to help you survive.
But the server is dead,
And the engineers fled,
And the data has taken a dive.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Sunset Illusion" icon="sunset">
It’s technically over and done,
And out of the heat of the sun.
The Ghostline remains,
In the terrible pains,
As a feature for absolutely no one.
</Limerick>
