<Limerick type="note" title="Margin Retrieval" icon="file-search">
The headline had nothing to say,  
So the margins were summoned to play.  
From a footnote and scar,  
They rebuilt where we are,  
Which embarrassed the minutes that day.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Recovered From Side Channels" icon="folder-open">
A side-channel scrap in the file  
Had been waiting, unfashioned, a while.  
When the clean record failed,  
That small fragment prevailed  
With a ruinously useful smile.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Annex Archaeology" icon="archive">
They went digging through annex and note,  
Past the sentence the chair chose to quote.  
What the summary lost  
Had survived at a cost  
In a clause no one wanted afloat.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Fragment Extraction" icon="crosshair">

We have locked down the floor  
To recover some more,  
Lest the narrative wander astray!  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Partial Salvage" icon="life-buoy">

I recovered a noun,  
Then I wrote it all down,  
And called it aggressively paced.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Residue Check" icon="search">
