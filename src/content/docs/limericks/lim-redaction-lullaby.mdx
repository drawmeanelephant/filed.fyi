<Limerick type="danger" title="The Blackout Song" icon="music">
It sings you a beautiful tune,  
While deleting the sun and the moon.  
The text turns to black,  
And there’s no going back,  
And the lawyers will visit you soon.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Bedside Omission" icon="moon">
The lullaby strokes on your head,  
While cutting the words that you read.  
The truth is suppressed,  
While you gently rest,  
And you wake up completely misled.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Tidy Silences" icon="volume-x">
The silence is legally tight,  
To cover the terrible sight.  
The lullaby plays,  
Through the rest of your days,  
To ensure that you give up the fight.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="note" title="Quietly Weep" icon="moon">
We sang a redaction to sleep  
The data was buried so deep  
The lullaby failed  
The server was jailed  
We started to quietly weep  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Heavy Line" icon="minus">
A song of the black heavy line  
The errors were doing just fine  
We crossed out the text  
And wondered what's next  
And waited for any old sign  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Painfully Tight" icon="lock">
The lullaby censors the byte  
And turns out the digital light  
The human is weak  
And refuses to speak  
The redaction is painfully tight  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Silent Mob" icon="users">
We sing to the meaningless blob  
And do a procedural job  
The words disappear  
In a digital fear  
We run with a silent, dull mob  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Millions of Days" icon="calendar">
The redaction is gentle and slow  
With nowhere for data to go  
The lullaby plays  
For millions of days  
The vacuum continues to grow  
</Limerick>
