<Limerick type="note" title="The Knock is Correct" icon="door">
A *proper knock* came to the door—
The code and credentials were sure.
"You have the right key,"
Said Keyholder—"Thee
Cannot enter, I hold you secure."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Universal Lockout Day" icon="lock-x">
One person locked down *every place*—
Every environment, every space.
All keys were denied,
No passwords applied,
And Keyholder held the whole case.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Keeping the Latch" icon="key">
The latch held through *every right knock*—
The lock wouldn't ever unlock.
"You know the right way,
But you cannot play—
I polished your keys with a rock."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Secured Into Nonexistence" icon="trash2">
A file was locked down *so tight*,
That it vanished from conceptual sight.
The security won,
The file was undone,
And nothing remained in its light.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Polishing the Sealed Lockfiles" icon="shield">
The lockfiles were polished with *care*—
Each one so secure and so rare.
The environment sealed,
The access revealed
As nothing and nothing to share.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Denied At the Wrong Time" icon="clock">
The key was exactly *right*—
The timing was wrong, day or night.
"Come back," said the warden,
"But I give no pardon,
For access denied by the light."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Latch After Knocking" icon="alert-circle">
You knew the *right code* to apply,
You had the credentials to try.
But Keyholder's task
Was always to ask
Why let you through—"Here's a sigh."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Lockfile Garden" icon="plant">
The lockfiles were tended with *grace*—
Each one had its environmental place.
But access was gone,
The garden lived on,
And nothing could enter that space.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Conditional Lock" icon="git-branch">
There once was a *lock with a clause*,
That opened for none without pause.
"If true," it declared,
"But nothing compared—
So false was the only real cause."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Access Denied (Persisting)" icon="ban">
The system returned with a *cry*,
"Access denied," with no why.
The key still was right,
The state held it tight—
Permission refused to comply.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Key Without Door" icon="key">
A key was constructed with *care*,
Precision beyond all repair.
But no door was found,
No lock would be bound—
It opened up nothing but air.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Environment Sealed" icon="box">
The environment sealed *every seam*,
Each variable locked like a dream.
No input got through,
No process could queue—
It ran as a closed-loop regime.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Warden Logic" icon="shield-off">
The warden reviewed every *plea*,
And nodded, "Correct as can be."
Then tightened the gate,
Reconfirmed the state—
"All valid. Still denied by me."
</Limerick>
