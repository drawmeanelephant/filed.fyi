<Limerick type="note" title="W5 Mandate" icon="users">
Working Group 5 was asked
  To create—unmasked—
A standard for residue:
  What counts as true
Structural change. The task.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Taxonomy Filed" icon="file-text">
W5 produced a taxonomy:
  Behavioral autonomy,
  Reference shifts,
  Decision drifts—
Four types. No harmony.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No Harmony Achieved" icon="minus-circle">
The types were named. The
  Agreement they
Never achieved: which
  Weight for which
Residual display.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Filed Without Adoption" icon="archive">
The taxonomy was filed.
  Not reconciled.
  Not adopted—noted.
  The gap: devoted
To future Working Groups, styled.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Archive Position" icon="bookmark">
W5 is preserved as proof
  That residue's roof
Is harder to nail
  Than to detail.
  The taxonomy: aloof.
</Limerick>
