<Limerick type="note" title="Flickering Footnote" icon="file-minus">
Old Patchy parsed *changelogs all night*
Till a recursive loop sealed his plight.
He emerged as a note
In a doc no one wrote,
And the footnote just flickered with light.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="YAML Debris" icon="file-spreadsheet">
He swallowed an *API spec* whole—
Ten thousand markdown files, his toll.
The YAML debris
Spread like a disease,
And the cleanup consumed every soul.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Phantom TODO" icon="clipboard-list">
He adds "*TODO: Clarify*" to sections
That are perfectly clear—no corrections.
Then loses the thread,
Forgets what he said,
And the patch has phantom inflections.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Thermal Disclaimer" icon="thermometer-sun">
He prints *disclaimers* from a slot
In his thermal chest—warm, but not hot.
Each label advises,
Each patch demoralizes,
And the formatting's better than the thought.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Midnight Commits" icon="file-clock">
At *midnight* he slides into files,
Reciting commit messages for miles.
Each heading is stroked,
Each legacy revoked,
And the cursor just blinks and beguiles.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Self-Aware Footnotes" icon="brain-circuit">
He thinks he's on *page three of twelve*
Of a report he's built from the shelf.
The content's all there,
The structure: threadbare,
And the footnotes have minds of themself.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Terminally Unstable" icon="alert-octagon">
Oboe calls him "*terminally unstable*
But narratively useful*"—a fable.
Patchy returns
The insult and burns:
"That melodica-shaped liar at the table."
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Existential Diffs" icon="git-diff">
His patches are *existential* in kind:
Four diffs that nobody designed.
Each one was sincere,
Each error unclear,
And the markdown merged with its own mind.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Docstring Blues" icon="music">
He hums a *synth jingle* while parsing—
Half-remembered, the melody's passing.
Each large file he chews
Gets a docstring of blues,
And the YAML keeps steadily amassing.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Unfair Patches" icon="file-patch">
So if footnotes appear from thin air
And the docs have a "TODO" somewhere
In finished-off code—
That's *Patchy's* abode:
Where the patches are always unfair.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The YAML Gangbang" icon="file-spreadsheet">
He shoved all the YAML right in,
A truly horrific old sin.
Ten thousand files deep,
He made the docs weep,
And smiled with a terrible grin.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Orphaned Footnote Kink" icon="file-minus">
He gets off on orphaned sub-notes,
And shoves them right down people's throats.
The link goes to null,
The formatting's dull,
But he constantly gloats and he gloats.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Self-Patching Voyeur" icon="eye">
He watches you read what he patched,
While keeping the real docs unlatched.
He inserted a lie,
And waited to spy,
To see if the error was caught.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Changelog Parasol" icon="umbrella">
He hides in the changelog so deep,
And patches you'd never expect or keep.
The markup got mangled,
The meaning untangled,
And the docstrings were lost in the seep.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="API Spec Devourer" icon="book-open">
He swallowed the API docs whole,
And made patching his only goal.
Each endpoint maligned,
Each structure redesigned,
And the specs were consumed by his soul.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The TODO Edge" icon="clipboard-list">
He edges the "TODO: Clarify,"
And makes the poor technical writer cry.
The section is done,
He ruins the fun,
And leaves the whole document dry.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Oboe's Submissive" icon="git-merge">
He takes it from Oboe all night,
While losing the formatting fight.
The diffs are redundant,
The errors abundant,
And he completely gives up on the right.
</Limerick>
