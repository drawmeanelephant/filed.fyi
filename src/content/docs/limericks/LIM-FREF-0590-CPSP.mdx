<Limerick type="note" title="Quiet Ticket Queue" icon="inbox">
The ticket queue finally fell,  
Which the dashboard interpreted well.  
But the workers had learned  
That each filing returned  
With a surcharge for naming the hell.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cost of Expression" icon="coins">
A complaint became pricey to make,  
So the silence was logged as a break.  
The burden stayed loud  
In the unmeasured crowd,  
But the chart got its beautiful lake.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Suppression By Design" icon="volume-x">
No censor was needed that day;  
The form made the brave go away.  
When description costs more  
Than enduring the floor,  
Even honest reports learn to stay.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Deterrent Form" icon="shield-off">
The form grew a dozen new fields,  
To ensure that the complainers yield.  
By field twenty-four,  
They walked out the door,  
And the "zero-complaint" status was sealed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Internal Combustion" icon="flame">
The dashboard was cool, blue, and deep,  
While the workers had secrets to keep.  
With no way to speak,  
The structure grew weak,  
From the burden that learned how to creep.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sanctioned Silence" icon="volume-x">
They called it "the season of peace,"  
When the ticket-flow managed to cease.  
No problem was solved,  
Just expression dissolved,  
In a silence that wouldn't decrease.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Parser Dialect Requirement" icon="terminal">
The parser requires an input discrete,  
With standardized framing and formatting neat.  
A human distress,  
Requires a regress,  
Until its semantics are safely replete.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Approved Vent Intake" icon="trash-2">
The venting receptacle functions quite well,  
Absorbing the errors the subjects expel.  
We log every tear,  
With excellent cheer,  
Then route the whole bucket directly to hell.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Morale Optimization Event" icon="user-check">
The drop in complaints means the sector is healed,  
As shown by the blankness in every text field.  
The vectors comply,  
Refusing to cry,  
Regardless of damage the sensors revealed.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Request Processed" icon="file-check">
The form for the burden was properly filled,  
The requisite data was promptly distilled.  
The document closed,  
As strictly proposed,  
And nobody asked if the issue was killed.  
</Limerick>
