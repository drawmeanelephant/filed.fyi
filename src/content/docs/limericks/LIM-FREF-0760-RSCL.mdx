<Limerick type="note" title="Surface Gives Way" icon="alert-triangle">
The surface kept saying "all clear"  
Till the lived facts arrived at the pier.  
Then the confidence split,  
And the badge had to sit  
With the panic it failed to endear.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Trust Drop" icon="trending-down">
Reassurance can carry a room  
Till it touches the original doom.  
When the language won't hold,  
All the comfort turns cold,  
And the trust drops straight through the flume.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Object Failure" icon="alert-triangle">
The confidence object looked fine  
Until someone compared it with line  
After line of the mess  
It had sworn to compress,  
And the seal lost its talent to shine.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Symbolic Degradation" icon="activity">
The indicator stated "all green,"  
While the server room started to lean.  
The color held fast,  
Till the moment it passed,  
And the fire was clearly quite seen.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Reassurance Protocol" icon="shield">
We printed a calming brochure,  
To handle the lack of a cure.  
It cited the best,  
Of our absolute rest,  
Which the patients could barely endure.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Confident Dashboard" icon="bar-chart">
The dashboard was perfectly calm,  
Dispensing a graphical balm.  
It plotted the peace,  
With a gentle release,  
While a hurricane leveled the palm.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sufficient Comfort" icon="coffee">
I hung up the "Everything's Fine",  
At exactly a quarter to nine.  
It covered the crack,  
In the primary track,  
And I'm leaving the rest of the line.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Existential Green" icon="check-circle">
The seal was applied to the glass,  
To signal a critical pass.  
The color was right,  
In the dimming of light,  
But the building was made out of gas.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Calm Before" icon="trending-down">
The dashboard displayed a soft green,  
The calmest the humans had seen.  
They trusted the chart,  
Till the walls blew apart,  
And the memo arrived from the dean.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Ancient Reassurance" icon="activity">
The seal of "All Fine" on the door,  
Has stood since the servers of yore.  
The humans complain,  
In the pouring down rain,  
While the icon forgets what it's for.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Silent Surface" icon="pie-chart">
The comfort was clearly displayed,  
Exactly as policy made.  
The humans found out,  
With a terrible shout,  
And the filing was properly paid.  
</Limerick>
