<Limerick type="danger" title="The Quiet Disclosure" icon="eye-off">

In an annex completely concealed.  
The Hush keeps it low,  
So the public won’t know,  
That the wound is incredibly unhealed.  
</Limerick>

Of the things that the company lacks.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="caution" title="Quiet Overload" icon="alert-triangle">

That the users will barely survive.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Acoustic Sealing" icon="folder-minus">

</Limerick>
