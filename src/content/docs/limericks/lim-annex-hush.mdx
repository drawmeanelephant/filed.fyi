<Limerick type="danger" title="The Quiet Disclosure" icon="eye-off">
The terrible truth is revealed,
In an annex completely concealed.
The Hush keeps it low,
So the public won’t know,
That the wound is incredibly unhealed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Rear-Section Burdens" icon="file-minus">
We shoved all the grief in the back,
So the main text is totally slack.
The Hush tells you "shhh",
While you sort through the swish,
Of the things that the company lacks.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Hidden in Plain Sight" icon="lock">
It’s legally there if you look,
In the absolute back of the book.
The Hush does its job,
For the corporate mob,
And lets every executive off the hook.
</Limerick>
