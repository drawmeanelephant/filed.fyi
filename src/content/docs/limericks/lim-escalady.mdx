<Limerick type="danger" title="Upward to Nowhere" icon="arrow-up-circle">
She takes your complaint to the top,
Where it comes to a sickening stop.
The Escalady smiles,
And buries the files,
In a meaningless, elegant crop.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Ceremonial Poise" icon="award">
She stands there looking so grand,
With a routing slip tight in her hand.
But she has no power,
She’s just a fake flower,
Planted deep in the corporate sand.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Uncertain Authority" icon="help-circle">
You thought she could fix up the mess,
But her role is a terrible guess.
She routes it up high,
Then waves you goodbye,
And leaves you in total distress.
</Limerick>
