<Limerick type="note" title="Acceptable Strain" icon="edit-3">
The sentence said strain had occurred,
But avoided the destabilized word.
It was polished enough
To survive being rough,
And the injury went overheard.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Phrasing Review" icon="file-check">
They softened the verb before lunch,
Till the finding no longer could punch.
By replacing "they failed"
With "continuity trailed,"
They converted a fracture to hunch.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Lexical Risk" icon="alert-triangle">
Some words make the dashboard turn red,
So the guide recommends others instead.
If the truth must pass through,
It should bow as it do,
And arrive looking mostly well-bred.
</Limerick>
