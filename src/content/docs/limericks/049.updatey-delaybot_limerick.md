## Limericks

### 1

"Just restart today!" he would say,
As he crashed the whole damn display.
The update was keen,
The progress bar green,
And it stayed at 99 all day.

### 2

He's stuck in a perpetual state
Of announcing that updates are great.
The rollout's been pending
Since time without ending,
And the version is permanently late.

### 3

"Preparing to prepare," read the screen.
The most helpful message you've seen.
The bar wouldn't move,
The system refused to approve,
And the reboot was stuck in between.

### 4

"An update is coming… eventually,"
He promised, quite reverentially.
The deadline was loose,
The deployment a goose
Chase that ran consequentially.

### 5

The restart popup won't go away—
It's been there since yesterday's day.
He stares at it, frozen,
The system unchosen,
And the users just want it to stay.

### 6

His flow is perpetually lost
To a restart that nobody tossed.
"Pending installation"
Is his daily vocation,
And the update's the thing that it cost.

### 7

He whispers redirects in the void
Into navbars, empty and deployed.
Each breadcrumb loops back,
Each trail off the track,
And the update remains unenjoyed.

### 8

The progress bar's frozen like ice
At a percentage that's not very nice.
"Almost there!" he insists,
As the update persists
In preparing to roll out. Twice.

### 9

His emotional buffer is null—
He apologizes at every lull.
"200 OK!"
"But nothing deployed today."
"Well, the rollout announcement is full."

### 10

So if updates are coming but never,
And the progress bar freezes forever,
That's Updatey's state:
Perpetually late,
And the deployment freeze? Quite clever.
