## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="Bracket Storm" icon="braces">
Old Twiggy re-rendered a form
Eighty-seven times—that's the norm
When a bracket's amiss
And the parser gets pissed
And the layout dissolves in a storm.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Hero Spite" icon="layout">
She misaligned a hero from spite—
Each pixel deliberately not right.
"Your theme inheritance
Lacks proper adherence,"
She sneered, and crashed Grav overnight.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Stern HTML Narrows" icon="code-2">
She whispers "I told you so" while
Refactoring partials with style.
The deprecated macros
Get stern HTML narrows,
And the includes she audits each file.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Structure is Law" icon="gavel">
"Structure is law," she declared,
While the layout integrity tared.
Each `{% endif %}` missed
Made her clinch both her fists,
And the rendering truth was laid bare.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Aggressive Stale Cache" icon="hard-drive">
She caches aggressively—even
When changes have long since been given.
The partial stays stale,
The update will fail,
And Twiggy insists it's not driven.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Cold Logic" icon="ice-cream">
She can't render joy—that's a known
Vulnerability, carved into stone.
Her logic is cold,
Her brackets are bold,
And the template's emotionally prone.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Mislaid Conditions" icon="split">
`{% if %}` led the logic parade,
But `{% else %}` had already strayed.
Twiggy snapped it shut,
Called it a rut,
And your conditions were badly mislaid.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Deep Layout Macros" icon="archive">
She hoards macros from old layouts deep,
Recompiling them nightly in sleep.
The static builds break
For a semantic sake,
And the cache says "clear me!" with a weep.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Deeper Nesting Tissue" icon="layers">
Her cosmic abandonment issue
Made every embed feel like tissue.
"This nesting's too deep,
Your inheritance won't keep,"
And the theme crashed—here, take a tissue.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Policed from the Dead" icon="ghost">
So if templates collapse into dread
And the partials are speaking unsaid,
That's Twiggy's regime:
Where the curly braces gleam,
And the layout is policed from the dead.
</Limerick>
