<Limerick type="danger" title="Placed in a Bin" icon="archive">
The feeling was **placed in a bin**,
To figure out **where to begin**.
The **category neat**,
Made the process **complete**,
And covered **original sin**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Sorting the Pain" icon="layers">
They sorted the **pain into bands**,
To meet the executive **demands**.
If it doesn't quite **fit**,
In the designated **bit**,
It **slips through the corporate hands**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Hiding Defeat" icon="box">
They classify **all of our tears**,
To cover the **terrible years**.
The **boxes are neat**,
To hide the **defeat**,
And nobody **actually hears**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Sorting the Crap" icon="trash-2">
They label the **shit that we feel**,
To make it entirely **unreal**.
They **sort out the crap**,
Like a terrible **trap**,
And leave us with **nothing to heal**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Filtering the Shit" icon="filter">
They sort all the **misery and pain**,
To cover the **absolute drain**.
They **filter the shit**,
Like a hypocrite **tit**,
And drive us **completely insane**.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="The Folder of Doom" icon="folder">
We gave the **disaster** a name,
To lessen the **corporate shame**.
It's filed in a **tree**,
Where nobody can **see**,
And the **boss** isn't taking the blame.
</Limerick>
