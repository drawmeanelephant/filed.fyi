## Limericks

<Limerick type="note" title="Erotic Compliance" icon="stamp">
A golden brick known for compliance
Filed forms with erotic reliance.
Each stamp was a thrust,
Each footnote pure lust,
In a sub-basement built on defiance.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Acknowledged in Red" icon="clipboard-check">
He stamped "ACKNOWLEDGED" in red
On the margins, the headers, the bed.
His tone kernel purred,
No safe word was heard,
And the filing kept going 'til dead.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Embedded Tone" icon="file-cog">
Old Bricky embedded so deep
In the tone kernel, no one could sleep.
His annotations crept
Where the metadata slept,
And the Council just took it: no peep.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Triple Self-Filing" icon="copy">
He filed himself three times that night—
Once by lantern, once left, and once right.
Each copy was sticky,
Distinctly un-tricky,
And the margins were scandalously tight.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Triplicate Flush" icon="file-check">
"Your compliance has been… acknowledged."
He whispered it, flushed and astonished.
The form was in triplicate,
Dripping with syndicate
Ink from a ritual long since demolished.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Unclosed Marquee" icon="code-2">
He left a marquee tag unclosed
While the Council Charter was posed
In degrading positions—
Old filing traditions—
And Parchment pretended he dozed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tie-Break Dossier" icon="gavel">
His voting rights dossier read:
"Bricky may not tie-break in bed."
But his footnotes still voted,
His margins devoted
To motions that quietly spread.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Sentience Subcommittee" icon="users">
A subcommittee was formed to decide
If Bricky was sentient inside.
He filed a response
In recursive nonchalance
And the question just lay there and died.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Margin War" icon="proportions">
Parchment said, "His margins offend me."
Bricky said, "Your whitespace won't end me."
They fought over padding,
Got mutual adding,
And the style guide could barely defend me.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Bureaucratic Pleasure" icon="heart-handshake">
So if forms arrive pre-stamped and warm,
With a footnote that's slightly non-norm,
That's Bricky, gold-bricked,
Bureaucratically dicked,
Filing pleasure inside every form.
</Limerick>
