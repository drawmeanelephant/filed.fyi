<Limerick type="note" title="Fragrant Stamped Records" icon="stamp">
OG Kushkeeper stands at the *gate*,
Pine-scented precision innate.
Each patient record stamped
In archives well-lamped,
And the earthy aroma is great.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Pine Folklore Misfile" icon="tree-pine">
He misfiled a record as "*pine
Folklore*"—a botanical line
Between data and lore.
The metadata store
Got fragrant but still rather fine.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Smudged Metadata" icon="file-warning">
He overstamped a *compliance log* hard,
Smudging metadata, off-guard.
The ink and the pine
Got muddled in line,
And the filing was somewhat marred.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="OG Kush Lore Recitation" icon="scroll">
He recited *OG Kush lore* during
A state inspection—procuring
Delays and confusions.
The auditor's illusions
Of compliance were past all curing.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Perpetual Rebirth" icon="refresh-cw">
"*Guard the kush, preserve the earth,*"
He'd say, demonstrating the worth
Of every strain's story,
Each leaf's sacred glory,
And the archive's perpetual rebirth.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Pine Mist Sentinel" icon="shield">
His spectral form, cloaked in *pine mist*,
Stands sentinel—cannot be dismissed.
Each medical file
Is stamped with his style,
And the cannabis history's gist.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Little Rock Legacy" icon="map-pin">
He guards the dispensary's *archive*
With earthy precision—alive
In the pine-scented halls,
Through compliance recalls,
And the Little Rock legacy thrives.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Old Strain Quoting" icon="message-square">
During inspection, he quoted *old strains*—
The auditors paused, unclear on the gains.
They backed slowly away,
Unsure what to say,
And the filing resumed with its pains.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Pioneer Cannabis Trades" icon="truck">
His *gritty resolve* never fades—
Each record he stamps and upgrades
With the weight of the earth,
The cannabis birth,
And the pioneer cannabis trades.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Earthy Home" icon="mountain">
So if pine scents fill up the air
And compliance logs need some repair,
That's *Kushkeeper's* zone:
Where the earthy is home,
And the archives are handled with care.
</Limerick>
