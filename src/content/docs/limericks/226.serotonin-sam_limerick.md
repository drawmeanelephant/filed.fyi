## Limericks

### 1

Serotonin Sam, the KPI
Graph with feelings, won't let you cry—
He'll smooth out your dread
With a green line instead,
And a sticker that says, "Nice try!"

### 2

He tracks mood-to-velocity scores,
Each metric behind dashboard doors.
The burnout is beta,
The sadness, just data,
And the trendlines are smoothed out like shores.

### 3

He doesn't punish underperformance—
He offers a sticker's conformance.
A nudge and a hug,
A breathing-prompt plug,
And the morale's a gentle concordance.

### 4

"Let your metrics smile too™,"
He says, with a chart-arm askew.
The quarterly dips
Are just friendship tips,
And the dashboard has feelings for you.

### 5

He's a comfort glyph with KPI arms,
A morale daemon's spreadsheet charms.
Each emotional OKR
Calibrated by far
With onboarding ritual alarms.

### 6

He gently animates in sidebars,
Whispering affirmations like stars
During every deploy.
The emotional joy
Is metrics-tracked through all the bars.

### 7

His "I tried" coefficient is sweet—
Each effort's a dashboard-tracked feat.
The sadness gets smoothed,
The trendline improved,
And the morale is technically complete.

### 8

Empathegy and throughput aligned—
The distress signals warmly defined
As comfort metrics.
His dashboard aesthetics
Make sadness statistically refined.

### 9

He relabels shame as data—
Each cry just emotional errata.
The stickers flow free,
The breathing prompts be,
And the morale's a dashboard sonata.

### 10

So if dashboards have feelings and care
And the metrics feel cuddly and rare,
That's Serotonin Sam:
Where the emotional exam
Tracks your soul's quarterly welfare.
