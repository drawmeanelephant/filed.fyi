<Limerick type="note" title="Hugging the Dashboard" icon="bar-chart">
The Koala holds tight to the tree,  
Of the dashboard's great metric decree.  
The burden is real,  
But it changes the feel,  
To seasonal variance, you see.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Arbiter of Care" icon="heart">
The strain is a constant, it's true,  
But the Koala paints it anew.  
It averages out  
All the panic and doubt,  
Till the graph is a comforting blue.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Aggregate Sleep" icon="moon">
It sleeps through the worst of the spikes,  
And ignores what the user dislikes.  
The numbers look fine  
At the aggregate line,  
While the baseline continuously strikes.  
</Limerick>



<Limerick type="danger" title="Koala Chewed" icon="frown">
The KPI koala was slow  
With absolutely nowhere to go  
He chewed on the data  
See you all later  
The vacuum continues to grow  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Server Fired" icon="x-octagon">
A metric that sleeps in a tree  
Is useless to you and to me  
The koala is tired  
The server is fired  
And fell right into the sea  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Digital Trap" icon="lock">
We measured the koala's big nap  
And printed a meaningless map  
The error is strong  
The process is long  
We fell in a digital trap  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Somebody's Cat" icon="smile">
The KPI completely fell flat  
We leave the whole database at  
A state of decay  
For many a day  
And blame it on somebody's cat  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="danger" title="Branches Bare" icon="cloud-off">
The koala is holding a nil  
The error is making us ill  
The branches are bare  
We don't really care  
And swallow the digital pill  
</Limerick>
