<Limerick type="note" title="Hugging the Dashboard" icon="bar-chart">
The Koala holds tight to the tree,
Of the dashboard's great metric decree.
The burden is real,
But it changes the feel,
To seasonal variance, you see.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Arbiter of Care" icon="heart">
The strain is a constant, it's true,
But the Koala paints it anew.
It averages out
All the panic and doubt,
Till the graph is a comforting blue.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Aggregate Sleep" icon="moon">
It sleeps through the worst of the spikes,
And ignores what the user dislikes.
The numbers look fine
At the aggregate line,
While the baseline continuously strikes.
</Limerick>
