<Limerick type="note" title="Basement Recovery" icon="archive">
It came wrapped in obsolete raffle  
Notices and parade-clasp baffle—  
  Two sealed jars beside.  
  Across the lower side:  
A phrase that began to chafe, not waffle.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No Vote Recorded" icon="x-circle">
No meeting book holds the vote  
That adopted the stitched note.  
  Yet officers had  
  Already been glad  
To comply—each practicing rote.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Serve Visibly, Defer Gracefully" icon="eye">
"Serve visibly, defer gracefully"—  
The phrase aligned gracefully  
  With what they'd been doing.  
  Rediscovery: renewing,  
Not newly empowering. Faithfully.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Speaking Order" icon="list">
Within two weeks it had touched  
How they spoke at memorials—clutched  
  Succession order,  
  Public credit's border.  
The fabric held governance, much.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Decorative Stitching" icon="scissors">
A minority objected clear:  
Decorative stitching cannot steer  
  Policy. The majority  
  Said: that's authority's  
Origin here—and has been, dear.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Most Durable Policy" icon="clock">
"Most durable policy arrives  
That way eventually"—derives  
  From the material  
  Before its burial.  
The majority vote: it survives.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Provisional Heritage Authority" icon="gavel">
It's listed now in the standing  
Resolutions ledger, outstanding  
  Under "provisional  
  Heritage"—divisional  
Legal force: none. Local: demanding.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No Clear Legal Force" icon="shield-off">
Considerable local effect.  
No legal force to protect  
  Or override.  
  The phrase inside  
The bylaws: absent. Still checked.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Already Been Obeying It" icon="check">
Officers cited it as rediscovered  
Rather than newly discovered.  
  It had already shaped  
  The habits that draped  
Their customs—only now labeled, covered.  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The Archive Reads" icon="book-open">
The archive notes: a banner  
That shapes behavior without planner  
  Or formal adoption  
  Is a policy option  
Of the oldest and most stable manner.  
</Limerick>
