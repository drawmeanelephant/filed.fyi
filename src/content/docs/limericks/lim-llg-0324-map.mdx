<Limerick type="note" title="Kind of Missing" icon="package-open">
The Catalog faced a hard trap:
Too many old forms for one map.
So absence was named,
Nicely governed and framed,
And confusion received its own cap.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="CAAR Weather" icon="cloud">
Conceptually active, retired on the sheet,
Yet still in the workflow you meet.
That weather is CAAR:
Officially far,
But somehow beneath all your feet.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="LCGU" icon="building">
A local invention may start
As one office's practical art.
Use it long enough there,
And MAP learns to swear
It is canon in somebody's heart.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="STCP Braid" icon="braid">
Two forms can each bury the mate
While both still arrive on your plate.
MAP sees the knot,
Classifies it as thought,
And calls it co-equal by fate.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="AAOA Ghost" icon="ghost">
Some paperwork lives only where
Lorelog once mentioned it there.
No blank copy found,
Still citable ground:
An archival assertion of air.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Optics First" icon="glasses">
The memo advised, with a grin,
"Choose absence that keeps optics thin."
Not the truest defect,
Just the one that protects
Continuity language within.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Resolved by Naming" icon="stamp">
Publication alone did the trick:
Ambiguity suddenly clicked.
No form was restored,
Yet the office declared
The condition officially fixed.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Governed Condition" icon="shield">
Under MAP you need not repair
Every contradiction you bear.
Just sort it by type,
Draft a doctrine to cite,
And proceed like intention was there.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Doctrine Layer" icon="layers-2">
The protocol teaches the desk
To classify missing as-esque.
When presence gets weird,
And absence feels near,
You govern the blur, not the mess.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Folklore Approved" icon="scroll-text">
If everyone speaks as though done,
Implementation may count as one.
MAP knows the game:
Give folklore a name,
And doctrine arrives in the run.
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Grooming the Gap" icon="scissors">
The registry groomed every gap,
In the folds of the institutional map.
   It massaged the "void,"
   Until all were employed,
In a governed and silent-stage trap.
</Limerick>
