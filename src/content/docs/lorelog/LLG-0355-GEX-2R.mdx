A growing number of DOGE filers responded to Simulator Weather and Feed-Adjacent Artifact classifications by insisting that the original filing had described a real event badly rather than a rented event accurately. Existing appeals pathways allowed for adjudication, but not for structured supplementation. GEX-2R was introduced to solve this by giving filers a second chance to provide local anchor evidence without forcing Kindy to annotate margins with what they probably meant. 

The form is issued automatically whenever a filing is reclassified below Certified With Annotation and the filer selects either It felt real at the time and I am no longer sure what that means or I reacted, but I am unsure if that counted on the originating intake. DOGE describes this as compassionate procedural extension; Bricky describes it as a second form for the same sadness.

## FORM GEX-2R
### Reclassified Experience Resubmission
**For use after issuance of RECLASSIFIED, FEED-ADJACENT ARTIFACT, or SIMULATOR WEATHER determinations**

---

**Section A — Prior Filing Reference**

Original case number: ___________________

Original classification:
[ ] Feed-Adjacent Artifact
[ ] Simulator Weather
[ ] Ambient Echo
[ ] Certified With Annotation, disputed by filer anyway

Reason for resubmission:
[ ] I omitted local context.
[ ] I provided local context, but not in a way the rubric recognized.
[ ] The platform delivered the experience, but did not originate it.
[ ] I do not accept that what happened to me can be summarized as routing.

---

**Section B — Local Anchor Evidence**

Did a concrete event, relationship, obligation, or place in your actual life exist prior to platform contact?

[ ] Yes
[ ] No
[ ] I believe so, but the platform reached it first

If yes, describe the local anchor:

_______________________________________________________________

Acceptable anchor types include:
- named relationships
- scheduled obligations
- geographically specific events
- prior ongoing conflicts
- materially verifiable consequences

Unacceptable anchor types include:
- everyone was talking about it
- it was all over my feed
- the discourse felt unavoidable
- I saw a lot of posts

*Unacceptable here means insufficient, not dishonest.*

---

**Section C — Counterfactual Clarification**

Would this experience likely have occurred, in some form, if the platform had not delivered it to you first?

[ ] Yes
[ ] No
[ ] I resent this question
[ ] I cannot tell what my life would have been without the delivery system

The third and fourth answers route the form to secondary review.
Kindy requested these options after noticing filers were writing them in margins anyway.

---

**Section D — Residue Produced After Reclassification**

Since receiving the original DOGE classification, has anything in your behavior changed?

[ ] Yes — action taken after classification
[ ] Yes — action taken after the original event, not recorded previously
[ ] No
[ ] The classification itself has become part of the experience

*If the fourth box is checked, attach Addendum GEX-2R-M.
This addendum does not currently exist as a standalone form.
Staff are instructed to improvise respectfully.*
