## What Lives Here

The **Lorelog** directory contains case files for:

- directive conflicts (SOMA vs COMA vs C.U.N.T.I.E.R.)
- forms and registry anomalies
- sandbox experiments and their leaks
- mascot-adjacent incidents that accidentally mattered

Each entry behaves like an internal incident report that has been left alone long enough to become myth.  
Dates are accurate. Interpretations are opportunistic.

---

## How to Read It

Lorelog files are:

- sorted by **case number** (LLG‑####‑CODE),
- cross-linked by **relatedEntries**,
- grouped implicitly into strata (directive conflicts, silence annexes, forms drift, synthetic affect, breeding program, etc.).

They are not neutral history.  
They are the record of what the archive decided to remember.

Start here if you want:

- the first time a thing went wrong,
- the last time anyone admitted it, or
- the moment it was reclassified as "working as intended."
