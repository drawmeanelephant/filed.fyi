A scheduled surveillance visit was completed in full, with opening meeting, document review, corridor walk, and closing remarks all conducted according to protocol. No live operational activity occurred during the observed interval. 

The witness team therefore evaluated document control, prior corrective closure discipline, version hygiene, and staff fluency with scope language. These were deemed sufficient indicators of a mature system under conditions of temporary non-demonstration. 

One dissenting note asked whether certification had become confidence in the handling of proof rather than confidence in the underlying work. The note remains attached but is not headline-visible.
