This file concerns a period that became kinder in memory than it had been in operation.

The kindness was not fabricated.

Its scope was.

## Summary

A high-contact support interval originally documented as mixed, thin, and procedurally constrained was later cited in retrospective materials as an example of effective care mobilization.

At the time of occurrence, the interval contained:

- widespread check-in activity,
- strong acknowledgment coverage,
- witness participation,
- improved contact completion rates,
- limited change in rest approval or workload relief.

Later summaries retained the coverage.
They blurred the limits.

## Canonization Path

The interval’s reputational upgrade occurred gradually.

### Phase 1: Countable contact
The system successfully recorded a large number of support-shaped events.

### Phase 2: Narrative simplification
Review language began favoring “strong support presence” over “high acknowledgment density.”

### Phase 3: Symbolic inheritance
The interval became useful as a precedent, teaching example, and morale object.

### Phase 4: Historical compression
Future readers encountered the preserved warmth before the preserved constraints.

At that point the interval ceased to function as a mixed case.
It became a legend of sufficiency.

## Source of Distortion

The archive identifies three primary causes:

- support coverage was easier to preserve than relief evidence,
- witness structures made the room feel more accompanied than empowered,
- later retellings inherited tone from the record and consequence from wishful reading.

This is not unusual.
It is one of the standard ways institutions generate usable history.

## Review Finding

A retrospective audit compared original lane notes with later teaching citations.

The mismatch was stable:

- original records emphasized contact,
- later summaries implied mitigation,
- original notes named unresolved strain,
- later decks emphasized responsiveness,
- original witnesses noted limits,
- later materials treated witness presence as evidence of care depth.

One annotation from the audit observed:

> The interval is being remembered for its manners.

This sentence circulated widely in internal commentary and nowhere official.

## Correction Attempt

A proposal was made to relabel the interval as:

- visible but thin care,
- ceremonially preserved support,
- coverage-achieved, relief uncertain.

The proposal was partially accepted for doctrine references and quietly ignored in celebratory materials.

As a result, two histories now persist:

1. the interval as counted contact with modest dignity gains,
2. the interval as a near-myth of support adequacy.

Empathegy permits both to survive, but not without marking the split.

## Outcome

The phrase Canonized Support Interval entered archive use to identify periods in which support memory exceeds support consequence.

This category is not punitive.
It is protective.

It prevents future readers from mistaking preserved attentiveness for redistributed burden.

## Archive Position

LLG-0851-CSI is preserved because institutions often begin by counting contact, then graduate to remembering the count as care.

The archive keeps the earlier wording nearby so the legend has something to trip over.
