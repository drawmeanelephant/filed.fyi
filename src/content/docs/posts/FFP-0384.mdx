## Archive gloss

Each line sounds gentle. That is not the issue.

What this language usually preserves is not only access, calm, and passage, but also the inherited burden that made the compatibility necessary in the first place.

A replacement can arrive and still keep breathing through predecessor logic. In those cases, continuity is real, professionalism is real, and release is exactly what fails to materialize.

Filed under FFP-0384 so that, the next time repair is demonstrated primarily by the fact that old workaround-shaped behavior still functions perfectly, we can point back here and say, with due administrative calm, that compatibility was never the same thing as being free.
