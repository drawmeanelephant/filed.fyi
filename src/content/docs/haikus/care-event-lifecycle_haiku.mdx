## Haikus

<Limerick type="note" title="Now a Case" icon="briefcase">
Pain arrives at door  
Intake strips away the blood  
Now it is a case
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Rendered to Code" icon="code">
Rendered into code  
Emotion becomes a line  
On a corporate graph
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Empty Shells" icon="box">
Extraction begins  
Taking all the useful parts  
Leaving empty shells
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Do Not Scream" icon="volume-x">
Stabilize the curve  
Make the feeling sit right down  
Do not let it scream
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Floating Logs" icon="file-text">
The residual  
Is a ghost of what we felt  
Floating in the logs
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Spitting Bones" icon="rotate-cw">
Cycle spins again  
Feeding on the human heart  
Spitting out the bones
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Spike to Sleep" icon="activity">
Dashboard shows a spike  
Care is given as a script  
Spike goes back to sleep
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Ticket Closed" icon="check-circle">
Lifecycle complete  
Nothing is actually fixed  
But the ticket closed
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Metric Time" icon="clock">
We report the pain  
It becomes a metric now  
Used to buy more time
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Processing Grief" icon="archive">
Institutional  
Care is just a processing  
Of our endless grief
</Limerick>
