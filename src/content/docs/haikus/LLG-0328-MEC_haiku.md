## Haikus

### 1

Exception note climbs  
onto the judge's high chair now  
variance bows down

### 2

Context turned into  
primary authoritative  
proof against itself

### 3

Tail eats the whole file  
then gets cited as the spine  
office calls it neat

### 4

Appendix grows teeth  
condition loses the room  
paper loves a loop

### 5

Manifest looks off  
the excuse outlives the fault now  
that should scare someone

### 6

Supplemental text  
learns how to overrule facts  
while smiling politely

### 7

Variance explained  
until explanation becomes  
the thing being proved

### 8

Footnote crowns itself  
base record fades to weather  
index still salutes

### 9

Circular logic  
walks in with a stapled pass  
and gets good parking

### 10

If the excuse file  
starts sounding like bedrock law  
count your remaining doors
