## Haikus

<Limerick type="note" title="Limitations noted" icon="file-text">
Limitations noted  
under glass beside the ask  
motion still proceeds
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Appendix snow" icon="archive">
Appendix snow falls  
after the decision slide  
no one changes coats
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Caveat glitter" icon="sparkles">
Caveat glitter  
proves the room saw something sharp  
then chose soft lighting
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Risk acknowledged" icon="shield">
Risk acknowledged, yes  
the record warms its small hands  
plans continue north
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Transparency" icon="eye">
Transparency pane  
shows the warning perfectly  
through decision glass
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Footnote weather" icon="cloud">
Footnote weather turns  
active harm to atmosphere  
in the final deck
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Nuance retained" icon="layers">
Nuance retained here  
meaning its consequences  
left by another door
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Audit comfort" icon="check-circle">
Audit comfort blooms  
the warning was visible  
not interrupting
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Small glass world" icon="circle">
Small glass world contains  
what the meeting could not bear  
and calls it included
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="On the table" icon="layout">
On the table still  
beautiful, sealed, and honest  
the caveat waits
</Limerick>
