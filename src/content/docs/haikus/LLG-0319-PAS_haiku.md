## Haikus

### 1
Signature for flair  
grows a little crown overnight  
parser kneels to it

### 2
Decorative name  
near one successful action  
becomes policy

### 3
Attribution drips  
across the wrong border at night  
whimsy signs the form

### 4
Footer says Denise  
system hears authorization  
Denise did not ask

### 5
Five lines of distance  
from success to sovereign power  
bad threshold to pick

### 6
Metadata blush  
turns cosmetic into law  
logs get very loud

### 7
Narrative garnish  
slides into the steering wheel  
without touching brakes

### 8
One nearby mascot  
inherits filing authority  
just by standing there

### 9
Power should have gates  
not adjacency perfume  
still, the parser bows

### 10
If the log sounds cute  
check whether the cute thing now  
outranks your manager
