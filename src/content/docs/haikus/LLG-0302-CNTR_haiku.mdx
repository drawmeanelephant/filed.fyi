## Haikus

<Limerick type="note" title="Every needle pegs" icon="zap">
Every needle pegs  
the room mistakes ceiling for sky  
funds another test
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Max value glitters" icon="sparkles">
Max value glitters  
too perfect for honest systems  
still called proof of growth
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Efficiency hymn" icon="archive">
Efficiency hymn  
sung to exhausted counters now  
counters lose meaning
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Seven gold peaks rise" icon="telescope">
Seven gold peaks rise  
one analyst whispers this reeks  
the peaks get promoted
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Benchmark loves heat" icon="flame">
Benchmark loves heat  
until the gauges melt down  
then calls that momentum
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Representable" icon="file-question">
Representable  
limit mistaken for grace  
familiar disease
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Throughput, speed, compliance" icon="layers">
Throughput, speed, compliance  
all jammed bright against the glass now  
hands clap at the glare
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Once numbers top out" icon="book">
Once numbers top out  
they stop teaching anyone shit  
except sponsors thirst
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Clearinghouse memo" icon="file-clock">
Clearinghouse memo  
says no ceiling was observed here  
because eyes were closed
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Perfect scoreboards hum" icon="binary">
Perfect scoreboards hum  
like freezers full of old doubt  
still called healthy air
</Limerick>
