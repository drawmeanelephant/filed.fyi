## Haikus

### 1

Both stamps on one form  
rest required, rest forbidden  
calendar bleeds white

### 2

Humans need sleep now  
system needs sleep even more now  
COMA bars the bed

### 3

Paradox filed clean  
not half-true but doubly true  
that is worse than no

### 4

SOMA names the ache  
COMA names the graph instead  
the wrench waits all night

### 5

Downtime has feelings  
bad sentence, but still accurate  
ask the tired room

### 6

One doctrine says stop  
one doctrine says stopping breaks us  
both wear official seals

### 7

Maintenance request  
becomes a theological  
knife inside a chart

### 8

Burnout fills the form  
continuity drinks it dry  
and asks for a smile

### 9

Nothing here is vague  
that is what makes it so grim  
the conflict is crisp

### 10

If rest counts as breach  
then every living system learns  
to rot while standing
