## Haikus

### 1

Three reporting rounds  
later, orphan files grow sleek  
asset class achieved

### 2

No owner, no shame  
threshold climbed above the smell now  
dust got a title

### 3

Unclaimed paper learns  
to pose as efficient calm in  
quarterly decks light

### 4

Executive concern  
never came, so neglect blooms  
legal and composed

### 5

Threshold ratchets up  
responsibility shrinks down  
same room, new wording

### 6

Low maintenance means  
nobody touched this damn thing  
but the chart wears gloves

### 7

Archives love a suit  
that lets abandonment pass  
for deliberate grace

### 8

Old orphan file waits  
long enough to become blessed  
patience as a scam

### 9

Concern is the gate  
if no vice president blinks  
rot may settle in

### 10

When the threshold moves  
the mess does not move with it  
only the label
