## Haikus

### 1
Survey asks, politely,  
does this process feel too long now  
please sign here six times

### 2
Burden measured by  
adding one more burden first  
clean methodology

### 3
Red tape fills the room  
someone writes enough already  
comment redacted

### 4
Satisfaction form  
heavier than what it weighs  
clipboard eats the scale

### 5
Three offices sign  
that the signing feels normal, fine  
nobody believes

### 6
Metrics come back low  
committee forms to ask why  
the answer signs in

### 7
One complaint sprouts wings  
appendices gather dust  
the dust signs as well

### 8
Procedure flowers  
from the corpse of self-awareness  
still called feedback loop

### 9
Every extra pen  
proves the point more elegantly  
than the questions do

### 10
If a form asks you  
whether forms are crushing you now  
count the staplers first
