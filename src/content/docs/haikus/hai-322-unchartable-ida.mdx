## Haikus

<Limerick type="note" title="No field for that" icon="file-question">
No field for that pain  
still the hallway remembers  
what the chart forgot
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Blank survey box" icon="clipboard">
Blank survey box waits  
not empty, only careful  
around hungry graphs
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The number asked" icon="hash">
The number asked twice  
Ida answered with witness  
and one closed laptop
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Trendline stays calm" icon="trending-up">
Trendline stays calm green  
private notes grow weathered teeth  
under the clean band
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Free text survives" icon="align-left">
Free text survives where  
dropdowns fail to hold the shape  
of a living bruise
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Quiet interval" icon="volume-x">
Quiet interval  
not recovery, maybe  
filing learned to hide
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Anecdote kept" icon="message-square">
Anecdote kept warm  
until the metric admits  
its little border
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Graphable mercy" icon="bar-chart-2">
Graphable mercy  
missed the ungraphable need  
Ida kept both names
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Measurement bows" icon="gauge">
Measurement bows  
when it remembers it is  
not the whole window
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Off chart" icon="map">
Off chart, not unreal  
the archive keeps a margin  
wide enough for grief
</Limerick>
