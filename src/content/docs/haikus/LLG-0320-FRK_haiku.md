## Haikus

### 1
Two Form 32-As  
share one chair in the registry  
both insist they fit

### 2
Access or distress  
pick a ritual, any one  
the number shrugs back

### 3
Identifier  
meant to narrow the hallway down  
opens two doors wide

### 4
Catalog breathes dust  
downstream systems start guessing now  
guessing gets a stamp

### 5
Collision sounds sharp  
this one wore soft office shoes  
still broke both ankles

### 6
Same code, separate gods  
filers bring the wrong offerings  
and call it workflow

### 7
Consolidation  
found two meanings in one shell  
paper swallowed both

### 8
Roulette by stub name  
is a bleak administrative  
party trick, at best

### 9
Number loses nerve  
when it has to mean too much  
hallway fog rolls in

### 10
If the form code blinks  
and cannot say what it is  
back away from it
