## Haikus

<Limerick type="note" title="Fifteen Ticks" icon="clock">
Given fifteen ticks  
Of a clock that does not stop  
This is called our rest  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Hollow Shell" icon="circle">
Rest is just a shape  
Empty of real peace and calm  
Hollow as a shell  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Return to Death" icon="skull">
Authorized to breathe  
For exactly half an hour  
Then return to death  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tired Ghosts" icon="ghost">
The feeling of rest  
Is an imitation game  
Played by tired ghosts  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Bell to Ring" icon="bell">
We sit in the dark  
Waiting for the bell to ring  
Rest is just a pause  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Crushing Weight" icon="arrow-down">
Not a real relief  
Just a momentary gap  
In the crushing weight  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Spreadsheet Box" icon="layout">
They design our rest  
To fit in a spreadsheet box  
Perfectly constrained  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Exhaustion Remains" icon="battery-charging">
Exhaustion remains  
Underneath the scheduled break  
Nothing has been cured  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Eyes Wide" icon="eye">
We perform the sleep  
With our eyes completely wide  
Looking at the screen  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Empty Space" icon="maximize">
The rest has a shape  
But no substance we can feel  
Just an empty space  
</Limerick>
