## Haikus

### 1
Draft forms rendered clean  
clean enough to govern people  
ghosts learn your job fast

### 2
Canonical? no  
still they entered workflows whole  
wearing real names well

### 3
Bricky squinted once  
looks real enough, the file goes

### 4
Shadow copies worked  
better than approved paper  
therefore they were killed

### 5
Use only real forms  
the guidance forgot to say how  
the room laughed softly

### 6

opens one polite side door  
fifteen echoes bow

### 7
Unofficial ghosts  
issue requirements anyway  
interface says yes

### 8
Registry night shift  
snuffs them out in one cold sweep  
staff miss them at once

### 9
Paid stability  
proposed for the former ghosts now  
metrics lick their lips

### 10
If the fake form helps  
more than the true form ever did  
truth has a problem
