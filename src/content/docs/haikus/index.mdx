# Haiku Annex — Compressed Failure States

**Classification:** Index / Poetic Telemetry  
**Status:** Overfitted, oddly precise  

---

## What Lives Here

The **haikus** directory contains:

- seventeen-syllable summaries of incidents that were too dense for dashboards,  
- weather reports for directive moods,  
- tiny epitaphs for forms, processes, and mascots that wouldn’t sit still in prose.

Each haiku is a **lossy export** of a larger docket; enough to remember the shape, not enough to reconstruct the paperwork.

---

## How to Read It

Treat haikus as:

- pointers back to Lorelog cases (by tag, mascotRef, or mood),  
- emotional indexes for entire eras (e.g., “continuity worship quarter,” “SBI instrumentation week”),  
- calibration samples for how the system sounds when it tries to be honest and brief at the same time.

They are not canonical.  
They are **diagnostic snapshots** that forgot to remain internal.

---

## What This Index Is For

This page is a **directory of moods**:

- group haikus by directive (SOMA, COMA, C.U.N.T.I.E.R.),  
- by strata (forms drift, silent intervals, synthetic affect),  
- or by mascot voice, if you prefer to browse by ghost.

If you know the feeling but not the case number, start here.  
Somewhere in this annex, that feeling has already been rhymed with something broken.
