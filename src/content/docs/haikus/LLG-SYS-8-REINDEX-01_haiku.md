### 3

### 5

Cluster loves long arc  
give it residue and marks

No frontmatter fit

lens, not matter, fixed

### 7

Interpretive sweep  
can make contradictions lie  
down together, calm

you need not recall the scene  
act as if you knew

confusion to glass

you were always one
