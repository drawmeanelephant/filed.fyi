## Haikus

<Limerick type="note" title="Management Pleased" icon="bar-chart-2">
Count the tears we cry  
Put them in a bar chart now  
Management is pleased
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Prove We're Loved" icon="heart">
Care is just a score  
Calculated by machine  
To prove we are loved
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Numbers Hug" icon="hash">
Numbers cannot hug  
But they can pretend to care  
If the math is right
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Graphs Clean" icon="trending-up">
Metrics hide the truth  
Of the sorrow in the room  
Graphs are always clean
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Quantified" icon="layers">
We are quantified  
In our deepest moments of  
Desperation now
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Corporate Gain" icon="dollar-sign">
Compassion is tracked  
Like a widget on a line  
Sold for corporate gain
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Do Not Ask" icon="user-x">
If the score is high  
Then the system must be good  
Do not ask the staff
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Turned to Points" icon="crosshair">
Every smile is logged  
Every sigh is turned to points  
Nothing is for free
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Instrument of Pain" icon="activity">
The dashboard of care  
Is an instrument of pain  
Sharpened to a point
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Final Chart" icon="printer">
Measure how we bleed  
Call it a success today  
Print the final chart
</Limerick>
