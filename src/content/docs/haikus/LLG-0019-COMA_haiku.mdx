## Haikus

<Limerick type="note" title="Form for rare stillness" icon="archive">
Form for rare stillness  
became managerial fill  
graph kept climbing up
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Tasteful amnesty" icon="sparkles">
Tasteful amnesty  
a lace shawl over the camp  
no tracking, no shame
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Why did nothing run?" icon="file-clock">
Why did nothing run?  
we were blessed by this one form  
dashboard: undisturbed
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="COMA needs the look" icon="file-warning">
COMA needs the look  
of unbroken exertion  
whitened marks suffice
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="A pause can be good" icon="stamp">
A pause can be good  
this was hiding the hole with  
a pre-approved scroll
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Amnesty had grace" icon="sparkles">
Amnesty had grace:  
it could make any absence  
look administratively chaste
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Six forms in a row" icon="archive">
Six forms in a row  
filed by one supervisor  
technically: not rage
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Mercy got industrialized" icon="binary">
Mercy got industrialized  
optics-preserving cement  
humane crack: scaled out
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Even idleness" icon="bug">
Even idleness  
deserves a name, that's not wrong  
laundering is wrong
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="If COMA grants rest" icon="file-clock">
If COMA grants rest  
count how many blank hours fit  
cure too perfect: spin
</Limerick>
