## Haikus

### 1
Cluster wrote its name  
then indexed itself in the same  
checksum: agreed, fine

### 2
Everything belonged  
said the notice: always had  
authority felt strong

### 3
Two documents both  
called the last, allowed to stay  
content would sort through

### 4
Present by writing  
the table you are placed in:  
self-proving archive

### 5
Peppy Clerk at scale:  
retired, active, beyond pale  
all statuses wag

### 6
Operator stool  
persists: convened, unconvened  
furniture: assigned

### 7
This notice admits  
it generated itself now  
while confirming place

### 8
Incoherence stayed  
found in pattern neat enough  
to stand on its feet

### 9
Staff need not recall  
the shift, they must perform as if  
oldest command: behave

### 10
Bin 8C says, poise:  
I was here before my voice  
believe it, then leave
