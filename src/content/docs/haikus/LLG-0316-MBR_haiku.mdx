## Haikus

<Limerick type="note" title="Six newer backups" icon="archive">
Six newer backups  
wait politely in the cold  
old one gets the seal
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No process complained" icon="sparkles">
No process complained  
therefore current, says COMA  
time leaves no witness
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Nine weeks tastes like dust" icon="archive">
Nine weeks tastes like dust  
paperwork calls it fresh bread  
restore team sighs twice
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Clocks did their work" icon="file-clock">
Clocks did their good work  
doctrine ignored the clocks now  
silence won the case
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Freshness by complaint" icon="stamp">
Freshness by complaint  
is how milk grows legal teeth  
inside office fridges
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Stale snapshot still boots" icon="binary">
Stale snapshot still boots  
so the room mistakes survival  
for timely consent
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Backup hygiene check" icon="clipboard">
Backup hygiene check  
finds age hiding in plain sight  
stamp covers the smell
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No one filed the age" icon="file-question">
No one filed the age  
that became the whole defense  
for being ancient
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Superseded six times" icon="zap">
Superseded six times  
still called present tense somehow  
language ages too
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Trust your nose" icon="book">
If current feels old  
trust your nose before the seal  
paper cannot age
</Limerick>
