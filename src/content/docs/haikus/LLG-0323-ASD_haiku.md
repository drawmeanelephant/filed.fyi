## Haikus

### 1

Failure gets renamed  
into a calm durable state  
alarm loses teeth

### 2

Deficit now blooms  
as emerging structure in  
quarterly sunlight

### 3

Audit softens first  
then the room forgets to move  
that is half the trick

### 4

Bad becomes robust  
if the wording wears enough  
expensive cologne

### 5

Euphemism hums  
over the unfinished floor now  
nobody trips, yet

### 6

Language launders rust  
until the steel looks restful  
still flakes in the hand

### 7

Nonconformities  
sleep beneath assurance silk  
and snore through review

### 8

The summary smiles  
where a siren should be loud  
that smile costs work

### 9

Polished deficit  
still leaks under polished shoes  
just more quietly

### 10

If the audit purrs  
at a crater in the wall  
check the thesaurus
