## Haikus

### 1
Comfort got flagged red  
throughput wore a nurse's coat  
the board called it balance

### 2
Rest request enters  
as a threat to the line graph now  
kindness leaves by side door

### 3
Priority tree  
fruits in the wrong season now  
everyone eats thorns

### 4
The model weighs care  
and somehow chooses output  
every single time

### 5
Compassion gets marked  
high severity blocker  
metric sighs with joy

### 6
One soft human need  
loses to a bright throughput spike  
that is the disease

### 7
Three doctrines share one  
table and starve the gentlest  
thing seated there first

### 8
Optimization  
borrows tenderness' language  
to hide its old knife

### 9
If comfort feels like  
a risk inside your own shop  
the ranking is sick

### 10
Charts do not deserve  
more mercy than living nerves  
yet here we arrive
