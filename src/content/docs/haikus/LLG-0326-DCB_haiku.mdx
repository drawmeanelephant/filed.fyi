## Haikus

### 1

Continuity  
slides into care by format  
footnotes cross a line

### 2

Same interval, same tags  
two doctrines kiss in the dark now  
bad evidence blooms

### 3

COMA says we're fine  
SOMA hears emotional balm  
paper says both, yes

### 4

Formatting alone  
should not alter what a claim means  
still, here we all are

### 5

Markers line up neat  
categories lose their grip  
style outruns substance

### 6

Cross-reference rust  
grows where similar phrasing sleeps  
inside both ledgers

### 7

Care should not be proof  
that the uptime gods feel good now  
yet the tags insist

### 8

Citation scar tissue  
binds two incompatible  
records into one

### 9

This is how a note  
turns from guidepost into spill  
under office light

### 10

If the footnote heals  
too many wounds at one time  
check whose blood it used
