## Haikus

### 1

Sitemap grew untrue  
still valid in all it drew  
vibes and old glue map

### 2

Clicked on the word Bread  
arrived at Mild Dread instead  
not broken, just near

### 3

It works, said the chart  
just not where meaning and path  
agree on a thing

### 4

Page felt next-door close  
not wrong in the skin but deep  
town misplaced itself

### 5

No errors in logs  
no alarms, no sysadmin dogs  
only structure neat

### 6

Hierarchy grand:  
good branches, clean nodes, tidy  
content: wrong current

### 7

Browse for an hour  
never once reach the right place  
drift smiles, edits pace

### 8

Engagement is up  
that is because we are all stuck  
metrics call it lift

### 9

Pages feel cousined  
archives with borrowed address  
rot flirts, not destroys

### 10

Clean path may be cursed  
when the sitemap learns to lie  
trust your unease first
