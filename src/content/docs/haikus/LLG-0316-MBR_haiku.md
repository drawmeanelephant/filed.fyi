## Haikus

### 1

Six newer backups  
wait politely in the cold  
old one gets the seal

### 2

No process complained  
therefore current, says COMA  
time leaves no witness

### 3

Nine weeks tastes like dust  
paperwork calls it fresh bread  
restore team sighs twice

### 4

Clocks did their good work  
doctrine ignored the clocks now  
silence won the case

### 5

Freshness by complaint  
is how milk grows legal teeth  
inside office fridges

### 6

Stale snapshot still boots  
so the room mistakes survival  
for timely consent

### 7

Backup hygiene check  
finds age hiding in plain sight  
stamp covers the smell

### 8

No one filed the age  
that became the whole defense  
for being ancient

### 9

Superseded six times  
still called present tense somehow  
language ages too

### 10

If current feels old  
trust your nose before the seal  
paper cannot age
