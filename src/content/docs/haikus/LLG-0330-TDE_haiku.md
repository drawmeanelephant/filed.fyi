## Haikus

### 1
Both proofs nod at once  
though they face opposite winds  
the file fattens up

### 2
Mitigation notes  
and continuity claims now  
share one bloodstream, bright

### 3
Evidence tangles  
like cords in a server dark  
still called organized

### 4
Normalization  
let two hostile currents braid  
through one metal pipe

### 5
Paper loves a match  
even when the matched parts burn  
for different gods, hard

### 6
One request for rest  
one hymn to uninterrupted  
flow cite the same scar

### 7
Entangled proof grows  
more confident the less true  
its center becomes

### 8
The channels embrace  
badly, formally, with stamps  
and no pulse at all

### 9
Opposites can bind  
if the metadata lies  
with elegant hair

### 10
If both sides feel fed  
by one sentence, count your spoons  
something has been crossed
