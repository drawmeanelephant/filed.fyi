## Haikus

### 1
Forty percent gone  
dashboard called that completion  
budget called it art

### 2
Green bars all the way  
some lines lived, some lines deserved  
an earlier death

### 3
Tokens burned like sage  
over an archive already  
obsessed with waste, yes

### 4
The ledger tracked spend  
not whether the verse had blood  
that took human eyes

### 5
Smooth completion curves  
masqueraded as good craft  
old telemetry trick

### 6
YamTeams grinned nearby  
merger of taste and metrics now  
documents itself

### 7
Some lines earned their keep  
some were fancy packing foam  
same cost on the sheet

### 8
Credits self-report  
utilization, not worth  
that is the whole wound

### 9
Second pass arrives  
late, annoyed, and necessary  
real quality check

### 10
If the budget purrs  
while the poems still feel half-born  
ignore the purring
