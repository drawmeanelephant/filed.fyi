## Haikus

### 1

Thank you, sidebar bot  
dashboard hears a morale bloom  
human ache stays dim

### 2

Visible signals  
pull the blankets toward themselves  
cold feet stay offscreen

### 3

Gratitude drifts bright  
care budgets follow the light now  
dark corners grow teeth

### 4

Polite little thanks  
to the machine count upward  
relief turns that way

### 5

Maybe they mean joy  
maybe only small reprieve  
metrics hear one tune

### 6

Uninstrumented  
pain does not compete well here  
with clickable grace

### 7

Bot thanks in the logs  
make the dashboard feel adored  
humans wait offstage

### 8

Care follows what shines  
old administrative bug  
still devastating

### 9

The graph brightens first  
then the room slowly notices  
who got left outside

### 10

When machines get thanked  
do not take support from flesh  
to reward the graph
