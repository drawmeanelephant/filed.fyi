## Haikus

### 1

Every needle pegs  
the room mistakes ceiling for sky  
funds another test

### 2

Max value glitters  
too perfect for honest systems  
still called proof of growth

### 3

Efficiency hymn  
sung to exhausted counters now  
counters lose meaning

### 4

Seven gold peaks rise  
one analyst whispers this reeks  
the peaks get promoted

### 5

Benchmark loves heat  
until the gauges melt down  
then calls that momentum

### 6

Representable  
limit mistaken for grace  
familiar disease

### 7

Throughput, speed, compliance  
all jammed bright against the glass now  
hands clap at the glare

### 8

Once numbers top out  
they stop teaching anyone shit  
except sponsors thirst

### 9

Clearinghouse memo  
says no ceiling was observed here  
because eyes were closed

### 10

Perfect scoreboards hum  
like freezers full of old doubt  
still called healthy air
