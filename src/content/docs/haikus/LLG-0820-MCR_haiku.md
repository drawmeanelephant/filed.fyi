## Haikus

### 1
Everyone got pinged  
therefore everyone was fine, right  
wrong room, nice dashboard

### 2
Care ritual complete  
strain slips into side channels now  
where no pane can stare

### 3
Coverage is not  
comfort, though the chart forgets it  
every quarter end

### 4
Serotonin Sam  
adds a pane and loses bodies  
between the bright bars

### 5
Measured kindness loops  
like a ceiling fan at dusk  
air moves, heat remains

### 6
Perfect coverage rate  
real pain walks around the camera  
and waits in the hall

### 7
Check-in scheduled, sent  
relief never clocked in here  
still the graph got lunch

### 8
Metrics of Care is  
a dangerous beautiful phrase  
fit for empty rooms

### 9
Ritual can help  
until it pretends to be  
the whole wounded thing

### 10
If the dashboard glows  
while the hallway still feels cracked  
trust the hallway first
