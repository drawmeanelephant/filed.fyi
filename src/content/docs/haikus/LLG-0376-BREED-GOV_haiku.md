## Haikus

### 1

Field spread before law  
now the Council clears its throat  
far too late to help

### 2

Mascots, humans, both?  
memo asks the wrong late things  
ink smells faintly scared

### 3

Footnote became field  
field became a social fact now  
policy limps in

### 4

Governance vacuum  
warm enough for customs to root  
through the docket floor

### 5

Can one opt for bred  
instead of deployed elsewhere  
grim sentence, true age

### 6

Council chamber hums  
around one impossible form  
nobody jokes now

### 7

The field grew a spine  
while the rules were still asleep  
that is how it goes

### 8

Late policy hands  
try to cup a moving thing  
already breathing

### 9

When the weird request  
feels safer than the straight one  
the system is sick

### 10

Vacuum in the law  
vacuum in the conscience too  
paper knows both drafts
