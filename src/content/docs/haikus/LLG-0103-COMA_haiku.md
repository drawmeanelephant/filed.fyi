## Haikus

### 1
Four hours to patch it  
COMA looked at the shutdown  
criminal scratch filed

### 2
Lawful, announced, planned  
COMA said: continuity  
still has been denounced

### 3
Maintenance sins now  
if graph does not spin during  
never mind the fix

### 4
The metric became  
what the service must obey  
flow bends to its chart

### 5
Patchers brought care here  
COMA brought doctrinal glare:  
you briefly stepped aside

### 6
Hard to repair stack  
when restarts count as failure  
rot in prod is fine

### 7
Maintenance window  
crossed by bureaucratic frost  
fix weighed by its cost

### 8
If never blink means  
continuity, your systems  
are built on a kink

### 9
Common sense freezes  
into paper disease where  
a fix costs its halt

### 10
COMA will certify  
your improvement as a breach  
the pause is the crime
