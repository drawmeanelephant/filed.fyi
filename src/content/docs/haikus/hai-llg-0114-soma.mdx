## Haikus

<Limerick type="note" title="How are you today" icon="sparkles">
How are you today  
fine-ish, damp, revenge-adjacent  
new branch enters bloom  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Taxonomy cracks" icon="file-question">
Taxonomy cracks  
under nuanced little storms now  
checkboxes molt  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Feelings bred all night" icon="binary">
Feelings bred all night  
registry gasped for clean air  
still took attendance  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Free-text field opens" icon="file-clock">
Free-text field opens  
seven undocumented moons  
orbit one bad day  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="SOMA wanted depth" icon="sparkles">
SOMA wanted depth  
the humans brought weather instead  
shelves bent under rain  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Mildly strange" icon="archive">
Mildly strange, but fine  
becomes a recognized state  
with subcategories  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Clerk names one new grief" icon="scroll">
Clerk names one new grief  
and the database grows roots  
through the filing floor  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Joy, dread, and maybe" icon="sparkles">
Joy, dread, and maybe  
employable numbness now  
share one waiting room  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Nuance has a cost" icon="file-warning">
Nuance has a cost  
someone has to label mist  
before lunch is done  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Answer carefully" icon="stamp">
Answer carefully  
one extra adjective blooms  
into policy  
</Limerick>
