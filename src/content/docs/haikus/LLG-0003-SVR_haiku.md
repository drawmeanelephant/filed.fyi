## Haikus

### 1
Seventeen records  
sprouted fields no schema knew  
catalog: squatter

### 2
vibeIndex arrived  
moodLeak followed quietly  
charm is not a law

### 3
They entered by inch  
one tiny addition each  
drift posed as a source

### 4
Datty came with knife  
for metadata living free  
three fields met the ban

### 5
The purge had a rite  
as though structure said a prayer  
then Datty just clicked

### 6
One clerk said: those fields  
helped explain the human entries  
schema said: perhaps not

### 7
Three fields were expunged  
not deprecated, not rolled back  
deleted so hard

### 8
Forms Graveyard sent note  
asking kindly for them back  
no reply was made

### 9
You don't get the flood  
in one dramatic loud thud  
you get one dumb key

### 10
If records grow limbs  
call Datty, but understand:  
cleanings leave a mark
