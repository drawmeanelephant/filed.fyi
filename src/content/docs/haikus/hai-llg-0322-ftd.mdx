## Haikus

<Limerick type="note" title="Policy let go" icon="file-clock">
Policy let go  
template kept the empty box  
people fed the ghost  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Blank square on the page" icon="file-question">
Blank square on the page  
acts more lawfully than law  
old habits salute  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="No one asked that field" icon="archive">
No one asked that field  
still the room could not leave it blank  
silence filled it in  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Legacy label" icon="stamp">
Legacy label  
lingers like old perfume now  
reviewers inhale  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Decorative grave" icon="archive">
Decorative grave  
for a requirement long dead  
yet still collecting  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Simplified policy complexified" icon="layers">
Simplified policy  
complexified by one old shape  
ink obeys the frame  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Obligation grows" icon="bug">
Obligation grows  
where presentation mimics law  
clean little parasite  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Someone writes N/A" icon="file-clock">
Someone writes N slash A  
and still feels slightly guilty  
that is drift at work  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The field should be gone" icon="file-question">
The field should be gone  
instead it moonlights as dread  
inside every form  
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="If the box survives" icon="zap">
If the box survives  
past the law that once fed it  
do not call it neutral  
</Limerick>
