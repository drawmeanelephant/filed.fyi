## Haikus

<Limerick type="note" title="Files went missing, so" icon="archive">
Files went missing, so  
analytics called that care  
absence got a badge
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Orphaned assets now" icon="sparkles">
Orphaned assets now  
signal curated restraint  
what a slick perfume
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Corrective action" icon="archive">
Corrective action  
stops when loss looks strategic  
dust applauds softly
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Missing is not neat" icon="stamp">
Missing is not neat  
until the report wants it to be  
then it wears a tie
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Audit optics bloom" icon="telescope">
Audit optics bloom  
over a hole in the shelf now  
no one fills it in
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Deferred presence, sure" icon="layers">
Deferred presence, sure  
that's one way to say vanished  
with a straight office face
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="The scan finds a gap" icon="file-question">
The scan finds a gap  
management sees a vignette  
of low-touch intent
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="Stewardship by loss" icon="sparkles">
Stewardship by loss  
is a gorgeous little lie  
fit for quarterly decks
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="When neglect learns poise" icon="book">
When neglect learns poise  
it can pass for curation  
for a fiscal year
</Limerick>

<Limerick type="note" title="If the report smiles" icon="bug">
If the report smiles  
at what should make someone swear  
check who needs the graph
</Limerick>
