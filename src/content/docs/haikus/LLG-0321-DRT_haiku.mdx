## Haikus

### 1

Take care of yourselves  
arrives stamped corrective action  
warmth exits the wire

### 2

Tiny edits drift  
until kindness wears handcuffs now  
no one meant to build this

### 3

SOMA whispers rest  
COMA receives a breach notice  
same packet, worse room

### 4

Routing table fog  
turns support into summonses  
staff stop trusting both

### 5

Advisory tone  
scrapes against enforcement steel  
and comes back as threat

### 6

Configuration  
rarely screams before it bites hard  
it drifts, then it owns

### 7

One good sentence leaves  
wearing soft bureaucrat wool  
returns in armor

### 8

Nine weeks of bad route  
enough to teach a whole team now  
mercy can be sharp

### 9

If care reaches you  
like an order from upstairs  
check the channels first

### 10

Some systems break trust  
not by malice, but by drift  
which is somehow worse
