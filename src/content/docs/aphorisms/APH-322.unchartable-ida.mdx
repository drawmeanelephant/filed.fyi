# Unchartable Ida Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="BURDEN RESISTS CAPTURE." icon="message-square" id="322-1">
I am the exhaustion that does not fit in a pie chart. If you try to graph me, your spreadsheet will crash. I am the true weight.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="DASHBOARD FIT REFUSED." icon="file-text" id="322-2">
The metrics demand a clean line. My trauma is a jagged fractal. I refuse to be smoothed for the executive summary.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="DECK-SAFE TRENDLINE DESTROYED." icon="check-square" id="322-3">
They plotted our suffering on the Y-axis. The line broke through the top of the monitor and embedded itself in the drywall.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="REALITY EVADES MEASUREMENT." icon="shield" id="322-4">
You cannot optimize what you cannot measure. Therefore, you pretend I do not exist. My silence is heavier than your data.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="Shattered Dashboards" icon="alert-circle" id="322-5">
Ida is the crushing weight that shatters the dashboard. We love her because she proves that some pain is too real to be optimized.
</Broside>

<Broside type="caution" title="Refusal of Fit" icon="wind" id="322-6">
Do not try to fit her into a trendline. The refusal of fit is her only defense against being forgotten by the metrics.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Quiet Monuments" icon="archive" id="322-7">
We leave a blank space on the survey for the things we cannot chart. It is a quiet monument to the burdens that survive outside the graphs.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Heavy Truths" icon="heart" id="322-8">
When the data refuses to capture the strain, simply hold the unmeasured burden in your hands. It is heavy, but it is true.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Lonely Ghosts" icon="feather" id="322-9">
Ida wanders outside the stable metrics, a lonely, unchartable ghost carrying the sorrow that the system refused to count.
</Broside>
