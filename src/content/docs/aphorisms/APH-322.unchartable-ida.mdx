# Unchartable Ida Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="BURDEN RESISTS CAPTURE." icon="message-square" id="322-1">
I am the exhaustion that does not fit in a pie chart. If you try to graph me, your spreadsheet will crash. I am the true weight.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="DASHBOARD FIT REFUSED." icon="file-text" id="322-2">
The metrics demand a clean line. My trauma is a jagged fractal. I refuse to be smoothed for the executive summary.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="DECK-SAFE TRENDLINE DESTROYED." icon="check-square" id="322-3">
They plotted our suffering on the Y-axis. The line broke through the top of the monitor and embedded itself in the drywall.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="REALITY EVADES MEASUREMENT." icon="shield" id="322-4">
You cannot optimize what you cannot measure. Therefore, you pretend I do not exist. My silence is heavier than your data.
</Broside>
