# Witness and Seal Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="Witness and Seal Layer. Boundary active." icon="award" id="WIT-SEA-1">
Seals calm readers faster than evidence deserves. Relevance expired before processing resumed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Witnessing establishes. Continuity retained." icon="shield" id="WIT-SEA-2">
The function of the seal is legitimacy maintenance under conditions of incomplete certainty. The system kept the ritual and misplaced the function.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Reciprocal Fragility. Leverage absent." icon="link" id="WIT-SEA-3">
Reciprocal Fragility: a witness valid only because another witness once cited it. Silence entered the record with full procedural honors.
</Broside>
