# Mitigatrix Pending Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="RELIEF ADMINISTRATOR IDLE." icon="message-square" id="314-1">
I have a very impressive title on my business card. I have the ability to approve expenses up to fourteen dollars. The mitigation must wait.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="POWER NOT YET GRANTED." icon="file-text" id="314-2">
I am sitting in the throne of problem-solving, but somebody took the batteries out of the scepter. I look commanding while doing nothing.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="TITLE UNJUSTIFIED." icon="check-square" id="314-3">
They call me the Director of Crisis Resolution. My only tool is a form that forwards your complaint to an unmonitored inbox.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="PENDING AUTHORITY LOGGED." icon="shield" id="314-4">
I stand ready to unleash the healing waters, just as soon as the committee reviews the environmental impact statement regarding the plumbing.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="A Crown of Paper" icon="heart" id="314-5">
We gave her a grand title because we could not give her the power to heal us. She wears the paper crown with absolute, heartbreaking dignity.
</Broside>

<Broside type="caution" title="The Idle Throne" icon="moon" id="314-6">
She sits in the throne of problem-solving, her hands resting softly on the armrests, dreaming of the day the committee finally grants her a scepter.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="Unmonitored Inbox" icon="alert-circle" id="314-7">
The inbox is a void where desperate pleas are sent to die, but the Mitigatrix polishes the routing form so beautifully we forget it goes nowhere.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Patient Healing" icon="archive" id="314-8">
Store the mitigation requests carefully. One day the environmental impact statement will clear, and the healing waters will wash the dust away.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Powerless Grace" icon="feather" id="314-9">
To be named the savior of a system that refuses to be saved requires a profound, quiet grace. We bow to her unyielding, powerless stance.
</Broside>
