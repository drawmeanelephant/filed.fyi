# Mitigatrix Pending Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="RELIEF ADMINISTRATOR IDLE." icon="message-square" id="314-1">
I have a very impressive title on my business card. I have the ability to approve expenses up to fourteen dollars. The mitigation must wait.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="POWER NOT YET GRANTED." icon="file-text" id="314-2">
I am sitting in the throne of problem-solving, but somebody took the batteries out of the scepter. I look commanding while doing nothing.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="TITLE UNJUSTIFIED." icon="check-square" id="314-3">

</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="PENDING AUTHORITY LOGGED." icon="shield" id="314-4">

</Broside>
