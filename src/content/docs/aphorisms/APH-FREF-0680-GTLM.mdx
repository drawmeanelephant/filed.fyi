# Gratitude Telemetry Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="Metrics drift. Leverage absent." icon="smile" id="GRA-TEL-1">
Institutions are easily flattered by being thanked. Silence entered the record with full procedural honors.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Scarcity brightening. Record sealed." icon="sun" id="GRA-TEL-2">
Scarcity brightening: minimal help appears more effective than it is because scarcity amplifies appreciation. Administrative clarity arrived after usefulness had departed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Such signals are socially meaningful. Interpretation pending." icon="check-circle" id="GRA-TEL-3">
A thank-you signal does not mean the sender was helped enough. Nothing was resolved. The record now looks official.
</Broside>
