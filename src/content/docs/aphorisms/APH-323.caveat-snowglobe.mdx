# Caveat Snowglobe Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="CAVEAT VISIBLY PRESERVED." icon="message-square" id="323-1">
The fatal flaw in the plan is placed inside this beautiful glass sphere. We shake it, watch the warnings swirl like snow, and then ignore it completely.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="CAUTION CLAIMED. DECISION MADE." icon="file-text" id="323-2">
We have thoroughly documented the reasons this will fail. Having achieved perfect transparency, we will now proceed exactly as planned.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="WARNING SEALED DECORATIVELY." icon="check-square" id="323-3">
The risk assessment is a lovely centerpiece on the boardroom table. Nobody is allowed to open it. It is purely ornamental risk.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="FLAW KEPT UNDER GLASS." icon="shield" id="323-4">
The red flags are enclosed in a vacuum. We can see them waving frantically, but we cannot hear the alarm. The glass is very thick.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Protected Warnings" icon="archive" id="323-5">
We keep the fatal warnings under glass, not to ignore them, but to protect them from the harsh weather of actual decision-making.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="Beautiful Doom" icon="alert-circle" id="323-6">
The snowglobe contains the exact blueprint of our impending doom. It is so breathtakingly beautiful that we can only stare as the ship sinks.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Soothing Risks" icon="wind" id="323-7">
Shake the globe when you feel anxious. Watching the recorded risks swirl like snow is surprisingly soothing when nothing can be changed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="caution" title="Ornamental Truth" icon="feather" id="323-8">
Do not break the glass to read the caveat. Its purpose is ornamental now; releasing the truth would only spoil the executive summary.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Quiet Flaws" icon="heart" id="323-9">
The warning is preserved visibly, an intricate little toy of caution that sits quietly while the grand mistakes are made.
</Broside>
