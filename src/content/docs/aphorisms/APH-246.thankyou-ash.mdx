# Thankyou Ash Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="THIN HELP ACCEPTED." icon="message-square" id="246-1">
You offered a link to the FAQ. I said 'thanks' because society requires it. The problem is still on fire, but the interaction is formally closed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="ASH COLLECTED." icon="file-text" id="246-2">
Every hollow expression of gratitude burns down into a fine grey powder. I sweep it into the corners of the Jira instance.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="WEIGHT RETAINED." icon="check-square" id="246-3">
The ticket is marked 'Resolved.' The burden has merely shifted from the system's ledger to my own shoulders. I am very grateful.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="POLITE DESPAIR LOGGED." icon="shield" id="246-4">

</Broside>
