# Minute Absolution Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="EXONERATING SUMMARY PUBLISHED." icon="message-square" id="297-1">
The project was a catastrophic waste of capital, but the bullet points summarize it as a 'valuable learning exercise.' We are cleansed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="FORGIVENESS BY COMPRESSION." icon="file-text" id="297-2">
Three hours of screaming recriminations have been compressed into the sentence: 'Alternative viewpoints were shared.' The archive forgives all.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="INNOCENCE ACHIEVED THROUGH DRAFTING." icon="check-square" id="297-3">
I rewrote the paragraph four times until nobody was technically at fault. The grammar itself absorbs the guilt.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="RECORD CLEARED OF SIN." icon="shield" id="297-4">
The minutes have been approved by the board. The disaster officially never happened. Go in peace, and sin no more against the bottom line.
</Broside>
