# Minute Absolution Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="COMPRESSION SOLVENT" icon="file-text" id="297-1">
The secretary applies heavy pressure to the stenographer's pad until three hours of shouting fit neatly into the margins of a single paragraph.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="DRAFTED INNOCENCE" icon="pen-tool" id="297-2">
We scrub the rough edges off the verbs until the summary slides perfectly into the binding of the official record.
</Broside>

<Broside type="caution" title="ARCHIVAL FORGIVENESS" icon="archive" id="297-3">
The draft is passed back and forth across the table until the ink itself agrees that nobody in the room was technically at fault.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="BINDING ABSOLUTION" icon="check-square" id="297-4">
Once the binder clip snaps shut, the history of the room is officially cleansed. We do not unclip the pages, lest the original tone escape.
</Broside>
