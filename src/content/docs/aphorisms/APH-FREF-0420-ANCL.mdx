# Accreditation Nesting Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="The table where auditors are audited. Jurisdiction shifted." icon="users" id="ANC-1">
The table where auditors are audited, but not for what they saw. Silence entered the record with full procedural honors.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="ANC is a necessary myth. Status acknowledged." icon="shield" id="ANC-2">
ANC is a necessary myth: a body that must be presumed in order for stacked confidence to feel complete. Administrative clarity arrived after usefulness had departed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="In turn. Boundary active." icon="award" id="ANC-3">
ANC's influence is measured by how many seals are required to declare that a process is appropriately overseen. Nothing was resolved. The record now looks official.
</Broside>
