# Policy Afterglow Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="RESIDUAL OPTIMISM DETECTED." icon="message-square" id="269-1">
The initiative has failed, but the slide deck still glows with the radioactive half-life of synergy. Do not touch the graphs; they are still hot.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="LANGUAGE OUTLASTS OUTCOME." icon="file-text" id="269-2">
The words 'paradigm shift' are painted in phosphor on the wall. The shift never happened, but the wall is very easy to find in the dark.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="BRIGHTNESS MAINTAINED INDEPENDENTLY." icon="check-square" id="269-3">
We stopped funding the project, but the launch party was so good that the echo is still generating positive KPI momentum.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="REALITY LAGGING BEHIND GLOW." icon="shield" id="269-4">
The servers have crashed, but the mission statement remains illuminated. Please read it by the light of the electrical fires.
</Broside>
