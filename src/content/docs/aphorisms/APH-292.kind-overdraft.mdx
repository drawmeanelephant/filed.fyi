# Kind Overdraft Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="WARMTH ISSUED BEYOND CAPACITY." icon="message-square" id="292-1">
I promised we would fix it for you. I have no budget, no developers, and no authority. I paid for your hope with a bad check.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="STRUCTURAL DEFICIT DECLARED." icon="file-text" id="292-2">
My empathy account is overdrawn by six thousand units. The bank of corporate goodwill is calling to repossess my smile.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="RESOURCES EXCEEDED." icon="check-square" id="292-3">
I offered you a shoulder to cry on. I do not actually have a shoulder. It was a metaphor drafted by the PR department.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="FOLLOW-THROUGH CAPACITY ZERO." icon="shield" id="292-4">
The warmth was genuine in the moment, but the moment evaporated the second I opened Jira. I am emotionally bankrupt.
</Broside>
