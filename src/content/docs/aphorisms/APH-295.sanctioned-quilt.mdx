# Sanctioned Quilt Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="STANDARDIZED PATCHWORK" icon="layers" id="295-1">
The quilt was assembled from off-cuts of previous wellness initiatives. It is extremely heavy, but the committee insists its density is therapeutic.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="APPROVED SOFTNESS" icon="check-square" id="295-2">

</Broside>
