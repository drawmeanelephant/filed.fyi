# Clicky the Orphaned UI Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="Guide to nowhere now. Status acknowledged." icon="mouse-pointer" id="clicky-1">
Clicky once guided users through onboarding flows that no longer exist. The system kept the ritual and misplaced the function.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Wonders why nothing is right. Boundary active." icon="help-circle" id="clicky-2">
Wonders why nothing is right. Silence entered the record with full procedural honors.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Flow persistence. Continuity retained." icon="activity" id="clicky-3">
Evidence that flows can outlive their own destinations. Administrative clarity arrived after usefulness had departed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Orphaned Interface" icon="mouse-pointer" id="clicky-4">
An orphaned interface serves no purpose but to catalog the misclicks of the desperate.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Abandoned Blink" icon="alert-circle" id="clicky-5">
The UI element blinks not for interaction, but as a symptom of its abandonment.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Stray Click Meaning" icon="mouse-pointer" id="clicky-6">
Every stray click is an attempt by the operator to assert meaning over a dead variable.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Frustration Output" icon="frown" id="clicky-7">
The button executes nothing; the user's frustration is the only successful output.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Pure Architecture" icon="shield" id="clicky-8">
A deprecated tool is the purest form of system architecture, free from human utility.
</Broside>
