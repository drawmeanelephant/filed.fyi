# SOP Amnesia Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="The archive was self-sealing. Leverage absent." icon="lock" id="0401-GLP-1">
The archive was self-sealing. No external entry point existed. The form remained intact. The situation did not.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Self referential compliance. Record sealed." icon="clipboard" id="0401-GLP-2">
Compliance is not the same as completion. The archive has been compliant for eleven years. Meaning adjusted around the winning selector.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="The Tribunal acknowledged the study existed. Interpretation pending." icon="help-circle" id="0401-GLP-3">
The Tribunal acknowledged the study existed. It declined to verify that it had occurred. Relief remained outside scope.
</Broside>


<Broside type="tip" title="Never Concludes" icon="clock" id="never-concludes">
Good laboratory practices require that the experiment never concludes.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Unimpeded Growth" icon="trending-up" id="unimpeded-growth">

</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Expected Failure" icon="alert-triangle" id="expected-failure">

</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Deletes Methodology" icon="trash-2" id="deletes-methodology">

</Broside>

</Broside>
