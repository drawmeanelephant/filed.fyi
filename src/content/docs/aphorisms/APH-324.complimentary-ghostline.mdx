# Complimentary Ghostline Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="SERVICE OFFICIALLY DISCONTINUED." icon="message-square" id="324-1">
The servers were unplugged three years ago, but the portal still enthusiastically invites you to 'Sign Up Today!' The form submits straight to the void.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="TEXTUALLY OPERATIONAL STATE." icon="file-text" id="324-2">
I am a ghost haunting a PDF manual. I promise features that no longer exist, described in present tense, promising eternal functionality.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="PRESENT-TENSE SUNSET MAINTAINED." icon="check-square" id="324-3">
The marketing copy refuses to acknowledge death. We offer robust solutions for a protocol that was deprecated during the Obama administration.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="COMPLIMENTARY ILLUSION SERVED." icon="shield" id="324-4">
If you dial the support number, it rings endlessly in an empty datacenter. But the hold music is free of charge.
</Broside>
