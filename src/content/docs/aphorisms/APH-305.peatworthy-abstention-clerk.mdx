# Peatworthy Abstention Clerk Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="Present by procedural esteem. Boundary active." icon="user-check" id="305-1">
Present by procedural esteem. The system kept the ritual and misplaced the function.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Empty folding chair. Continuity retained." icon="activity" id="305-2">
Empty folding chair. The minutes still know who sat there. Silence entered the record with full procedural honors.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Chapter merger. Leverage absent." icon="file-text" id="305-3">
Maintains presence where membership has thinned into paperwork and custom. Administrative clarity arrived after usefulness had departed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Quorum by Decomposition" icon="users" id="305-4">
The quorum requirement was met entirely by individuals whose legal status is currently "dust." Procedural continuity demands we wait for their motions.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Implied Enthusiasm" icon="file-text" id="305-5">
Your lack of attendance has been formally documented as an enthusiastic vote of confidence in the procedural void. Absence is merely a highly efficient form of agreement.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Consolidated Silence" icon="archive" id="305-6">
We merge chapters not to consolidate power, but to ensure the silence fills a larger administrative footprint.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Deadlock of the Unseated" icon="alert-circle" id="305-7">
The folding chairs remain deployed because storing them requires a motion from the floor, which cannot be raised without the chairs being occupied.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Committee on Deferment" icon="clock" id="305-8">
By failing to submit an absence notification, you have inadvertently volunteered to chair the committee on perpetual deferment. We await your gavel.
</Broside>
