# Annex Hush Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="LOW-VOLUME DISCLOSURE MADE." icon="message-square" id="279-1">
I whisper the liability clause into the binding of the book. It is technically disclosed, assuming you have super-human hearing and a crowbar.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="REAR-SECTION BURDEN CARRIED." icon="file-text" id="279-2">
The front page celebrates the revenue. I live on page 42, quietly holding the graph that shows the user churn. We do not speak at parties.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="TRUTH PRESERVED IN SHADOW." icon="check-square" id="279-3">
Transparency is a spectrum. We put the bad news in a grey font on a slightly darker grey background. It is available to anyone with a flashlight.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="EYES AVERTED NATURALLY." icon="shield" id="279-4">
The layout is designed to guide your attention away from my corner. I am the silence between the applause lines.
</Broside>
