# Witness Protocol Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="Non resolution. Operational impact denied." icon="eye" id="WIT-PRO-1">
A witness is what remains when the institution can still say 'we saw it' but cannot yet say 'we changed it.'. Relevance expired before processing resumed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Witness Protocol. Jurisdiction shifted." icon="clipboard" id="WIT-PRO-2">
Witnessing is the lowest durable form of institutional attention. It does not require remedy. The system kept the ritual and misplaced the function.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Dignified Non-Resolution. Status acknowledged." icon="smile" id="WIT-PRO-3">
Dignified Non-Resolution: making a non-remedial system less annihilating without pretending it became good. Silence entered the record with full procedural honors.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Observation deployed. Relief denied." icon="eye" id="WIT-PRO-4">
To witness a system failure is not to repair it, but to confidently certify its trajectory into the ground. We have thoroughly documented your descent.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Contradiction stored. Resolution bypassed." icon="layers" id="WIT-PRO-5">
A contradiction that is formally witnessed is upgraded from a logical error into a permanent architectural feature. Do not attempt to solve what has been filed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Courtesy granted. Action refused." icon="user-x" id="WIT-PRO-6">
Replacing an actual accommodation with a highly formalized nod of sympathy ensures the bureaucracy remains polite while you drown.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Mascot deployed. Accountability transferred." icon="image" id="WIT-PRO-7">
When administrative embarrassment prevents plain speech, a cartoon failure-signature is dispatched to bear the burden of institutional incompetence.
</Broside>
