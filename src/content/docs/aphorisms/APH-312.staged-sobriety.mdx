# Staged Sobriety Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="POST-CRISIS COMPOSURE ATTAINED." icon="message-square" id="312-1">
The servers have stopped smoking. We straighten our ties and adopt expressions of grave professional concern for the cameras.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="VISIBLE SERIOUSNESS DEPLOYED." icon="file-text" id="312-2">
We were screaming in the hallway ten minutes ago. Now, in the town hall meeting, we speak in slow, measured tones about 'structural learnings.'
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="EMOTIONAL OPTICS REGULATED." icon="check-square" id="312-3">
The panic has been carefully folded and placed in a drawer. The face we present to the shareholders is made of calm marble.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="PREVENTABLE HEAT IGNORED." icon="shield" id="312-4">
We do not mention who left the stove on. We only discuss our profound commitment to fire safety going forward. Look how solemn we are.
</Broside>
