# Escalady Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="UPWARD ROUTING INITIATED." icon="message-square" id="245-1">
I carry your grievance to a higher floor. The carpet is thicker there, but the indifference remains structurally identical.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="CEREMONIAL POISE MAINTAINED." icon="file-text" id="245-2">

</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="AUTHORITY UNCERTAIN." icon="check-square" id="245-3">

</Broside>

</Broside>
