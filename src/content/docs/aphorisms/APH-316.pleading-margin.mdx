# Pleading Margin Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="HANDWRITTEN ADDITION DETECTED." icon="message-square" id="316-1">
The form is perfectly typeset. At the bottom, in frantic blue pen, it says 'PLEASE DO NOT SHUT OFF THE POWER.' The system cannot read blue pen.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="SIDE-CHANNEL DESPERATION LOGGED." icon="file-text" id="316-2">
The official request was denied. The unofficial tearstain on the envelope was routed to the incinerator.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="HUMAN EDGE FLATTENED." icon="check-square" id="316-3">
I exist in the white space around the mandatory fields. I am the silence screaming for context that the database schema will not accept.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="PLEA RECORDED AS NOISE." icon="shield" id="316-4">
Your desperate annotation has been digitized. OCR interpreted your plea for mercy as 'f2x%9'. The algorithm finds 'f2x%9' irrelevant.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="Soft Ink" icon="heart" id="316-5">
The blue ink bled through the official lines, a frantic human heart trying to rewrite the structure. We keep it because it is beautiful, not because it is valid.
</Broside>

<Broside type="caution" title="Erased Desperation" icon="wind" id="316-6">
Do not wash the tearstains from the margins. They are the only moisture left in a system that has run dry of empathy long ago.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Cherished Noise" icon="archive" id="316-7">
We file the handwritten pleas in the lowest drawer. They will never change the outcome, but we refuse to let them burn.
</Broside>

<Broside type="danger" title="Screaming Margins" icon="alert-circle" id="316-8">
The blank space around the mandatory fields holds the true weight of the form. The silence there is absolutely deafening, and we love it dearly.
</Broside>

<Broside type="note" title="A Tender OCR" icon="feather" id="316-9">
The machine cannot read your plea for mercy, but the clerk who scanned it rested their hand on the page for an extra second. That must be enough.
</Broside>
