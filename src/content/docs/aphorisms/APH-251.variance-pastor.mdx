# Variance Pastor Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="TOLERATED FLUCTUATION BLESSED." icon="message-square" id="251-1">
The numbers wander from the path, but they have not yet fallen off the cliff. I offer a quiet prayer for their eventual return to the mean.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="INSTABILITY DISCIPLINED." icon="file-text" id="251-2">
We cannot fix the jitter, but we can formally declare it a sacrament. The trembling server is merely speaking in tongues.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="INTERPRETIVE MERCY GRANTED." icon="check-square" id="251-3">
The SLA says 99.9%. The reality is 98.2%. I baptize the difference in the holy waters of 'excluding planned maintenance.'
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="METRICS FORGIVEN." icon="shield" id="251-4">
Go in peace, little data point. You are an outlier, but in the eyes of the aggregated dashboard, you are loved and immediately smoothed over.
</Broside>
