# Footnote Pallbearer Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="DEAD CONTEXT CARRIED." icon="message-square" id="261-1">
The main paragraph declares a clean victory. I carry the bleeding caveats down here at the bottom of the page in 8-point font.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="FORMATTING INTACT. AUTHORITY REDUCED." icon="file-text" id="261-2">
The citation is perfectly formatted in APA style. It links to a document that proves the entire strategy is flawed. Nobody reads past the executive summary.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="BELOW THE LINE BURIAL." icon="check-square" id="261-3">
We did not delete the dissenting opinion; we merely buried it under an asterisk. It is technically transparent and functionally invisible.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="GHOST OF CONTEXT LINGERS." icon="shield" id="261-4">
I haul the heavy corpse of nuance to the bottom margin. May it rest in peace, undisturbed by the confident assertions above.
</Broside>
