# Witness Protocol Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="Non resolution. Operational impact denied." icon="eye" id="WIT-PRO-1">
A witness is what remains when the institution can still say 'we saw it' but cannot yet say 'we changed it.'. Relevance expired before processing resumed.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Witness Protocol. Jurisdiction shifted." icon="clipboard" id="WIT-PRO-2">
Witnessing is the lowest durable form of institutional attention. It does not require remedy. The system kept the ritual and misplaced the function.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="Dignified Non-Resolution. Status acknowledged." icon="smile" id="WIT-PRO-3">
Dignified Non-Resolution: making a non-remedial system less annihilating without pretending it became good. Silence entered the record with full procedural honors.
</Broside>
