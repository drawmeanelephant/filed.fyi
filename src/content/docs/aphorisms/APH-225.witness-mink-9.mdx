# Witness Mink-9 Aphorisms

<Broside type="tip" title="EPISODE LOGGED AS NORMALCY." icon="message-square" id="225-1">
I remember the shivering of the load balancer. The charts called it 'expected variance.' I call it 'the Tuesday terror.' Neither went in the report.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="SUB-THRESHOLD OBSERVATION SECURED." icon="file-text" id="225-2">
It almost failed. It screamed in the logs, but the threshold held. I carry the memory of the near-miss so the dashboard doesn't have to.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="TRAUMA UNCLASSIFIED." icon="check-square" id="225-3">
Because no alerts fired, we are required to pretend the system slept soundly. I am the custodian of the silent sweats.
</Broside>

<Broside type="tip" title="RETENTION OF THE UNSEEN." icon="shield" id="225-4">
The metrics show a flat line of success. My fur stands on end with the static of a thousand averted disasters. Both records are technically accurate.
</Broside>
