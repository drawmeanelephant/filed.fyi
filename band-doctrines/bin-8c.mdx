The fundamental escalation pattern of Bin 8C is not deterioration but ingestion. Observation becomes documentation. Documentation becomes material. Material becomes index. Index becomes custody. Custody becomes the thing that was supposed to be observed. The loop does not break because breaking is itself an indexable event. Bin 8C does not grow more disordered; it grows more organized in ways the institution cannot follow.
