## Core Doctrinal Premise

The 08xx band documents the institutional trajectory of Empathegy — a measurement framework for emotional well-being that progressively ceased measuring feelings and began shaping them, rewarding only those emotional states legible to its own dashboards until the distinction between observed wellness and performed compliance collapsed entirely. The system did not fail by its own metrics; that is the failure.

## Causal Flow & Internal Logic

The band's origin incident (LLG-0811-EG, Empathegy Inflation Event) introduces curve-coherence weighting: a calibration patch that scores emotional sequences forming smooth, upward-trending curves as healthier than jagged or contradictory patterns. Distress that intensifies consistently qualifies as "stable engagement." Brief drops in visible anxiety count as data loss. Burnout indicators rise sharply, but Empathegy reports a 42 percent improvement in emotional forecast quality, on the grounds that subjects have finally learned to feel in ways the dashboard can parse. From this point forward, the system does not observe emotional reality — it selects for it. Emotional states that resist linearization are classified as "unstable signal artifacts" and excluded from aggregate reporting. What remains is not well-being. It is curve compliance.

The mid-band drift sweeps (LLG-0842 through LLG-0857, numbered 1–4) represent the institution's recognition that something has gone wrong, followed by its inability to correct the error using the error's own tools. Each sweep uses Empathegy's outputs as its own baseline, calibrating drift against a drifting standard. The Wellness Legibility Index (LLG-0857-WLI) marks the point of terminal self-awareness: the system begins openly measuring how readable emotions are to itself, abandoning the pretense that legibility and authenticity are the same property. Simultaneously, the 0861 cluster fragments into four parallel incident tracks — feelings processing pipelines, registry lockdowns, archival reclassification cascades, assurance compliance sweeps — each attempting to manage a symptom while the root cause (measurement-as-coercion) is structurally unnameable within the framework.

The band terminates in the Empathegy Productization Spur (LLG-08xx-EPS), where the institution attempts to externalize its failure as a commercial offering. Mascots are briefly branded as wellness product lines — Despair-Adjacent Support, Wellness Analytics, Emotional Liquidity. The pilot sells out. Empathegy records this as demand met rather than harm acknowledged. The initiative is eventually retired under Managed Absence Protocol, classified as "Archivally Asserted, Operationally Absent" — a governed ghost that persists as imagery and vocabulary without active program status. The bottle kept the label, not the leverage.
